Title: Halloween Parade 2011!
Post by: toddg on October 15, 2011, 12:44:07 AM
The JHBG Halloween Parade starts at 5 pm on Halloween. Line up near PS 222, which is 87th Street and 37th Avenue. March, skip, dance, stroll to PS 69, where the goodie bags are handed out. All are welcome.

The Jackson Heights Beautification Group Halloween Parade Committee organizes the annual parade. At the end of the parade, more than 3,000 bags of treats are handed out to children.  The success of the parade is thanks to the volunteers who put the parade (and the bags of treats) together, and to the many local businesses that contribute financially and otherwise.  Each year we need volunteers to help stuff the goodie bags (usually the day before Halloween) and to give them out at the parade. We need to sign up the volunteers in advance, so we know how many people will be helping and with which tasks. All volunteers who help with stuffing the goodie bags are treated to pizza and soda, and all of the volunteers are invited to a thank-you party the night of the parade. Contact the JHBG by phone (718-565-5344) or email (info@jhbg.org) if you're interested.

The Halloween Parade Committee thanks all volunteers and business supporters who make the parade possible!
