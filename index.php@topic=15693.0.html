Welcome to JH!

My favorite Indian places are Delhi Heights (74th st and 37th Road) and Indian Taj (the buffet) - it's on 74th near the big Patel Brothers supermarket.

Espresso 77 is great for coffee (77th st right off 37th Ave.).

Pizza:  Sac's or Louie's

Burgers:  Emoji Burger

Chinese:  Saw Shack

And try some of the momo carts!  Amdo Kitchen and Potala Fine Food (green van outside Diversity Plaza) have really good ones.

I've been living out here for 4 years.  And I'm really amazed and delighted at how often great new places keep cropping up. 

My favorite go-to places--for quality, vibe, ambition and all-around tastiness:  Phayul, Ayada, Rico Tinto. 

And the hidden-jewel one or two trick ponies:  Esquina De Cameron, Arapa Lady and 969 Cafe.

BUT...here's one that some of you might dig, seriously tucked away, above Playground Thai on Roosevelt and 72nd.   A recent favorite and really nice staff to go with good food and fun vibe:

https://www.yelp.com/biz/jeaw-hon-new-york-queens
