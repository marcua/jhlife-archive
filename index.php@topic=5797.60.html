We goto Juju's as much for the welcoming atmosphere as we do the food itself.  The owner is a nice guy, clearly he loves what he does.  He takes a personal pride in his product and it completely shows.  The decor is simple yet attracting and comfortable.

And the opening sentence in no way diminishes the food.  His vegetable cream cheese has a subtle nuance to it that makes it great.  Recently he started with his semolina almond cake which is point on perfect.  His salads are also well proportioned and well balanced.  And his homemade iced tea (summers only I think) is fantastic.

Only chink is in the bagels.  They are decent for what they are and as good as you are going to find in the neighborhood, but for proper bagels, we still make the occasional hoof to Bagels & Brew on 30th.
