Warning:  If you happen to enter Orchid Liquors for the first time be certain to ask for the price since prices are not marked.  I have a friend who visits occasionally and drinks Smirnoff Vodka.  I usually get a pint at either the liquor store on Roosevelt & 78 St or Broadway & 74 St.  Both places its $6.99 + tax or $7.61.  I happened to be shopping at Food Dynasty yesterday and entered Orchid Liquors and noticed they had Smirnoff pint.  I asked the price and was told $12.99.  I asked him (he's not the owner) to repeat the price and again $12.99 and I walked out.
