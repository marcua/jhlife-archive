Title: ACLU fundraiser this weekend at Espresso 77
Post by: espresso 77 on February 02, 2017, 07:50:52 PM
Just a quick word to let everyone know that starting tomorrow, February 3, Espresso 77 is participating with over 500 independent coffee shops in a nationwide fundraiser for the American Civil Liberties Union.  We will donate a percentage of our profits from every sale of brewed coffee that we make over the weekend.

The ACLU is a defender of our inalienable right to free speech, and they are also currently working overtime to defend immigrant and refugee families caught in the current confusing web of executive orders and court decisions.  As a proud member of the vibrant, artistic and diverse neighborhood of Jackson Heights, Espresso 77 is committed to supporting immigrant, refugee and civil rights.

You can learn more about the fundraiser here:
