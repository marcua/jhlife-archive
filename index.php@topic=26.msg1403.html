Hair and Nail Salons
« on: November 04, 2007, 09:29:45 AM »
I've been going to Gulzar Beauty Salon, 74th and Roosevelt, for traditional eyebrow threading for several months. For the price $5 and the efficiency you can't beat this place. I really enjoy the no frills yet all business approach. No appointment necessary and I've only had to wait, for literally one minute at most, before a chair opened up and I was taken care of in five minutes. The women who work there are nice and the place is clean enough. Before moving here I used to go to a swanky threading place in SoHo where I thought it was a deal at $25, but no more. Gulzar may not have the trendy scene of a Manhattan spa, but it's the place for me to get what I need.

Hope other people can check it out.

Anyone recommend a place for pedicures?

« Reply #11 on: January 06, 2008, 09:43:22 PM »
Todd, eyebrow threading is a dipilitory (sp?) technique whereby hairs along the eyebrow line are removed using thread and a twisting motion rather than the more conventional methods like waxing or tweezing.  Threading is quicker and if done correctly, beautifully sculpts the eyebrows.  It's a traditional method that is popular in India, perhaps this explains its popularity in Jackson Heights.  I've had this done twice, and on each occasion, my eyebrows looked amazing.
