Nothing fancy at first, maybe just some padding to protect the roof, some lawn furniture, etc. Does anyone have experience with this? I could do the work myself, but I don't want to do anything illegal, and I would want to know about relevant codes and have a good estimate of costs before I propose it to the board.

If you want to legally occupy the roof you either need to build a structure on top of the roof to span between structural walls or reframe the entire roof structure to support the load of people being up there.  A roof is designed and constructed to carry snow and a few people for occasional maintenance.  It's not built as sturdy as an apartment floor.
