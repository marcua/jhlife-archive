Neighborhood Chat / Re: Should the 2020 US Census ask about citizenship?
« on: April 05, 2018, 11:39:57 AM »
I say no.

While I do think that is information the federal government ideally should have, we have to be pragmatic. Asking this question will negatively impact the accuracy of all the other questions being asked. If gathering accurate information about who is living where is your goal - no matter what your intentions to do with that information - then asking about citizenship status will do more harm than good.

As for the argument that it is only intended to count citizens, that is simply not true and literally never has been. It has always been intended to count residents.

Furthermore, as members of a community that presumably has a large number of undocumented residents, it is specifically in our direct interest to oppose such a measure. Indeed, we have good reason to believe it is targeted against us.
