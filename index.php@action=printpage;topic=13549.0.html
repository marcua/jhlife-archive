Title: Jackson Heights in the 1970s - a fantastic homemade video!
Post by: alexnaveira on August 05, 2015, 12:08:52 AM
Hi all! I'm new here. Hoping I post in the right section. :)

I found this cool video - Jackson Heights in the 70s filmed by high-school students. It's really neat to get a good look at the neighborhood 30 years ago in film.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5x1ILIsN7lU (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5x1ILIsN7lU)

Name any areas you can find! I found:
-Bulova Park
-79th street on 31st Avenue (near the Lady of Fatima Church going towards Northern Boulevard, you can get a clear look at the church in the beginning of the video. Doesn't look like the school accompanying it was built yet!)
-Walbaums at 75st on 31st Avenue, formerly titled Pantry Pride, along with the area directly outside.

Enjoy the video!
