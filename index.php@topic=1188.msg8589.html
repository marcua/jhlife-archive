When i moved her about 5.5 years ago Northern boulevard had three antique/used furniture stores all of which have closed.  The most recent to close was the place on Northern between 80 and 81st.  This place relocated and turned trendy and is now in Long Island city.  It is called nook and crannie and it also has a sister store in Ditmars.  They are still reasonable although more expensive than when they were located in our neighborhood.  Try them out, they may have something you like. 

I also love antiques.  There is a place in flushing called golden oldies that is huge and expensive but very fun to browse through.  Have fun. 

P.S.  MIke has a space in the basement of the building accross from where his shop is and he has some old chairs etc.  He took me for a look once.  The stuff is pretty run down, but if you can afford for him to redo them they could turn out very nice.
