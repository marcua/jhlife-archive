HATEFUL Flyer put on Car Windows
« on: August 30, 2015, 04:34:14 PM »
Today I noticed a flyer that was on car windows on 82nd street for the second time this month. The content of the flyer is very alarming and I was wondering whose attention we should bring this to. The flyer (go to this link for a copy of the flyer https://docs.google.com/document/d/1tFaBOxXm_RdQsdegVHHPRnm2UIfwqtKKd7Hg3J4r3ZM/edit?usp=sharing ), which is in English and Spanish, bolds a section of bible scripture that states a hateful message towards members of the LGBT community and states that they "deserve to die". This is very concerning as Jackson Heights is a very culturally diverse neighborhood and very accepting of all kinds of people. There is clearly someone or some group of people posting this around the neighborhood which makes me worried for people's safety. The flyer doesn't say who its from or any affiliation to any organization. I understand that people have freedom of speech and are allowed pretty much whatever they want to say, but does anyone else think we should be concerned about this? Someone is clearly putting a lot of energy and money to produced these flyers and post them around our neighborhood. I think we need to bring this to some organizations attention and our local congressman.
