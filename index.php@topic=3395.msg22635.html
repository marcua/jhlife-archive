Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

JHFG - One Year Anniversary
« on: September 27, 2009, 10:40:04 AM »
Hello everyone,

First off, Thanks for making the Jackson Heights Food Group such a success. In the beginning, my aim was to continue this group until I ended up eating by myself twice in a row. Well, it doesn't seem like that's going to happen. Every month, we end up staying out later and later, and  it becomes less about the food and more about us getting together. I'm very happy with all the enthusiasm and friendships made through the group.

This is the 1 year anniversary, and on October 18th we are going to return to one of our favorite winners in the past year.

Because of the amount of choices, you can vote for multiple restaurants - but please vote for only your top-tier picks. Also, keep in mind that this is going to be late October so weather might be a factor. Your choices:

Himalayan Yak
Chow Thai
Delhi Heights
Hornado Ecuatoriano
Mi Estrella
Casa Colombia
Anzuelo Fino
New Broadway Seafood (saturday, Oct 17th dim sum)
Tortilleria Nixtamal (then timmy o's)
Ayada
Spicy Mina
and Fiesta Mexicana closed, so that's out.

Looking back I see how lucky some of us are to live in the neighborhood. This is Best in the City stuff. Queens represent.

There are still many exciting restaurants out there to discover and new ones pop up all the time. I have no worries of running out of places to go.

Thanks again for making it so enjoyable. Much more Stuff to come.

Let's discuss! what were some highlights? lowlights? where would you like to go back and where did you miss?
