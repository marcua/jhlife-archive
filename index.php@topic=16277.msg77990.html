Regarding the Fillmore: I too am curious as to what happened to those beautiful towers. I'm familiar with the bottom photo but not the top (the building looks like it's still under construction; and indeed, the Fillmore was built in 1930, the year the photo was taken), so thanks for posting this. Here are two theories as to why the towers were removed: Perhaps at some point somebody deemed the towers structurally unsound, or maybe, sometime in the late forties or fifties when dreary, boxlike buildings devoid of architectural ornamentation seemed more popular, the towers were thought to be too fussy and old-fashioned.
