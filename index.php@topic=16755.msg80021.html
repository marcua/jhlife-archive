DINERS BEWARE!

Okawa Sushi at 77-05A 37th Ave does not have their letter grade posted and for good reason. It's not just their very dirty windows.  Their last Health Department inspection flagged 3 critical violations including live roaches in the food area.  Yikes!  When I inquired inside the restaurant as to why no letter grade was posted, they showed me a B letter grade shoved under the counter, out of sight, and said they were not posting it - in violation of the law!

Saw Shack's (84-22 37th Ave) was even worse, with 4 critical violations, including live mice in the food area, and food not protected from contamination - double yikes!   Too bad, I really did enjoy their Crab Cheese Rangoon!

All the disgusting details, as well each restaurant's inspection history, is available on the Dept. of Health's website:
http://a816-restaurantinspection.nyc.gov/RestaurantInspection/SearchBrowse.do

Also, before everybody piles on here note that it's 2 critical violations for Okawa, not 3 (as the original poster said), and that the roach violation explicitly says "Live roaches present in facility's food and/or non-food areas" so we don't know if it's in the food area or in a non-food area.
