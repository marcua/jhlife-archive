This is what I found out about that strip of stores.

Although they have different addresses, that is exclusively for the United States Postal Service (USPS) use. The exact address of the property is:
7801/09 37th Avenue
Jackson Heights NY 11372-6641

The property was built in 1941 and last legally altered in 1997; it has Landmark status.

There are only 3 commercially leased tenants currently listed on the property:
C & S Mart Inc
Ingrid's Hair & Nail Salon
Newman's Jewelers Almani Corp

The property may contain 7 commercial units and does not have a certificate of occupancy for any residential units. It consists of only one floor but does have a common cellar running the length of the property.

On 5/12/2004 the property was purchased by:
Jackson 37 Company
7801 37TH Ave
Jackson Heights, NY 11372-6641

with the 1st lien holder
Flushing Savings Bank, FSB

and a 2nd lien holder added on 7/1/2004
Dovenmuehle Mortgage, Inc.
4225 Queens Blvd
Long Island City, NY 11104-2805

In the total amount of $1,815,321.00.

On 10/8/2004 the NYC DOB disapproved an application to convert existing stores into a church. In violation of the disapproval notice, a church was located on the property and the property owner has never paid the fines and assessments on the multiple violations they have been served by the NYC DOB amounting to thousands of dollars for work done without permits, for work done in violation of Landmark status, for work done without NYC DOB approval and for work done contrary to their Certificate of Occupancy.
