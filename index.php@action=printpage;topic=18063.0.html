Title: Re: Planet Fitness - 86th and Roosevelt
Post by: wlirfan on July 30, 2018, 01:16:07 PM
Became a PF global member because we go upstate most weekends and there's a PF up there that we go to.  The upstate PF is amazing -- super clean, new machines, all in great shape, about 10 tanning rooms with different machines, a couple of massage chairs, a UV room, etc.  The one by us is more limited, but I only use it when I want to do some cardio and I don't want to travel to Equinox in the city so I only go downstairs to the weight area to change in the locker room if it's winter.

The downstairs weight area looks small and is pretty much always crowded.  I find it less appealing than the Blink in our area because it has zero natural light and the air circulation isn't that great.  The upstairs cardio area is great, with a ton of machines, but upkeep is an issue.  Many TV's on the machines don't work anymore, some need to be oiled and adjusted and there are signs that the machines are fraying.  They need better maintenance.

On the plus side, it's always clean, the locker room is big and I never had a problem finding a locker.  If you mainly do cardio, this gym is pretty good.  The people that work out here clean the machines after working out and are friendly.  If you occasionally do weights, then I'd say you can make due here.  If you want to focus on weights, find another gym (not Blink either).

All in all, it's a good gym, especially for the price.  Become a black card member and you can use any PF anywhere.  The PF's outside the city are amazing, since I've been to quite a few.
