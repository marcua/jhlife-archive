Long before the gang of four and the summer coup antics, I was shocked to watch him throw Willets Point under the bus a year ago and then had the gall to show up less than a month or two later to "support them" with Councilmember Ferreras, who by then had replaced him. I've found Assemblymember Peralta to be responsible, intelligent and thoughtful. Unlike other (outgoing) elected officials, he and those in his office have been friendly and responsive to my queries.  I for one will be doing what I can to see this through to the end.

Now, what about a replacement for Mr. Peralta?

First things first, I know, but doesn't he have to relinquish his seat in order to run for state senate? Seems like the Queens County organization will have some ideas about this, so shouldn't we?

Seems like this is all part of a larger piece that should be thought-through, especially by those who will be doing street-level campaign work, as I will be.
