Real Estate & Home Improvement / Re: Do you know someone who can reglaze my tub?
« on: March 26, 2011, 03:03:05 PM »
Yes. I got it done by a sub for a guy called Chris whose company is called pro star home improvements. (Tel: 347-600-8645) He was cheap, less than $400 as far as I recall. There were some bubbles on it the first time he did it. And I just discovered that my upstairs neighbor's shower was leaking into my bathroom. So it was ruined. (You have to wait min 24 hours+ for the glaze to dry.) He came back and fixed it all the very next day, put up a tarp to catch the water from upstairs so it wouldn't be ruined again. I'd highly recommend him. And Chris is a nice guy too.

Just one piece of advice. He will paper up around the tile/wall around your bath so that the spray doesn't get on your walls. But I would advise also doing it on the rest of the tiles around the bathroom if you have any. A very fine film did get on the tiles. Mine are white like the bath so you can't see anything but you can feels a slight grittyness on the tile.

Real Estate & Home Improvement / Re: Aris Investments
« on: March 06, 2010, 11:09:25 AM »
I lived in a building on 34th Ave and 82nd St managed by Aris Realty for 5 years. Their offices are in Astoria. I know they run a few buildings in the area. There's  one on 81st (?) opposite the library that's theirs. I never had any problems with them. They were generous with heat/hot water (unlike other buildings I've lived in), had decent supers in the building that maintained the building well. I never heard of anyone in the building having trouble with them either. My neighbors lived in the building for a lot longer than me but then it was a rent controlled building. It was by far the best apt I've lived in.
