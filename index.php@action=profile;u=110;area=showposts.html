We wanted to find out if any other buildings/coops in the neighborhood have already converted, are in the process of making the change or at the beginning planning stages as we are.

We'd like to learn:

1. What engineering firm(s) are you working with?
2. Are you making the conversion to dual-fuel (oil and natural gas) or just to #2 oil?
