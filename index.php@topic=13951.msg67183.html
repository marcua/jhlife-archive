iHola! Jackson Heights neighbors hope your all having a lovely week. I just wanted to let all families know our Spanish immersion program- Bilingual Sprouts will start its winter registration on December 4th. If you are interested in our program please feel free to attend one of our sessions for a free trial on Friday November 13th from 10:15 to11:00 am for our 20 months and 3's class Or 11:00-11:45 for our Spanish Baby music class for babies 6months-19months. Or on Saturday November 14th from 10:00-10:45 for 20 months to 3's and 11:00-11:45 am for 4yrs to 7yrs. Below is a description of each of our classes and our contact information. Please let us know if you have any further questions and want to reserve a spot for a trial class.

Our Classes
Bilingual Sprouts is a (Mommy daddy/ Caregiver) and me Spanish immersion program for children ages 6 Months to 7yrs old. Using an early childhood Spanish curriculum that strongly emphasizes and facilitates adult involvement, Bilingual Sprouts provides developmentally appropriate activities that are engaging, interactive and hands-on such as read-alouds, art, games, music and movement. Bilingual Sprouts will immerse your child in a new language while having fun!

Spanish baby music class
Our Spanish baby music class for babies 6months to 19 months is a program created to give children a multi-sensory experience while being immersed in the Spanish language. Our classes are taught through the use of verbal input (Visual and auditory), which includes original and traditional music, movement, puppetry, storytelling and singing.

Our 20 months to 7 years Spanish ​​class
Similar to our Spanish baby music class in that it is a program created to give children a multi-sensory experience while being immersed in the Spanish language. Our class is taught in a full immersive format and our taught through the use of verbal input (Visual and auditory), which includes singing, literacy, puppetry, art and exploration, as well as take home experiences to bring back and share with class and group work. Each experience is purposely engaging the child, in a multi- sensory learning process. Both teacher led and structured classes where we encourage exploration, discovery, socialization and creative thinking.

Winter Registration will begin on December 4th! Refer a friend that registers for the winter semester and get $10.00 discount!
