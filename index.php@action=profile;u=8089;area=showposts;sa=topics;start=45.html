Neighborhood Chat / Queens Botanic Garden
« on: April 25, 2015, 07:03:58 PM »
I went today, trying out my new Brooklyn Botanic membership which carries reciprocal privileges, and which I got a year free with my new NYC ID card.

Very happy to report than most (not all) of the paths are now open, though there's still construction going on. Lovely daffodils and tulips et al, and white cherry blossom - the trees in the cherry circle look close to blooming, as do the crab-apples.

And they finally finished the paving etc for the footbridge. I don't know if the gates will be open to the garden at some point (perhaps during free admission times?). Anyway, I walked along College Ave when I left the garden and climbed the shallow stairs and walked across the bridge to the park.

I wish they had at least an exit turnstile at that point, but for the moment I'm enjoying the improvement!

Just a reminder that it's free Sunday 4 to 6, and Wednesday 3 to 6.
