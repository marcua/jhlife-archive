You need sky to make this work.

I have a Mohu and it works great.   But I'm on the 5th floor and am able to position it near a window with decent sky exposure.  I'm not sure it would work as well on the 1st floor staring out at the walls of my building's courtyard.

Another option is to check if your building has an antenna.  There's nothing magic about an HD antenna... it works on the same frequencies as your parents' old UHF antenna.  Some buildings still have these on the roof, and will run a feeder line down to your apartment.  That can provide the best reception of all, if it's an option for you.
