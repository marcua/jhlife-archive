I used to live on that block and we parked for a few years at the lot on Roosevelt b/w 78 & 79. Your car will get dirty from being under the 7 and they have to maneuver it in and out for you (open 24 hours as far as I recall). I had considered renting a private garage but the alleyways seemed kind of sketchy to me.  After a while, we realized that our "failure rate" (as measured in parking tickets) was less than the monthly parking rate, so we stopped paying for parking and just paid tickets here and there. I am not sure how much the lot is now, because I haven't parked there since 2005 and the rates have gone up.  Also, you will probably not have that much trouble parking if you use your car every day and are not parked during alternate side of the street parking anyway.
