Real Estate & Home Improvement / Contractor Recommendations
« on: September 21, 2012, 11:53:22 AM »
Hi all -

I'm in desperate need of recommendations for good, reliable and reasonable contractors in JH.

I have been trying to get my mother's kitchen and bathroom done over for nearly a year now. My mother lives in a co-op - Northridge II - and so far the bloody Board has rejected 5 different contractors. These contractors have all worked in JH in places like the Chateau, the Warwick, the Berkley, the Greystones and other Northridge sections. All came recommended from friends or on here (or both) and all were rejected for silly reasons - one was rejected because they thought his insurance coverage was "too convoluted", another because he wasn't a licensed plumber (the only one to get that reason even though all would have been installing a new dishwasher - didn't know a licensed plumber was needed for that!) and silly stuff like this. Needless to say, the contractors weren't happy and I'm fairly furious over it all. I also have to admit, I don't understand why the Board rejects them - these contractors have worked and done far more construction-like work (ie; moving walls, putting in pass thrus, combining closets to make one big one, etc.) in buildings that are, in fairness, much better than Northridge and sell for much more - all without any issue. I suppose when a co-op is self managed like Northridge II, this is what you get - Board members making decisions that they have no qualifications or clue about. Annoying nonetheless.

I need a contractor who carries all the normal licenses and insurance and who also has a $5 million dollar umbrella coverage.

The job, as it is in a co-op, is pretty simple:

1. Kitchen - remove the old cabinets, flooring and appliances; lay new floor, paint walls, install new cabinets and appliances

2. Bathroom - remove old vanity, medicine chest and light, paint and install new vanity, medicine chest and light

3. Painting - rest of the co-op needs to be painted - dining room, living room, foyer and two bedrooms. Removing old ceiling fans in the bedrooms and installing new ones as well as removing chandelier in DR and fixture in foyer and replacing.

As I said, fairly desperate to get this done as soon as possible - the mother is insisting it be done by Christmas - so any recommendations, advice, etc. would be greatly appreciated!!!


THANKS!
