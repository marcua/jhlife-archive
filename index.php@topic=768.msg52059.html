I remain convinced that the problem -- yesterday was horrible -- is due to runway rerouting, not additional planes.

the takeoffs over our neighborhood are either once every 60-90 seconds or non-existent. that's clearly a runway closure issue. if it were merely more planes, we'd have a barrage of takeoffs every three or so minutes, six to midnight, seven days a week, since that is LaGuardia's schedule.

we had a break during the week, had a horrible, horrible Saturday, and have silence today. this may be the way it goes. honestly, i'd rather have five quiet days and two really bad ones than seven days a week where we share the noise evenly with everyone else. and, yes, I am ruling out the option of having 100 percent of planes avoid flying over Jackson heights 100 percent of the time.

let's see how the fall and winter play out before we all panic, sell our homes and move to Rockville centre.
