I had some guests over on Saturday, and they wanted empanadas, so we ordered delivery from Mama's.  I've got to admit, they were yummy, and the tremendous variety available makes them fun.  But I much prefer the larger, baked-style empanadas that I've been told are more typical further south in South America.  The bakery that preceded Lety's used to have great empanadas that included ingredients like olives and raisins mixed in with the meats.  A somewhat more yuppified version is Ruben's Empanadas in Manhattan and my favorite of all, Julia's Empanadas in Washington D.C. 

Anybody know a place where we can get these kinds of empanadas closer to home?
