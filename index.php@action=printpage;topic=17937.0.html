Dear neighbors,

Thank you all so much for coming in last night! We Hoped you enjoy our soft opening menu. Many of you had suggested I should post the menu here instead of an attachment. I appreciated your feed back.
By next week, we will have a full menu and menu are rotated every two weeks.

Here is our soft opening menu.

FIRST COURSE

Roasted Duck & Goat Cheese Salad
Truffle cream, peaches, figs, blend of seeds, arugula, sorrel & grape must dressing

Fondue Bar
Bread sticks, roasted potatoes, cauliflower, cherry tomatoes, strawberries, Bayonne dates & fried quail eggs



SECOND COURSE

Fillet Mignon in Au Jus
Smoked potato foam, zucchini powder, baby carrots & asparagus

Lobster Thermidor Gratin
Marble potatoes, caviar, tarragon cream, peas, quail egg yolk



THIRD COURSE

Box of Sweets
Opera cake, passion fruit & raspberry petit fours, black berry tart, plum & peach Danishes.

Chocolate Mousse
Red wine butter cream, candied orange & almost burnt cream
