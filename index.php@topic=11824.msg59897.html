Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

I worry about some of the old school stores that give this area its charm. For example, the art supply/stationary store on 37th Ave & 81st St, Lety's Bakery on 37th ave & 78th St, & Coral Aquarium (pet store) on Roosevelt & 75th. And of course the "Little India" section. Even the Jahns - when I was a kid Jahns was a small chain in Queens & Bx. Now the one on 37 Ave is the last. They give free ice cream sundaes on your birthday! Not just to kids. We've lost some antiques & thrift shops in the past year, I miss them. It's the little "mom & pop" stores that give this area its personality. That's what I'd like to see more of.  Of course, getting a museum or a park would be fantastic, but I really don't expect that.
