Title: Jackson Heights Greenmarket Gets Cheese!
Post by: anastasia31 on September 16, 2010, 01:50:22 PM
You asked and we answered! Come out this Sunday, September 19th and welcome our newest farmer to the growing Jackson Heights Greenmarket. Cherry Grove Farms http://www.cherrygrovefarm.com/ (http://www.cherrygrovefarm.com/)will be bringing their beautiful hand-made artisan cheeses to supplement the sustainably raised beef, chicken, pork, eggs and beautiful fall vegetables currently available. This Sunday, we'll also be welcoming back the magnificent mushrooms of farmer John Madura. Lots of new goodies to go with the late summer tomatoes, corn and squash. We hope to see you there!

Queens Greenmarkets have joined the twitter community. Check us out @QnsGreenmarkets on Twitter for the latest news about the all the markets in Queens, including Jackson Heights.

Jackson Heights Greenmarket is located on 34th Ave between 78th and 77th streets and on 78th street between 34th Ave and Northern Blvd, every Sunday until December 19th. For more information about vendors and events visit us at http://www.grownyc.org/node/278 (http://www.grownyc.org/node/278)
