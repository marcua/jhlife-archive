I went to Himalayan Yak for lunch today and it was very good...  I was pleased to see separate "vegetarian" sections on the menu by category.  I did try the thali, and it was a nice, spicy assortment of food... my favorite was (what I think was) mustard greens.  My friend had the vegetarian momos, and she said it was good, but not very unique (although I saw she did not try any of the dipping sauces with it--just ate it plain).  I saw other reviews (maybe on yelp?) complaining about small portions, but it was plenty of food.  I had extra left on my plate, and more than 1/2 of the rice given left.  I didn't get any refills.

And to answer my own question, they do take credit cards.
