I love Delhi Heights, located on 37-66 74th St. It's got the classiest interior design for any South Asian restaurant in Jackson Heights, a great buffet at a competitive price and great live music/DJ and bartender for the night time. This is a great place to hang out with friends or for going on a date.

I usually go there Sundays around 1 for the delicious lunch buffet (you can usually find me sitting by the window). The owner, Sumit is an awesome guy and if you  get to know him, your food will seems to taste even better.

As per decor...it's one thing I think Jackson Hts lacks.  While there are many interesting interiors in the multitude of restaurants we lack truly memorable and beautiful interior designs in the area.  As per food, it is my opinion Uncle Peters has the most versatile and tasty menu...even the plating is generally nicely done.  The bar is very cozy (small) and this discourages the mob hang out.  The place has a an odd interior to it because I think it used to be a oriental Steak house or something like that.  The owner is very eccentric as well but has been in the restaurant business for a long time...Nicest down to earth guy you'll meet in the business.
