I am mystified that Donovan's burgers are considered to be great.  I love the atmosphere there, and I so want to like the burger. So much so, that I've given them 5 or 6 chances.  I went again the other day and yet again, the burger was dry and way overcooked.  I usually ask for medium, but the last two times I asked for very rare and specifically asked that it not be well done.  No luck - it was well done. 

But it's still one of my favorite places to grab a beer wtih friends - the bartenders are really friendly.  I'll just have to lower my expectations when it comes to the food...
