Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Community Groups, Activities & Events / Come Over For Coffee - Storytelling Home Concert Sunday, 2/25
« on: February 11, 2018, 06:45:42 PM »
Everyone,

I created this event, "Come Over For Coffee," because it's the kind of evening I'd like to attend, and I thought it would be a good way to bring neighbors together.

If you've never been to a storytelling home concert, it takes place in the living room with about 15 people sitting around (without a campfire), sipping beverages, and listening to 3-4 performers tell stories (usually non-fiction, but sometimes folktales). We have a sofa, chairs, meditation cushions, and pillows. We're featuring Refreshments by Connie (aka My Mom). Her homemade noodle pudding, potato omelettes and chocolate chip cake are still being talked about. Oh, and heads up, we have a cat (Milo, he's very sweet) and bird.

We're asking for $15 donations to pay the performers but it's just a suggestion.

Please email me at heightshub@gmail.com for reservations and our address. Seating is limited.
