I believe Astoria became popular with young people because it is close to the city and close to LIC. The artists started moving there after they got priced out of brooklyn. They will be spreading all over now, you can see it already. there was a time when williamsburg was IT. now, people are all over the place.

Jackson heights seems like a good logical step. The trains are nearly unbeatable and you know the food is as well. The apartments are huge too. There isnt much of a bar scene as they know it, but people that love this stuff - like 24 hour korean bbqs  - will love it here.

I would love to do real estate with younger people. If they see what I see, more young people would come here. When I ride the train now, me being this young white skinny kid with a beard, I can't help but think the locals are saying "there goes the neighborhood"

I could really care less how old everyone is. I can see that the people here know something that other people don't and that's awesome.
