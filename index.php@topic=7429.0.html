Hi,
I was wondering if anyone could recommend a good place to sit down and have chinese. I'm thinking of the area that would actually be considered Elmhurst, on broadway between the hospital, and, well...elmhurst ave, near the large asian supermarkets.

My very un-scientific guage for chinese restaraunts, is whether or not they have roast ducks in the window. That, to me, indicates an old-school type place with potential.

Can anyone recommend a good place, and some good dishes that I may not have had at the neighborhood takeout places? (ming wok is my usual TO chinese)

While not Chinese, I would recommend Taiwanese Gourmet on 84th Street and Broadway:

http://www.yelp.com/biz/taiwanese-gourmet-elmhurst

It's hard to recommend dishes not knowing how adventurous your palate is.  Some basic staples/representative dishes may include: basil clams and San Bei Ji (also known as three cup chicken), oyster pancakes, sauteed eel with yellow chives, stinky tofu etc. etc.

it sounds like the OP is looking for something more old-school. most likely cantonese.

if it's still there, Great Wealth on Broadway, just a few blocks up from the hospital, would fit the bill. the roast meats are good, and the soups and noodles are excellent -- even though we're rapidly entering the no-soup zone, weather wise
