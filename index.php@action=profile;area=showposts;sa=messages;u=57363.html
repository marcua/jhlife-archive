Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Looking for a fun and meaningful activity over the midwinter break?

If you don't know Nuala (what you don't know her???) she is a big part of what makes this community fabulous. Community Groups, Activities & Events / JH Dads *Family Meetup* @Night Market, 5-7PM SATURDAY Oct 5
« on: October 02, 2019, 03:03:58 PM »
Hey There Dads!


We are doing a different kind of JH Dads meetup this month...a FAMILY meetup. And it’s on SATURDAY at the Queens Night Market in Corona, next to the NY Hall of Science.


I know we usually meet up late at a bar, but this time we are doing something for the whole family. This is a chance for families to meet families! (Don't worry, regular late night dad meetups will resume after this one...


When: Saturday, Oct 5, 5-7PM

Where: Queens Night Market, next to NY Hall of Science

Who: Bring whatever family members would like to come!

How to get there? SUBWAY: Take the 7 train to the 111th Street station. Walk south 4 blocks until you pass under an overpass. You will see the Night Market behind the New York Hall of Science on the Left.


What is the Queens Night Market?


“The Queens International Night Market is a large, family-friendly open-air night market in Queens, featuring up to 100 independent vendors selling merchandise, art, and food and featuring small-scale cultural performances, all celebrating the rich cultural diversity and heritage of NYC and Queens.”



So you can buy food, or bring your own.


How to meet up? The Queens Night Market is pretty big, so if you want to meet up with us,, come to the grassy area where people are rolling around in inflatable balls (it’s hard to miss). We’ll see you there!



RSVP if you think you are coming to Jonny.Goldstein(at)gmail(dot)com



P.S. weather is supposed to be cool on Saturday....high 50's. Dress accordingly!
