Neighborhood Chat / Re: Garden Thief
« on: June 20, 2012, 08:14:36 AM »
A few years ago two people climbed our Co-op fence, dug up a small apple blossom tree, lifted it over the fence and then were arrested by two undercover officers who just happened to be sitting in a car across the street watching the whole thing.

The officers woke the Super and asked how much the tree was worth, explaining that anything over a certain price (think it was $350) was a crime people get arrested for.  Naturally my Super said that was the worth of the tree.

Off to jail. 

So yes, there are people who will dig up and steal shrubs and trees.  It is sad.
