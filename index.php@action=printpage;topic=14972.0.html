Title: Animal Care and control Brooklyn
Post by: dotley on June 13, 2016, 05:20:19 PM
Not preaching just informing on how bad it is at AC & C.  A woman from the JH area listed her 11 month old orange female cat for re homing on Craigslist.  I answered the ad offering to help but she was in such a rush she took the cat to AC & C on June 10th.  It took me until today to find her online because AC & C changed her name from Luna to Saffron and you have to have her number A1056682.  After only 2 1/2 days she is now on death row and will be euthanized tomorrow morning.  The only way she can get adopted now is if a rescue group "pulls" her.  I tried calling both AC & C and rescue groups but only got voicemails and email contacts.  I wanted to find out if there was some physical reason for euthanizing an 11 month old house cat and why they deemed her unadoptable.  I am writing this because I think more people should know how bad this system is.  There is no Care here. Every morning at least a dozen if not more animals, both healthy cats and dogs are euthanized  A while ago when now comptroller Scott Stringer was running for office he sent out a petition with a good plan to change the way AC & C was run, but promptly forgot all about once elected.
My point here is PLEASE do not take any animal to AC & C.  It is almost certainly a death sentence.

thank you for listening.

Title: Re: Animal Care and control Brooklyn
Post by: madalyn on June 14, 2016, 06:14:30 PM
I volunteered at Brooklyn CACC until my heart couldn't take it anymore.  It is hell on earth for any animal that ends up there.

The only thing I can say in their defense - because there are certainly people working there that care about animals, is that they MUST accept every animal that comes through their doors.  That means they are constantly overloaded - especially in the warmer months, when people too cheap or too clueless to spay/neuter their pet, bring in box after box after box of litters of kittens and puppies.
