The park at night has always been an issue, going back to the 1980s where a good friend of mine was almost beaten to unconsciousness there when we were teens.

The park itself is not well lit.  I recall many 'couplings' taking place at night in secluded places at that park as I would pass through it in the 80s and 90s, even with the then-new construction circa 1992.

I do see the original posters thinking in that no parked cars makes the street side more desolate ...
... but one could argue just a easily that the OPENS up teh area more, as characters can't lurk on or between or behind parked cars, nor can people idle in parked cars, etc.

Frankly, the warmer weather brings out more people at night in the summer, so regardless of the street being closed, open, etc., there will be more troublemakers out proportionately in July and August period.  And teens in general, trouble makers or not, will be out more as there are no 'school nights.'

That all said, there would be nothing wrong with better lighting .... THROUGHOUT THE YEAR ... at night.
