Jackson Heights Life Photo Contest #4- Spring 2010

Welcome to the Fourth Jackson Heights Life Photo Contest!  We are looking for an excellent photograph to use on the banner of our website.  Here are the rules for our contest:

1. Three submissions per person only - show us your best work.
2. Images should be in digital format, taken with at LEAST a 3mp camera. Scanned images must be HIGH quality.
3. Photos should not portray identifiable individuals.
4. Photos should have a horizonal composition to look good as a banner, which is approximately 5 times as wide as it is tall.
5. A small version of the image should be posted or linked to this thread with a brief title or description.
6. The contest starts immediately and will run until April 30, 2010. At that time the contest will be closed to new entries.
7. After the submission period is closed, the JHLife community will vote on the submitted images.
8. In mid-May, a winner will be announced and the graphics will go live on the homepage shortly thereafter.

Further details:
By your submission, you are giving us permission to use your image on the Jackson Heights Life homepage.  

If selected, your image will run for several months.  It should be a picture that captures some aspect of Jackson Heights... photos that capture a sense of the season are especially welcome.  

There is no need to crop your photo before posting it for this contest.  If your photo is selected, we will work with you to turn the photo into an appropriate banner.  We will put your name, username or email on the bottom of the banner so people can contact you.

FYI, there are two ways to post photos on this site.  The first way is to upload them to another site, like Flickr or Photobucket, and link to the image from here.  An explanation of how to do this is over here (http://www.jacksonheightslife.com/community/index.php?topic=374.msg1700#msg1700).

The second way is to attach a file directly to your post.  At the bottom of the page, click on "Additional Options," and then enter the filename in the box next to "Attach:".  Files must be smaller than 128 kB.


Good luck!

signed,
Moderators shelby2 and chuckster
