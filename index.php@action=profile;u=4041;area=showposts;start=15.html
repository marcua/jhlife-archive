Neighborhood Chat / Re: Too Many Liquor Licenses?
« on: May 06, 2012, 08:26:24 AM »
Last night my husband and I were talking about this very issue - how many many bars there are in Jackson Heights. And this was before I stumbled upon this thread. We live on 82 St. 82nd is a kind of thruway between Northern to Roosevelt. Every single night (middle of the night, probably around bar closing time, which I guess is 4:00 a.m. or thereabouts) cars idling at the traffic light on our corner, windows open, are BLASTING disco music, salsa, hip hop - you name it. They must be leaving the bars, most likely drunk or high. It is horrible. I haven't gotten a good night's sleep in the two years I've lived on this street. Yes, I agree, there are too many bars and not enough police. Not only that, the mayor is talking about more budget cuts, which will make things even worse. It's terribly frustrating. I want to move out of here and I've lived here on and off for a long time.
