Where are all the trash cans?
« on: September 15, 2013, 10:08:13 PM »
This morning I was walking my dog down Northern Blvd and noticed that almost all the corner trash cans were missing. If my memory serves me correctly, I'm almost certain that every corner had one on  both sides of the street. Of course, that didn't mean there wasn't any garbage because there sure was plenty of that. I  noticed bags of what appeared to be household  trash on many of the corners next to lamp posts or next to those newspaper dispensers. I was once told that  garbage cans in JH are often overflowing because people who  rent rooms throw their home garbage in city trash cans as to not call attention to an illegal conversion. I guess having no cans at all hasn't really solved the problem. On a somewhat more positive, yet questionable note, there was a garbage can finally placed at Diversity Plaza.....smack dab in the middle as if it were some sort of monument. Why they just cant place a garbage can next to the entrance of the subway  or on the corner is beyond me.
