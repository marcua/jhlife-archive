News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

I ordered a cake for my youngest son's Birthday at Nitin by Roosevelt on the Elmhurst side.  It was a Dominican B'day Cake so In my opinion it can be hit and miss.  I was very happy with the cake, it was beautiful and very delicious.  The larger square cake was made with Dulce de leche filling and the smaller round cakes were made with fresh strawberries.  For those not familiar with Dominican cakes it is made with a sudo traditional french sponge cake.  I have eaten it many times and personally feel most of them are over rated but when it is done correctly it can be some of the most delicious Birthday cakes You can get.  So, two thumbs up Nitin for making a great Bday at a very affordable price...
