Title: Ethical Humanist Society of Queens: Last Meetings Until September!
Post by: craffaele on June 12, 2021, 01:09:26 PM
Hello! Please catch one of the last meetings of the Ethical Humanist Society of Queens (EHSQ) before the summer!! We’ve got some good ones:

  • 6/13 Celebrating Juneteenth: Where is the Joy in Freedom? (https://www.facebook.com/events/483310116263214) [put on by the Brooklyn Society for Ethical Culture—make sure to fill out their EventBrite we have included!]
  • 6/20 Climate Change Can’t Be Ignored! / Summer Solstice: A Global Dichotomy / History of Fathers (https://www.facebook.com/events/952744828844003)
  • 6/27 Reflecting on Getting Through This Past Year / Planning for the Summer (https://www.facebook.com/events/348316210239639)

All three Facebook event links above contain info on how to join the events—otherwise, you can find the information at our website, at http://queenshumanist.org/sunday-meetings.html! Please let me know if you have any questions too (you can comment here, or email me at contact@queenshumanist.org). Hope you can make it and discuss ethical issues with us and become part of our growing society :D.
