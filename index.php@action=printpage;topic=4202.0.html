Title: Finally a Place for a Decent Burger in the Neighborhood ( sort of)
Post by: jimbo in jhts on February 20, 2010, 11:12:41 AM
Been passing it a number of times and decided to try it last night.  Cassidy's Ale House on 75th & 31st Ave opposite the shopping plaza with Canelle!!!!!!  :D is exactly what you would expect. An Irish style pub, tap beers and pub food.  I finally found a hamburger that is drip down your chin good. :rockon:  The place was packed and a bit noisy with partyers (sp!) and the requisite TV's tuned into every sport channel and alas one FOX channel, but I won't hold that against them.  Burger was delicious, my partner had a grilled chicken sandwich with ham & cheese and Blue Moon on Tap.  Just what the nabe needs, now why can't we get something on 37th Ave like this????  Could greedy landlords charging exorbitant rents have something to do with it????Hmmmmmm :o :o  >:( sorry into icons today.
