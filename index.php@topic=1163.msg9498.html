Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Another thought occurs to me. With the economy being what it is, and where it's headed I'm not sure it's the wisest time to open a wine bar / tea house. A few weeks ago some friends and I were out at a wine bar in Astoria that we enjoy going to. I would think that I'm not the only person doing this right now.
