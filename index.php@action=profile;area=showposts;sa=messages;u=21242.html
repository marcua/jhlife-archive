News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Restaurants & Food / Jackson Heights Greenmarket Reusable Bag Giveaway!

Restaurants & Food / Jackson Heights Greenmarket
« on: August 31, 2012, 10:24:39 PM »
This Labor Day weekend, come on out to the Jackson Heights Greenmarket!  Our featured item this Sunday is heirloom tomatoes.  Learn everything you need to know and more about them, join our "tomato raffle" for your chance to win a Greenmarket tote bag full of local produce, and taste our Greenmarket Succotash packed with cherry tomatoes!

The market has a variety of delicious local products that could be perfect for a Labor Day feast: cheese, wine, meats, ice cream, honey, mushrooms, baked goods and, of course, fresh fruits and vegetables.

Open every Sunday, 8:00-3:00.  Located at 34th Avenue between 77th and 78th Streets.
