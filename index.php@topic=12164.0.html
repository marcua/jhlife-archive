Roosevelt Diner
« on: July 05, 2014, 03:56:03 PM »
Just stopped by the new Roosevelt Diner.  It's between 79th and 80th.  Grabbed a menu and it looks really promising.  A good mixture with some things that lean towards the healthier palate (salads, salmon over greens, egg white omelette) as well as heartier fare.  Looks like a good hang--clean, plenty of seating--and the people I spoke to briefly seemed very nice.  And it's open 24 hours.  All that's left is to actually try the food.  Anyone out there been there yet?  Seems like a really great addition to the neighborhood.

Anyone try out Roosevelt Diner?

I passed by the new diner on 79-09 Roosevelt Ave. the other day. I haven't had time to try it out, so I don't know how the food is, but I will soon. It's 24 hours, which is great because there's not too many places open late in Jackson Heights. I always end up having tacos, tortas, or halal food because that's the only decent food available after midnight, and after awhile I get sick of it. It's nice to be able to go out at 1-2 AM and have more options to eat. They also are on Seamless and GrubHub.
