I'll tell you who NOT to go to: Will De Jesus on 37th Ave.

My husband and I went there this past year, thinking we would be meeting with the same awesome CPA we had last year. OHMYGOD. He has ZERO customer service, so don't expect him to make friendly chat or make you feel at home.

Second, after doing my taxes online twice, I decided to file with a CPA to save me money. And guess what? I ended up owing the same amount AND having to pay the CPA fee. There was absolutely no going out of the way to find us savings when we saw Will De Jesus. That's what I expect when I go to a CPA. I expect them to know the ins and outs of tax law and be able to find loopholes that save us money.

Next year I will either file myself or get a different CPA who knows what they're doing, because going to this guy was just a joke. He might've been using TurboTax Online for all we know.
