First of all, congratulations!  May you all have good health, good nights' sleep, and a good time getting to know your newest family member.

Proximity is probably key for your parents, so I think the places you've identified are probably your best bets.  But if they're willing to travel a bit further on the 7 line for a higher-service hotel, there's a big Sheraton in Flushing, and possibly some nicer new hotels in Long Island City (some were under construction, but I don't know if any are open yet).

Good luck.

It's always good to be able to get local recommendations from a local website like this one.  However, because many of us don't use hotels nearby since we live here, in this case I would suggest also checking reviews on www.tripadvisor.com .  This is my go-to place anytime I want to book a hotel.  Sometimes the reviews range widely, but you might be able to get an overall sense and also see if there are any immediate issues with a property such as current construction.

Depending on the purpose of the visit, the Pan American on Queens Blvd. might not be a bad option. It's close to JH, and while it's not the highest end hotel is certainly not a no tell motel either. Aside form LGA shuttle service, the Pan Am is extremely close to the G&R trains which makes taking the train to JH a breeze.  It's also not very far from the Queens Center Mall.
