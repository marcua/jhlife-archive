Title: Re: These Queens police precincts had the highest & lowest crime rates in borough du
Post by: JDinJH on January 18, 2019, 08:39:52 AM
Quote from: dssjh on January 17, 2019, 02:16:53 PM
Actually, no.

https://www1.nyc.gov/assets/nypd/downloads/pdf/crime_statistics/cs-en-us-115pct.pdf

I don't know what stats you were looking at, but you are mistaken. 

In 2018, the 114th Pct had 42 rapes and the 115th had 41; robberies, the 114th Pct had 193 and the 115th Pct had 248; felony assaults, the 114th Pct had 356 and the 115th Pct had 368; burglary, the 114th Pct had 171 and the 115th Pct had 195; grand larceny involving an assault, the 114th Pct had 132 and the 115th Pct had 149.  The only areas where the 114th Pct had higher crime stats as compared to 115th Pct was with murder (114th Pct had 10 and the 115th had 3) and grand larceny (114th Pct had 787 and the 115th Pct had 653).

Please note that I derived the above statistics from the same sources that you cited; specifically, the historical perspective stats covering 2018.

I am not surprised that the 114th had a higher murder rate.  Astoria has a big night life scene and the 114th covers the projects as well.  As far as the grand larceny stats go, I am not surprised - Astoria has a lot more stores that have expensive stuff that shoplifters steal.  Aside from murder and grand larceny, the overall crime rate is higher at the 115th Pct.
