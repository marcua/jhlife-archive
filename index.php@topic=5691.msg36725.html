There are many Greek vegetarian dips that are amazing......melitzanosalada - eggplant dip, tzantziki - yogurt and cucumber dip, scordalia - garlic dip, tahini dip, taramosalada - red caviar dip (not sure if your friends eat seafood)  all these lovely dips can be purchased at Zenon Taverna (Greek and Cypriot Cuisine) which is located four blocks from "N" train's 31st Ave. stop.  They also have a wonderful cured olive bread and great pita bread!  Yes it is a little out of the way but hell it's Christmas time aren't your friends worth it!

34-10 31st Avenue, Astoria, NY 11106    Tel: (718) 956-0133
