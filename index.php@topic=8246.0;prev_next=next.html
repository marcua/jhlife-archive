Dog stolen in front of Growing Farms today, Sunday, 2/24 at 1pm
« on: February 24, 2013, 10:16:56 PM »
My friends' dog, Sammy, was taken today, Sunday, 2/24 around 1:00 or 1:15pm. He was tied up in front of Growing Farms on 37th Avenue between 76th and 77th Streets.  They were inside for just a few minutes, and when they came out he was gone.  A man promoting Green Mountain Energy who had a table a few storefronts down the block said he saw a light haired, medium height woman, anywhere from late 40s-60 years old and well-dressed, walk by saying something about how people shouldn't leave their dogs outside, and "this dog is mine now" and walked away. 

A picture of Sammy is attached.  He is a 55 pound, extremely friendly shepherd-ridgeback-lab/other mix, mostly tan in color with some black on his face. 

If anyone sees him or has any information, please contact Sammy's owner, Josie, at 917-617-0502, or email at josiewhittlesey@yahoo.com.  Thank you.
