It's finally here!
Opening ceremony and ribbon-cutting at 4:00 this Thursday (May 27)! Which means it will be open just in time for the long weekend!

Thanks to a huge number of individuals. Thanks, Jeff - who brought this idea to the Community Board 3 years ago and started the whole thing - and the many individuals and not-for-profit groups (big kudos to JHBG) city (CB 3) and stage agencies (NYS DOT) who have given so much time and energy in making sure that this park wasn't forgotten or blocked by red-tape.  

If you don't already have an application, you can download one from the website http://jhdogs.ning.com.
Membership is free (although we do have a suggested donation to help cover operating expenses) and open to all - we just need to ensure that all dogs using the park are vaccinated and licensed.

See you all there!

I think we're willing to accept proof of registration with the city (canceled check, online printout of registration etc.) as an interim solution while waiting for a dog license to be processed - if you've filed your registration/renewal, just send us a copy of that. However we won't allow any compromise on an updated valid vaccination certificate -  a vet will give that out instantly as soon as the dog has received the shot.
 
Someone from the group will also be on hand to accept membership applications handed in in-person on Thursday at the opening at 4:00, and we'll work on finding additional times when in-person registrations can be completed as well.

We've also adopted the change in rules about children in the park - children will be permitted in the dog park (but ONLY with direct supervision by an adult parent or other caretaker).
