Title: Fuska House cart
Post by: dssjh on February 17, 2019, 06:26:24 PM
another new Indian snack cart, in front of the Duane Reade (next to the kebab cart and around the corner from the other). shiny, clean, fresh new cart smell. handful of veggie chats, all under $5. had the Fuska (basically a pani puri with a few tweaks) and it was delicious, spicy as requested and made to order. highly recommended if you like south indian dishes.
