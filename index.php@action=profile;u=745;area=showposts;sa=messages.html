News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Community Groups, Activities & Events / Lockwood's "Kids Rule" event, March 25, 10 a.m-1 p.m.
« on: March 19, 2017, 10:38:40 AM »
Join us for a day of celebrating Jackson Height's youngest citizens and the makers who love them.

When: Saturday, March 25, 10 a.m. to 1 p.m.
Where: Lockwood, 7713 37th Ave., Jackson Heights, ph: 347-730-6357
Facebook event page: https://www.facebook.com/events/1579801255367973/

Schedule:
+ 10 a.m & 10:30 a.m., Salvador of Martinez Yoga (https://martinezyoga.com/) will be doing a complementary kids' yoga session. Bring your own mat!

+ 11:30 a.m. Victoria Adler, author of "All of Baby, Nose to Toes" will be doing a book reading and signing. (http://www.penguinrandomhouse.com/books/286230/all-of-baby-nose-to-toes-by-victoria-adler-illustrated-by-hiroe-nakata/9780803735316)

+ 10 a.m. to 1 p.m. POP UP SHOPS by some of our favorite kid-centric designers & artists:

--Mighty Real Skin baby skin products by Salvador Martinez and Enrique Mosqueda (http://www.mightyrealskin.com/)

--Knoobies knee protectors and hand muffs by Maia Fedderly (http://knoobies.com/knoobies/Welcome.html)

--Jai Jai Hooray Indian Mythology Flashcards by Rupa Parekh (http://www.jaijaihooray.com/)

--By Anna earrings and hair accessories by Anna, student at the Renaissance School, and her mom Yukako

In addition, we'll be offering 20% off all our merchandise during the event, giving away Lockwood helium balloons to kids, and serving juice boxes and veggie straws. We'll also have printouts of coloring pages from our Queens Coloring Book that kids can color as an activity throughout the event.

See you there!

Ellen Niz
Manager
Lockwood
