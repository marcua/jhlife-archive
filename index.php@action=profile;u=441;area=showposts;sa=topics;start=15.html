Neighborhood Chat / Mayuli Multi (lousy) Services
« on: August 27, 2014, 11:19:53 AM »
Just want to tell people about the bad service I received from Mayuli Multi Services at 88-14 37th Avenue. They cater to Spanish speaking people (their flyer is written only in Spanish). Among their services is faxing. I have faxed from there numerous times, and now I have to wonder if my faxes ever went through. This time I was informed by the fax recipient that my fax had not arrived. I returned to Mayuli and had them refax, I paid for the refax and when that one didn't arrive, the recipient gave me an alternative fax number. We faxed again. Convinced that the problem was Mayuli's scanner, I asked for my money back. They refused. nada.

I then went to another fax service and my fax went through immediately.

Mayuli's attitude is their fax cannot be the problem and that there is something wrong with me. 

Mind you, the real issue was the loss in time in getting my college transcript to its destination by the deadline. I may just be out of luck.

Community Groups, Activities & Events / PRE-BALLET CLASS AGES 3-6
« on: January 18, 2014, 09:44:29 PM »
Hi. I am teaching a pre-ballet class at Mimi For Me Yoga. I have room for two or three more children. Below is the gist of what we're doing. You can find more info about the class in this site's Business Listings under "Pre-Ballet for 3-6 year olds" or by contacting Mimi For Me Yoga, 76-11 37th Avenue, 2nd Floor, 718-505-9642 (YOGA), www.mimiformeyoga.com

Mimi For Me Yoga is offering a once-weekly pre-ballet class for the littlest dancers aged 3 to 6. Pre-ballet training introduces children to the basic requirements they must meet in order to learn the art of classical ballet. Sans barre, I focus on pointing, posture, spotting, turn-out, gracefulness, counting music, and French dance vocabulary. Methodology is inclusionary games, exercises, repetition, visual aids, and mimicry. The goal is to support and encourage children to step up to the barre with confidence and become enthusiastic dancers.
