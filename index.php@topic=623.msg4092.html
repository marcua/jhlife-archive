Ideas for language exchange partners program
« on: March 18, 2008, 11:06:00 PM »
I was thinking that it might be nice to have a language buddy matching service in JH.  My idea is to have something very simple - one or two people could coordinate it.  There would be a form similar to this one for interested people to fill out that could get basic info such as your name and contact info, native language, language you are learning, outside interests, best times to meet, etc.

The coordinator could simply match people and would do no more than give each person the other's contact info.  Also each pair of people would get some basic info about how to make the exchange successful such as the one on page 2 of this pdf http://www.leeds.ac.uk/languages/facility/LanguageEx-Guide.pdf

I think other ways to make this successful would be to
#1 do recruiting, especially for non-native speakers of English.  This could easily be achieved by asking around at the various ESL programs and immigrant centers in the neighborhood
#2 have a system in place if the match does not seem to be working well so that people can request a new partner.

Is there anyone who thinks this is a good idea and might want to brainstorm more about it and possibly be involved in setting it up?

Or is there a program I might not know about already in existence?
