Restaurants & Food / Re: Gustosa's Pizza now Mario's Pizza
« on: September 04, 2015, 10:11:16 AM »
Tried the regular slice last night.  I'm more of a NYC thin crust, specialty pie fan, but I thought this was pretty good.  It brought back memories of those old school, New York City, pizza by-the-slice joints... the kind that offered slices generous on the stringy cheese paired with a cup of flat soda.  The sauce wasn't overly sweet, and the the crust was thin, yet firm enough to carry the weight of the cheese.  Overall, a better plain slice than Famiglia on Roosevelt, or Bruno's in East Elmhurst.

The manager seemed super excited about the planned menu additions, so I'm looking forward to what materializes.

Neighborhood Chat / Jackson Heights - Boundaries
« on: August 21, 2015, 12:36:44 PM »
I came across this DNAinfo interactive map [see below link] where users can draw lines identifying a neighborhood's boundaries.  The finished drawings represent what each individual perceives as his / her neighborhood.  I found this interesting, considering that Jackson Heights proper is frequently misrepresented in news media.  The neighboring communities of East Elmhurst, Elmhurst and Corona tend to mistakenly fall into "Jackson Heights" territory.  Upon completing my drawing, I noticed that my boundary lines were way off of what others consider Jackson Heights.  The question is, who's right?
