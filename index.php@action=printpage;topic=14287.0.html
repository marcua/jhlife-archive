Title: Queens World Film Festival
Post by: Katha Cato on January 21, 2016, 03:36:52 PM
The 6th Annual QWFF returns to the P.S 69 (Jackson Heights),Museum of the Moving Image (Astoria),  Secret Theatre (LIC), and the All Saints Episcopal Church (Sunnyside) March 15 - 20 2016. We are screening 142 films from 25 nations. We will screen narrative, documentaries, LGBTQ, animated films and we are adding a new category: Family Films. Join us for exciting films from around the world and around the corner!
Please Check Website for special events, special guests and other exciting updates for the festival!

Tickets $10-$20, for venue information please check website for more details

Title: Re: Queens World Film Festival
Post by: Pat Lowenhaupt on February 23, 2016, 01:38:38 PM
There are a few events in Jackson Heights of which I am proud. I am a life-long resident of Jackson Heights and this event tops the list. What better way to learn of the cultures of the people in this neighborhood, who speak 167 languages, and celebrate cultural diversity than by showing films from over 20 countries of the world. The other side of this coin shares the Queens genre by showing the films of the best filmmakers who live in Queens. We see the best possible of both worlds right here. BRAVO QWFF.
