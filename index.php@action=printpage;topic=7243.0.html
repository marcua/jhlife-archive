Title: Farm Spot Sign-ups (Until April 15th)
Post by: civita on March 24, 2012, 04:29:28 PM

Join Farm Spot, Jackson Heights CSA (Community Supported Agriculture).

A farm share from a local, organic farm. The season runs for 26 weeks - you get a share every week,
from May 31 until just after Thanksgiving.

Vegetables come from organic Golden Earthworm Farm <http://www.goldenearthworm.com/> in
Jamesport, Long Island.

Fruit comes from Briermere Farm <http://www.briermere.com/>
(minimally treated) in Riverhead, Long Island.

Registration is open only until April 15th!

The Details:
Season: 26 weeks. May 31, 2012 - week after Thanksgiving
When: every Thursday, 5:30-8:15pm
Where: St. Mark's Church (82nd St. & 34th Ave.)

Prices:
Vegetable Share - $575.10
Fruit Share - $180.09
Herb Share - $51.84
Subsidized Vegetable A - $374.92
Subsidized Vegetable B - $224.90 (wait list only)

Click here to sign-up. <http://csa.farmigo.com/join/farmspot/summer2012>

For more info on Farm Spot: www.farmspot.org
Questions: farmspot@gmail.com

Thanks!

The Farm Spot Core Team
