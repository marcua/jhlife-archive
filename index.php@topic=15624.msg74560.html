Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Voted this afternoon at PS 222. Waited online for an hour. When I got closer to the front door we were allowed to skip ahead to our district table. After signing in, they didnt say much. I had to ask what the next step was (filling out form at any open little desk). Then where to go after I filled in the ballet? Had to ask in order to be directed to the scanning machines. Wasnt super clear without grabbing someone and asking. Actually voting took mere minutes.

When I woke up this morning at the crack of 11am, the line was in front of my house. Have never seen that before. I live a little less than half way down the block. So I have a speaker outside my window providing some music to the line that is CURRENTLY too far down the block that I cant see where it ends. Hope you all like 70's scenario music like Bee Gees!!!
