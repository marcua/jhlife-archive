Community Groups, Activities & Events / Help this Sunday with JH Green Agenda tree care group, 84th St & 34th Ave
« on: August 11, 2011, 07:18:20 PM »
 This Sunday, a group of volunteers is meeting at 34th Ave and 84th Street to focus on street trees in that area. This means helping in whatever
way the trees need. Mulch, staking, widening the tree pits by taking out bricks. Anything that will help the continued growth of the street trees.
Come join this roving band of helpers, even if you have only an hour to spare, as we need all the help we can muster. Keep Jackson Heights
as green as possible with caring for our street trees in need. We are doing this on monthly basis all around Jackson Heights.
  Starting at 9:00 and ending at 12:30. All ages and experience levels welcome. Tree pruners especially welcome. Need someone to work the
table and give information at the site too. Bring gloves and trowels if you have them.
  Come out, meet your neighbors, help the community and get dirty!
