This Saturday Jackson Heights Beautification Group will be having a special clean and green event.  In this "garden neighborhood" there is limited public green space, that is why it is so important that the little green space we have is maintained.  There is an open space on 69th Street between 37 and 34th Avenue next to the BQE which has a nice wall and open green space.  Unfortunately, it has become an area for dumping trash and letting weeds grow. 
 
This Saturday we want to make a statement.  We want to clean up the area and then show our politicians that the city must maintain this area.  Community Board 3 has agreed to put a dog run next to the area (under the BQE) which I hope is the first step to better utilizing this green space.
 
I hope you will come and pitching in, but more importantly, by showing up you  show our local politicians that we want our green spaces maintained!
 
Please come and bring a friend!   If possible, please post this flyer in your building to encourage your neighbors to pitch in for a better Jackson Heights.  If you have any questions, please call myself at 646-210-4495 or Beatrice Ruiz at 347-665-6063.
