Title: Re: Optometrist recommendation?

My recommendation is to go to an Ophthalmologist and get the tests done correctly. I go to NYU on 41st St and 2nd Ave and their staff is very nice and professional and they have state of the art test equipment. A refraction is $55, and your health insurance may cover most of the exam cost if you get a referral from your primary physician.
