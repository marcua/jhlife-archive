Quote from: buddy on April 06, 2008, 01:07:06 PM
You mean where we all read the same book and discuss?  Or just swap books among each other? 
Both.

My book group is in my building. We meet once a month in someone's apartment for coffee and book discussion and swapping. Even if you don't have a book to swap, you may take one or two home with you. We keep a running list of all those books that are currently in circulation at the house of the next meeting. All new neighbors are invited. It becomes quite an eclectic group of people and reading material. Some also scrounge second-hand stores looking for good books, which many of us have not yet read.

It's a great way to get to know your neighbors and make new friends . . . and get some good reads for free.

I especially like free!
