Title: Re: NY Health Insurance Options
Post by: jeanette on October 02, 2018, 10:04:42 PM
I threw thousands away on COBRA. Don't let your friend make the same mistake.

If one is out of a job and really needs medical care, NY Presby is right around the corner. They will consider current income and adjust the bill and work out installments.

I don't qualify for Medicaid. I was hospitalized last year for a week (NYP in UES Manhattan), had every test imaginable and three blood transfusions. My bill was I think 16,000, maybe more. It was reduced to something ridiculous, like $800. There were no deductions, however, for the doctor's bills. Those I had to pay in full, but we agreed on an installment plan. They worked with me every step of the way.
Title: Re: NY Health Insurance Options
Post by: the80s on October 03, 2018, 12:28:08 PM
Your friend might want to look into short-term insurance, which is extremely limited but also generally MUCH cheaper than buying on the individual market. One catch though is that short-term plans were not available at all in New York State when I last checked; might be worth looking again though, since a lot of the rules around short-term insurance changed recently at the federal level.
