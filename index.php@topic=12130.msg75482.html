News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

"In Jackson Heights" will be broadcast on PBS tomorrow.

Via an email by Councilmember Daniel Dromm...

Quote
Dear Friend,

I am pleased to announce that Academy Award winning filmmaker Frederick Wiseman's film In Jackson Heights will receive its television premiere on PBS Thirteen tomorrow, Friday, January 6 at 9pm.
 
In Jackson Heights provides an insider's view into daily life in what is arguably the most diverse neighborhood in the world.  The film features numerous clips of my work to make the neighborhood the accepting, tolerant, multicultural place it is today. 

Also, be sure to tune in earlier that morning — on Friday, January 6 at 10am — for a special Facebook Live event on PBS’ Facebook page featuring me and Frederick Wiseman talking about Jackson Heights.

Social media info:
Facebook: /PBS
Twitter: @PBS #JacksonHeightsPBS
Instagram: @PBS
Website: www.pbs.org/injacksonheights

I am excited to have this opportunity to discuss Jackson Heights with Mr. Wiseman. I hope you will join us for what will be an engaging conversation.

Sincerely,
 
Daniel Dromm
NYC Council Member
