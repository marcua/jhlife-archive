Re: Juju's Bagel Cafe

I wish them well, but lets be honest: The space is tiny, and the service very slow. It's only fair to mention this, as any of you would be put out at having to wait 15 minutes for coffee (weak and warm), or not having a seat for your party of 5.
They need to be encouraged to hire competent wait staff. I understand how hard it is to open a shop, and I hate seeing empty restaurants, having been in the business for many years. But,if they can find a way to put some money aside to hire Manhattan style- fast and clear waitstaff, it would make another sandwich shop in the neighborhood a bit more bearable. Asking for a cookie 3 times, and seeing it in the bag, but not on the counter by the time I paid, is frustrating when you know how competitive and tough the restaurant business is.

I do want Juju to succeed and grow. I liked the place just fine. Maybe someone with a design background could go in and suggest improvements to the workspace as well as the seating area. I don't know how they can manage if they ever get a "rush".
