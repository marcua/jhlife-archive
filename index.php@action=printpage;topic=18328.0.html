Title: Re: Converting 8MM and VCR tapes to DVD
Post by: BEB on September 23, 2018, 08:58:51 PM
I use and recommend dijifi.

Whomever you go with, I would not recommend you transfer to DVD. That is a dying (some say already dead) medium. You don't want to go through this all again in 4-5 years.

Do you have a computer? If so you are better off spending the money to have these transferred as a HD film transfer file and saved to a USB thumb drive or hard drive. Then you can play the files through a quicktime player and easily add to youtube to share with family and friends if you wish.

Feel free to DM me if you have any questions. I have a good deal of experience with this type of work.
