1) no need to make an appointment, but you might have to drop off your car and pick it up at a later time if they
are busy.

2)they know the correct type of oil to use, you tell them what you want though, whether it be conventional
or synthetic oil.

3)yes, they will also check your other fluids and since your car is on the lift anyway check under there also.
Oil changes are a very good time to inspect different things, preventive maintenance goes a long way.

Snickers is right on all counts.  In addition, the oil change place ("Classic Lube"?) at 75th & Northern is pretty user-friendly.  They have a sign on the wall detailing all of the checks that are part of their basic package.  They also have a few more expensive packages, also detailed on large signs on the wall. 

For emissions inspections and repair work, I recommend the garage at 78th Street & 31st Avenue.  It doesn't look like much, but I have found them to be honest, helpful and well priced.
