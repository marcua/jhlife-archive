News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Community Groups, Activities & Events / 2nd ANNUAL HOLIDAY SING & STROLL
« on: December 08, 2010, 02:38:12 PM »
*** ANNOUNCING ***

The 2nd Annual Jackson Heights Winter Holiday Sing-A-Long
An Outdoor Carroling Event -- Snow or Shine !!!

SATURDAY, DECEMBER 18
6pm until ?

Meet on 31st Avenue, between 76th Street and 77th Street, near the bus stop on the Modell's side of the avenue. There is a flyer posted about this event at the bus stop that says "STARTS HERE".

Dress warmly and according to the weather!
Bring a flashlight or well-protected candle for each person in your group.
Wear comfortable / appropriate shoes or boots.
School Groups welcome and encouraged to join us.
All children under age 16 must be accompanied by an adult!
(Service animals welcome; all other pets should remain at home.)

We will sing winter and holiday songs from all faiths & cultures as a group, while walking thru the local Jackson Heights area. Please be respectful and courteous to others, even if you don't know the particular song we're singing at the moment.  We will sing in every and any language the members of the group know, including American Sign Language from students and faculty at the Lexington School.

The group will determine the direction and how long we stay out there singing.


Santa Hats encouraged, but not required.
Small, non-electric musical instruments welcome.


***** SPREAD THE WORD & PLEASE INVITE ALL YOUR FRIENDS TO JOIN US *****
