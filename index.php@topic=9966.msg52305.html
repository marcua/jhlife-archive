Three Children Too Many- March for Pedestrian Safety
« on: October 27, 2013, 11:27:47 PM »
I had this posted in Community groups section, but there wasn't even one comment. Maybe here you'll feel more at home chatting your suggestions or support. Thanks. Feel free to RSVP to the yahoo group- 3 Children too Many.

ok gang,
Some neighbors are organizing a March called Three Children too Many. Here is the original post on JHfamilies. If you would like to help with the organizing, have money to offer for signs, buttons, or educational materials, or if you have contacts in the media who might like to know...Or...Or....Or....if you simply think it's a good effort for JH, please let us know by joining the yahoo group or commenting here. Thanks so much.

WHERE: In front of Langston Hughes Library at 100th Street & Northern Boulevard

WHEN: Tuesday 11/12/13 at 5pm

 

Please come and bring your families so that together we can gather our united strength as we ask for our 3 Points.




1-WE must have more resources for Police enforcement of existing traffic laws.

2- WE must have more traffic-calming zones.

3-WE must have continued traffic-safety education for the children of our community.

 

Last December a young boy was killed on the corner of 81st and Northern Boulevard while walking to school one morning. With that bitter loss still on our minds, 3 weeks ago another young man had his life cut short at Broadway and 58th Street. 2 weeks later, again, this time a small boy, not yet three years-old was struck dead at the fast intersection of Junction and Northern Boulevards.

 

We repeat, "No More!"




Please come and lend your support to the other families of our community. Please March with your neighbors as we travel down Northern Boulevard to the 115th Precinct and then on to 82nd Street as we move towards the Post Office at 79th and 37th Avenue. With song and voices raised high we will be joined by community action-facilitators to point us towards  greater safety for our children.

 

Much has been done. We need more and we need it NOW!

Let there be no mistake that 3 children dead for walking through the streets of our community is 3 Children Too Many.




Please join us at www.3-children-too-many@yahoogroups.com for continued updates and more ongoing information about how to access safer roads for your family.




Meet at 5PM Tuesday November 12 at 100th St & Northern Blvd

OR join us ON OUR ROUTE on Northern Blvd.or 82nd Street or at 37th Avenue & 79th Street.

 

We will march Rain or Shine.

 

Please do not miss this important opportunity to support safety in your community.

 

Whether you own a car or use public transportation, we all walk.

Let us tame the recklessness that plagues our residential streets with important safety measures.

Our children deserve no less.

Dear Concerned Neighbors, and fellow Pedestrians- Drivers too!
We would like to invite you to join us at Café Uruguaya at 6:30 pm to discuss logistics and other sundry topics of our March.
The planning is incredible and the outreach to Public Officials and Elected Representatives is surpassing our goals.
We are a small group doing a big thing, hence its hard to update interested parties.
Join us tonight, if you'd like to sign on to help or learn more about any of the following-
.       Finalization of the March stops and the times we will be at the various stops

2.       Who will “man” each stop outpost

3.       Identification of agencies, organizations, businesses which need further outreach from us and dividing those up to contact early next week

4.       List of committed speakers and where they will speak

5.       People we would like to invite as speakers or agencies we want to encourage to provide a speaker

6.       Supplies needed and who will be responsible for getting the various supplies and bringing them to where they need to be

7.       How to best utilize the offer of funds from Queens Museum

8.       Talking points and determination of unified voice and effective approach for the Councilmember meetings. When we walk out of those meetings, what do we want each of the Councilmembers to have committed to?

Three Children Too Many- March for Pedestrian Safety

Please feel free to share this info with contacts who might be interested.

Jackson Heights/Corona- Alarmed and outraged by the tragic death of innocent children run over by cars, dozens of Jackson Heights and Corona residents will march to reduce pedestrian deaths by 1) demanding increased New York City Police Department enforcement of traffic laws; 2) demanding 20 mile per hour speed limits and traffic calming infrastructure to protect all pedestrians 3) involving our entire community in changing laws and increasing local action to save pedestrian lives.

Join Us 11/12/13, as we March and Rally for safer streets and honor the families...our neighbors, who have had their kids killed by reckless driving. Personally, some days  I know its just luck that separates me from those families who have lost loved ones due to reckless driving. I think we have a  better chance than mere luck. That we can improve the amount of enforcement the NYPD does in these neighborhoods...Jackson Heights, Corona, and East Elmhurst. The concept of Vision Zero, meaning eliminating deaths from traffic may be the second biggest deciding factor on which our next Police Commissioner will be chosen.
To RSVP, or learn more about where we're coming from check out our event page on facebook https://www.facebook.com/events/484223118352033/

A separate Three Children Too Many group page is also there, with more articles and posts.
Thanks all!
