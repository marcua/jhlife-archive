Neighborhood Chat / AB Winery Wine Tasting tonight 6 to 9 PM
« on: February 07, 2014, 11:03:25 AM »
Wine Tasting tonight 6 to 9 pm.

Featuring wines are Dolce Bianco,  Domaine Mas Barrau (our wine of the day) and Bodega Sur de los Andes.

Domaine Mas Barrau - Cabernet Franc has a wild ripeness of sweet scented fruit twinned with an edgy woodland perfume. The palate is incredibly long and very rich - scrumptiously juicy. Best pair with roasted or grilled red meats and vegetables or a good cheeseboard.

Dolce Bianco - a semi-sparkling white wine is elaborated from 100% Verdejo grapes accompanied by a smooth and fine sparkle. It is a semisweet wine with intense freshness, very fruity on the nose. Can be substitute for champagne or sparkling wine during dinner functions, and even as an aperitif or a cocktail.

Bodega Sur de los Andes - featured wine of the day a few days ago. This Malbec has deep purple game color. Sweet jammy raspberry and blackberry and graphite aromas. A round entry leads to a dryish medium-bodied palate with sweet vanilla, tart black berry, and toasty pepper spice flavors.
