    Hi Everyone,

    I hope you can all make the upcoming party on Wednesday, August 20th at Novo to support the 2008 Jackson Heights Film and Food Festival. Another short reminder will go out closer to 8/20 but please mark your calendars now!

    + Time: 7:00 p.m. - 10:00 p.m.
    + Place: Novo at 78-23 37th Avenue between 78th and 79th streets
    + Suggested donation: $20 or whatever you can do - everyone welcome - no one is turned away!
    + All proceeds go to production costs -
    + Hope you'll come and bring a friend! [/li]

For those of you new to the nabe - the Jackson Heights Film and Food Festival kicks off its third year on Saturday, September 13th with the much anticipated Taste of Jackson Heights. The festival will continue on Thursday, September 18 through Sunday, September 21st with film screenings and the Kid's Fest at the Eagle Theater.  Check back at www.jhfff.org for the specific festival shows and times.

Come to the Novo party on August 20 and with your donation enjoy a complimentary glass of wine or soft drink , see old friends and make new ones, enjoy fun raffles and support the production of this years Jackson Heights Film and Food Festival!
 
The festival is a program of the Community Journal and celebrates the diversity of our neighborhood and works to strengthen the JH economy and vitality of our local businesses. The festival relies on the support of scores of neighborhood volunteers and has grown larger and more popular each year. The primary sponsor of the festival is Capital One bank.

For more info about the Festival or the Novo party e-mail Bryan Pu-Folkes at the communityjournal@gmail.com.
