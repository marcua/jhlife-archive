I love the Jewish Community Center thrift store!  It's small, but it's a real thrift store- not a store calling itself as such and selling used stuff for a ridiculous amount of money, nor is it a thrift "boutique" for hipsters. It's staffed mainly by elderly folks, and there can be some real treasures, there (depending on your definition of that word - we got a vintage McCoy figure from the old Star Trek series and some great records). They are also the area's #1 headquarters for crazy 80's clothes.
