Restaurants & Food / Sign up For Farm Spot CSA Summer 2015!!
« on: March 22, 2015, 07:08:59 PM »
Hey Potential Farm Spotters! 

We are are currently accepting applications for Farm Spot's Community Supported Agriculture program (CSA) for Jackson Heights' Summer 2015 Season!

This year we have Golden Earthworm Vegetable Shares, Briermere Fruit Shares and this year we are partnering with Said Atiq and Sali Artisans to offer olive oil, amandou (nut butter) and Morrocan spice options.

As in previous years, our vegetable distributions will be at St. Mark's Church 5:30pm - 8:15pm every Thursday for 26 weeks starting on May 28th and ending on November 19th.

The fruit share will run for 16-20 deliveries, starting at the end of June/beginning of July, depending on when the fruit is ripe.   The Sali Artisans shares are on specific dates mentioned below.


Click here to sign up!!  https://www.farmigo.com/join/farmspot/summer2015

Golden Earthworm Organic Vegetable Share ($587.08)

This share contains 6 to 10 organic items each week, depending on the season and availability. Shares may include items such as: In spring: Arugula, Lettuce, Radishes, Strawberries, Sugar Snap Peas, Swiss Chard, Beets. In summer: Watermelon, Potatoes, Garlic, Red Tomatoes, Basil, Green Beans, Eggplant, Zucchini In fall: Fennel, Potatoes, Lettuce, Broccoli, Sweet Potatoes, Carrots, Toscano Kale, Winter Squash, Celeriac.  The amount is enough to feed a family of three to four for a week.Many Farm Spot members split one share between two households. Please note that members organize this on their own and Farm Spot does not coordinate half shares.


Briermere Fruit Sharimirganic) ($218.40)

Golden Earthworm Farm is teaming up with Briermere Farms to offer a non-organic, yet responsibly grown, minimally treated fruit share. Beginning in July, the fruit share includes blueberries, raspberries, blackberries, peaches, pears, apples, and possibly apricots, plums and nectarines. (Fruit share is available without the vegetable share.) The value of each fruit share is deducted from the total season’s payment and fruit is delivered weekly until the full value has been delivered.

Sali Artisan Shares

Mini Share - $31.80 This is a one time/ one delivery share consisting of 2 - 8oz. bottles of olive oil, 1 almond butter and one tin of Moroccan spices. (Distributed on May 28th only)

Olive Oil Share - $31.80 - 3 x 8 oz bottles  This olive oil is cold- pressed, with no refinement or chemicals.  (Distributed on May 28, Sep 3 and Nov 5th)

Regular Share - $79.50 - Olive Oil - 6x 8 oz bottles (or equivalent), Almond Butter- 3 x 8 oz jar, Ras Hanout Spice - 1 x 5 oz tin  (Distributed on May 28, Sep 3 and Nov 5th)



Subsidized Shares

We have a limited number of subsidized shares.  Please check the income requirements to make sure you are eligible before signing up for these shares.  We are also accepting donations towards our subsidized share program.  We really appreciate even small donations, it helps us to bring CSA shares to folks who might not otherwise be able to join the Farm Spot community!


VOLUNTEERING

There is a volunteer commitment of 4 hours per membership (remember, if two households split a share, it is a total of 4 hours per share, not per household). Please feel free to contact Jessica Ramos, our volunteer coordinator, with any questions: volunteerfarmspot@gmail.com



SHARE PICK-UP

Thursdays between 5:30 pm - 8:15 pm, at St. Mark's Church, 33-50 82nd Street, Jackson Heights. If you do not pick up your share during those hours, it will be donated appropriately, we are unable to save shares. If you are unable to pick up your share yourself, you may ask a friend or neighbor (even if they are not a Farm Spot member) to pick up your share on your behalf. Please remember to give them the principal share member's name (the name that your share is listed under in the sign-up book).


TERMS

Members understand that it is their responsibility to stay informed of CSA announcements each week either through our website, e-mail or at the distribution site. In order to minimize our expenses, we will be communicating all Farm Spot related information via email.


Farm Spot is a community-based, volunteer-run group. We appreciate your interest, support and feedback. Looking hope to seeing you in May! Feel free to forward this information to anyone who is interested.  Email us at farmspot@gmail.com if you have any questions. 

Warmly,

The Farm Spot Core Group

Restaurants & Food / Sign up for Village Fishmonger through Farm Spot's CSA!
« on: April 01, 2014, 06:07:01 PM »
Village Fishmonger CSF (Community Supported Fishery) is offering deliciously fresh, local seafood shares to the members of Farm Spot for the 2014 Summer season!

Our fish and shellfish are sourced directly from local fishermen and shellfish harvesters, which we then process and package for you in our kitchen in Brooklyn.  You'll be able to sign-up to pick-up fresh, local seafood on a weekly and bi-weekly basis during the season on the same days and times that you pick up your vegetable share.

We will be offering a 1 lb or 2 lb size share of mostly fin-fish, plus add-on shares of clams, oysters, and scallops as well as a la carte items that can be ordered on a week to week basis.  Low-mercury, allergy-sensitive, and Kosher options are available too. You can sign up any time, and you’ll be prorated for deliveries until the end of the season. Note that only those households that sign up for the season can order a la carte items.

To sign up for a seafood share as a new member:
Go to the Village Fishmonger website (www.villagefishmongernyc.com)
Click on 'Sign Up For Fish Shares'
Choose your pick-up location, in this case, Farm Spot CSA
Choose from these 2 share sizes and frequencies by choosing the appropriate 'Farm Spot CSA' schedule:
Small Share:  ~1 lb per distribution, which will comfortably feed a family of 2 for 1 meal.
Weekly deliveries for the season --> 24 weeks x 16.50 per week = $396.00
Bi-weekly deliveries for the season --> 12 weeks x 16.50 per week = $198.00
Medium Share:  ~2 lb per distribution, which will comfortably feed a family of 2 for 2 meals.
Weekly deliveries for the season --> 24 weeks x 33.00 per week = $792.00
Bi-weekly deliveries for the season --> 12 weeks x 33.00 per week = $396.00

Choose your Add-Ons: You have the ability to add the following to your distribution to be received along with your main share: Clams, Oysters, Scallops

Choose the 'CSA Local members' payment plan
You will need to then create a Farmigo account to continue with the sign-up process.
For payment, please choose the one time payment with a debit or credit card.
In sign-up comments box, note any allergies (shellfish) or dietary restrictions (low mercury for expectant and nursing mothers, kosher for those who would like to eliminate non-kosher fish from their shares), if any.
Go to our pick-up on the delivery date and pick up your seafood!


PLEASE NOTE:
Though Farm Spot CSA's summer season will be 26 weeks long, Village Fishmonger shares will only be be available for 24 distribution dates during the 2014 season.  We will be off the week of July 4th and Labor Day.
Sign-up deadlines for the seafood share is midnight the Monday before delivery.
Only those households signed up for the subscription can order a la carte items.
To ensure that dietary and allergy restrictions are applied to your account, please make sure you enter that information during sign-up or send us an email shortly after sign up with the pertinent information.  If you had restrictions for the previous season, they will still be noted on your account.
If you do not pick up your fish share during the distribution time, it will be forfeited and donated.
If you need to sell your fish shares, you must notify Village Fishmonger. There are absolutely no refunds for canceled shares.
Be sure to read through their FAQs (http://www.villagefishmongernyc.com/#!faqs/cmkz) and CSF (http://www.villagefishmongernyc.com/#!policies/c1u8w) policy before signing up.


If you haven't signed up for Farm Spot yet....don't wait!!! Click here to sign up for vegetables, herbs and fruit!!!! http://www.farmigo.com/join/farmspot/summer2014

The Farm Spot Core Team

Restaurants & Food / Re: Join Farm Spot - Jackson Heights' CSA!
« on: March 25, 2014, 08:42:18 AM »
Here is the reason we only accept

electronic checks...can I pay by cash or check?
Farm Spot only accepts electronic checks. They are a safe, simple way for members to pay, and an efficient way for us to process the hundreds of applications we receive. In the past, volunteers spent weeks processing these applications manually, resulting in numerous complaints about delays in cashing checks. Electronic checks are a safe and secure alternative, and allow us to quickly receive funds, issue a receipt, and pay the farm in a timely manner. Feel free to email us at farmspot@gmail.com with any further questions.
