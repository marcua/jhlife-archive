Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Free world premiere music concert: Jackson Heights Suite
« on: September 13, 2011, 11:02:43 PM »
Free world premiere music concert: Jackson Heights Suite is an original musical chamber work, composed by Emiliano Valerio, 2011 Queens Council on the Arts Grant Winner!  The Suite, dedicated to the neighborhood of Jackson Heights, Queens, draws on the traditions of Indian Classical, Latin Jazz and Western Classical musics.  Musicians include:  Steve Gorn (Bansuri/Sax), Emiliano Valerio (Congas, CajÃ³n & Tabla) and members of the Mivos String Quartet: Joshua Modney (Violin), Victor Lowrie (Viola), and Isabel Castelvi (Cello).  Funded by Queens Council on the Arts, the performance is free and open to the public.

Date: Friday, September 23, 2011
Time: 7:30 PM - 8:30 PM
Location: Renaissance Charter School (35-59 81st St., Jackson Heights, NY 11372)
Closest Subway stop is on the 7 train at 82nd St stop.  The school is two blocks away.
FREE!
