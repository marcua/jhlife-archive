I've lived in 2 coops and never had any problems.

As long you you practice common sense with courtesy to other you'll be fine. There were some silly rules in my houserules too, like no TV after 11pm... but really, unless you're blasting the tv at 3am no ones going to have a problem with you watching Leno at 11:30...lol.

I much prefer a coop where all you have to worry about is the maintenance charge, vs a house that comes with a lot more responsibilities and monthly costs, not to mention the double or triple up front cost.

I like prewar buildings, but even those I could not justify the higher upfront costs. You can get a nice 2 bed post war coop for under 300k with low maintenance and little worries.
