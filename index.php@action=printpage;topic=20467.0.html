Title: NYC Health's rapid test Corona location
Post by: AndyJH on March 30, 2021, 10:37:04 AM
It might be well-known by this point, but I wanted to plug the NYC Health Corona location for rapid COVID tests on Junction Blvd. They're PCR, you make an appointment online (no waiting in long lines!), I'm usually in and out within 5-10 minutes, and I personally have never had to wait more than 2 hours for my results. Earlier in the pandemic, the appointments would disappear very quickly, but lately I've seen a lot more availability.

https://www1.nyc.gov/site/doh/covid/covid-19-rapid-testing.page
