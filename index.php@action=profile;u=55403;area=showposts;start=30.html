Restaurants & Food / Re: Japanese Cafe on 80th: 969 NYC Coffee
« on: November 08, 2016, 07:23:09 AM »
My husband and I went here on Sunday. Our iced matcha and hot matcha lattes were really good and the onigiri were tasty.
The owner is very nice and good humored, you definitely feel right at home when you go in. It's a nice, chill spot to get a snack in.

He's offering miso soup as well. He gave us a free cup to try, and I felt it was better than what I normally get at sushi places. He also sells some western-style pastry (there were croissants and apple turnovers in the case) which looked okay. I hope he'll consider stocking Japanese pastries instead, like melonpan.

An idea I've heard before is for the city to give some kind of financial incentive for businesses to allow for staggered starting times.
