For those of you who do occasional shopping at Whole Foods, you might be interested to know that a new store is opening up at Bryant Park, right near the 7 and F trains. The location is 1095 6th Ave, on the corner of 42nd and 6th Ave.  I think this location will be smaller than the others and be focusing on prepared foods.
