I'll be teaching Open-level 2 hour Yoga workshop on Friday 6/12 in Maspeth from 7 to 9pm.
The workshop is focused on opening our heart in physical and emotional level to deepen our listening abilities to ourselves and others. All the money that is paid for this event will be donated to Clown One Italia Onlus, Italian NPO who works closely with Italian NGO based in Nepal.

We'll start with Yin yoga, with quiet and long-held poses , then move to slow gentle flow. It will end with Yoga Nidra, simple relaxation through guided visualization and meditation. Reservation: sukhastudio@gmail.com
