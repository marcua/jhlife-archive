Restaurants & Food / Re: Yun Cafe under Diversity Plaza
« on: August 29, 2020, 09:16:39 AM »
Visited the Yun Cafe yesterday.
It’s an odd “only-in-Queens” kind of place. A tiny space with some shelves of SE Asian snacks & condiments; display case of tired looking donuts, croissants; and a small menu of freshly made Burmese salads.
I ordered the Tea Leaf salad and Fermented Ginger salad with a side of the longest grain rice I have ever seen. Funky, salty, spicy, sweet. Delicious!!

Restaurants & Food / Farm Food Delivery
« on: April 20, 2020, 09:27:40 AM »
Beiler Family Farm in PA delivers to various locations in NYC, primarily meats & dairy.
I've been ordering from them for years but their home delivery is now via FedEx, for me shipping cost is prohibitively expensive.
But they also truck to pickup spots and at one time there was a drop-off in Woodside but now the closest to JH is in Astoria.

If enough folks out there are interested they would gladly restart service to a central location somewhere along the #7 train corridor.
Please check out this small family-run farm's offerings. All organic, pasture raised meats, raw dairy and more.
https://beilerfamilyfarm.grazecart.com/store
