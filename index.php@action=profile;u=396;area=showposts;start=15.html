Authenticity is certainly an important factor for me. If you've tasted the difference between sloppy American-Chinese takeout and the real deal in China - there is just NO comparison. Neighborhood Chat / Re: Chinese Decorations?
« on: October 21, 2008, 07:05:23 PM »
I can't think of any place in Flushing that would suit your needs. Most items that you are seeking will be sold in Chinatown (any vendor along Canal or Mott) to tourists; also check out Pearl River Mart. I know there's a new furniture store in Queens Crossing Mall in Flushing, but it's very western. There may be a few Chinese tschokes in there though.

As for food - check out chowhound for recommendations. I like Imperial Palace for dimsum - that's right next to the mall.
