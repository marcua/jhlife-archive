Title: Community Dance/Movement class in JH- Saturday, November 26th, 2-3 pm
Post by: GC12 on November 24, 2011, 02:05:56 PM
Hello JH Families,


I am happy to share with you the new location/time of dance classes I'm offering to this community.
This is a fun mix of rhythms and movement drawn from Afro-Caribbean dance traditions. No worries about prior dance experience or lack there of: the main focus here is in sharing dance in a fun
environment and a relaxed setting. It's a great way to work out, move to the rhythms, and learn some new steps. Class is OPEN level and the community -children and adults- are all encouraged to participate!


Class info:

Community Dance/Movement class - Saturday, November 26th, 2-3 pm
1 hour dance class at PS 69 ground floor-cafeteria

Class is $15-adults; children are free, with accompanying parent/caregiver, otherwise children who are dropped off are also $15.


Class schedule: Saturday, November 26, December 3, 10, 17. This is Just a trial run of classes for a month, and will resume after the Christmas/New Year holiday.


Come and kick off the holiday season with some fun community dance with loved ones right in the heart of Jackson Heights!
