The current lease will end in 2050. However, there is a renewal clause and that is what will happen.

I moved into Roosevelt Terrace with my parents in 1954. We bought our apartment in 1981. There is nothing to be concerned about with regard to the land lease issue. The Board of Directors has the situation under control. They will take the renewal and keep the annual rent as it has been since the beginning.

I would advise anyone who is considering looking and buying a place to come over and check us out. Not only is the land lease not an issue, but this coop has a large reserve fund and a great operating fund as well. This is definitely a safe investment but also a great place to live
