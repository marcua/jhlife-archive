Neighborhood Chat / Re: List of Local Non-Profits In Need
« on: December 23, 2010, 09:02:10 PM »
Operation Santa
Though it's too late this year...

It is run out of the main post office in NYC across from Penn Station. Children, Mother's and Father's write letters to Santa asking for gifts. A few years ago we helped a single father with 2 boys, they wanted boots, mittens, and socks! Other years we have bought barbies, cars, and mostly toys. Basically you go the the office and chose your letter (the hardest part) they are often heartbreaking and I wish I could help all of them. You then buy the items you chose and head back to the post office to mail them.
