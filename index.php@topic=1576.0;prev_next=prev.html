Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

A few volunteers needed for JH Film and Food Festival
« on: October 14, 2009, 09:43:29 PM »
Help out and meet some neighbors by volunteering for the JH Film and Food Festival!

We have a few spots open for volunteers on the day of the event: Sat. Oct. 24, 2009 in time slots ranging between 3pm and midnight, with duties ranging from picking up food from restaurants, putting up banners, taking tickets, ushering, serving food, etc.  A coordination meeting for volunteers is scheduled for Monday, Oct. 19 at 7pm.

If you are interested in volunteering the day of the event, PM me right away and I will give you contact details for the volunteer coordinator.   Those who are assigned volunteer tasks for the day-of will be given a complimetary ticket to the festival.

We also have opportunities to help out this Sunday, Oct. 18, 2009 from 11 to 2pm with postering in neighborhood businesses.  You will be assigned a partner if you come alone.  PM me if you can commit to this and I will send you additional details.   Sorry, no complimentary tickets for poster volunteers.
