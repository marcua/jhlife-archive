Just a reminder, classes begin today at the Community Church, classroom #2.

We have two (2) seats left if anyone is still interested.

Name of the Program:    Cuentos y Vocabulario    (Vocabulary and Stories)

Day(s):                           Tuesday(s) and Thursday(s)   -     (2x week)                     

Time:                             6:15 P.M.  - 7:00 P.M. (45 minutes)

Where:                            United Methodist Church (Classroom #2)

Price:                               $175.00 for a 6 week program
                                       There will be a registration fee of $20 per child (nonrefundable).

Beginning:                        Tuesday/September 16, 2008

Child (Age):                      4 - 6 years old

Synopsis of Class:   This is a structured Spanish program, where story time, songs and Spanish culture is
                               introduced. In this program, emphasis on accent and pronunciation is reinforced.
                               Literature that illustrates the theme of the class will be displayed as well as didactic
                               material. Occasionally handouts are given to practice at home.  Previous instruction is
                               not required. Spanish heritage students are welcome too.

                              We will be using the Spanish Immersion Method:



Spanish Immersion means that your child is immersed, or surrounded by, the Spanish language during his/her session. Your child will enjoy music, books and games in Spanish. In this language immersion environment, when a child is enjoying an activity he/she learns the educational concept and the language at the same time.

Since the first class your child will immediately be hearing Spanish words, however, the teacher will utilize English as required to ensure children understand the rules and expectations, get to know their teacher and are made to feel comfortable. Throughout the session, the teacher, when necessary, will show or dramatize what they are saying, however, in a preschool environment virtually every opportunity provides self-explanatory translation and so the children can grasp meanings from their very first day in class.



Teacher:               Maria Jose Penaherrera, Co-Founder of "Easy Espanol Inc."
                            BA -  Social Communication and Literature, Universidad Catolica de Quito,
                             Ecuador
                            MA- Communications, Universidad Andina Simon Bolivar
                            Solomon Schechter School of Manhattan
                            7th and 8th grade Spanish Teacher
                            Full time Spanish Teacher at Easy Espanol Inc.
                            www.easyespanol.org             

If you are interested, please email me "off-list" at moeandvic@yahoo.com
and I will send you a registration form.

Please note, space is limited and I am only taking 12 students at a time.  First come, first serve basis.
