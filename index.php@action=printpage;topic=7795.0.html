Title: "In Montauk" (partially filmed in Jackson Heights) has week run at ReRun 10/5
Post by: kcummings on October 03, 2012, 12:09:00 PM

Jackson Heights filmmaker Kim Cummings is thrilled to announce that her film, "In Montauk" will be screening for a week (excluding Saturday & Sunday) at the reRun GastroPubTheater in Brooklyn.

Times: 6:45 pm Doors for 7:30 pm screening
           9:30 pm Doors for 10:15 pm screening
Where: reRun GastroPub Theater (http://reruntheater.com/index.php)
            147 Front Street (DUMBO)
Closest train: F to York Street stop
Tickets are $9
Buy tickets online at: http://www.eventbrite.com/event/4109323096
