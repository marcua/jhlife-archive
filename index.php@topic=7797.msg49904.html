$219k Newly Renovated Sponsor Unit - No Board Approval - Low Down! Flushing
« on: October 03, 2012, 06:57:34 PM »

144-16 78TH RD
FLUSHING, NY

3rd floor walk up - Garden Style Complex with close to
500 Apartments spread throughout almost 3 city blocks.

Newly renovated

Original Hardwood Flooring - restored on Sedona Red

Solid Wood Cabinetry with Black Galaxy Countertops
& Back Splash.  Stainless steel Appliances including
Side by side fridge with water & ice dispenser, Range,
Over the range Microwave hooked to exterior exhaust and
full size Dishwasher

Gutted bathroom - tiled to the ceilings with small subway
Multicolored Mosaic glass border on walls and floors. 

Temperature sensor LED Showerhead

Updated electrical with lots of accent lighting

Breakfast Bar / Open layout

Unit opens into hallway and bedrooms at
the end of small corridor for extra Privacy

Motion Activated Lights at entrance

Too much to list but at least you will be glad to know that this unit is fully renovated and details such as new screens, through the walls coaxial connection and dedicated AC outlets in every room have not been overlooked.  The $760 maintenance is partially tax deductible and you may sublet your apartment after 1 year of possession.  Relatively easy street parking at most hours and assigned parking spaces are available for $125 and $150 for indoor garage.  Walk to trains (10 to 12 minute walk or 2 bus stops to the E express) and into midtown in 25 minutes.    While not as convenient or as diversified as Jackson Heights this unit is located in School District 25 and the supermarket, pharmacy, spirits store and the cleaners are a mere 2 blocks away.  There are many other conveniences such as Kosher & Non-Kosher restaurants and several banks within 4 or 5 blocks.

This unit is Sponsor owned and will not require Board approval to purchase.  While the complex allows 10% Down we would prefer at least 15% to 20% Down.  Brokers / agents representing buyers with Pre-approval letter in hand may text me to set up appointments as well.   

Thanks for reading!

917-577-2868
