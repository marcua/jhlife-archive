Title: Robbery in drugstore
Post by: Garsleat on February 20, 2021, 08:33:15 PM
I was shopping at a Rite Aid on Kissena Blvd., while it's not in JH, I thought it's important PSA to let people know about this incident. As I was paying, a manager was screaming at a male, telling him he just called the cops and will arrive soon. It turned out this guy was swiping merchandises into his backpack with in intention of stopping. I was freaking out, but my transaction was already in process, so just praying this guy didn't have any weapon. He continued to steal as I left. That's why drugstores are locking up merchandises and having security guards at entrance now. So everyone just be alert and be safe. Yes these employees are just as essential and also our everyday heroes. This manager wasn't afraid and approached the perpetrator. Thank God nothing happened.
