 I order often through Seamless and Grubhub and when you ask for pick up they always ask if you would like to leave a tip. My very flexible rule of thumb is one dollar for every $20 of a pickup. I mean, you give a bartender a dollar or two for pouring a drink, it’s a lot more effort to pack up food, put them in bags with silverware, napkins, etc.

 I know this is a little off topic, but I can’t help but notice some restaurants in Brooklyn only give you silverware and napkins if requested. In our neighborhood, I don’t think one place that does that. I often forget to tell them, and even when I do it’s still there I think because they’re used to putting it in. So I got home and I have six new plastic forks and knoves, a ton of napkins and condiments. Such a waste.
