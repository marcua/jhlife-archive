Title: YOGATHON Spring 2016
Post by: mimiformeyoga on March 12, 2016, 09:22:57 AM

The 108 Sun Salutations Spring Yogathon is great way of breaking out of the winter darkness, a way to "Spring Clean" the body and mind as we celebrate the return of longer days , our community, and our own amazing spirit!

Join us for this Spring 2016 Challenge.  108 Sun Salutations will be divided (54 Salutes) into 2 days:
Sunday March 20th and Saturday March 26th - The first week of Spring.  3-5pm.

There will be treats, goody bags, and best of all your own sense of accomplishment as you spring into Spring2016 with an awakened, and renewed sense of self.

Limited spots so please be sure to grab one asap!
Pre-registration until Friday March 18th - $36 for the complete series.
If spots left thereafter -  $45
