Hello!

My name is Melissa and I'm the communications coordinator at PianoVerse on 82-07 Northern Boulevard. We've been in the Jackson Heights area for years and recently converted the location into a new piano center. We aim to offer affordable piano lessons, piano sales (digital & acoustic), practice rooms and piano tuning for the Jackson Heights community and surrounding areas. We believe that it is really important to make music education affordable and accessible to people of ALL ages

We've joined this forum to communicate with members of the community because we truly do care about hearing out your questions, interests, opinions and ideas to help us become better at accommodating your musical needs. We're also always trying to think up new promotion ideas to make music more accessible and manageable.

Piano lessons (levels 1-8) come in 4-lesson packages allowing students to easily track their progress and know what they're paying for. All lessons are private so that every student has the teacher's undivided attention.
