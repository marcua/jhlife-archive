Re: How to get to CitiField
« Reply #2 on: April 04, 2010, 11:19:51 PM »
To get from Citi Field to 92nd Street & 34th Avenue, take the 7 Train toward Manhattan.  Get off at Junction Blvd. (not exactly the closest station, but either a "local" or "express" train will get you there).  Walk three long blocks north to 34th Avenue (you'll pass 37th Ave. and 35th Ave. on the way).  From Junction you'll make a left on 34th Ave. and walk 2-3 short blocks.  The walk should take about 10-15 minutes.   Make sure you buy your subway ticket before the game, or you'll be waiting on a long line when the game is over!

To get to Penn Station, you'll need to get back on the 7 Train, again toward Manhattan.  There are several ways to reach Penn, but the easiest may be to travel all the way to the end of the line, Times Square.  Transfer to the 1 Train toward downtown, and take this one stop, to 34th Street.  Penn Station is toward the front of the train.

I hope this helps!  Enjoy the game.
