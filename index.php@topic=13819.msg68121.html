Tired Emoji yesterday for lunch.  We had what is basically their cheeseburger on the sesame bun and the fries. My reaction was mixed--was lacking a bit in taste...maybe the burger seems overwhelmed by the bun and lettuce inside.   I enjoyed the fries.  Will want to try some of the other burger choices.  Don't think this compares to Shake Shack--although I think their burger quality is not what it was now that it is a global company vs. when it was just a shack in Madison Sq. Park.   As someone else noted, one thing Emoji can improve is the quality of its ketchup and mustard--the yellow mustard and ketchup packs don't live up to the quality burger joint they are going for.  Appreciate the thought in the decor and creative menu.  By the looks of it, this place will be successful, I will definitely give it another try.
