Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Award winning indie film, Entre Nos, will have a special screening in Jackson Heights, Queens for one weekend only.

Location:
Natives Theatre / Restaurant
82-22 Northern Blvd.
Jackson Heights, NY 11372

Dates:
Friday, May 7th, 2010 -- 8:00 pm
Saturday, May 8th, 2010 -- 3:00, 6:00 & 8:00 pm
Sunday, May 9th, 2010 -- 4:00 & 7:00 pm

*** Cast is scheduled to be present at all screenings ***


Ticket Pricing:  $10.00 each
To purchase tickets in person please call (212) 684-2333 ext 320

Movie Screening Tonight - Entre Nos - Natives
« Reply #2 on: May 07, 2010, 03:46:41 PM »
I saw posters for what looks to be an interesting movie shot mostly in Jackson Heights - ENTRE NOS. They are screening the movie at Natives Theater on Northern Boulevard starting tonight and all this weekend.

There was a full page article written about the movie in El Correo interviewing the main actress who grew up in Jackson Heights. So glad to hear that they're bringing the movie back into the neighborhood to share with the community.

Looks like the cast will be at all the screenings starting tonight at 8PM.  
Check the site for more info: entrenosfilm.com

Off the site....
In Entre Nos, adoring mother Mariana has toted her two children from Colombia to New York City to indulge her husband's whim. But when he abruptly abandons the family, she'll have to rely on her own imagination and courage of her remarkable kids to survive insurmountable odds during their first summer in the United States.
