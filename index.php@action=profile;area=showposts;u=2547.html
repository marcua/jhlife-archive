Neighborhood Chat / Women for Moya Rally last Saturday
« on: August 29, 2010, 03:12:59 PM »
Did anyone else go to this? 

I went with my girlfriend and the turnout was really good.  My girlfriend really loves Nydia Velazquez and she gave a very empowering speech about domestic violence and how this year was the 90th anniversary of women getting the right to vote.  She also talked about how Moya used to work for her and how he would be strong asset to the community in Albany.

There were five or six other elected officials there and they all stressed how Monserrate was not someone that cared about womens issues.

Francisco Moya introduced his mother which was nice to see.  I thought he also gave a strong speech about how issues that effect women were something which has been important to him and how he helped set up a women's clinic in Queens when he worked at Elmhurst Hospital.

All in all it was pretty cool.
