Hi, I just saw your post about yoga classes. I can tell you about the new place in the neighborhood I have been attending. I am new to yoga and had a predisposed notion of what yoga was about. But when I took Mimi's class it was a total eye opener. It was relaxing, invigorating, inspiring, overall a great experience. That was about a month ago, since then I have taken a couple of classes by the other instructors and it was just as satisfying. The guided meditation class by Jared was again an eye opener in that you're not just sitting cross legged chanting ohm! There is movement and thought and insight. A recommended class.

Just want to say welcome to the yoga community and I hope you give Mimi for Me Yoga a try. It is located at 79-05 Roosevelt Avenue on the 2nd floor and their number is 718-505-YOGA (9642). The studio is newly renovated and extremely comfortable.

PS They also offer a variety of dance fitness classes, as a matter of fact there is a Tango series starting soon, sounds like alot of fun. Check out their website www.mimiformeyoga.com for schedules
