Neighborhood Chat / Re: Next JH Artist's meeting on October 3rd, at the Y Gallery
« on: October 13, 2008, 09:00:00 PM »

In response to your question:

I think that the meeting went quite well!

there were about ten of us (all local artists).

Cecilia (the Y Gallery director) gave an interesting talk about the gallery, where she described her vision, her artists, her choices to open a space in JH and some of her future plans.  We all enjoyed her original and non compromising artistic direction, her optimism and resourcefulness.

Dulce Pinzon gave an intriguing presentation of her artwork (photographs), some of the concepts behind her projects and the process that lead to them. Hearing about her process and how she collaborates with her subjects gave us an even more expanded view about her photography.

If you want to learn more about her work, please do check out her web site listed in the above email.

At the end of our meeting we touched briefly on the subject of real estate and our search for studio space.  That conversation was not very conclusive.  we need to dedicate more time to it next meeting.

We are planning on meeting next time on a weekend, so more people can attend. It was nice to have an intimate group, but it will be also amazing to join forces with more artists.  Everyone who showed up expressed their interest in coming to more meetings. 

Please keep spreading the word!!!

So the meeting went quite well although it was a bit chaotic towards the end with my kids (sorry). Let's consider finding a solution for the toddlers/babies for next time (maybe an in-house sitter or a play corner or Sesame Street in a portable video).

Here is what we discussed in brief:


We spoke bout the possibility of creating a special list serve and web site for JH artists where we can share info, exchange ideas and have links to personal sites.

We will hold monthly meetings with guests speakers, presentations of our artworks and continue discussing different topics that are relevant to the community.

We will put together a "Space Committee" that will be in charge of searching for a large commercial space (walking distance) where we can rent studios.

We will keep everybody posted about up and coming events and our next steps.
