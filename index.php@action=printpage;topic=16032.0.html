Title: Re: Seatide Fish Market
Post by: JA on February 23, 2017, 06:13:03 PM
Pacific is good albeit sometimes chaotic and not the cleanest. They have good deals on salmon and shellfish. Food Dynasty sells flash-frozen bengali river fish - these are good but not very versatile for non-bengali cooking. FoodTown sometimes has specials but be weary of the age of fish there as I have gotten some weird smelling shrimps at times. Greenpoint Fish and Lobster has a new shop in LIC off the Hunters Point 7 stop that I have been to a few times. Pricy but the stuff is incredible quality, very clean and fresh, and sustainably sourced.

One of the things I really wish we had here was an independent fishmonger.
