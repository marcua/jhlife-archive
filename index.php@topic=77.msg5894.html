I'm very sensitive to street noise and noise from surrounding apartments when I am trying to sleep.  Whenever I move I keep the following in mind:

Is the bedroom located in the rear of the building?
Is there a traffic light close to my window where traffic would back up and car horns would blow?
Does the bus go down your block?
Are you across the street from a park or a school?

These tips help for outside noise.  Inside noise is harder to avoid.  Sometimes it is just the luck of the draw.  Although being on the top floor helps you avoid the sounds of those walking above you.  If you have a couple who enjoys letting the world know how much they are enjoying themselves I just scream YES YES YES and that seems to quiet them down right away
