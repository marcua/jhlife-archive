Title: Praise for the uncongested V train [AM New York article]
Post by: michaelw on December 31, 2007, 04:31:23 PM
Some (like me) prefer the V train because you get a seat and it is only a bit slower:
http://www.amny.com/news/local/transportation/am-vtrain1231,0,6068788.story

I will always take it if it comes, rather than wait for an F. As the article says, it takes maybe 5 minutes longer.

Coming home in the evening, E and F trains are often queued up to get into 74th-Roosevelt because it takes so long to get all those passengers off at the station. The V train can sail by on the local track and make into the station sooner.
