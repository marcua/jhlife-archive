Hi Joe ! Welcome !

While the early evening bar scene in JH is lacking there are a couple of spots that might suit your needs.

Novo on 37th Ave. bet 78 & 79 has a decent happy hour and good bar tender (Brian) on Mondays and Tuesdays.

Terraza 7 Train Cafe on Glean St. just off Roosevelt Ave & 83rd St. is small, funky, and fun.

Legends on 72nd St. and 35th has draft beer, and old style townie bar feel, and GREAT barbecue (except on Tuesdays)

Cassidy's on 75th and 31st Ave. lots of beer options and food.

Jardi on Northern and 86th St. Good Sangia and GREAT Tappas.

These are the places where I go most often, I am sure there are a couple more. Certainly there are a few folks out there with places to add to the list.

Hi JoeG24.  Welcome to Jackson Heights Life!

Apart from the suggestions provided above by Aronan, nearby Woodside has a nice bar scene.  Cool in Your Code did a Woodside Pub Crawl at some of the more popular ones. What's nice is that these pubs are a hop, skip and a jump from Jackson Heights on the 7 train or Q32 bus.  You can even walk to some of them if you enjoy nice, long walks.  Here's a link to a video from Cool in Your Code:

Woodside Pub Crawl

By the way, they also did a Long Island City Bar Crawl.  You can check the Cool In Your Code site for more info on that.  And while on the subject of Long Island City, that neighborhood just debuted a new bar called Dutch Kills.  It seems like an interesting place to visit at least once.  Here's Time Out New York's take on it:
