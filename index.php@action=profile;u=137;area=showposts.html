Neighborhood Chat / A Good Word for InstaMedCare on Northern
« on: November 10, 2015, 07:39:12 PM »
For the past week I have had bursitis in my left shoulder. I wouldn't wish this on my worst enemy. No amount of painkillers or heating pads did a thing.

Yesterday I went up to InstaMedCare on Northern & 80th St. I saw Dr. Schorr. He confirmed it was bursitis (ouch!) and said it would be treatable by a steroid injection. Unfortunately he didn't have a dose, but would by this morning. Meanwhile there was a gel available, but he didn't want me to order it because it cost $50 for 24 hours relief (until the injection was available.).

Sure enough this morning the shot was available. I received a phone call as soon as it arrived. I won't say it was painless, but the relief was almost immediate.

He has a good patient manner, the nurse was excellent and I'm no longer taking gobs of Tylenol.

Thank you, Dr. S.

This is an unsolicited recommendation, I was only compensated by lack of pain!
:-)
