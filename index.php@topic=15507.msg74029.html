Notices stapled to trees
« on: October 15, 2016, 12:14:43 AM »
Another of my pet peeves is seeing trees with flyers stapled to them. So today I had a verbal altercation with a officer from the 115th who I watched as he did just that, from 90th to 74th. He was stapling No Parking signs for Sunday's parade. When I called him on it, he informed me it was not illegal. After much back and forth, with me telling him it was in fact illegal, as it harms the trees, and the fines start at $150 per violation, he just did not care. After much back and forth, and a crowd gathering, I was so disappointed that a enforcer of our laws, was here blatantly breaking them. So frustrating, since I volunteer to help give these street trees a chance. He eventually relented saying he was just following orders, showing me the staple gun he was given to complete his job. What an idiot. I think someone needs to educate the 115th as to what laws are on the books. I only relented when he appeared to reach for his handcuffs, to deter me from any other further talk. I did report it to 311, but the damage was already done. He also blamed the Community Board, and said it had to be stapled to the trees because of the wind and rain. When I told him that's not an excuse to break the law, and no rain was forecast for the weekend, he said he was just doing his job.
