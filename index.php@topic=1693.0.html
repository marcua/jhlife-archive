I think Terraza 7 Train Cafe (as it's officially known) is a great date place. Funky, just slightly off the beaten path, tables,a hammock , good happy hour prices. Probably best for an early to mid evening encounter as later (post 9pm) they tend to have bands perfomring which I imagine gets int he way of good conversation.

40-19 Gleane St. (off Roosevelt between 83rd and 84th, behind Sovereign bank)
www.terrazacafe.com
