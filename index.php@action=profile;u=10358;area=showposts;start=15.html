Pets and Animals / Re: Dog stolen in front of Growing Farms today, Sunday, 2/24 at 1pm
« on: March 10, 2013, 06:20:51 PM »
I now have a new 'secret angel' mission. If I see a dog waiting outside for its owner and I have the time, I stand a discreet distance away reading emails or the news on my phone until the owner comes out and reunites with the dog. I don't accost the owner, I don't mess with the dog, I'm just standing silent watch. I don't know, it just makes me feel like I'm doing a random act of kindness and that's a good feeling. I would be devastated to lose my Rosie that way and yet I understand the wish to have my dog with me whenever I'm out in the neighborhood. Now I tie her up only if I can see her at all times when I run into a store for a moment. This often means that I walk her, drop her back home and then go right out again for a long shopping, but her safety is worth it.
