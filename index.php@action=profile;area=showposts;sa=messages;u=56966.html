I think it is more about the litter than the box.  I use feline pine and it is SO much better than scooping, but not the best for odor control, just dump the whole box every few days.  One of my older cats decided he didn't like it any more and I have just started using litter made from walnut shell and it is amazing.  No smell, not a pain to scoop (some are horrible and the litter smell is awful), doesn't track much.  Good luck with your kitty!
