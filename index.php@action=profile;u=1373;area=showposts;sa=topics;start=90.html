Neighborhood Chat / Duane Reade @ 73rd - shady!
« on: August 17, 2014, 03:14:13 AM »
i'm not a huge fan of the chain in general, but they have some items I just cannot get (or would have to wildly overpay for) in the neighborhood, so I end up there a couple of times a month. I've noticed that about one of every three times I buy an item that's on sale "with card," the card discount does not get reflected, requiring a complaint, and a delay for people behind me on line.

this past week, the branch on 73rd lost me for good, though. had the circular, which had a deep discount on Colgate toothpaste, and tried a purchase on Tuesday or Wednesday, and no discount was reflected. I asked the cashier, who summoned the manager -- and he pointed to the two varieties in the ad and said "only the ones in the picture are on sale, the others are regular price." I didn't want to make a fuss, so I just asked the cashier to void that item and went on with my purchase.

needed to stop in again yesterday and happened to see that one of the pictured toothpastes was back in stock -- and the sale was still on -- so I grabbed a couple and went to the register. again, no discount. this time, the same manager replied with "it says selected varieties only." I said that the item I had was in the photo with the sale price and he told me "that's not one of the selected varieties on sale." when I brought up our conversation from 72 or so hours earlier, he shrugged and walked away. I shrugged, left my otherwise rung up and bagged order on the counter,  and walked out. done with that place until i'm sure he's gone.
