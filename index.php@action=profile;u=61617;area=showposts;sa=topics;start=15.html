Neighborhood Chat / AC Falls Out of Window
« on: June 05, 2018, 08:30:50 AM »
On the way home last night I saw a bunch of people standing in front of the the Korean grocer on 37th Ave and 80th Street and it turns out a very large air conditioner had fallen from the second story of the building. The AC hit the awning of the Kim Brothers store and busted their awning and luckily no one got seriously hurt. One woman seemed to have a few scratches.

The AC was sitting on one of those platforms that are now required for window ACs. Now it's sitting on the street. If you have a window AC please be sure it's mounted correctly and securely.

Btw, the Dept of Buildings was called to inspect the building. Not sure when they will get to it. They were called also because no one from the building seemed to be present during this emergency.
