Creating a greener downtown JH tomorrow!
« on: October 18, 2013, 09:28:50 PM »
Saturday, Oct. 19, 9 am-1pm
Meet at Diversity Plaza (37th Road).
We’ll be putting mums in 37th Road planters and caring for street trees on 73rd and 74th Streets.
Wear clothes for getting dirty and bring gardening gloves and tools if you have them.
(We’ll have some, too.)

We will be working with merchants, residents from the immediate area and people from all around Jackson Heights. Please join us.

Details - The tree care work will involve:
* Removing bricks from tree pits to improve water reaching roots. We will add compost.
* Adding compost from JHBG’s Scraps composting center to improve the fertility of soil around the tree.
* Topping it off with mulch.

More trees was one of the recommendations that came out of JHBG’s Green Agenda
 for Jackson Heights - part of our plan for bringing Mother Nature back to the city.
