Restaurants & Food / Re: Best low-end Chinese?
« on: November 10, 2010, 09:03:40 PM »
I love JJ Garden too.  They are priced a bit higher for a smaller portion in comparison to other places in the hood, but you get what you pay for.  Also, it's the only Chinese place I've seen in the hood with a nice seating area...it is actually a pleasure to sit and eat there. 

As for your average place in the area, I like Ming Wok on Northern and 84th Street.  Ive been going there so long that the lady recognizes my voice on the phone and my face when I walk in.....not sure if that is a good thing but at least it says I like it enough to go back often.  I haven't gotten sick from their food (yet) or found anything unsavory in my meals (yet).  Also, when they make the occasional mistake, they are always apologetic and very quick to fix my order.

NOT A FAN of China 81 also on Northern.....
