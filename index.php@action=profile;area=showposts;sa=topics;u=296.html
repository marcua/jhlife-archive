Neighborhood Chat / Looking to hire TWO JH residents for 2009 map
« on: April 22, 2009, 03:38:48 PM »
Looking to hire TWO neighborhood residents to sell ad space for all-new 2009 Jackson Heights Map.

Resident cartoonist David Heatley is teaming up with MPC Properties to produce a revamped Jackson Heights map for 2009 featuring all new artwork and twice as many business listings.

We will supply you with a list of businesses to approach. You will go door-to-door with our sales kit, explaining the concept of the map. You will then follow up and get people to send in their checks by the deadline. When the map is printed, you will drop off copies to each business. Most of the job will involve the sign-ups and will span approximately 2 weeks, but the hours of work are entirely flexible. Drop-offs will be a month later and should only take a day.

Salary is $1,200 per person, dependent upon signing up 50 local businesses each and completing the drop-offs. You will be paid in two installments, as each phase of the job is completed. Okay for 2 people to work as a team, either to sign up 100 businesses (for $2,400) or to split up 50 businesses (and share the $1,200). Also okay if one person feels confident about doing all 100 for $2,400.

Qualities we're looking for:

Upbeat attitude
Mature, professional appearance
Good, clear communication skills
Sales experience a plus, but not essential (we're interested in authenticity, not slick sales gimmicks)
Bi-lingual English and Spanish speaker (preferable, not essential)
Knowledge of Jackson Heights and its local businesses
Knowledge of / experience navigating the diverse communities of JH

More opportunities for working with MPC and / or David Heatley are available for the right candidate.

To set up an interview, email a paragraph about yourself to: jacksonheightsmap@gmail.com
