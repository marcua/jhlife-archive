Title: Volunteer for JH event July 27-28 to benefit families at the border
Post by: NYer1008 on July 19, 2019, 09:28:02 PM
Volunteers needed for BROWNIES FOR THE BORDER, to be held Saturday 7/27 & Sunday 7/28 in Travers Park.

Make brownies or something else for the bake sale, help set up and/or run the tables, lead other special activities.

This block party & bake sale, presented by JH Kids for Social Justice, will raise funds for Immigrant Families Together's Rapid Response Fund, aiding families who have been detained and separated at the border.

If you can make or donate baked goods or drinks to be sold, sign up at tinyurl.com/unitedcakes-food (http://tinyurl.com/unitedcakes-food)

To help out one or both days of the event, sign up at tinyurl.com/unitedcakes-volunteer (http://tinyurl.com/unitedcakes-volunteer)
We are looking for people to do facepainting, lead an exercise class or run whatever activity you come up with!

BROWNIES FOR THE BORDER kicks off with a pay-what-you-wish yoga class (by 2nd Story Pilates+Yoga) at 9 AM Saturday, at Travers Park amphitheater on the 78th St. Play Street (between 34th Ave. & Northern Blvd.).
The bake sale/block party follows at 10 AM and runs till 2 PM on Saturday, July 27 and Sunday, July 28.

For more info, go to tinyurl.com/closethecampsjh (http://tinyurl.com/closethecampsjh)
