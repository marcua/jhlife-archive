JH Clean Streets Campaign - March 15
« Reply #1 on: March 09, 2008, 12:01:47 AM »
Reposted from JHFamilies by permission of the author.   More information on the Clean Streets Campaign is available here.


Dear Neighbors,
 
We hope you will join us for breakfast, to kick off the Spring season of the Jackson Heights Clean Streets Campaign.  We'll meet at 9:30 AM on Saturday, March 15th, at Community Church on 35th Ave. & 81st Street.  There's no work this time - just socializing and planning for the new season.
 
In order to improve our Clean Streets program, we are attaching  a brief survey, to solicit your views on how we can shape our goals for the future.  It would be very much appreciated if you could complete the survey and email it back to: info@jhbg.org.
 
We sincerely thank all who have helped in the past and look forward to a new season of beautifying our neighborhood.

Jackson Heights Beautification Group
Clean Streets Clean Up


Special Planting Event

On June 21, 2008, the Jackson Heights Beautification Group will be doing a special project to clean and green the area next to the BQE on 69th Street between 37th Avenue and Northern Blvd.

When:   Saturday, June 21, 8 a.m. to noon

Where:   Meet at White Castle Parking Lot on 34th Avenue and 69th Street

What:   Wear you old clothes and help with cleaning and planting in the area. 

Why:   Help make a statement in favor of a cleaner and greener neighborhood!  Brooms, gloves, and other equipment are supplied.  White Castle is         donating refreshments.

Who:   All are welcome.  Please bring your parents, neighbors or friends. 

Any question you can call Beatriz @ 718 802-0259

JHBG -- Clean Streets
« Reply #4 on: June 18, 2008, 09:37:07 AM »
 
This Saturday Jackson Heights Beautification Group will be having a special clean and green event.  In this "garden neighborhood" there is limited public green space, that is why it is so important that the little green space we have is maintained.  There is an open space on 69th Street between 37 and 34th Avenue next to the BQE which has a nice wall and open green space.  Unfortunately, it has become an area for dumping trash and letting weeds grow. 
 
This Saturday we want to make a statement.  We want to clean up the area and then show our politicians that the city must maintain this area.  Community Board 3 has agreed to put a dog run next to the area (under the BQE) which I hope is the first step to better utilizing this green space.
 
I hope you will come and pitching in, but more importantly, by showing up you  show our local politicians that we want our green spaces maintained!
 
Please come and bring a friend!   If possible, please post this flyer in your building to encourage your neighbors to pitch in for a better Jackson Heights.  If you have any questions, please call myself at 646-210-4495 or Beatrice Ruiz at 347-665-6063.
