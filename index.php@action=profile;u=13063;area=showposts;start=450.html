Restaurants & Food / Re: Best Chinese Delivery/takeout
« on: April 10, 2012, 05:49:35 PM »
I've been ordering with very good results at Cheong Hei Restaurant located at 8148 Baxter St which is around the corner from the
Jackson House Cinema on 82nd St.  It's slightly outside of JH's but they deliver.  I'm always in the area so I haven't used the delivery yet.  What I like about this place is that some of my favorite dishes are orange chicken, orange beef, Tso's Chicken & Sesame Chicken which can be ordered small ($5.75) or large ($9.25).  I always order small which is quite a generous portion and the price includes tax.  The place is always crowded with customers ordering take out.  I've always waited between 20 - 25 minutes which is fine considering the price and quality.  I've used just about every chinese takeout in JH's and I like this one the best.  The only dishes I've ordered are the one's mentioned above.  Strange but I've been there five times and I never once heard the phone ring but they do deliver.

Neighborhood Chat / Re: Soundproof windows
« on: February 29, 2012, 02:13:42 PM »
The Company Rep advised me against 95%.  You'll eliminate almost all external noise but you will have increased internal noise.  You don't want to end up resolving one problem and inheriting another.  The Company Rep was incredibly honest and wasn't looking to increase the price with 95% noise free which he felt that I didn't need.  I went with 70% and probably would have been just fine with 50% which is what he recommended.  Since I was just doing the bedroom the price difference wasn't that much.  You may just want to consider doing the bedroom and see how that works before you do the entire apartment.

This is one improvement that will certainly help if you should decide to one day sell.  I did contact the Better Business Bureau and they had no complaints on file for this company.  That was 15 years ago.  You need to do the same.

Real Estate & Home Improvement / Re: Is 90th St area a good place to buy?
« on: January 19, 2012, 03:49:52 PM »
I was curious and took a look at craigslist and noticed two for sale.  One for $119K & the other for $129K.  The one for $119K maint is $440, the other $526.  This is very tempting for someone on a modest budget.  I would go for space rather than a studio in a prime area.  Someone mentioned Roosevelt Terrace.  I did notice a one bedroom with a 20 ft terrace for $169K with a similar maint which is a lot more for someone on a budget. 

I wouldn't be too concerned about when it comes time to sell.  Selling is a problem all over JH's.  When JH's was at its peak in 2006 apartments sold quickly regardless of where they were. 

Don't stretch your budget.  I would go with Southridge.  I know a senior couple who raised two children in Southridge.  They're financially well off and could move to prime JH's but they won't.  They really like Southridge.
