Neighborhood Chat / Beware of Home Depot Parking Lot
« on: October 06, 2010, 01:32:33 PM »
Just an FYI to everyone... Monday night I walked out of Home Depot on Northern Blvd around 6 PM and the back window of my car had been smashed out.  I only had a small soft sided cooler in my backseat that someone must have thought looked extremely valuable, but unfortunately for them it was empty.  I then walked back inside to alert the store manager and she told me that mine was the 3rd car broken into that day.  I was parked 3 spots from the front door, and I could visually see the "security" officer standing 50 ft away at the entrance to the store.  To add to the frustration, evidently the city owns the parking lot, so any cameras in the lot can only be viewed by a police officer.  When the police came, it was pouring rain, and the officer seemed really annoyed about having to deal with filling out the paperwork.  She didn't want to go look at the video and told me to call today for the report number.  So, save yourself the trouble and DO NOT LEAVE ANYTHING VISIBLE IN YOUR CAR IN THE PARKING LOT.  It will prevent a lot of headaches, because neither Home Depot or the police are going to help you out.  I hope this helps everyone here out.
