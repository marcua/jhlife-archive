Title: Theatre 167 presents JACKSON HEIGHTS, 3AM--in JH January 13-22!
Post by: Theatre 167 on January 04, 2012, 11:52:48 AM
Dear Friends and Neighbors,

We're thrilled to present the third installment of our Jackson Heights Trilogy, JACKSON HEIGHTS, 3AM! Our run in Jackson Heights is a bit shorter this time around, as we're at PS 69 for two weeks and then moving to Queens Theatre in Flushing Meadows Park for two weeks.  We're very excited about the transfer, but also want to make sure that all of our Jackson Heights friends get to see the play while we're here!  We've had such amazing experiences researching the piece in Jackson Heights for the past 8 months!

So....here is all the info! Tickets are available via our website, www.theatre167.org!

Happy New Year!
Ari Laura Kreith
Artistic Director--Theatre 167

A new play inspired by late nights in the most diverse neighborhood in the world!  From the creators of 167 TONGUES and YOU ARE NOW THE OWNER OF THIS SUITCASE...

Theatre 167 in association with Queens Theatre is thrilled to announce the world premiere of
JACKSON HEIGHTS 3AM!
Conceived and Directed by Ari Laura Kreith
Written by Jenny Lyn Bader, J. Stephen Brantley, Ed Cardona, Les Hunter, Tom Miller, Melisa Tien and Joy Tomasko 

At PS 69 in Jackson Heights Jan 13-Jan 22
At Queens Theatre in Flushing Meadows Park Jan 27-Feb 5, 2012

A Bangladeshi cab driver working the midnight shift yearns for an Ecuadorian woman who rises at dawn to bake bread, but does not speak her language. A closeted policeman from Long Island comes to Jackson Heights for a date.  At Club Atlantis, a drag pageant contestant has a run-in with one of her fans, while farther down Roosevelt Avenue a Russian immigrant discovers that redemption comes in the strangest ways.  A rookie cop tries his best to do right by everyone, a beloved dog mysteriously disappears, and in Elmhurst Hospital a heartbroken ER doctor helps her patients fulfill their deepest dreams.  Car dispatchers, sex workers, drag queens, E.R. doctors, gamblers, and insomniacs collide in the colorful world of Jackson Heights after hours.
