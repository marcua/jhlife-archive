Title: Re: Upstart literary magazine seeks graphic/web design intern
Post by: StevenGrey on May 21, 2012, 11:15:08 AM
Tim, you don't state whether or not this literary magazine is a not-for-profit effort or not, but as a graphic designer of 20 years and someone who supports the Working Families Party and is active in promoting workers' rights and a living wage in this city and nation, I find it offensive that people still think it's fair and reasonable to offer unpaid internships to college students and recent graduates, or to solicit young designers to do pro bono work solely to "build" their portfolios. This practice needs to be stopped and students/graduates need to be educated about being fairly compensated for their creative and technical skills.

Here is information posted by the AIGA regarding what constitutes a valid internship experience. http://www.aiga.org/search.aspx?searchtext=unpaid%20internships
