Real Estate & Home Improvement / Support City Council Co-Op Bill 188
« on: March 10, 2011, 07:18:27 PM »
Hello everyone,


Support City Council Co-Op Bill 188

 
This bill will require Co-Op Boards in Manhattan, Queens, Brooklyn, Bronx and Staten Island to say why they rejected a buyer within 45 days. By the way, this is the law already in Suffolk County.

To let your council person know, that you support this bill and that you want it to become law, go to Real Estate Board of New York (REBNY) web page and click on the link Support City Council Co-Op Bill 188 and follow the instructions and they will send a letter to your council person on your behalf.

Real Estate Board of New York (REBNY) www.rebnyactioncenter.com
