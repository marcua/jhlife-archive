I've been ordering with very good results at Cheong Hei Restaurant located at 8148 Baxter St which is around the corner from the
Jackson House Cinema on 82nd St.  It's slightly outside of JH's but they deliver.  I'm always in the area so I haven't used the delivery yet.  What I like about this place is that some of my favorite dishes are orange chicken, orange beef, Tso's Chicken & Sesame Chicken which can be ordered small ($5.75) or large ($9.25).  I always order small which is quite a generous portion and the price includes tax.  The place is always crowded with customers ordering take out.  I've always waited between 20 - 25 minutes which is fine considering the price and quality.  I've used just about every chinese takeout in JH's and I like this one the best.  The only dishes I've ordered are the one's mentioned above.  Strange but I've been there five times and I never once heard the phone ring but they do deliver.

I didn't order any Malay dishes last night but ordered traditional Chinese Teocheow/Cantonese dishes - the kind I eat at home and the kind you can get ordering with a Cantonese person at Chinatown or Flushing.  It was definitely the most authentic Chinese delivery I've had in the area - I'm not talking about dishes like mooshoo pork or general tso's chicken.  Even better the delivery came within 20 minutes - I went out to get cash at an ATM and they rang my doorbell as soon I stepped back in my apartment.

I ordered: pork and thousand year egg congee, chinese broccoli with salt fish, hainan chicken, and fried wontons.  It was all very good, especially the Chinese broccoli dish. Not mindblowing, and not Flushing or Chinatown quality, but definitely great for the area. I can't speak to the quality of their Malay dishes but I suspect that perhaps their owners are more Chinese than Malay, which is why their Malay as others have mentioned is just okay. So, even if you are unfamiliar with the food, I definitely recommend their service - very prompt and courteous.
