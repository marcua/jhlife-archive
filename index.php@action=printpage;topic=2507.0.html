Title: Re: Any iPhone folks ...
Post by: Griswold Girl on July 29, 2009, 11:23:32 AM
I like the Urbanspoon App for finding places to eat depending on cuisine, price and location.  You can use GPS to pinpoint where you are so for example I am in Long Island City and am in the mood for sushi but don't know where a sushi place is I can find one in 30 seconds and click on reviews to see if it is well regarded.

Showtimes Apps for movies.  Also, uses GPS and tells me what is playing and the times at cinemas closest to me.

New York Times App a must for reading on the bus. 

Google Reader is great for their New York thread which includes Curbed, Gawker, Gothemist etc...

Translator useful when looking at menus and you don't know what certain ingredients are.

Wikipedia app - perfect for when you need to bet your friend $$$ and look something up quickly

Trivia Pursuit - great for long train rides by self.  Does not require internet.

Voicenotes.  I don't use it much but good to have in the event that you need to record something.
