Title: Steinway piano factory tours in Astoria
Post by: Shelby2 on January 02, 2008, 06:00:56 PM
I think this might be the closest available factory tour to us in JH -
it's the famous Steinway piano factory. 

This is from an email from them:
"We schedule tours of the factory every Monday and Tuesday - the tour officially starts at 9:30 AM and ends at around Noon. Due to our very busy schedule tours do have to be booked well in advance."

However, some of the dates I was offered were less than 2 weeks from the date of the email, which doesn't seem too bad.

All you have to do to book a tour is email info@steinway.com and they will send you information on what to do.

http://steinway.com/factory/steinway_history.shtml
