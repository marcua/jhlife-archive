News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Real Estate & Home Improvement / Re: Dunnolly Gardens
« on: September 21, 2017, 12:35:14 PM »
The garden at Dunnolly Gardens is not a playground. While the co-op is certainly child-friendly, there have been many complaints about the use of the garden for noisy play that unfairly disturbs other residents. This has become a major problem over the last several years due to real estate agents and others promoting the garden as a playground. Please do not add to this false impression. The board is currently reviewing garden rules so that all residents can enjoy the use of the gardens and their apartments.
