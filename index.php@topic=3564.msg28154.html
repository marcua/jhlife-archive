There is another new pizza place coming soon - this one is on 82nd St. on the Elmhurst side, about a half a block from the 82nd St. train station on the left, before you get to the Jackson Triplex.

They are doing construction on the space, but the sign is up -- and it's a real sign, not one of the cheap awnings.
It says "Mario's Pizza" and when I googled, I found this http://www.mariospizzaonline.com/ but I'm not sure it's the same place.  All the existing ones within this chain on the website are on Long Island.

I happened to be in the area at 11:30.  I counted 6 employees for this small place (2 in the kitchen).  Seating is 4 small tables each seating 2.  I had the margarita slice ($2) which was excellent.  The pizza is on par with Louie's.  Regular slice is $1.25.  Many hot pasta choices for $3.50.  Hot hero's with a beverage is $6.  This place is perfect for someone who can eat out either at work or home.  I'm not certain about delivery but you can call 718 205 6860.   Address is 40-25 82nd St.
