Find out more here about one of many interesting things going on this weekend:

http://www.regoparkgreenalliance.org/#!qai-2015/cuxl

JH's Suran Song has a unique exhibit, a mobile meditation and jazz tribute installation. Here's a message from her in part:

Why not come visit me Saturday after dusk in Rego Park, and take supine repose in my yogic truck installation?
Mobile Meditation on The Queens Jazz Trail
 will be installed from dusk-10pm at
92-14 63rd Drive, Rego Park, Queens

Why not come and rest in the calm, cushy  truck while gazing up into a mandalic sky of music-video portraits of Queens jazz legends Louis Armstrong, Russell Jaquet, and Lennie Tristano? Their song portraits are: "It's A Wonderful Life", "Suede Jaquet", and "Blue Boy" respectively!

There will be free flower mandala kits and free mobile meditation viewfinder souvenirs for you!
