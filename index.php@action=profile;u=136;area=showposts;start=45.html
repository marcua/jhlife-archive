Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

I wanted to pass along this information about a brand new CSA
(community supported agriculture) in Long Island City that still has
available shares. Since Farm Spot is sold out for the season, this
might be a viable option for Jackson Heights residents.

L.I.C. CSA
- organic vegetables (no fruit share) from Miller's Crossing Farm in
Hudson, NY
- sliding scale CSA (also offers scholarships, monthly payment plans
and accepts EBT)
- full vegetable share from $400-$515 depending on income, for 22 weeks
- half vegetable share from $200-$275 depending on income, for 22 weeks

- pick-up is mondays, 4-7pm, June 2 - October 27 at Jacob Riis
Neighborhood Settlement, 10-25 41st Avenue

contact:

Danielle Seidita,Long Island City CSA Coordinator
dseidita@hungeractionnys.org
212-741-8192 ext.5
