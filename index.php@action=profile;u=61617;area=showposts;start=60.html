It's pretty rare for the health dept to shut down a restaurant these days so Starbucks's infractions must have been pretty bad. These days if you fail a health inspection you get a "grade pending" designation which allows you to make things right and get re-inspected. That was one of the concessions that the de Blasio admin made to the restaurant industry which had complained that the city was killing their business with restaurant closures and inspections.
