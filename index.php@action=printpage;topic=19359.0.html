For the last few weeks I have been forwarding Roosevelt Avenue-related threads from this site to our elected officials (Dromm, Moya, Cruz and Ramos) and the NCOs at the 115th precinct.  Yesterday, in an email, the NCOs reiterated that any and all complaints be made to 311 when you see something.  Prostitution-related complaints will likely be transferred to 911.  The sole elected official I have heard back from was Councilmember Dromm.
