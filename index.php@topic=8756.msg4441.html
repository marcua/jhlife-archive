I know there are a few other Korean places nearby, including Unidentified Flying Chicken.
Anyone have any recco's or places to avoid?

I have eaten at UFC on 71-22 Roosevelt many times.  The chicken is absolutely incredible.  I usually order the half spicy/half soy sauce recipe.  This is the best Korean Fried Chicken in the city and possibly the world.  Friends who visit from South Korea say UFC makes better chicken than they can get back home!  How very convenient that it's right in our own backyard.

So what are you waiting for?

More about Korean Fried Chicken found here:
  • http://www.nytimes.com/2007/02/07/dining/07fried.html?pagewanted=1
  • http://gothamist.com/2007/07/02/korean_fried_ch.php

Be warned these places are no compromise about freshness so you will wait about 30 minutes for your food.  Once you're familiar with the menu try phoning ahead and taking a walk.  It'll be ready when you sit down.
