Jackson Heights Halloween Parade
« on: September 30, 2008, 12:32:01 PM »
The 19th Annual Jackson Heights Halloween Parade will be held on the afternoon of October 31st.  This wonderful event is the second-largest Halloween Parade in NYC, and (I've heard) the city's largest children's parade.  All are welcome to participate.

The Jackson Heights Beautification Group Halloween Parade Committee needs volunteers to help stuff goodie bags for the kids, and to help distribute them for the parade.  To find out how you can help, contact them at 718-565-5344 or info@jhbg.

The parade starts at 5 pm at 89th Street and 37th Avenue.  It proceeds down 37th Avenue to about 76th Street, where goodie bags are distributed.

Here's a report on last year's parade from Sudden World.  And here are some photos from past years.
