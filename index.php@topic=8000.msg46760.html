The letter grades aren't 100 percent, but there is a correlation. Larger restaurants, like Jackson Diner, will have trouble with the letter grades b/c the grading is based on quantity of violations. For instance, mice droppings. The inspectors will count the amount of droppings, so a large restaurant will always have trouble with inspections compared to small places.

Everytime I read a post with this title, I groan (so do you who had the poisoning, I bet). It's a little alarmist to post that, though if many people have had poisoning, it should be investigated.
