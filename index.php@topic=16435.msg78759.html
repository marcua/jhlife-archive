Verni's Garden Center. It's not far. It's Astoria Blvd, and Hazen St, just next to the Jackson Diner.
This is a pretty big shop with outdoor greenhouse, and it's been around for a long, long time.

I went last week, after a trip to Home Depot, and was bummed I though of them second. If you walked from central JH, it would take about 20 minutes. If you drive, it's tricky, but the best way is to take Ditmars to Hazen, which correlates with about 75th st. http://www.vernisgardencenter.com/aboutus.html

He convinced me to buy a smaller amount of landscape fabric than I intended. He was right, too.
