Title: Re: "Black Thai" on Northern & 81st
Post by: dssjh on June 17, 2014, 09:55:29 PM
so we just had our first delivery experience from Black Thai, mixed, leaning towards the positive. we ordered:

Duck Wrap: duck, pickled veggies that could double for banh mi fixins and hoisin sauce in a flaky roti. i'd have liked a little more pickle in the veg, but the portion of duck was generous, and well cooked.

Black Thai Salad: a sort of chicken larb with a few additions. way, way too bland. ground chicken needs a whole lot more than just a little lime juice to make it palatable.

Pad Prig Khing: we got a chicken version. this had all the kick the salad was missing. slow burn spicy, really well cooked string beans and a nice sauce.

Drunken Noodles: pork version. not as saucy as some i've had. good pork to noodle ratio, just enough veggies to make you feel moderately virtuous. oddly, this dish is usually very spicy, but only hit a few hot notes.

overall, i'd try a few more things. they have a few fish dishes on the menu that look good, but at 20 bucks a pop (not bad for a whole fish, really), we wanted to test the waters on the lower end of the price scale first.
Title: Re: "Black Thai" on Northern & 81st

I just finished dinner from there. Bottom line: it was ok.

Fast delivery, polite delivery person, and he was able to find my door, something that some other deliverers can't seem to figure out.

Food is relatively expensive, and just not that great.
Chicken Lettuce Wrap was very good, best thing we had. Hoisin sauce was great.
Fried tofu was watery.
Pad See Ew was ok.
Thai Rama was fair. "Mixed vegetables" apparently means cabbage and onion.
Pineapple fried rice was a bit bland.
Jasmine rice was dry.

Unfortunately, there are exactly zero other Thai delivery options for me, so I will probably order from them again.
