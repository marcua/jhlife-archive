Roommates and Apartments Wanted
« on: August 10, 2010, 09:19:28 PM »
Stop wasting your time with CraigsList. Roommates Wanted NYC is a public event where you can meet potential roommates in a safe, fun, spam-free environment.

The idea is like a singles meetup but for roommates. We will have identifiers to help narrow down your search as well as discounted drinks to help reveal the true sides of potential roommates. There will be events on the 2nd and 3rd Sundays of every month starting now.

Jackson Heights people will be served by the Astoria even in Break Bar and Billiards. This place is cool b/c there is free pool and ping pong. So come down:

A: If you are looking to move in somewhere
B: If you have a space to rent in your apartment
C: If you want to find people to live with

http://roommateswantednyc.com/

Locations and times:
2nd Sundays (starting August 8th)
Astoria: Break, 32-04b Broadway, 5-7pm (All of Queens)
Williamburg: Alligator Lounge, 600 Metropolitan Ave., 8-10pm (Greenpoint, Bushwick)
3rd Sundays (starting August 15th)
Prospect Heights: Soda Bar, 629 Vanderbilt Ave, 5-7pm (Clinton Hill, Ft Greene, Bedford Stuyvesant, Crown Heights, Park Slope)
LES: Niagara, 112 Avenue A, 8-10pm (All of Lower Manhattan)
