Bicyclists Ambushed by Ticketing Police on 34th Ave
« on: July 22, 2016, 09:43:15 AM »
 Police are using the 34th Ave bike lanes to ensnare riders. Twice in 3 days I have witnessed unreasonable tickets supposedly
for running red lights- other eyewitnesses agree- and it feels predatory. First time, the officers drove like a bat out of hell with
sirens on and we think they are going to an emergency. Then a young hispanic biker goes to put his foot down to stop and they
cut him off and say he ran a light the street before. Not true! We saw it. So a $50. ticket for him. Then another officer today
in an unmarked car stops another biker saying the same thing at the same place! Did the word go out that you can trap bikers
in this area?

 This is exactly the kind of thing we don't need in a summer of violence. Please stop ticketing bike riders for non-offenses! I
am all for enforcement of traffic laws but when we have a free-for-all on 37th ave of 3 pt turns and double parking and endless
honking on 73rd and 37th, this seems like really MISPLACED POLICING.

 To the 115th- STOP TICKETING THE BIKERS FOR RED LIGHTS WHEN YOU ARE IGNORING VEHICULAR VIOLATIONS THAT ARE
 A COMMUNITY THREAT. WE DON'T NEED THIS!

drop off areas on 34th Ave have outrageous violations and we can't scare up a policeman in those hours. It's a miracle there has

on the same street! Why?

The injustice here is appalling. When we all get tickets for traffic violations, it's how the system should work but when one group
is singled out and harassed, I believe we need to correct course.
