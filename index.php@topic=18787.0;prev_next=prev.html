This place sounds like exactly what I am looking for here in JH.  It's called Ripe Juice Bar and has much more than just juice.

Here's a bit about it from an article in NewsBlaze

"RIPE's modest space still boasts a large kitchen. "When you believe in something good, well, it's a great feeling to go to work," owner Peter Kambitsis motions, "I pick out fish at the market, myself. When I cut a strip of fresh tuna, it looks just like Sushi. Everything is fresh and healthy, large portions. Much of my crew are personal fitness trainers."

From egg white omelets with the works (no exaggeration), to whole wheat personal pizza with nonfat mozzarella, if you cannot decide amid an everything fresh variety of salads, chicken, fish or steak grills, Panini, wraps, bowls, soups, juices, smoothies, workout drinks and guilt free deserts, then, you're getting the picture."

And the folks at Queens Central can't say enough good things about it.
