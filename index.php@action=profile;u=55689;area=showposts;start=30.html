I guess it's mainly demand and supply. However, the original ingredients are pretty different from Chinese cuisine unless one orders Thai-Chinese food like Pad See-Ew (soy sauce stir-fry). Most American Chinese carryout restaurant the  food supply cost them around 30%. While Chinese open Japanese joint is around 20%, my guess is Thai one is around that.
