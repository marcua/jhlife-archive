Quote from: JIF on July 02, 2013, 10:57:45 AM
Di_11372....by E and F trains from Roosevelt Ave Station....it's 12 to 15 mins to Manhattan from Jackson Heights.

On good days,  the train ride itself on E or F is 12 to 15 minutes from 74th st to Lexington Ave. On bad days, I've experienced that E train is slower than R/M train between Queens Plaza and 74th St.  Considering most people's Manhattan destination will not be Lexington Ave, just see how many people got out of E train at lexington ave to transfer to 6 in the morning rush hours. It will take around 10 minutes for most Jackson Heights people to walk to 74th st station.

The door to door commute for most Jackson Heights people to Midtown Manhattan is about 30-45 minutes. For commuting to uptown or downtown or for people living around 82d st subway station,  the commute time will be 10 or 20 minutes longer.
