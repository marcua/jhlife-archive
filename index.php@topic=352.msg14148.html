What's new in Elmhurst?
« on: January 18, 2008, 10:42:25 AM »
As many Jackson Heights residents know, Elmhurst is a much-overlooked gem of a neighborhood.  Sort of like a sibling to JH, every much as diverse, but minus the architectural charm of our historical district and with more of an East Asian flavor.  And, unfortunately, saddled in the public's mind with the memory of the Elmhurst Gas Tanks, even though they weren't located there.

This thread is for brief news items about what's going on with our neighbors to the south.

  • Kate noted yesterday that a new Esparks Coffee opened at Broadway & Baxter

  • Today, the Daily News has a long real estate article on "Will Elmhurst Get Noticed?"

Anybody heard any other Elmhurst news?
