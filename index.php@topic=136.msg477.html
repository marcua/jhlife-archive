I have a problem with the long lines, so I usually go to the tiny little satellite place called "Kellys" on 37th Ave just before 85th St.  It's considered 'authorized' to do certain postal transactions.  The counter has limited hours but every time I've gone in, it's been open.  If I have a small package, they can weigh it and put postage on.  I can also buy stamps there.  I've never waited more than 5 minutes.  I also send fedex and UPS packages there, but I suspect there is some sort of surcharge just like you would pay at Mailboxes Etc. 

That said, the main branch really doesn't seem that bad to me compared to other branches around the city.  When I lived up near Columbia, there would sometimes be a 45 minute wait to get to the head of the line.

I saw on another blog that Sunnyside residents are not too happy with their branch either! http://www.queensrocks.com/search?q=post+office
