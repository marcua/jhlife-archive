News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Neighborhood Chat / Flushing Meadows loses acreage on paper
« on: June 01, 2013, 02:58:29 AM »
Turns out the size of FMCP has been over-reported for a long time. A new land survey has determined that the park 897 acres, noticeable smaller than the previous measurement of 1255 acres. This makes it New Yorks fourth largest park, not third. The cause of the large discrepancy was that previous surveys did not subtract the land used for the expressways which run through the park.

http://www.nytimes.com/2013/06/01/nyregion/surveying-effort-alters-sizes-of-some-new-york-parks.html?_r=0

I had often wondered if that was the case. The article does not address if the new measurement includes Citifield and the USTA Center.
