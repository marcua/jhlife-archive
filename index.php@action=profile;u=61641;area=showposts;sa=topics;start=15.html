Neighborhood Chat / Build the community and help other who have less. Lions Share pantry
« on: June 08, 2017, 07:14:51 AM »
Probably refreshing an old topic here:

Lion's Share food pantry happens every second Saturday at St Marks Church, 82nd and 34th. They are always looking for donations and new people to volunteer. You can just show up at 9am and it runs til noon or so. Usually people focus on canned goods, rice, and pasta etc. But, other items like personal care (soap, toothpaste, toilet paper) are also needed and often over looked. Drop offs can be done around 9am and they start handing out at 10am. Cynthia and Bill who run it are incredibly welcoming and warm people.
