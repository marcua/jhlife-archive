Title: Freelancers & Solopreneurs meetup - the first Tuesday morning of each month!
Post by: jonbstrong on July 01, 2019, 03:44:56 PM
Any local freelancers, homeworkers or entrepreneurs out there?

If so, we'd love for you to join us for our Soloist Collective meetings every first Tuesday of the month from 9am-10.30am!

Made up of independent local professionals in Jackson Heights (including small business owners, freelancers and entrepreneurs), these meetings allow us to connect, learn more about each other, and discover how to help each other succeed.

For more info, check out our main website and sign up to the newsletter for updates here: jhsoloistcollective.com.com (http://jhsoloistcollective.com.com)

Looking forward to seeing you at our next meeting! Feel free to contact me directly if you have any questions :)
