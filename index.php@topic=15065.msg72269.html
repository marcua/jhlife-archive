It's not an e-waste recycling center. The assembly person who has an office in the shopping strip runs periodic e-waste drives there -- he had one this past weekend. An electric fan is not e-waste, that goes out for reg garbage pickup.

The NYC.gov site for the Dept of Sanitation has a feature that lets you type in "how to get rid of"...and you put in your item
and it tells you the procedure. The Bureau of Recycling and Sustainability, which is a separate unit of Sanitation, is one of the
most innovative and well-run government agencies I have dealt with in NYC. Try this link and if you don't find an answer,
contact them directly.

    http://www1.nyc.gov/site/dsny/index.page

Also -we got the electronic recycling pick up in our co-op recently. They will give you a metal locker and exchange it when
full with your buildings recyclables. So far, it's working.
