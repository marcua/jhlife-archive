There are at least four different yoga classes here in JH:

1. Yogi Baby offers classes at Mambo Bravo for parents and babies, as well as separate classes just for adults.

2. Margaret offers Pilates class at the Garden School.  Contact her at margaretomurray@gmail.com.

3. Rolling Ohms offers yoga at the Garden School.  My wife takes this class and loves it.

4. Doreen offers intermediate-level yoga classes on Thursday evenings at the Garden School starting this week.  Nine week session Sept 11th to Nov 6th, 7-830pm, for $125.  Call 718 426-1493 or email queensyogini@yahoo.com.
