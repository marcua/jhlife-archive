purple dough
« on: April 24, 2019, 03:41:55 PM »
Thought I'ld let everyone know that there is a really cute little pastry shop on 68th street about half a block north of Roosevelt called Purple Dough. The young couple who own it, he's Filipino she's American, take a lot of pride in their product. They make American style things like cookies and donuts, but with more tropical flavorings, so, for example, they had a mango donut with coconut on top.
The cookies I got were delicious, not too sweet, and the flavors were well balanced.
Something different to try, and the owners are super friendly.
