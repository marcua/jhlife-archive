Title: ANTIQUE CHINESE WINDOW AND DOORS EXHIBITION (Opening Reception)
Post by: FCCA on January 20, 2012, 12:59:39 PM
Fri, Jan 27, 5:30pm - 7:30pm

From the collection of Thomas Chen, Crystal Windows and Door Systems

$5 Suggested Donation/Members FREE

Antique Chinese wood windows and doors were considered both functional architectural elements and works of art. These intricately hand-carved pieces, collected from numerous towns and provinces throughout China, depict detailed scenery and characters. Gallery Hours: Sat and Sun, 12-5pm (Wed, Jan 25-Sun Feb 26)
