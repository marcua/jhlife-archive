Title: Re: JH Good for single Gay male?
Post by: StevenGrey on May 11, 2011, 01:44:20 PM
Not sure how to answer your query. A neighborhood is what you make of it, and unless you live someplace that is completely devoid of places to socialize (like Roosevelt Island, where I once lived  :P), you can find possibilities anywhere. I guess the answer to your question lies in what you are looking for and expect to find? I presume you're not just looking for quick hook-ups (you can go to Chelsea for that or use Grindr ;D) but for places to hang out and meet other guys like yourself? Jackson Heights has great places to eat and an abundance of gay bars (and no, you don't have to speak Spanish fluently to enjoy them, though it can help ;)), and its only shortcoming is the lack of parks and greenspaces. Hey, did I mention we have a Starbucks now?

The annual Queens Pride march and festival is Sunday, June 5th: http://www.queenspride.com/QueensPride2011/prideinfo.php
The JHBG historic tour is Sunday, June 19 (rain or shine): http://www.jhbg.org/events/events.html

Maybe attending one or both events will help you decide if it's worth the move from Forest Hills?
