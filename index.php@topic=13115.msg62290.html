Chinese take out in the area
« on: April 09, 2015, 03:54:20 PM »
As a long time resident of the Heights I occasionally order Chinese take-out.  When I first arrived about 30 years ago I found the best food was from Woks on 37th Ave.  Well, as years passed I have seen the management come and go as the quality came and went as well.  My last meal from Woks was a disappointment.  The meal was mostly flavorless and bland.  The soup was watery and lacked substance, the meal was just uninspiring.  Since Empire on 83rd street closed, which made a decent hot and sour soup, I find my options limited.  Saw Shack on 37th is kind of okay, but not a favorite.  I am not looking for authentic Chinese cuisine as can be found in areas such as Flushing or on Broadway in Elmhurst, just a decent takeout in the area.  Any recommendations?

JJ Garden, which is in the little strip mall near Canelle/Waldbaum's, is my go-to, and has been for ages. they do good seafood dishes (the shrimp are big, the sauces clean) and seem to actually make their own dumplings. i really like the chicken ones. they have a few interesting dim sum items on the delivery menu and the veggie dishes are a solid B-plus. the hot and sour is good, not amazing, but not watery.
