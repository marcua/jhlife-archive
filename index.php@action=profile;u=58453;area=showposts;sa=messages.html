News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Neighborhood Chat / Re: Frederick Wiseman movie: In Jackson Heights

Hi everyone, Emily from Zipporah Films here. We are so excited to be back in Jackson Heights in a couple of weeks.

The free tickets for the screening on October 23 were gone very quickly but we hope some of you were able to reserve a spot.

Otherwise IN JACKSON HEIGHTS will be at Film Forum November 4-17 (with some appearances by Wiseman and also Daniel Dromm!) http://filmforum.org/film/in-jackson-heights-film.

Museum of the Moving Image, in Astoria, is currently doing a series of all Fred's NYC films: http://www.movingimage.us/films/2015/10/09/detail/frederick-wisemans-new-york/

And someone else posted this link, but we are updating this page with theatrical and festival screenings of IN JACKSON HEIGHTS at http://zipporah.com/films/44 - please also check there for the new official trailer!

We hope to see some of you soon! Thank you for your support and excitement about the film and about Jackson Heights!

Neighborhood Chat / Free community screening of IN JACKSON HEIGHTS documentary
« on: October 05, 2015, 05:15:55 PM »
Please join documentary filmmaker Frederick Wiseman at a free community screening of IN JACKSON HEIGHTS! Tickets are limited and must be reserved in advance. Reserve your tickets here: http://injheights.bpt.me or call 1-800-838-3006.

The screening is supported by the Ford Foundation’s JustFilms in association with Council Member Daniel Dromm and the Queens World Film Festival.

Refreshments will be served at 5:30pm, and the film screens at 6:30. Afterward, Wiseman and CM Dromm will host a conversation/Q&A.

PS 69 Auditorium
77-02 37th Avenue
Jackson Heights, NY 11372

Please join us!
