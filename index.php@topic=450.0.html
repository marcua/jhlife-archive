With the advent of warm spring weather and our own recent trip to pick up compost in the Bronx, we at e77 have started to think about the beautiful gardens in Jackson Heights.

Coffee grounds can be very helpful in gardens, used as a slow-release fertilizer for most plants when mixed in with soil and as a soil-modifier for acid-loving plants such as azaleas, rhododendrons, dogwoods and so on.  They are also great for composting!

With this in mind, local gardeners are invited to bring one small bin each (8-gallon max, with a cover please!) to the shop and we will fill them up with our leftover espresso grounds for you to use in your gardens.  Less waste for us and more fun for your flowers!

Happy planting!
