Title: Edible Queens Book Launch Party at Espresso 77
Post by: julesnyc on June 22, 2010, 02:36:36 PM
This Wednesday (tomorrow!), 5:30-8:30pm at Espresso 77:

Join Espresso 77, Queensbound and Edible Queens for the local Launch of Edible Communities' first book and a celebration of all things Queens!

Leah McLaughlin, editor of Edible Queens, will read from the book and will be on hand to discuss all of her interesting discoveries while exploring the foodie scene in our fair borough. Ciara Elend, creator of the Queensbound line of t-shirts already featured at e77, will join us with new designs from her line of hand-blocked prints (hint - they are totally fab!). Last but not least, we'll have lots of tasty nibbles from our friends at Edible and from Queens favorite Beverage Artisan, Afzal Hossain of e77!
