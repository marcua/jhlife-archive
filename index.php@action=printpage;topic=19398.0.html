Title: Re: I need a Bangladeshi (or South Asian) dessert
Post by: toddg on October 01, 2019, 05:19:09 PM
Maharaja at 73-10 37th Ave. has a nice selection of Bengali sweets that should be stable enough to travel well: http://maharajasweetsny.us/bengali-sweets/ 
(I'm not sure how similar these would be to Bangladeshi sweets).   

And Rajbhog, Al Naimat, and a number of other places are nearby if you want more choices!

These sweets are pretty dense, so I find it works best to cut each piece into quarters before serving.
