I was confused too. I thought I could vote in this race, but when a neighbor in a nearby building just posted on FB that she didn't see the Moya/Monserratte race on her ballot, I realized I should check the district map. There are very few blocks in JH that are in District 21. Approximately 82nd St. up through 94th is on there but ONLY between Roosevelt and 37th Ave. So, for example, Linden Court residents could vote in this race.

After 94th St. the district expands north. http://vote.nyc.ny.us/downloads/pdf/maps/2017/co/co21.pdf
