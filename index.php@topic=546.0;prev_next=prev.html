Dear neighbors,

Just wanted to start a new post here so I can consistently postings our rotate menus from now on. At Addictive 87 - Fine Dining, our menus are rotated monthly. Since this month is our opening month, we started with a French menu. You can also find update menus on our website www.addictive87.com/menu as well.

This week will be our last week for French menu. If you have not tried our roasted duck & goat cheese salad, may I suggested you give us a try? It's the talk of the town and so of the fillet mignon in au jus. Come July 4th, our new menu is Italian. I hope to see you guys soon!

Thank you. 
Patrick

Here is the new Italian menu.

Appetizers

Caprese
Burrata, tomato jam, basil oil, crushed parmesan croutons, honey balsamic reduction

Sarde Fritte
Fried sardines, chunky citric tomato sauce

Zuppa di Patata
Leek and potato soup


Main

Vermicelli Trifolati
Angel hair, olive oil, garlic, sundried tomatoes, cremino mushrooms, marcona almonds, fresh parsley & white wine

Rabbit Cacciatore
Rabbit stew with carrots, onions, potatoes, tomatoes and herbs served with bread

Grande Palla di Carne
Thin spaghetti, meatball wrapped in caul fat and tomato sauce



Desert

Zabaione
Reserve vermouth, dry apples, dark chocolate chunks, marcona almonds, ladyfinger crumbs

Strawberry Panna Cota
Feta cheese, honey balsamic reduction, pistachio macaroon
 
Frutta e Merengue
Fruits in their natural syrup with mint & lemon zest

Italian Menu

Appetizers

Caprese   14
Burrata mousse, heirloom tomato, basil, mint, honey balsamic reducton and crouton flakes

Sarde Fritte    13
Fried sardines topped with tomato sauce and orange zest

Pork & Fontina Tortellini   16
Beech mushrooms, pickled onions, caramelized apples, rhubarb, vegetable powder and light beef broth served tableside

 

Entrees

Gurguglione   25
Roast of vegetables and heirloom tomatoes. Layered with a mozarella goat cheese sauce, served with green bell pepper coulis

Cornish Hen Cacciatore   31
Half cornish and roasted vegetable stew with carrot and potato gnocchi

Grande Palla di Carne    28
Caul fat wrapped meatball, pancetta, mushrooms and thin spaghetti with tomato sauce

Mackerel Crudo Salad   27
Frisse, arugula, fennel, granny smith apple, pomegranate, crisp carrot crumbs, white grapes, marcona almonds & grape fruit vinaigrette

 

Desserts

Strawberry Panna Cota   14
Feta cheese crumbs, grape must, pistachio dust and Disaronno coral cake

Frutta e Merengue   13
Clementine oranges, raspberries and cucumbers in their natural syrup with mint and lemon zest

Zabaione    14
Light custard foam of reserve vermouth topped with dry apples, dark chocolate chunks and ladyfinger crumbs
