Title: Upcoming DACA Information and Legal Assistance Sessions at Queens Library
Post by: itsjustme on September 20, 2017, 12:08:43 PM
As part of the Queens Library is for Everyone campaign, the New Americans Program has scheduled two DACA Information and Legal Assistance Sessions this week to provide assistance to Dreamers who need to complete the legal paperwork.

1. Long Island City Adult Learning Center, Thursday, September 21, 6:00 pm - 7:45 pm (NYS Office for New Americans)
http://www.queenslibrary.org/event/community-information-forum-daca-know-your-rights-workshop-in-english-and-spanish

2.Central Library, Main Floor Conference Room, Friday, September 22, 3:00 pm- 5:00 pm (Queens Legal Services)
http://www.queenslibrary.org/event/information-session-on-daca-and-immigration-options-know-your-rights-workshop

There will be lawyers present at each session to provide guidance and answer individual questions.
