Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Neighborhood Chat / Re: 84-01 37 Ave to be Heard by Landmarks Commission
« on: February 04, 2011, 07:46:56 PM »
84-01 37th Avenue will be up for review at the LPC this coming Tuesday, February 8th,  at 11am. 
 
At this hearing the developers will briefly review the plans they presented at the last meeting. The commissioners will have the opportunity to comment on the proposal. The commission will not vote on the proposal at this hearing, but will ask the developers to come back and present their plans to address the  commissioners comments. At that point the commission will vote to accept the plans as modified or send them back to the drawing board. There will be no opportunity for public commentary at these hearings.

It would be great if some people from the community would attend.
