I just thought I would share this information with all TimeWarner Cable customers: I posted in this thread a few weeks back but then all the problems seemed to clear up for a few weeks... until three weekends ago when my service (phone and internet) went down for three days. I thought my cable modem to blame and TWC sent a serviceman to come replace it. He checked the line and informed me that the signal coming into our building was at a frequency that was higher than the standard operating range of the cable modem... a problem that TWC technicians had to fix from the street level. Sure enough, since then my service has been fine again.
