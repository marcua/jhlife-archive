Eat Drink Local Week w/ Anthony Road Winery at Jackson Heights Greenmarket
« on: September 23, 2010, 12:09:55 PM »
Jackson Heights Greenmarket is celebrating Edible Queens Eat Drink Local this week with Anthony Road Winery. They will be making a guest appearance at the market this week to show off some delectable offerings from their Yates County cellars. Come by, taste some wine, and pick out the perfect pairing for your Cherry Grove cheese. Cherry Grove's Farmer Ian got a warm Queens welcome this past Sunday; thanks to all those who came out to sample their delicious cheese. He will be back this week with creamy brie and sharp Stinging Nettle Jack among other delicious handmade cheeses. If you missed him last week, come by this Sunday from 8-3. Hope to see you all there!

Jackson Heights Greenmarket is located on 34th Ave, between 77th and 78th street and 78th Street between 34th Ave and Northern Blvd at Travers Park every Sunday through December 13. For more information click here: http://www.grownyc.org/node/278

For a list of Queens restaurants and businesses participating in Eat Drink Local Week, click here: http://tiny.cc/ytflwtml5g
