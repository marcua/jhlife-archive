Community Groups, Activities & Events / Transportation Alternatives Queens Committee Greenmarket Bike Ride
« on: July 17, 2012, 07:30:49 PM »
22 Jul '12: 9am meet at Jackson Heights market (34th Ave and 78th St) -- look for a group with bikes!

For this ride we will be partnering with Grow NYC and will be touring 3 Queens Greenmarkets:

Jackson Heights: Coffee and a light breakfast provided
Forest Hills: We will tour this new market and learn about their New Farmer Development Project (NFDP)
Douglaston: Cooking demo followed by lunch

(For those of you who did this ride last year, these are three different markets.)

The pace will be leisurely and the ride from Jackson Heights to Forest Hills to Douglaston is 14 miles. From that point, you have several options to get home. You can either get on the LIRR at Douglaston to make your way back. Or we will be riding together back to the Jackson Heights market (13 miles). On the ride back, you can also pick up the 7 train in Flushing for a slightly shorter ride.

TA's Queens Volunteer Committee is open to everyone who has an interest in biking, walking, and public transit in Queens.

http://www.facebook.com/events/470777109601925/

Community Groups, Activities & Events / Transportation Alternatives Queens Committee leads walk
« on: April 30, 2012, 06:57:48 PM »
Jane Jacobs walks mission to help people walk, observe, and connect with their city outside of the automoble. TA Queens Committee is leading a walk to highlight recent pedestrian and bike safety/complete streets wins, and point out spots where improvements are needed. And along the way take in the great places that make our neighborhoods special!

We will visit Queens Blvd, highlighting TA's vision of a complete street; the site of the closed LIRR Elmhurst station, planned and unplanned order and chaos in Jackson Heights, highlighting the garden apartments built by the Queensborough Corporation. We'll visit Travers Park where we'll be met by Georgia Southworth of the Jackson Heights Green Alliance who will describe the effort to close 78 Street and incorporate it into the park; finally we'll walk thru LittleIndai on 74 Street, visit the new pedestrian plaza on 74th street and the improved street crossings at 74 and Roosevelt.

The walk will be approximately 2 miles! (1 1/2 hours)

Meet us: 11:30am @ in front of Georgia Diner, 55th Ave & Queens Blvd, one block east of Grand Ave (R) station.

http://www.facebook.com/events/319389778130385/

for more information on Jane's Walks: http://www.janeswalk.net/
