...in front of the Post Office on 37th avenue between 78th and 79th We will not be silent when two transwomen are brutally attacked on the streets of Queens.

and beaten with belt buckles- suffering wounds in her scalp and legs.

towards our transgender sisters and brothers.

We extend solidarity, care & compassion for victims of transphobic hate crimes.
