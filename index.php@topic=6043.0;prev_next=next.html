The fine Italian restaurant, Sapiro D'Ischia, in nearby Woodside is having a special today on Groupon*. I believe it's the standard
half-price off so if you buy the coupon for $20. you get $40. off your bill. It's at 55-15 37th Avenue in a kind of desolate-looking

a plus in my book.
