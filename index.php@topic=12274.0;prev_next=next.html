On one of the cushions, there is a bleach stain, so the cushion case will have to be changed for perfection.  Otherwise it is a great sofa with an easy to pull out queen bed.  One of the comfiest sofa beds I've slept on.  The bed itself was used exactly 7 times.

Frigidare 8000 BTU Portable Air Conditioner.  Bought for $329, selling for $50.

A good cooling unit, will cool a single room (our room 13'x15') well.  The connection from the hose to the window is flimsy, though that flimsy connection makes it very easy to attach and remove to your window, so you can have both the a/c and your window when you want it.  We only used the a/c at night, so whenever we used it, I'd disconnect it in the morning, takes 30 seconds at most.  Frankly, I wouldn't ever buy it (again) for $329, but I'd buy it for $50.  We bought it 3 years ago, used it about 20 times (we are not huge a/c folks).   Oh, and its also on wheels!

Sofa plus a/c unit = $225 sale!

French Door Refrigerator with water dispenser and ice maker, $200

The co-op we bought came with a huge french door stainless steel in black cabinet refrigerator.  I have no idea of the actual size, but its dimensions are 69" high, 36" wide, 30" deep.  I reckon between 23 and 26 cubic feet.  The person b4 us actually left the protective tape on the fridge, so when you look at it, it appears scuffed, but pull off the tape and its perfect.  Its a lovely fridge, but its just too overpowering for our small kitchen.
