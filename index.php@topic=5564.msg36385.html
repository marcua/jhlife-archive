Inner Peace under new management!
« on: November 21, 2010, 03:53:32 PM »
Hi, I moved out of Jackson Heights about a year ago but I always go back for the dining and the shopping. I was walking down 37 avenue and I noticed an "under new management" sign on the door of Inner Peace. I entered and I was hoping to hear that magic phrase "he's not working here any longer". To my great delight, that is exactly what the young lady said. This was my favorite gift shop until I had a bad experience with the guy who used to work there. I loved the convenience of buying a gift and having it ready to present. This store was one of my favorites in all of Queens. Just walking in seems to put your heart and soul at ease. I don't know if its the aroma of all the candles and oils or the mantra style music. Maybe its all the divine statues of Buddha's and angels. All I know is that I am happy to be able to shop there again.

I was raised in Jackson Heights. Although I no longer reside there, I still support the neighborhood businesses. For years I have enjoyed good relationships with many of the shopkeepers and their employees. I appreciate the personalized service you receive when you patronize a small business. In the age of e-commerce and big super stores, good customer service is often traded for convenience and cheap prices. My motive for writing here was simply to inform any one that had an issue with the previous manager to please give the store another visit. Allow the new staff the opportunity to meet your gift shopping needs. Inner Peace is one of the finer establishments in the area and it's uniqueness makes it an asset to the community. Again I just wanted to spread the good news, because we all know bad news travels fast, but I would hope that good news tralvels a little quicker. Thank you all for allowing me the opportunity to express my thoughts. Everyone have a blessed and safe holiday.
