Title: Re: House Cleaning Service
Post by: ljr on May 20, 2021, 05:49:46 PM
They did arrive late--minor flaw--but they brought all their own equipment and supplies, and they are cleaning this apartment within an inch of its life. They started at 10:30 AM, took a brief lunch break, and are now in the last room at almost 6PM. They likely have an hour to go. Very thorough--we have a biweekly cleaner come in, but the job she does is nothing like this "deep clean." I'm thinking I should have this done several times a year. They dusted EVERYTHING, cleaned inside of baskets then put the contents back, vacuumed curtains, cleaned blinds, cleaned ceiling light fixtures, cleaned windows, dusted shelves and all the books and LPs--really thorough. You choose what you want done, though--I think I clicked on everything they listed online. You can likely do less and pay less. I have two people cleaning. I think they are excellent and plan to tip very well on top of the not cheap (but reasonable IMO--it's a lot of work) price. I expected to pay a substantial price for this kind of cleaning. Now I have to have the furniture steam cleaned as well...(getting rid of cat dander...)
