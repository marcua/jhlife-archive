Sompong Thai on 37th Avenue does a good lunch deal for 7.95
Includes a main + spring roll + soft drink, soup or salad



Another great pitstop for a cheap bite - "Los Chuzos Y Algo Mas" - a tiny Colombian place on Roosevelt that serves delicious Guava Juice ($3.50) and Chorizos on skewers with Arepas ($3.50) - which you can order from a side window.
