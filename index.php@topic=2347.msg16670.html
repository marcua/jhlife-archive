Come and Work out with a trainer in Traver's Park!!!!
« on: February 28, 2009, 04:57:00 PM »
Hello everybody!!!

A workout group has started a week ago in Traver's park.  Leading us is a very experienced and incredible trainer (his name is Kiki) who worked with me before.  You will see and experience results very fast (from my own experience). We do some cardiovascular and we exercise almost every part of our bodies.  It's not too hard, but it's also not too easy. It is perfectly fit to your level!.  The goal is to help everyone (whatever level, age) to get fit, lose weight and feel GREAT! Working out in the fresh air really makes you feel so energized and satisfied.

This is our second week and we already have a set schedule for next week.  We will stick to this schedule until more people will join.  Then we will open up more classes.  All of you are welcome to come by, try it out (first trial class is for free), and join!


Weekly schedule:

Monday Evenings at 7:15PM-8:15PM

Wednesday mornings at: 10:00AM-11:00AM

Saturday mornings at  12:00PM-1:00PM

Sunday mornings at: 10:00AM-11:00AM

The price for each class is $15.  You can purchase class cards.  A 12 class card comes with 2 bonus classes.

Please remember not to eat two hours prior to class.  Drinking a smoothie or a protein drink two hours prior to working out is a great idea!

Please bring along: A jumping rope, water and yoga mats. Do wear good shoes so you don't harm your feet. If you do not have all this equipment, come with whatever you have.  we change locations in the park according to whatever activity is taking place.  we will be recognized easily, though.


Looking forward to seeing you tomorrow at 10:00AM and next week on Mon evening and Wed morning.

If you are interested, please email me and let me know when you think you can attend, or just show up.  I am just helping Kiki organize this and keep track of the students, schedule etc...


Cheers,

Deborah
