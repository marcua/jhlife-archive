JHLife 101:

After you have successfully signed onto the forum, click on PROFILE.


On the left side of the page, under Modify Profile, click on Look and Layout Preferences.


Use the pull down menu to set the USE QUICK REPLY ON TOPIC DISPLAY to Show, On By Default.


From now on, there will be a little Quick Reply box at the bottom of the postings on each page.


If you want/need more options, simply click on the preview button after you have typed in your posting. A new window will open giving you the option to make some words bold, or italic, or bigger; or attach a picture or simply just add a smiley face! Or, if you are happy with what you wrote, do a Spell Check so you don't get embarrassed, and then click on POST.

VOILA! You are now a regular contributor to JHLife!

When you post an image attachment on this site, the file size has to be under 128K.  So maybe you are wondering why some people's photo attachments come out looking very tiny and others seem to fill the screen when you click on them.  After all, everyone is dealing with the same file size limits.

If you keep the image quality at its highest (this option comes up when you "save as" in photoshop) then you are going to end up with a very small image when you post it here, even if your file size is around 128K.

If you reduce the image quality as much as possible (try "4" in photoshop), you can use photos that have higher number of pixels, and therefore your photo is going to appear much larger.

I hope I explained this in a way that's understandable.
