Neighborhood Chat / Re: NOISE...every Sat morning into Sun...NOT Happy!

hey dawnie... I live nearby on 79th street and have experienced the noise from drunk, rude, Novo patrons at 4 am many sundays, I feel your pain!!! I've heard screaming, arguing, hooting, cheering....you name it, all at 4-4-30 am. I called 311 about 5 months ago and registered a complaint. I've resorted to having a white noise (fan) on every night now. I keep it on high and have adjusted to the sound it makes and I have to say I hear very little of what's happening outside. I realized its probably impossible to get people who've been drinking all night to be quiet, although I can't imagine being that loud and thoughtless at 4 am (or anytime) anywhere. Since Novo has probably received many complaints about this and the problem persists, the only other action to take would be a more organized protest. I had drinks there one night with a friend and left at 2am and there appeared to be a bouncer standing outside. That person should be there to disperse loud patrons at closing time, in my opinion.
