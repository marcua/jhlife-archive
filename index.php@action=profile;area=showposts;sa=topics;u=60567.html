News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

« on: January 23, 2018, 11:38:09 AM »
This Sunday, Jackson Heights Creates is holding a planning meeting to discuss events for 2018, and we are inviting interested community members to join us.  We had a good year in 2017 with 3 fun public events, and we are looking forward to making 2018 even more art-ful in Jackson Heights!

Sunday, February 28
3-4pm
82nd Street Academics, Room 1
8110 35th Avenue, Jackson Heights, NY 11372

If you have any questions or can't make it, but would like to participate in our planning/volunteer group, please email us at JacksonHeightsCreates@gmail.com.

About Jackson Heights Creates:
Jackson Heights Creates supports and promotes visual, video, literary and performing artists and connects them with each other and with the community.  We hold networking events for artists, as well as performances and art workshops open to the public.

Community Groups, Activities & Events / Jackson Heights Movie Night - Back on Board: Greg Louganis
« on: April 24, 2017, 05:59:40 PM »
Join Jackson Heights Creates on Thursday, May 4th at the PS 69 Auditorium for our first neighborhood movie night!
We will hold a SCREENING + Q&A with Producer and Jackson Heights resident, Will Sweeney.

Back on Board: Greg Louganis
Thursday, May 4, 2017 at 7pm
Location: PS 69 Auditorium
(enter at main door on 37th Avenue between 77th and 78th Streets)
Admission: $5 per person.

All proceeds from ticket sales will go to supporting future community art activities organized by JH Creates.

BACK ON BOARD explores the captivating, compelling, and complicated life of an Olympic star whose athletic talent spurred a worldwide fascination with diving. Part biography, part social exploration – this film reveals Louganis’ evolution from childhood diving prodigy to Olympic champion, and from pioneering openly gay athlete with HIV to almost forgotten sports icon. BACK ON BOARD is an engrossing story of an American legend.

Community Groups, Activities & Events / JH Creates Community Art-making Event - February 5
« on: January 28, 2017, 07:17:20 AM »
Jackson Heights Creates invites you:

Constructing Communities with Art and Heart
A hands-on workshop for exploring citizenship and network-building through drawing and sculpture

Sunday, February 5th, 2017
2-4pm, The Renaissance Charter School
35-59 81st Street, Jackson Heights
FREE!
To visit our RSVP page and reserve your spot:
https://www.eventbrite.com/e/constructing-communities-with-art-and-heart-tickets-31563393961

What does it mean to be a citizen, a neighbor, a community member?
Let's get our imaginations going and have some fun with art, while getting to know our neighbors and fellow artists.  We'll be sculpting and building a new model city, complete with citizens to inhabit it.  The more fantastical, utopian, weird and wonderful the better!

We are excited to see what the Jackson Heights community of artists and art supporters will create. We hope to welcome not only studio artists, but also writers, musicians, dancers, filmmakers and creative professionals in every discipline.

This is not a child-centered event, but we would be thrilled to welcome parent-accompanied children 7 and older who are ready to engage in a meaningful way.

Neighborhood Artist Sampler and Networking Meetup

Community Groups, Activities & Events / Jackson Heights Creates October Update
« on: October 26, 2016, 05:58:04 PM »
Hello from the Jackson Heights Creates organizing team!

We hope everyone's Autumn is off to a great start. Since we hosted our community visioning session over the summer, we've been active on social media, but we realize some folks may not be connected to those outlets or would prefer message boards for their news.  We have been busy working on follow-up items to the issues that we discussed at our summer meeting, and we are finalizing plans for another community meeting to take place before the end of the year. Watch this thread for further details about that event!

In the meantime, please be sure to check us out on Facebook, Twitter and/or Instagram. "Like" us to receive regular postings about artist opportunities, arts events in Queens and news about local artists, as well as updates on our meetings and projects to support the arts in Jackson Heights.

Jackson Height Creates on Facebook:
www.facebook.com/JacksonHeightsCreates

Jackson Heights Creates on Twitter:
www.twitter.com/JHtsCreates

Jackson Heights Creates on Instagram:
www.instagram.com/JacksonHeightsCreates

Thanks again for all of your feedback and great ideas - please feel free to get in touch with us with any questions or ideas at JacksonHeightsCreates@gmail.com.

We'll see you later this fall!

Community Groups, Activities & Events / New JH Arts org Visioning session / Meet and Greet Potluck - Saturday, July 23rd
« on: July 18, 2016, 11:58:35 AM »
Saturday, July 23rd, 2016
5:30pm to 7:30pm
Library and Garden, Community United Methodist Church
35th Avenue at 81st Street, Jackson Heights

Local artists and Jackson Heights residents who are interested in fostering arts in our neighborhood are invited to a Potluck gathering to discuss and share ideas for a new arts organization for the Jackson Heights community.  We invite artists who work in all creative mediums to join us for a visioning / brainstorming session to identify the needs of the local creative community as well as opportunities for neighborhood arts enrichment activities that we might explore.  We need everyone's help and ideas to move this project forward!  Please spread the invitation to all of your artist friends in the communities that make up our wonderful neighborhood.

Please bring your energy, ideas and a dish to share for this potluck event!  If you have any questions about what to bring, please contact us at JacksonHeightscreates@gmail.com

If you are planning to attend this free event, please register at our Eventbrite site so that we know how many folks to expect:   https://www.eventbrite.com/e/jackson-heights-creates-visioning-session-and-potluck-meet-and-greet-tickets-26529021016?utm-medium=discovery&utm-campaign=social&utm-content=attendeeshare&aff=esfb&utm-source=fb&utm-term=listing
