A small group is working on a documentary video and trying to 1 - get landmark designation, 2 - raise awareness and ultimately 3 - repurpose the structures for something new.  I attended the first presentation at Queens Theatre in the Park.  They have made progress in a short amount of time.

You can also get more info on the effort via Facebook and search for the page People for the Pavilion.  This link should take you there
https://www.facebook.com/pages/People-For-the-Pavilion/631159810259726
