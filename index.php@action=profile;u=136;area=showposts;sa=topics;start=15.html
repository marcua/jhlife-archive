Neighborhood Chat / Landmarks Commission Designates Oct. 26 "Queens Day"
« on: October 26, 2010, 07:26:18 AM »
Below is a description from the Landmarks Commission website:
Landmarks Commission Designates Oct. 26 Queens Day
As part of its continuing efforts to protect historically and architecturally significant sites in Queens, the Landmarks Preservation Commission has designated Tuesday, Oct. 26, 2010 as Queens Day, when it will consider proposals to protect more than 1,100 buildings in the borough. If approved, the measures would increase the number of landmark properties in Queens by 40 percent, from approximately 2,700 to 3,800.

Included in today's vote will be a row of houses on 75th St. in Jackson Heights.

for more info:
http://www.nyc.gov/html/lpc/html/home/home.shtml
