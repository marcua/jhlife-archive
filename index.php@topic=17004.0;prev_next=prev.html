News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

I should clarify that the horrendous customer service I encountered was at the pharmacy. Multiple times. I am just waiting for my prescriptions to expire so I can start completely over at a different pharmacy. I actually did get a new Rx and sent it to the Duane Reade at the corner of 82nd and Roosevelt and they were a dream to deal with compared to that Rite Aid.

One example - the Rite Aid made a mistake and only gave me one of the two prescriptions I had ordered. I didn't notice til I got home. I called to ask them for an extension of two days on the 2nd prescription to give me time to pick it up (it had been almost 2 weeks since I ordered it so they would have been about ready to send it back.) They agreed to the extension. Then I got a call the very next day telling me to pick it up right then or it would be sent back that very afternoon. I explained that #1, it was their initial mistake, and #2 that I had been given an extension. They actually accused me of lying! They started in on 10 questions about the description of the cashier on the original transaction (when they could have just looked it up in their computer) and when I said that it was a male cashier, they expressed doubt that I was telling the truth. At that point I started yelling. I have to admit it wasn't my finest moment. When I called back to speak to the manager, I was hung up on several times on purpose.

I have also been in there other times when the person I believe may be the lead pharmacist was speaking in a nasty tone and using swear words in full earshot of customers.

I checked the Yelp reviews after my negative experiences and found that I wasn't alone. They get 1.5 stars out of 5 with 16 total reviews. https://www.yelp.com/biz/rite-aid-jackson-heights-6?osq=rite+aid

Perhaps the rest of the store is ok-ish, but the pharmacy should be avoided at all costs.
