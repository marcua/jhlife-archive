Here it is again - The Jackson Heights Food Group comes to August. Where will we go this time? The choices for the month are:
Legendary Irish Pub featuring "NYC's best hamburger". Donovan's
5724 Roosevelt Ave
http://nymag.com/listings/restaurant/donovans-pub/

New Thai restaurant in Woodside, highly recommended by most. Ayada
7708 Woodside Ave
http://www.villagevoice.com/2009-01-28/restaurants/ayada-s-elmhurst-thai-dazzles/

and the South side of the street's perhaps former Roti Boti. The group will NEVER go to Jackson Diner. Here is one of the better Indian restaurants of the neighborhood.

Here we are, searching for the next great meal. Send me your vote, think of the feel. Think of the embibing, coercing your throat. Beware of biryani, chicken and goat. Consider the weather, consider the walk, consider adventure, consider the talk. Email me your choices and come late to mid August, we shall exchange all our voices.

Looking forward to it!

I think that is unkind to label the Jackson Diner the McDonald's of Indian food.  Bobby, the owner, started his restaurant in a small diner  in JH back in the 1980's when there was no Indian community here at all and the sentiments towards him were almost all negative.
