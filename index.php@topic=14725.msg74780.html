I went to this place on 73rd/Roosevelt, and although I did not wait long to be seen, I was charged $200 to be treated for what was essentially a cold, which I thought was over the top. I have insurance with a high deductible, so before I have met the deductible, I have to pay the negotiated rate that my insurance feels is reasonable. In this case, apparently they felt that $200 out of the $201 the urgent care charged me was reasonable and the $200 is what they passed on to me.

It's possible that if I went to any urgent care, I would have been charged this rate. But in any case, for anyone with a high deductible plan, think twice before going to this place or any urgent care. I believe I would have been charged much less for an office visit with my regular GP.

I am guessing that we are seeing urgent care facilities pop up all over the city because they are able to charge the insurance companies (or in my case, charge me) much more than regular medical offices can get away with charging. Perhaps this is because it's sort of a middle ground between an emergency room at a hospital and a doctor's office.  Buyer beware!
