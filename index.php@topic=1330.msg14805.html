As for other listings in the building, I don't know. I can ask the seller of the apartment we're buying. We'd originally started looking up in Washington Heights (we work on 54th and 9th in Manhattan) but didn't like what our money bought us up there. A friend who grew up in Linden Court (and still lives there) suggested we look out in JH, and we fell in love.

We've actually been looking hard all summer, working with our broker - he spotted this listing in the MLS services, and even though he couldn't attend, we went to the first open house, and made our broker go back the next business day. As soon as we walked in, the place just felt 'right' (and huge - and nicely renovated). We're putting 20% down - I don't know if this building will accept a lower amount - we didn't ask.

We'd made offers on other units in the neighborhood  - just about every building that allows bigger dogs - we saw. And we've often lost out to all-cash offers or dropped out over the asking price. (And one place we were informed that we didn't make enough - they had a maximum of 25% mortgage+maintenance to income ratio - and for us that building would have been 27%)  We made our first offer in early May, and are just so happy to have gotten this far. 

We'd love to do a September get-together - I'll keep an eye out for the date!

Thanks for the welcome messages, all!

Best,
