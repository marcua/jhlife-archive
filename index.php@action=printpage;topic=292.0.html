Concert of Love with Melania Maldonado

Saturday, January 19, 3:00 p.m.
Queens Library, Corona Branch
38-23 104 Street, 718-426-2844

Saturday, January 26, 3:00 p.m.
Queens Library, Woodside Branch
54-22 Skillman Avenue, 718-429-4700

Admission is free.  For more information, see http://www.queenslibrary.org/UserFiles/event_pdf/1296-11-07_1296web.pdf

Be part of our Concert of Love with the beautiful, alluring and sweet voice of mezzo-soprano Melania Maldonado.
Ms. Maldonado, who was born in Ecuador, specializes in lyric and popular music. She has performed in concert and recital halls in South America, the Caribbean and in the United States. In New York City, she has performed among other places at Town Hall, Symphony Space, Queens Theatre in the Park, Flushing Town Hall, and the Ecuadorian Embassy.
