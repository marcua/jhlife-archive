Hello Folks-

I'm new to Jackson Heights and love the historic architecture surrounding us. As we all know,  Jackson Heights is the best example  of the garden apartment movement in the U.S., so I wanted to create a blog where potential members like you would contribute photos of buildings and architectural elements from your camera phone as you walk around.  The purpose of the blog is to create an architectural and visual presence of Jackson Heights on the web. Let me know if you are interested in contributing photos  and if you have any ideas to make the blog more interesting... More info on the blog to come ( I am still in the process of launching it).

Thanks! :D

Post by: taggie on February 24, 2014, 02:24:01 PM
 Are you familiar with Daniel Karatzas? He has literally written the book on Jackson Heights and it's architecture,
gardens and planning. I would consult or join heads with him as he has a fabulous collection of historic photographs
and seems to always be wanting to profile Jackson Heights architecture in the same way you describe. If I am not
mistaken, he is singlehandedly responsible for the Historic District designation and the vigilance needed to make
sure this is taken seriously in the neighborhood. His spring tours through the historic district are a must so look
for them through the JHBG group.
  Plus he has a great wry sense of humor and champions Jackson Heights like no one else. Works in real estate here somewhere also and would be easy to collaborate with I think. Check out this link which is a little older _
   http://www.jhbg.org/history/history.html
