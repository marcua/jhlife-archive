Post by: dssjh on November 05, 2009, 09:07:48 AM
ranger -- and pearson's before it -- have been serving up great texas bbq there for about nine or ten years now. the original proprietor passed it on to his pitmaster, who's still there, a few years back.

stick to the texas items -- ribs, brisket, sausage -- and you'll be thrilled. for me, the other offerings are only so so (and "pulled chicken" is blasphemous  :)

the bar has an excellent beer selection as well, if that's your thing. it's very comfy, and unless you're a sports-phobe, a nice place to hang out.
