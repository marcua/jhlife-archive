Title: 969 NYC Coffee - New menu items
Post by: BEB on March 04, 2020, 11:11:54 AM
I was just visiting 969 NYC Coffee and I noticed some new menu offerings. In addition to his usual chicken and pork versions, Mr. Oda is now making his delicious Katsu (fried) sandwiches with fish, shrimp and oyster. They look so delicious. As always these are made with love fresh each morning.

They also have fresh sushi which sells briskly every time I am there. Their adorable and tasty onigiris are popular with young kids and adults alike. I also enjoy their ramen, which is basic and comforting. Literally every time I am there, someone is walking out with a matcha in hand.

This place is a real gem in the neighborhood. If you haven't check them out, please do.

Please note though that they are now closed on Thursdays. Otherwise it seems they are open 7am-9pm.

https://www.969nyccoffee.com/
