Community Groups, Activities & Events / Learn how to access funding to "green" your home and reduce your utility bills!

Chhaya CDC, a non-profit housing organization, is hosting a free information session on government and utility company programs that homeowners and renters can take advantage of to reduce their energy use and utility bills.

September 20, 2011, Tuesday
6:30-8:00pm
Chhaya CDC
37-43 77th St., 2nd Fl.
Jackson Heights, NY 11372

For more info or to RSVP: 718-478-3838 x15

Most homes qualify for a free energy audit, when a professional identifies where and how energy is being wasted in the home, and ALL households qualify for some level of subsidy for the home improvement work that reduces that waste--measures such as insulation, replacing boilers and old appliances, and sealing cracks around windows and doors.
