News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Hello everyone!

It seems progress is being made despite rumors of delays in the park's opening! If you haven't been down to the site (69th between 35th & 34th Ave), then I strongly suggest you check it out! Fences and signs are up, gates are pretty much installed - the only thing missing as of this past weekend are the benches, mulch and waste receptacles. Therefore, we will be having a meeting on Monday, March 22, 2010, at 7PM @ the Community Church, to discuss next steps.

At this meeting, I hope to have a better ETO for the run. We will also be finalizing the membership application and scheduling a clean up for the area, which is filled with litter. If you cannot make it and wish to contribute something to the meeting or if you have any questions, please feel free to e-mail (petronyc@gmail.com) or call me (347.826.1878).

Also, if you haven't already, you may want to consider joining our Ning Group: http://jhdogs.ning.com/

Hope to see you there!

Happy tails,

John Petrozino
