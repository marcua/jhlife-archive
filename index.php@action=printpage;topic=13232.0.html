Title: Formerly Feral Cat Needs a Good Home
Post by: annie on May 12, 2015, 08:12:41 PM
This is Grace, the last of the 2 dozen or so cats that lived in our co-op's backyard.  We have been able to place virtually all of them, but Grace, who has been well fed by the residents of the block, has remained on the street until a few days ago.  A good Samaritan and lover of cats took her in and has been caring for her.   She cannot keep her because she is returning to her home in Arizona, but describes her as "sweet", lets herself be petted and uses the litter box. She is well fed and quite beautiful--looks rather like a Russian Gray.  She has abandonment issues, is shy, but not afraid of either people (who have been feeding her for the past year and a half) or dogs.  She has been spayed, is healthy, but if we can't find a home for her in the next week or two, we will have to return her to the street, where, it must be said, she has fared quite well thus far.  All in all, we'd rather not.  Takers anyone?
