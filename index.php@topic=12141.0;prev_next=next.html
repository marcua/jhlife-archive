HAMPSHIRE HOUSE APT FOR RENT ~ 79TH/37TH
« on: June 18, 2014, 11:03:58 PM »
TASTEFUL APT FOR RENT - $1950/mo incl heat/water. 1 month deposit, + One-time Coop Management fees, and refundable $300 cash or certified check commitment deposit to be applied to your 1 month deposit if rented.  Un-refundable if commitment terminated by you outside of Management/Board rejection.  Aside from all of that formality, it's a really laid back Coop run by professionals while not being a stick in the mud.

- 875sqft, large 1 bedroom,4 closets,
- Lease can start as early as August and looking for 1 to 2 yrs minimum,
- 3 windows in bedroom with cross-ventilation (unique),
- 2 windows in kitchen (to watch snow storms while cooking...),
- 2 windows in living room (to let the breeze in),
- Ceiling fan in bedroom and kitchen,
- Dimmer switches,
- Plantation blinds,
- Gorgeous floors (dark Jacobean oak stain),
- Built-in old world bookcase
- Unfurnished but could discuss,
- W/D on site,
- Fully renovated, high-end (beautiful if I say so myself) bathroom,
- Upgraded kitchen (new appliances),
- great laid back neighborhood with all basic day-to-day stuff you to run your life w/in a 3 block walk,
- E,F,R,M,7 lines a 6 short blocks (not long aves). Great to have an El when the subway is screwed up...always alternatives here,

- 20-25min commute on express lines to Union Square,
- 10 minutes to Flushing Park, the US Open, Mets Stadium and LGA, Astoria, Century 21 in Rego Park, Forest Hills, and LIRR,
- New large non-chain health food store
- Lemon Farm (awesome choices of cheeses, sopprasetta, Bread Alone, and things i've never heard of but come to love)
- Table Wine (beautifully curated and down to earth staff / owners)
- Starbucks

I've lived in this neighborhood for 10 years and love love love it's ease, diversity, and sense of community.
