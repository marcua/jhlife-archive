New York City Transit President Andy Byford announced Thursday he is stepping down from his post.

The decision comes months after he had submitted a letter of resignation and then retracted it following reports of tension with Governor Andrew Cuomo, who oversees the Metropolitan Transportation Authority.

Restaurants & Food / Oceanic Boil
« on: June 26, 2019, 10:06:27 PM »
Just a quick shoutout for a place that doesn't seem to get enough attention here. we were looking for dinner out after voting last night and thought this would be a good choice.

it was.

really crowded, but not uncomfortably so, for a Tuesday night. cool vibe. pop music soundtrack at reasonable volume, low lighting, and yes, television sets (for those who are frightened off by such things). simple, fresh seafood. excellent crab legs and shrimp. a nice "boil" sauce. bibs and gloves, crackers and digging forks like in Louisiana. copious dinner for two, two cocktails each, didn't break the bank. check it out - but expect a bit of spice.

Restaurants & Food / Songkran Celebration today
« on: April 27, 2019, 01:14:20 PM »
On Woodside Avenue from 75th to 78th. it's a Thai New Year celebration, with entertainment, festivity and a lot of food -- about a dozen vendors selling things that, for the most part, are good to eat while you walk. there are tables for sit down eating, and everyone is extremely friendly.  yes, there are typical noodle dishes and curry puffs, but there are some other items that you can eat if you're adventurous (the whole little fish) or if you're not. vendors are happy to explain things if that's needed.
a great event -- and a nice day to enjoy it.
