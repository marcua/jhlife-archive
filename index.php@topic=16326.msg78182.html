Some love for Appettito off 79th!  Been going there off and on for a bit now and never disappointed.  Got myself some time out of purgatory for making my wife a brunch with the German Ham, Danish Bacon (cut it as thick as you like!), Russian cheese and an awesome sweet which looks alot like rugelach I bought from them.  A wonderful part of the neighborhood.

Blintzes. I like the cherry cheese. The salads can be excellent--I love the eggplant/walnut/cranberry one they had in the fall--but there are no ingredients listed or a date when they were made.
