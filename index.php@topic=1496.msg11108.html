News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

THE GREYSTONES TWO BEDROOM OPEN HOUSE - SATURDAY, 9/20, 1-2:30 P.M.
ADDRESS: 35-21 80TH STREET, UNIT #32, JACKSON HEIGHTS

A beautiful two bedroom unit which is approximately 900 sq. ft. and located in the heart of The Historic Landmark District of Jackson Heights. Renovated and in mint condition with spacious bedrooms and two huge walk-in closets, one of the closets can be used as an office; prewar details include original transoms, original crown moldings, and 9 foot ceilings; lovely hardwood floors throughout; one of the largest eat-in-kitchens in Jackson Heights with all new appliances; washer and dryer combo; seven windows; eastern and western exposures; dumbwaiter for your garbage removal; block long interior private garden for your quiet enjoyment; storage bin in the basement for your convenience; laundry in basement; super lives on site; close to all shopping, bus depot, and trains, which include the E, F, G, R, V, N & 7.
