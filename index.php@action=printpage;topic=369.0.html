Title: Valentine's Clothes & Toy Drive, Feb. 9
Post by: toddg on January 23, 2008, 12:26:08 AM
The Baruch College and NYU Chapters of Por Colombia are helping an organization in Colombia called "Antorchas de Vida" that provides basic needs for poor children.  They are sponsoring a Valentine's Day party and clothes and toy drive with the band Por Que No!

Here are the details:
Date & Time: February 9, 2008, 9:00 pm
Location: D'Antigua, 84-16 Northern Blvd., Jackson Heights
Minimum donation: $10
