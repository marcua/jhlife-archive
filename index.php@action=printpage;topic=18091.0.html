Title: Re: Ugly Rumor about Poisoning Cats
Post by: am315 on August 27, 2018, 11:54:52 AM
One thing to keep in mind: Anyone who is feeding the cats by leaving cans of food outside is actually feeding the rats in the neighborhood and causing the rat population to grow. I live on 80th St. and someone leaves open cans of food in the gardens in front of the two buildings at the corner of 37th Ave., the ones adjacent to the dry cleaner and the yogurt shop. That's attracting rats.

I spoke to a rep at the NYC Dept of Health about this issue and she says it's one of the reasons Jackson Heights has a higher rat population than many other neighborhoods.

Please spread the word and discourage folks from doing this so your neighbors and their children don't have to dodge rats every day on the way to work and school.
