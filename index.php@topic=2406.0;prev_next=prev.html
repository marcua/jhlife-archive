Here's a photo of a house that my grandmother's sister owned once upon a time (I think in the 1960s).  It's at 92-35 53rd Ave. in Elmhurst.

One of the things I notice when I cross Roosevelt and walk in Elmhurst is that there is a noticeable change in terms of how the streets seem in terms of upkeep of the properties.  However, I know a few people who live there -- one lives near the Grand St. station not far from the library and the other lives across Broadway from the Esparks-- and both of them are happy there and feel like Elmhurst is convenient and safe.

I have walked from JH to the restaurants and shops near the corner of Whitney and Broadway numerous times and find it to be a pleasant enough/safe enough walk, but I still much prefer JH.
