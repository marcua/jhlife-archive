There is a house at 37-29 86th street (between 35th and 37th) that has been in steady decline for the last few years. It was once a beautiful block of homes opposite Roosevelt Terrace. In the days before landmarking several of the homes were sold to developers and shoddy 3 story rental structures with balconies and sliding doors were put up. A few of the houses remain. This home has what once was a doctors office on the ground floor, the old sign remains in the window. There are always white vans parked in the driveway, bags of garbage, a boarded up garage and no signs of life. Now there is an old fridge sitting in the driveway. Anyone know???
