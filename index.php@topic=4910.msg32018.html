If a building is lacking these things, the maintenance will be lower. If the maintenance for a 2 bedroom is in the mid 900s that is fair.

Rhydewithdis:  I know the building you live in and its fabulous.  A few years ago, I thought about purchasing in the building but didn't for various reasons.  I'm paying $0.80 a SF which doesn't include utilities, gym or PT Doorman.   The reason your maint is low is not self management.  Our Management Fee (I'm in a slightly larger complex) is $70,000 a year which is what you're most likely paying your two office staff.  It has to be a very low underlying mortgage with a low interest rate and low real estate taxes.  Our Mortgage is $475K a year and RE Taxes $690K.  Compare this to your building and you'll have the answer to your low maint.
