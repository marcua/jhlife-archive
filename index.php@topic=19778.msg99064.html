If you have a few extra bucks and feel so inclined, where do you plan to donate?

I made a donation today to NYC Health and Hospitals (Elmhurst is in this system) at https://nychhc.networkforgood.com/

Excerpt:
Your non-restricted donation can help us provide these services and support to our doctors, nurses, and other health care workers on the front line:

-Meals. There’s not time to take lunch and dinner breaks.
-Prepackaged groceries to take home. There’s no time to food shop in between long shifts.
-Hotel rooms. Our staff are taking care of COVID-19 patients and don’t want to go home and risk exposure to their families.
-Laundry service. There’s not time to clean work clothes and scrubs.
-Scrubs. We need more to ensure staff does not have to wear work clothes home from the hospital.

Our goal is to provide as many resources as possible, as quickly as possible, and prioritize the health and wellness of our essential workforce so they can continue to provide their best care for patients without exposing themselves or their families to undue infection risk.

Email received from Assemblywoman Catalina Cruz:

Dear Friends and Neighbors,

Corona, Elmhurst, and Jackson Heights are at the center of this pandemic. Many of us have lost people we love, lost our livelihoods, and struggle each day with this new reality. However, we continue to fight for each other! While our state and federal government has offered some relief, many of our neighbors have been left out of these bail out and stimulus programs because of their immigration status or their inability to be deemed "employees" under the law.

These community members have paid millions of dollars into our tax system but cannot obtain the same help when they need it the most. Many of you have asked how you can help and how you can invest back in our community. Below are four fantastic organizations providing resiliency funds to help their workers & members and we hope you will consider supporting them. I am pledging to donate, will you?

Help us get through this together!

Catalina Cruz
