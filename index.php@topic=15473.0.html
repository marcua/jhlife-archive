I was walking this morning and saw a new little cafe/bakery on 80th, between roosevelt and 37th. I think it's just opening today? The sign is generic- it just says "NYC Coffee." Signage indicates they have coffee and matcha. The owner was out front and he said that they'd be serving a variety of onigiri/rice balls, and it looked like they had some pastry-type offerings in the case. He said that they make all the onigiri on site.
anyway that's all I know but I plan to go back and grab a matcha at some point...
