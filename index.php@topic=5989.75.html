S Prime Steakhouse id LIC (bordering Astoria) - Very pricey for the locale but a really nice looking restaurant with exposed I beams and dark wood.  There is valet parking, coat check and as you enter you face a bar with a couple of plasma TVs. The place is 2 stories, open, screams Steakhouse and had a wonderful ambiance with an extensive wine selection (for an outer boro locale)

We had the Bone marrow and Oysters Rockefeller as starters.  Since I had a t-Bone the day before I did the Tuna as main course which was very thick but somewhat bland for my taste.  The wife ordered the 28 Oz Ribeye which was well marbled (a little too much for my taste).  The side were generous very good - we had the creamed spinach which was very tasty, the Mac & cheese (my favorite) and an enormous baked potato that brough all the condiments on the side.  The condiments were chunks of bacon, sour cream, grated cheese and something else (I cant remember) - we had fun putting it together ourselves  .
