Join Village Fishmonger through FarmSpot!!

Farm Spot has a great new vendor for fish and shellfish!!

Village Fishmonger is offering deliciously fresh, local seafood shares to the members of Farm Spot for the 2014 Summer season!

Our fish and shellfish are sourced directly from local fishermen and shellfish harvesters, which we then process and package for you in our kitchen in Brooklyn.  You'll be able to sign-up to pick-up fresh, local seafood on a weekly and bi-weekly basis during the season on the same days and times that you pick up your vegetable share.

We will be offering a 1 lb or 2 lb size share of mostly finfish, plus add-on shares of clams, oysters, and scallops as well as a la carte items that can be ordered on a week to week basis.  Low-mercury, allergy-sensitive, and Kosher options are available too. You can sign up any time, and you’ll be prorated for deliveries until the end of the season.  Note that only those households that sign up for the season can order a la carte items.

To sign up for a seafood share as a new member:
Go to the Village Fishmonger website (www.villagefishmongernyc.com)
Click on 'Sign Up For Fish Shares'
Choose your pick-up location, in this case, Farm Spot CSA
Choose from these 2 share sizes and frequencies by choosing the appropriate 'Farm Spot CSA' schedule:
Small Share:  ~1 lb per distribution, which will comfortably feed a family of 2 for 1 meal.
Weekly deliveries for the season --> 24 weeks x 16.50 per week = $396.00
Bi-weekly deliveries for the season --> 12 weeks x 16.50 per week = $198.00
Medium Share:  ~2 lb per distribution, which will comfortably feed a family of 2 for 2 meals.
Weekly deliveries for the season --> 24 weeks x 33.00 per week = $792.00
Bi-weekly deliveries for the season --> 12 weeks x 33.00 per week = $396.00
Choose your Add-Ons: You have the ability to add the following to your distribution to be received along with your main share: Clams, Oysters, Scallops
Choose the 'CSA Local members' payment plan
You will need to then create a Farmigo account to continue with the sign-up process.
For payment, please choose the one time payment with a debit or credit card.
In sign-up comments box, note any allergies (shellfish) or dietary restrictions (low mercury for expectant and nursing mothers, kosher for those who would like to eliminate non-kosher fish from their shares), if any.
Go to our pick-up on the delivery date and pick up your seafood!

PLEASE NOTE:
Though Farm Spot CSA's summer season will be 26 weeks long, Village Fishmonger shares will only be be available for 24 distribution dates during the 2014 season.  We will be off the week of July 4th and Labor Day.
Sign-up deadlines for the seafood share is midnight the Monday before delivery.
Only those households signed up for the subscription can order a la carte items.
To ensure that dietary and allergy restrictions are applied to your account, please make sure you enter that information during sign-up or send us an email shortly after sign up with the pertinent information.  If you had restrictions for the previous season, they will still be noted on your account.

If you do not pick up your fish share during the distribution time, it will be forfeited and donated.
If you need to sell your fish shares, you must notify Village Fishmonger. There are absolutely no refunds for canceled shares.
Be sure to read through their FAQ's at http://www.villagefishmongernyc.com/#!faqs/cmkz before signing up.


If you want to sign up for Farm Spot's vegetables, fruit or herbs, click here!! http://www.farmigo.com/join/farmspot/summer2014
