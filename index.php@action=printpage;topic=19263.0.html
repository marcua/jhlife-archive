Title: Open House Sat 8/17 11a-2p - City Island Luxury Condo
Post by: ltorres50 on August 13, 2019, 01:13:24 PM
Looking for a quieter life with a lot more space, water views, and parking; but not ready for Westchester prices and taxes, then look to City Island, NYC's small town! 

Great 3BR/3.5BA condo for sale in a gated community with many luxury touches, including 10' ceilings with crown molding, hardwood floors throughout, gas fireplace, washer/dryer, custom walk-in closets, modern appliances, and spa-like master bath resplendent in marble.  Common charges/maintenance include swimming pool and clubhouse with fitness room.

Open house Saturday, August 17th, 11am-2pm.

Sale Price:  $759,000     Common Charges/Maintenance:   $583/month     Taxes:  $3,952/year   

More info and pictures at:  https://www.onthesoundnyc.com/ (https://www.onthesoundnyc.com/)
