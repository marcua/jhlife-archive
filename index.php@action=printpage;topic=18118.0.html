Title: Coral Aquarium Pet store closing its doors!
Post by: dotley on August 12, 2018, 02:59:42 PM
Sadly, Coral Aquarium Pet Supplies will be closing their doors on August 31st.  They have been in business at that location for over 50 years.  They have a lot of inventory to sell before then.  Many items are on special.  They also have a large selection of frozen and refrigerated pet foods, some of which cannot be found in the chain stores or ordered online.
