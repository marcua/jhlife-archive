Title: Film: Finah Misa Kule - Voice of the King, 12/28
Post by: toddg on December 04, 2007, 11:30:53 AM
Finah Misa Kule: Voice of the King
Presented by Bandenya
December 28, 2007, 8pm
Community United Methodist Church
81-10 35th Avenue, Jackson Heights, NY 11372
Admission/Suggested Donation: $20 adults/$10 children
RSVP 888 286 5557 or info @ badenya.org

In celebration of Kwanzaa, Bandenya is sponsoring a presentation of this epic film from West Africa.  The film will be accompanied by live narration, music, and dance.

See the Jackson Heights NYC Blog (http://jacksonheightsnyc.blogspot.com/2007/12/west-african-epic-to-be-presented-in.html) for details.
