Just went to Spicy Tibet for the 2nd time.  It's on Roosevelt and 75th, south side of the street.  Nice, comfortable interior and really friendly staff.  The food was great--and the thenthuk soup perfect for a cold, rainy day like today.  Not up to the level of Phayul but, really, what is?  But the advantage of this new place is that it's less crowded, easier to find, subway-walk friendly and warmer than Phayul which gets pretty cold when the weather drops down.  Anyway, definitely worth trying and patronizing--so nice to see another new place making the extra effort and I hope they're rewarded.
