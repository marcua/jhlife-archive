This isn't going anywhere. A response I received from Dromm's Office:

Thank you for writing to our office.  We take your concerns seriously.  Please know that at present, there are no plans to establish dedicated hours for dogs/dog owners at Travers Park. 

As you may know, the plans for the Travers Park reconstruction were made years ago in close consultation with the community.  Our office held four visioning sessions which provided local residents and community groups an opportunity to weigh in on the plans.  These visioning sessions were open to the public and were well attended by hundreds of residents.  A dog run was not brought up.   You can read a little more about the visioning sessions in these articles from Fall 2013:

https://www.dnainfo.com/new-york/20131024/jackson-heights/residents-outline-vision-for-jackson-heights-parks/

http://queenstribune.com/community-considers-future-for-green-space/

Again, thank you for contacting us.  We value your feedback and will keep you updated on any developments.
