Title: Re: World Cup bars
Post by: Di_11372 on June 16, 2018, 09:30:57 AM

Went to newly renovated Raices on 37ave/ 36th st to watch a game yesterday. I had never been there prior. I am big on ambiance ,and in regards to all the Colombian places in our 'hood, this is probably the best I've seen. Big space, hi ceiling, very clean, nice seating layouts , large full bar, good lighting. I was there during the day, and that place gets very nice natural lighting, lots of windows. The reviews online are mixed, that being said, I was pleased with the food I ordered, I keep it pretty simple when I'm ordering south amer food.
