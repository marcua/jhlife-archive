Neighborhood Chat / Jackson Heights Orchestra prepares for second concert
« on: March 03, 2013, 09:58:53 AM »
The JH Orchestra is getting ready for their second concert. They had almost 150 in the audience at the first concert and the orchestra has grown since then.

Come join them on March 13th at the United Methodist Community Church. The concert will start at 7:30 PM and feature local baritone John Petrozino. They will perform works by Piccinni, Donizetti, Wagner, local composer James Cohn, Faure, and Gounod. A reception will follow in the church's social hall.

There is a suggested donation of $10, and $5 for seniors and students. The JHO continues under the banner of the JHBG.

Restaurants & Food / Pio pio
« on: March 16, 2010, 08:58:33 PM »
Just had dinner at the new Pio Pio across Northern Blvd.  Very disappointing. The decibel level was so high, that conversation was a strain and the place was only half full.  The food was the same as before the move but I could practically count the number of beans they served with the rice...odd how few there were.

The place is just cavernous with nothing welcoming or intimate like the old one.  Even Pio Pio to Go now has more ambience than the new place.  I hope the owners make some changes to do something about the noise - like installing half high walls to divide the room or something to absorb the sound.  The enormous glass windows and concrete floors make it feel very cold.

Looks like it will be takeout for me from now on...
