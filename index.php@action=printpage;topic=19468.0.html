New Parent / Breastfeeding Support Group
Second Tuesday of the month - next meeting November 12, 2019
10:00 – 11:30 am
$10
2nd Story Pilates & Yoga, 81-10 37th Ave, Jackson Heights, Queens NY
https://www.secondstorymovement.com/ (https://www.secondstorymovement.com/)

Please join us for a support group for new parents! It can be isolating at home with a new baby, so many questions, conflicting advice, and sleep deprived to boot. Meet some neighbors and have your questions answered with compassion and experience to help navigate what works best for your family. We will have snacks!

As a postpartum and birth doula and certified breastfeeding counselor, Rachel Goldstein has helped countless NYC families with the transition to parenthood over the past decade. Please wear your baby as there is a flight of stairs to climb and no stroller parking. We can’t wait to meet you! All are welcome, you don’t have to be breastfeeding.
