Free Sports Programs for kids in Queens- registration opens today!
« on: April 27, 2016, 12:15:44 PM »
 These might be sports your group already plays or the chance to try something new. Check the link and listings below:
 http://www.cityparksfoundation.org/wp-content/themes/cpf.sd.custom.full/images/logo.png

Free kids sports programming this summer in QUEENS neighborhood parks! Spring is here and our summer sports program schedule is now finalized!   Below are live links to the registration applications for various sports.
 
These links below allow you to register for the sports program before general registration on the City Parks Foundation website April 27th. Even if you don’t register today, please register as soon as possible in order to reserve a place.
 Please forward the information to your other park groups members and park partners in the community
 
 
GOLF - http://www.cityparksfoundation.org/cityparks-golf-registration/
 Park of the Americas, QN - Pee Wee (6-8 yrs) - M/W, 9:30am-10:30am
Park of the Americas, QN - Beginner (9-16 yrs) - M/W - 10:30am-12:00pm
Kissena Corridor Park, QN - Pee Wee (6-8 yrs) - T/Th, 9:30am-10:30am
Kissena Corridor Park, QN - Beginner (9-16 yrs) - T/Th, 10:30am-12:00pm
 
You can register for one of two 4-week programs this summer. Complete the form below to register for programs meeting 2x weekly from July 5 - July 28. Registration for the August 1-25 session will open in June. Class sizes are limited and available on a first-come basis. If you have any questions, please contact sports@cityparksfoundation.org or call (718) 760-6999.
 
 
TRACK & FIELD - http://www.cityparksfoundation.org/cityparks-track-field-registration/

Forest Park, QN - Beginner (8-16 yrs) - M/W, 10:30am-12pm
Juniper Valley Park, QN - Pee Wee (5-7 yrs) - T/Th, 9:30am-10:30am
Juniper Valley Park, QN - Beginner (8-16 yrs) - T/Th, 10:30am-12:00pm
 
City Parks Track & Field offers enables kids to learn the fundamentals of the sport at 11 citywide parks. The program is open to boys and girls ages 5-16 and runs each July and August in parks throughout the five boroughs. Activities includes long jump, shot put, hurdles, discus, javelin, and track.
If you have any questions, please contact sports@cityparksfoundation.org or call (718) 760-6999.

GET UP & GO!
1.     http://www.cityparksfoundation.org/get-go-registration-2016-playground-children/
Playground for all Children, QN - T/Th, 9:30am-12pm

Kissena Corridor Park - M/W, 9:30am-12pm
GET UP AND GO! is a free, multi-sport program designed to get kids ages 6-10 active in their local park. The program incorporates fun weekly contests, races and obstacle courses with core skills such as running, jumping, and throwing. If you have any questions, please contact sports@cityparksfoundation.org or call (718) 760-6999
