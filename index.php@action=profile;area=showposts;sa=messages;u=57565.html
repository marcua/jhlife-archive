Business Listings and Services Directory / Pediatric Neuropsychologist
« on: June 01, 2017, 06:50:18 PM »
Hello all, I would like to introduce myself to the JH Life community. My name is Dr. Susan Epstein and I live here in Jackson Heights. I am a pediatric neuropsychologist with many years of experience. I conduct evaluations for children as young as 24 months of age through young adults when cognitive, developmental, or learning questions are present, in order to obtain a diagnosis and provide a detailed profile of a child's strengths and weaknesses to inform educational and treatment planning. I have offices in Manhasset and Manhattan, but would like to offer services to the JH community as well, so I am beginning to offer testing in your homes, as long as a suitable area can be made available.

In addition to evaluating children suspected of having ADHD, learning disabilities, and other neurodevelopmental/learning issues, I am a specialist in the diagnosis of autism spectrum disorders. I spent five years as a senior staff member of the Center for Autism Research at Children's Hospital of Philadelphia and held a clinical faculty appointment in the medical school at University of Pennsylvania. While there are many who offer diagnostic evaluations for ASD in our area, I have particular experience and expertise that is not found in most practitioners. I have experience with evaluating very young children, also an area that is fairly specialized.

In addition to diagnostic assessments, I am able to provide evaluations to support requests for special education services and track levels of progress over time.

If you have an interest in learning more about me and what I offer, please contact me here and I will be more than happy to get in touch. You can also find information about me on LinkedIn: https://www.linkedin.com/in/susan-f-epstein-bb19865/
