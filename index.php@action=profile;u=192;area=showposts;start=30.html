Neighborhood Chat / Re: Metal screw found in McDonald's apple pie (Astoria)
« on: July 25, 2008, 04:56:07 PM »
Quote from: Chuckster on July 25, 2008, 12:11:53 PM
The New York Daily News reports on a woman that found a a metal screw inside her McDonald's baked apple pie earlier this week.  Apparently, she purchased some food for herself and children from the McDonalds located at 21st in Astoria.  According to the story, no one seems to have been hurt, but the woman claims to have been very frightened by the ordeal.

From skimming through a few of the reader comments posted on the Daily News website, it seems like people think this may be a hoax and that the woman is looking to cash in by filing a phoney lawsuit.  I wonder how these cases are investigated.  How do they verify if the story was a complete fabrication or if this did actually happen?

Screw this! Mom gets big Mac surprise as she chomps on apple pie

They'll investigate it by tracing back the apple pie to the source.  If the screw was inside the apple pie, than it had to be put there at the maunfacturing level.  Each bulk box of apple pies (or any industiralized food for that matter) has a lot number on it.  This number correlates to the julian (actual # day of the year) date the product was produced.  It will also contain information as to the location of manufacturing plant the pies were produced at.  All major maunfactures are required to provide this traceability in their HAACP plans.  After they trace it back to the actual facility, QC then takes over and finds the individual machinery items used in processing and a full top to bottom inspection takes place to try and locate the hole missing the screw.  If nothing is found, then they call the lawyer and pray that the victim doesn't try and take them for a lifetime supply of apple pies or something stupid.  Usually all foodservice manufacturing facilities run each individual item through a metal detector before it gets pacakaged just to prevent something like this.  When all is said and done, it usually amounts to the victim is the one who put the foreign object in the food trying to make a buck, and they end up just looking stupid.  We'll see how this plays out.
