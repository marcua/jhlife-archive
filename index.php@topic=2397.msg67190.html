As an initial matter, I wouldn't trust Seamless reviews as far as I can throw them -- people don't take time to leave positive reviews on there as often as they take the time to go on there and zing restaurants for some minor delivery malfeasance.  Yelp is slightly better, except that, as with predecessors like Chowhound, reviewers seem to delight in nothing more than remarking that "X restaurant has gone downhill," or that "there is no good [insert type of food] in Jackson Heights anymore."  But enough with my rant.  As for your specific question, I have had great luck with Juquila.  They are on Seamless, and while I haven't had their enchiladas, their burritos, tacos, various platos, and their Mexican Cobb salad have all been solid, and really quite tasty indeed! I order from them on a fairly regular basis, and have not been let down yet.  And if you are in the mood to leave the apartment, their physical location has a pretty cute atmosphere too (not your standard former-travel-agency drop-ceiling-and-plastic-tables delivery joint).
