Title: Re: Landscape Architecture at I.S. 145
Post by: intercaecos on February 12, 2008, 03:43:46 PM
The Chateau, on the south side of 34th Avenue between 80th and 81st streets, has probably the most elaborate and beautiful of the private gardens. In contrast, the garden at the Towers, across 34th Avenue from the Chateau, is simpler but also more elegant, with its long, shady vistas and architectural features, as well as being wonderfully serene.

Unfortunately, however, no garden looks its best at this time of year.
