Slim Bagels on 86th Street
« Reply #50 on: July 05, 2010, 04:41:00 PM »
Today I got a chance to get lunch from Slims Bagels on the corner of 37Th Ave. and 86Th Street and have to say it was surprisingly delicious---Not to mention that it is about the only "normal" deli in town.. I had a Cuban Paninni which was great! He has a large selection of bagels, spreads and sweets along with all the regular Deli stuff....Naked juices, bagel chips, cookies, starbucks, and flavored coffees.  There is counter space if your into eating there. It is a great addition to the neighborhood and I would recommended trying it
