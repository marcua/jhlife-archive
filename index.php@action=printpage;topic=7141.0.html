Title: 4th ANNUAL TAP EXTRAVAGANZA - QUEENS at Flushing Town Hall
Post by: FCCA on February 22, 2012, 03:19:53 PM
Flushing Town Hall
137-35 Northern Boulevard
Flushing, New York 11354

4th ANNUAL TAP EXTRAVAGANZA - QUEENS

Friday, May 18, 2012, 8:00 PM
$20/$15 Members/$10 Students

A co-presentation of Flushing Town Hall and presenter/producer Cobi Narita, Jazz Center of New York
 
Hoofers, bring your tap shoes and join the Tap Jam and Shim Sham Finale! Featuring Dormesha Sumbry, Baakari Wilder (lead, Savion Glover's Bring in Da Noise); Keitaro Hosokawa and Masata Nishitani, Savion Glover Company; Ali Bradley; and Ebony and Jeremia Edwards, our "youngest stars." Co-produced by Traci Mann and Al Heyward; with Frank Owens, Music Director; and Michela Marino Lerman, Dance Captain.
 

Conveniently located on Northern Boulevard in Downtown Flushing, a block and half east of Main Street, FTH is easily accessible by subway and bus; we offer free parking for our patrons. For tickets and more information, visit: www.flushingtownhall.org, or call the Box Office: (718) 463-7700, ext. 222.
