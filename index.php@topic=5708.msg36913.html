Best Indian Buffets
« on: December 29, 2010, 06:16:52 PM »
Just had the buffet at the new Tawa Tandoor on 74th Street.  It was definitely one of the best buffets in Jackson Heights.  All the dishes were very tasty and fresh--good variety.  Service was pleasant, as well as the decor...I prefer it to Jackson Diner (I think the quality has gone downhill), although they don't have the Masala Dosa.  Tawa Tandoor and Delhi Heights are my favorites by far. Haven't yet tried the buffet at Mehfil which I know is a favorite on this site.

I liked Mehfil for offering fish curry at its buffet, but otherwise agree that it's bland in general.  My favorite had been Delhi Heights for awhile, up until they toned down the spice.  Tawa is spicier, so now it moves up as the best Indian lunch buffet in my book.  They have a nice selection of dishes and they're good with bringing the naan to you.  The space is also very inviting and clean.
