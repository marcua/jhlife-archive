Xtasis is a great place to have in the neighborhood.  My 5-year-old loves it, and it's a quirky and fun alternative to the corporate fast food joints.  But it's not worth a special trip.

So Buddy and I had a very informal meet-up this afternoon and since it was lunchtime, we decided to go to Five Guys in College Point.  This was my first time there, and it certainly won't be the last!  O.K., so I need to pack on 5 more lbs....yeah right.   Anyway, I ordered the single burger topped with fried onions, lettuce and ketchup.  It was absolutely delicious!  All burgers are cooked well done, nonetheless, my burger was juicy and flavorful.  No extra seasonings whatsoever, just the natural flavor of the meat in addition to the added toppings.  We also shared an order of the fries which is huge.   Prior to today's visit, I had a different idea of what Five Guys would be like.  I pictured it somewhat more of a diner type setting.  It's very casual...you order at the counter, stuff yourself with peanuts and wait for your number to be called.  Definitely go!

And, as if we hadn't had enough to eat, we also decided to check out the new place on 31st Avenue in Woodside, called Patio.  I'll post about it on another thread.
