Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Music this week at Espresso 77
« on: March 29, 2016, 10:36:23 PM »
First off, just want to say thanks for the overwhelming show of support and kindness from our Jackson Heights friends in the first few weeks of the newly expanded Espresso 77.  It has been so terrific to see everyone again and to share our new space with our community!

This week, we are starting up our music program with 3 of our favorite performers.  Each week we present 3 shows (on Thursday, Friday, and Saturday nights) and we have a diverse lineup of talented musicians.  Please join us this week for:

Thursday, March 31
Silbin Sandovar: Queens-based singer/songwriter plays Rock/Americana.

Friday, April 1
Bellweather: a Bard band comprised of three pals who weave music making in and around their romantic struggles.

Saturday, April 2
Jon Allen: Guitar Slinger/Songwriter

Hope to see you soon for music, art, coffee, beer, wine, and food at e77!

Julie and Afzal
Espresso 77
35-57 77th Street (just off of 37th Avenue)
Jackson Heights
718-424-1077
www.espresso77.com
follow us on Facebook, Twitter and Instagram for event info and specials!
