I haven't seen it, but the Queensboro does have a private event space downstairs.  Check in with Dudley... he can provide details.

Closer to the subway, there's an upstairs portion of Jackson Diner that I've seen open for private events.

I have held very low key events (a teenager's birthday party) at BQE Billiards at 70th & 34th.  They can't provide a fully private space, but it's a fun atmosphere.

There's also 39th Avenue Arts N Events in Long Island City.

This doesn't sound like it's for you, but for anybody looking for something glitzy that can handle a much larger crowd in JH, there's the newish Bellozino (https://www.bellozino.com/) at 72nd & Roosevelt and the medium-sized Perlas (https://www.perlas.website/) at 69th & Roosevelt.
