Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Community Groups, Activities & Events / Story Garden
« on: October 01, 2013, 09:39:08 AM »
Story Garden is a musical storytime for children ages 0-4, led by Alexia Tate, an area mom and children's performer.  It runs every Monday (excluding some holidays) at 10:00 am, 10:30 am, and 11:00 am.  Each session is limited to eight children.  Sign up is done on a weekly basis, starting one week in advance (Tuesdays).  There is a suggested donation of $5 ($8 for two children). The location is Mimi for Me Yoga Studio at 76-11 37th Ave., 2nd Floor.  There is no elevator, and strollers must be brought upstairs (but I can help!).  For more information, complete contact details and updates, please visit Story Garden online at www.facebook.com/StoryGardenWithMissAlexia. You do not need a Facebook account to view the page.

Miss Alexia
