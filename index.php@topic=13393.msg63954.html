Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

« on: June 16, 2015, 10:42:17 PM »
Community Board 3 has updated its calendar with some street closing events for the summer and fall... 

  • Saturday, June 20: Antioch Baptist Church - Takin' to the Streets. 103rd Street, 34th Ave to Northern Blvd.
  • Sunday, June 21: Father's Day Religious Ceremony, 91st Street, Roosevelt Ave to Elmhurst Ave.
  • Sunday, June 28: Peruvan Parade, 37th Avenue, 69th Street to 86th Street
  • Sunday, July 12: Festival de Las Flores Parade, 37th Avenue, 69th Street to 86th Street
  • Sunday, July 26: Colombian Independance Parade, Northern Blvd., 69th Street to 86th Street
  • Sunday, August 2: Ecuadorian Parade, Northern Blvd., 69th Street to 86th Street
  • Sunday, September 20: Queens Hispanic Parade, 37th Avenue, 69th Street to 86th Street
  • Sunday, October 4: Dominican Parade, 37th Avenue, 69th Street to 86th Street
  • Sunday, October 18: Bolivian Parade, 37th Avenue, 69th Street to 86th Street
  • Saturday, October 31: Halloween Parade, 37th Avenue, 86th Street to 76th Street

Hi everyone! The Festival de las Flores is a celebration of the Colombian "silleteros" who carry large medallion flower arrangements on their backs. The parade will include dance groups, floats decorated with many flowers, and of course the silleteros and their "silletas".

The parade will be this July 12th between 12pm and 2pm on 37th ave between 69th street and 86th street.

After the parade, we will continue the celebration at the Queens Botanical Garden where we will have food and craft vendors and special performances.

For more info and ticket purchases for this special event, visit www.festivaldelasfloresus.com or Visit Us on Facebook: Festival De Las Flores US
