News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

It is true, this past week or so, the landings have been over western Jackson Heights every day (including this morning)  We live on 34th Ave and 73rd street and the landings here are quite loud, engines "cut" or not...  We now gave up and installed $1000 citiquiet windows in the bedroom which keeps the noise down so long as you keep the windows shut..... The landings may not affect those in the east, but a large swath of Queens, including Woodside, and certainly Elmhurst north of Northern Blvd are affected.  Being an unwilling expert on the landing route for the past 5 years, I agree the planes seem to be lower, thus a bit louder.  We have resigned ourselves to the noise, LGA has been here much longer than most of us, but I don't know how much longer we can live here, only for that reason.
