Title: Re: Car alarm
Post by: CaptainFlannel on April 10, 2016, 11:24:37 AM
Make noise complaints via 311 for this sort of thing. A couple weeks ago there was a car horn blaring nonstop for hours. Not even a pattern, just a constant blare. I called it in, and my SO went out and found the car and took down the license plate, location, etc., and also called it in. It's a noise complaint, so the police aren't going to give it top priority, but if everyone who heard these kinds annoyances would make a noise complaint, maybe they'd respond a little faster.
