debit card precaution at Met Food
« on: July 21, 2009, 08:43:01 PM »
Yesterday I shopped at the Met on 37th at 76th street. Cashier scanned everything, at the point of paying I mentioned Debit. She hand-keyed the dollar amount (I don't recall if that is standard procedure with debit cards) and I swiped, and approved the amount displayed. 
Then I looked at the monitor near her and saw "Change: $2.00"  When I pointed it out to her, she flushed and seemed embarrassed, apologized and gave me the change. I had not requested cash back.
Anyone else experience this at Met?
Human Error? Possibly, but since the 2 and 4 keys are not side-by-side I am suspicious. I too often do not look at the amounts, and just happened to this time because my bill was unusually high (I had not shopped in several weeks).
Keep your eyes open, folks!!
