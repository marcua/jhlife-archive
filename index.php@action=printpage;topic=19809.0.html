Title: Re: JHBG COVID-19 Podcast Series
Post by: itsit on May 15, 2020, 10:31:42 AM
 JHBG has another podcast available in its coping with COVID19 series here in Jackson Heights. Stefan Kenney, the proprietor of Stand Alone Cheese, speaks with the host, Alfonzo Quiros, about the issues facing a very small store that was less than one year old trying to survive the pandemic.
 
Note- this is an uplifting story! It's true that many have taken an enormous hit in the retail sector here. But there are a few bright spots and Stand Alone Cheese is one of them as they pivoted quickly to online orders in an effort to keep their business alive.

Give a listen here on Soundcloud:
https://soundcloud.com/user-570401025/small-businesses-surviving-coronavirus/s-sbHoa5HTdZN

The previous stories are Ed Wesley, President of JHBG, talking about the impact that he sees on the group and it's JH activities going forward in 2020. And our beloved Catalina Cruz on the type of needs she is seeing and hearing about with her constituents during this time. Both on Soundcloud.
