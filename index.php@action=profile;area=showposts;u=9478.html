I don't mean to wax nostalgic just for the sake of doing so.  However, I grew up here and I can remember when there were no roll-down gates in Jackson Heights, let alone mobile SkyWatch cranes.  Times have changed and for all the good reasons mentioned in the posts prior to this, it is as important as ever to use every piece of crime control available to us, regarless of asthetics.
