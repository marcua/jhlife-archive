I second both Shelby2 and DSSJH recommendations.  Legends is a little closer and is male-dominated but definitely diverse in every sense of the word.  Both straight and gay couples are regulars and people from many different ethnic backgrounds.  Age range from 20's to 60's (youngest on Friday and Saturday night).  In addition to sitting at the bar there are tables in the back and high tables  directly across from the bar against the wall.  Lots of flat screen TVs.  If you don't feel like walking home Mexicana Car Service is two doors away.

Quote from: ECG on October 18, 2011, 05:36:31 PM
Anyone been to Molly Bloom's on Queens Blvd? Around the 40's and on the Q32 route.

My family and I have been to Molly Bloom's a few times, and we highly recommend it. Pleasant classic pub interior, nice beer on tap, good food, outdoor seating in their back garden, the staff is great, and (I know you didn't ask about this, but it's unusual for a bar) it's kid-friendly. I get the feeling they really want to be a community hub. It's not in the immediate Jackson Heights neighborhood -- I'd be thrilled if the owners would open an identical place in JH -- but it's a quick ride on the 7 train.
