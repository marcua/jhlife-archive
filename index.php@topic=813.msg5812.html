So Thai thread or not, you got me all jazzed up for Rice.  Went there today and got lots of takeout.  Pretty tasty.  I liked the interior of the place: small but tasteful looking.  I got the curry puffs - I liked them.  They were big and fluffy and tasty.  The fried tofu - meh.  Dip it in the thai sauce and everything tastes good but pretty bland.  Crab fried rice - yummy.  And masaman chicken curry.  I liked it but it could have been spicier.

I'd definitely eat their food again.
