Jackson Heights fares well in NY Mag's "Most Liveable Neighborhoods" in NYC
« on: April 12, 2010, 01:07:43 PM »
We're at #15 out of 60 of the city's Most Livable Neighborhoods.

http://nymag.com/realestate/neighborhoods/2010/65374/index8.html

According to NY Mag, Jackson Heights fares well on:
Diversity, Housing Costs, Schools, Safety, Housing Quality and Health & Environment.

According to them, JH fares less well on (and I'm sure some of us will dispute some of these)
Transit, Restaurants, Shopping, Creative Capital, Greenspace and Nightlife.
(I'd pick a fight with them on Transit and Restaurants...and they define "creative capital" by number of arts venues (we don't have many) and number of individuals involved in the arts (we have a ton).

There's also a fun sliding calculator that lets you adjust your priorities:
http://nymag.com/realestate/neighborhoods/2010/65355/
