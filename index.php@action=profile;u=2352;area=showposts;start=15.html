Restaurants & Food / Re: Los Amigos Pizza
« on: June 16, 2018, 10:35:52 AM »
 Ordered five pies for a party yesterday. Was at a party last weekend where they ordered a whole bunch of different pies from los amigos. I would say arguably the best pizza in the neighborhood. I think my guests at yesterday‘s party felt it was better than Sac’s.  Good quality ingredients and the thing that makes it mostly unique is that paper thin crispy crust. Fold it, and it still probably isn’t as wide as a regular slice.

 I would say definitely worth a try. Don’t let the fact it’s in the back of a nondescript bodega throw you off!  Also, the pizza place is owned by local Jackson Heights parents. Let’s support our own!
