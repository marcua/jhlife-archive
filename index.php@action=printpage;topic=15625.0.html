Title: Re: Volunteer opportunities in JH?
Post by: itsit on November 10, 2016, 12:15:34 PM
 On 75th street near Roosevelt, there is a penguin statue affectionately named Wink which is dressed seasonally -or for a sports
team- by Elcy, a woman working in an office a couple doors in from 37th Road. Ask around for her if you want to help very locally
with what she does for some of the immigrants on Roosevelt and in the area. She provides immediate needs which is sometimes
to use the bathroom or the computer as well as more complex problems with translation and followups on government applications
and policies. She is an unsung hero in Jackson Heights in that her organization is just her. But she is beloved and was a recipient
of a JHBG Good Neighbor Award last year.
