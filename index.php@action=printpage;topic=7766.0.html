Title: Tulcingo Tacos
Post by: madalyn on September 17, 2012, 09:12:06 AM
I want to put in a good word for this restaurant which is located on the old site of Meson Asturias.  It's at 40-12 83rd St., just south of Roosevelt Ave.

We had Chicken in Mole sauce and cheese enchiladas, both of which were quite good.  The accompanying rice and beans were also very full of flavor.

The place is clean and friendly, if not terribly fancy.  It's more of a homey ambiance.

I intend to return and try more of their specials.
