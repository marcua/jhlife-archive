Post by: JH3525 on August 11, 2018, 10:53:49 AM
Au Bon Pain is very easy to find.  Enter 79-01 Broadway, walk straight ahead down the hall, when you get to the elevator area walk downstairs two flights.  Au Bon Pain is awesome.  Incredible coffee, muffins, oatmeal with many toppings, breakfast sandwiches, regular sandwiches, several home made soups, salads, etc.  Prices much lower than Manhattan - I'm guessing they are subsidized by the hospital making prices affordable for hospital workers.
