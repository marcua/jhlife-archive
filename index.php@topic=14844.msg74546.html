When I sold my Carroll Gardens place I knew it was going to under-appraise, so I put together a comps package and handed it to the appraiser in person. It's really hard to get them to change after the fact - it can seem like they didn't do their homework. But if you make their research easy, you make their jobs easy and you ensure they get best info. No one knows the neighborhood as well as we do.
