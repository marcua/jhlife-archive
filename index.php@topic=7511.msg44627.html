This is a "perennial" problem. I've discovered people in my own building digging up garden plants.

The woman in my building who takes care of the garden wires the shrubs together to make it more difficult to pilfer new plants.

This type of problem is more common in working class neighborhoods like ours, especially where you have a population of people with uncertain income sources. But then again, someone in your building might just think it's something they can get away with, like shoplifting. In any event, it's tough to live with this type of thing, you try to make the place you live nice, then someone comes along and digs up your plant or scrawls an obscenity on your sign than says" Have a nice day, please enjoy the garden."

On a positive note: I've noticed more lovely and tended front yard gardens in JH, when I'm walking my dog in the morning , it makes me happy.
