Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Neighborhood Chat / Parents with kids at IS230 PLEASE READ
« on: June 29, 2011, 07:28:15 PM »
A friend asked me to post her statement below.  She is not a member on this list serve.

I am very upset to have walked by the school today about 4:30PM to find tons of student exams, paperwork and PERSONAL INFORMATION in the recycling UNSHREDED, not in bags, NOT HIDDEN nor covered!!
I called 311 and filed a report to Sanitation Dept for improper disposal of recycables AND to Department of Education for possible violation of students privacy ( that's what I would call it).  This and a call to the local precint were all I was advised to do.  Precint said they couldn't do ANYTHING because it's not in thier jurisdiction..."no crime has been committed".
To further explain to you what I saw....Document heading "IS 230Q"
Body of first page states, child is being referred to Elmhurst Psychiatric ER for eval. and proceeds to state bodily injury and statement from child.  Childs name, date of birth and school authorities signature.  Page two states childs home address, parents names, emerg. contact names, number and relationship to child.  Childs primary care physician and number along with student ID number. 
Other than what I have already done, I don't know what else I can do.  I can't possible remove all this paperwork myself.  I just thought I would let my community know because we DO look out for each other and our children.  Those who should and don't should be held accountable.  I really hope this information doesn't get into the wrong hands.  It would be socially and emotionally devastating for a peer to find what I found and use it against the child in September!!  We don't want to think of this getting into the hands of a ill thinking adult.

V.Panchame
Mother in JH
