At the Greenmarket there is a stall that takes old clothing and fabric scraps and recycles/re-purposes/resells them. It's great because you can give them things that are worn out and they will recycle them. http://www.grownyc.org/clothing

(You can also try selling old clothes that are in good condition on eBay, I've been fairly successful!)
