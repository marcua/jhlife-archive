 Merry Christmas and for those of you enjoying a real Christmas tree and want to see it go back to nature,
here is info on Mulchfest 2014. Once again it will be held in Jackson Heights at Travers Park. The trees
are put into a chipper and you can take a bag of chipped pine back home for personal use. Join in on
Jan 11-12, 2014. Last year JHSCRAPS took in a truck load of pine chips which were distributed locally
in tree pits and planting projects sponsored by the JHBeautification Group. Now there is local recycling!!!

How to 'Chip In.'
This year, MulchFest will take place on January 11 and 12, 2014. It’s easier than ever to chip in! There
are two ways you can help.

Chipping Locations
You can bring your tree to a chipping location on January 11 and 12 from 10:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m. We’ll
chip your tree, and give you your very own bag of mulch!

Drop Off Locations
Or you can bring your tree to a drop-off site from Saturday, January 4 through Sunday, January 12. Leave
your tree with us, and we’ll recycle it for you later! The NYC Department of Sanitation will also be conducting
special curbside collections  for mulching and recycling of Christmas trees from Monday, December 30, 2013
through Wednesday, January 15, 2014.

What to Bring
Please remember to remove all lights and ornaments before bringing the tree to a MulchFest site. Biodegradable
bags will be provided if you wish to take some free mulch home. Mulch is available at the sites marked with
an asterisk (*) below.

Want to volunteer at MulchFest ? Register to join the GreenTeam and MillionTreesNYC volunteer group.
