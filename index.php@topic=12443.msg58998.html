Re: Frank's Pharmacy Grand Reopening party Oct 25 -- public welcome!
« Reply #6 on: October 16, 2014, 03:59:00 PM »
Six months after a devastating five-alarm blaze destroyed Frank’s Pharmacy, this well-loved neighborhood institution has reopened and will hold a special public celebration on Saturday, October 25th from 12:00 noon until 3:00PM.

The Grand Reopening event for Frank’s Pharmacy will take place at the store’s new location at 77-01 37th Avenue in Jackson Heights, just steps away from its former address. The celebration will take place from noon until 3:00PM. The opening ceremony will take place from 12:30 until 1:00PM and will include a ribbon-cutting by owner Frank Buonagurio, as well as speeches of support from New York State Senator Jose Peralta (District 13) and New York City Council Member Daniel Dromm. Both Senator Peralta and Council Member Dromm will officially proclaim October 25th “Frank’s Pharmacy Day.” The public is invited to attend.

Frank’s Pharmacy will distribute refreshments and treats for young and old alike, so long as supplies last. There will also be prize raffles held for adults and children in attendance.

“I am humbled by the outpouring of neighborhood support that followed the fire. Everyone did what they could to ensure that we would be back on our feet as soon as possible,” said Frank Buonagurio, the owner of Frank’s Pharmacy. “Keeping our neighbors healthy is a responsibility we take very seriously. So we are really glad to be back!”
