Title: "What a Wonderful World" turns 50
Post by: toddg on October 25, 2017, 07:49:37 AM
A little bit of optimism we could all use these days... Louis Armstrong's recording of "What's a Wonderful World" turns 50 this month.

According to the NY Times (https://www.nytimes.com/2017/10/25/nyregion/new-york-today-50-wonderful-years.html), the song was inspired by comments he made about his block on 107th Street.

Hear the great man perform the song on YouTube (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CWzrABouyeE).

The Louis Armstrong House Museum (https://www.louisarmstronghouse.org/) has a special exhibit dedicated to the song.
