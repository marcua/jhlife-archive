This is the San Antonio Farm and Grocery. I like this place a lot. I have the utmost respect for the owners. They aim to please. They prepare mango and pineappple for me at no charge, using clean utensils and with clean hands (they have hot running water in the back, and use it).
It's not trendy and you won't find mesclun there, but they pick their fruit & veggies well and don't overprice. It is a "fruit store" in that they don't sell meat, with the exception of enchiladas (but not made there in the store). They make juices and will be selling hot chocolate and homemade arroz con leche soon.
If you cook Mexican, you will find everything you want there. And if you're a vegetarian, you'll have what's important to you. Please keep an open mind and check them out.
