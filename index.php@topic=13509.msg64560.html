I took a couple Wusthof knives to Boris today and it was pretty ugly. I wouldn't say they are ruined because I am not a fancy chef and they will still cut things, but it was not the experience I was expecting. (I was expecting to be like "OMG, I can't wait to go home and chop things!") Instead I am a little bummed...

I have Wusthof knives and just sharpen them at home when needed.  It may take a little practice at first but it's pretty easy once you get the hang of it.  All you need is a combination waterstone, such as this one off Amazon...

http://www.amazon.com/King-KI001-Combination-Waterstone-1000/dp/B0000Y7LAS/ref=sr_1_3?ie=UTF8&qid=1442789657&sr=8-3&keywords=water+stone

For a casual user one stone like the above will last a very long time.  You just need a little time, a little patience, access to water, and a steady hand.

@antparty - I mourn for your knives.  At least they still cut ok.
