Neighborhood Chat / Re: My tap water is brown, how about yours?
« on: January 06, 2010, 11:14:15 PM »
I had this problem when I lived in Manhattan and I called the landlord and he told me it was due to construction in the area and just let the water run for a bit.  Well, I never bought that explanation and I hated the murky water coming through the bathtub spigot and the showerhead.  It was very noticeable when I was out of town for a week and came back and turned the water on.  I complained several times and was told that was just the way it was.  Well, one day, the super came up and he had a big piece of plumbing in his hand that was caked with rust and gunk and he said "Oh, you were right, there was something wrong with the pipes!"  If I remember correctly, it was called a thermocoupler or coupling valve or something that had to do with the water heater and that was where the disgusting brown water was coming from.  Landlords and supers will tell you a problem is your imagination until they find out that it really was something wrong.  Not much you can do about that, though!
