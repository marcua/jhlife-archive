I did a search on this topic and it brought up really old results.  I thought it would be nice to start a new thread for people to post their opinions on where to go and where avoid as far as beauty treatments in the area.

Here's my very limited list of experiences... (Note: I'm SHY so if a place is really crowded I'd usually just walk by instead of going in and asking about wait time etc.  If someone can recommend some great spots I will try to get over it. lol!!) Also - I bite my nails so I personally skip manicures. haha.

Pedicures -
I've only been to 78 Nail & Spa in this hood.  I've gone about 4 times and had someone different each time.  I really really like the sander - but I really hate that every single time I go there are 3-4 people in front of me and then on top of that someone comes in and has an appointment.  It takes forever. (I'd make an appt - but I don't "love" any of them"  Also when you walk in, nobody acknowledges your presence AT ALL - no greeting, no nothing. 

Facials-
I've been to the place on 37th Ave & 80th (mostly does threading) for their facials.  It's no frills, but cheap.  I prefer when one of the older women does the facial - but I feel it would be rude to say so, so I just deal with whoever.  The last time I went, the girl annoyed me and I haven't been back.  So... I'm looking for other options.

Threading-
The place on 37th & 80th is fine for this.  It's much cheaper than in Manhattan. 

I used to go to Dulhan on B'way across from the Korean market.  I wasn't a fan of their threading, but the facials were nice.
