Best JH meal under 8$?
« on: February 26, 2019, 09:22:44 PM »
Whats yours? Always looking for a quick smallish portion for late breakfast, lunch, first dinner and/or second dinner (besides pizza or deli sandwiches)

Currently mine is at Papa's Kitchen (83rd and 37th). Strange that the menu I found online doesn't include the Silogs.  They are 7 and 8 dollar plates with a big scoop of garlic fried rice (could be a little more garlicky for my taste) but its not greasy like the fried rice at the chinese take-out places.  The rice is topped with a perfectly cooked overeasy egg and your choice of viande . They're all good but I highly recommend the longanisa, a sweet and garlicky sausage.  Thats the guilty pleasure right there.  Woodside has a few places that serve this and have grilled sardines as an option.  Hopefully Papas Kitchen can add this option.

Pretty high on the list was the falafel pitas, and the shakshouka at Zaytouns.  Tried to make the hike across Roosevelt at least once a week.  Best falafel I've had this side of the mediteranean.  So sad they are gone.  Does anyone have any recommendations for a place with similar dishes nearby?

Occasionally, I will visit Jose Fish Market at 8104 Roosevelt Ave only for their $2.75 fish chowder which is incredible and made fresh all day long.   The fish chowder is not a meal but for those who want a full meal they have many choices of fried seafood such as whiting, shrimp, scallops, flounder, crab stick which includes either french fries or rice priced from $4.00 to $7.75 and the portions are large.  You can dine in or take out.  No table service plus no tax.  Incredible bargain.  The least expensive is the whiting with the fries or rice for just $4.00.  I don't eat fried foods but considering how busy this place is I'm certain you won't be disappointed.
