Rent hikes displace merchants from Roosevelt Avenue
« on: July 08, 2014, 12:11:48 PM »
https://www.facebook.com/photo.php?v=705785902826561
In this video, a small business owner from Roosevelt Avenue describes how he was forced to find a new, more affordable location for his business. When the commercial property at 81-08 Roosevelt Avenue was sold recently, Transworld Equities, a large real estate management company based in Manhattan, acquired the property. The commercial tenants had been paying $12,000 in monthly rent. Now that their lease is up, the new owners are charging $20,000, citing higher property value as the reason for the rent hike. Unable to afford the increase in rent, the tenants at 81-08 Roosevelt Avenue are being forced out
