Title: QUEENS JAZZ ORCHESTRA (Jimmy Heath, Music Director) at Flushing Town Hall
Post by: FCCA on February 22, 2012, 04:24:08 PM
Flushing Town Hall
137-35 Northern Boulevard
Flushing, New York 11354

QUEENS JAZZ ORCHESTRA (Jimmy Heath, Music Director)

Friday, May 25, 2012, 8:00 PM
$40/$32 Members/$20 Students; Package Price: $120/$100 Members (Table seating for 2, wine and snacks)

Launched in 2008 by Flushing Council to honor Queen's jazz history and support the next generation of jazz artists, this year's concert will showcase female jazz artists on the Queens Jazz Trail: Ella Fitzgerald; Lena Horne; and Billie Holiday.


Conveniently located on Northern Boulevard in Downtown Flushing, a block and half east of Main Street, FTH is easily accessible by subway and bus; we offer free parking for our patrons. For tickets and more information, visit: www.flushingtownhall.org, or call the Box Office: (718) 463-7700, ext. 222.
