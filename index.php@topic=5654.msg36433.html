I don't know about getting it dismissed but you can probably get the fine reduced, usually to half the amount.

Write a letter -- it doesn't matter what it says, they process it automatically and send you a reduced fine. Then you can either pay that or have it reviewed by an administrative judge.

Do try not to double-park though. It's a huge problem in the neighborhood especially since people here block traffic and honk like crazy.

Good luck!

In NYC double parking is prohibited at all times if you are operating a passenger vehicle.  Commercial vehicles have some leeway when making deliveries.  That being said double parking is like jay walking it is rarely enforced.  Most of the time a police vehicle will come up behind you and announce over their speaker to move your vehicle and will only ticket you if you don't comply.
