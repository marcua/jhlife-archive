As others have in the past, I feel compelled to revive this thread to say that I finally had dinner at Casa del Chef Bistro in Woodside, and it was so lovely! It was, sadly, not very crowded on a Friday night, so I thought it worth plugging this again- if you haven't been, truly worth it. We did the (reasonably priced) tasting menu, and everything was delicious, particularly the first course of ricotta gnocchi with greenmarket vegetables. Also had a perfectly seared white fish, and super flavorful steak (flat iron I think? would have to go back and check).

quaint and cozy interior, attentive service, and most importantly, excellent food.

I can also add to the chorus above that they are very accommodating to vegetarians (I am one). There are always veggie apps and at least one veggie entree, and if you call about a tasting menu when you make a reservation, I believe they will do an all-veg tasting menu for you (as long as they get a heads up in advance, of course). They've had some special vegetarian tasting menu dinner events, too, so it's definitely something they can do.

A wonderful restaurant overall that I hope thrives for many years to come. Warm, excellent service and flavorful, creative food that is truly out of this world.
