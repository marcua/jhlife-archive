Chocopie, the way I've uploaded photos to JHL is by using an image hosting site like Photobucket.  Once you have a photo that you like uploaded to that site, what you do is just copy the image code that automatically appears at the base of the photo when you hover over it with your mouse.  Look for the link that has the following codes surrounding it:

(IMG) (/IMG)

Keep in mind that the codes must be surrounded by brackets rather than parenthesis like I have above.  Hit copy, and then paste that into the message box on your reply in JHL.  The image should appear on the screen when you hit preview.

Other members...feel free to chime in also.
