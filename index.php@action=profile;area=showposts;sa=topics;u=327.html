Restaurants & Food / Lao Bei Fan ????? has moved
« on: April 11, 2010, 08:49:21 PM »
Lao Bei Fan the dumpling/handrawn noodle shop on Whitney Ave(just off Broadway before the train underpass) in Elmhurst has moved to the former Oz Tea Cafe at Cornish Ave and Broadway. They now offer amazing self serve hot pot. Unlike the former location, which really was like a Beijing street style dumpling stand this one has space and tables. BTW, Lao Bei Fan translates as "old north style food"
