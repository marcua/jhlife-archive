Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Aronan - here is a little info on packing goodie bags.  Sounds like they need to know in advance if you plan to help:

from the JHBG website
"Each year we need volunteers to help stuff the goodie bags (usually the day before Halloween) and to give them out at the parade. We need to sign up the volunteers in advance, so we know how many people will be helping and with which tasks. All volunteers who help with stuffing the goodie bags are treated to pizza and soda, and all of the volunteers are invited to a thank-you party the night of the parade. Contact the JHBG by phone (718-565-5344) or email (info@jhbg) if you're interested."
