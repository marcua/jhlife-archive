Recommending auto body/repair shops is always a dangerous thing to do, so bear in mind usual disclaimers apply.

I've been to the auto collision shop on Northern Blvd once:

P & M Automotive Inc?
9502 Northern Blvd, Jackson Heights, NY
(718) 457-4451?

Smooth transaction for a very bargain rate.


For claims, my insurance company sends me to LIC:

2219 37th Ave
Long Island City, NY 11101
(718) 784-1321
- twinscollision.com

I've gone there for three separate incidents over the years.   They did a great job every time and the work was done on-time or earlier.  Your mileage may vary, significantly, of course.  After all, this is auto work we're talking about.
