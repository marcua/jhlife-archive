Title: NYPD Community Meeting Sunday, March 3
Post by: am315 on March 01, 2019, 09:10:22 AM
The NYPD officers serving Jackson Heights & Corona are having a community meeting Sunday to address any concerns residents have regarding crime and policing.

Here are the details:
Sunday, March 3, 2019

MEETING TIME:   Doors open at 2:30 PM for refreshments
                    Meeting starts at 3:00 PM

MEETING SITE:   St. Joan of Arc – Gymnasium
                                      (Next to Ward Meehan Hall)
               35-27 82 Street, Jackson Heights, NY 11372

SPONSORED BY:  New York City Councilman Daniel Dromm AND
YOUR NEIGHBORHOOD COORDINATION OFFICERS
Police Officers Tyler Scala and Bryan Liebold

This is for the 115 PRECINCT, SECTOR C. See the 115th pct website for exact boundaries of Sector C. But it doesn't matter if you live in those boundaries.
