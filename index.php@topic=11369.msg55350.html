Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

JH Orchestra Concert
« on: March 02, 2014, 10:58:32 AM »
A (much needed) Spring Celebration

March 12, 2014 at 7:30 PM at the Community United Methodist Church
35th Avenue and 81st Street (81-10 35th Avenue)

Music by Barber, Bach, Bizet, Grainger, Mancini and Queens composer Steven L. Rosenhaus.

Featured cello soloist Jeffrey McFarland-Johnson and some traditional Irish tunes too!

Suggested donation is $10 for adults; $5 for seniors and students; post-concert reception in the church library

They are actually from all over the city but the majority live in Jackson Heights.  This concert features a local (Queens!) composer and guest soloist on cello.  Poster is attached - hopefully you've seen it up in neighborhood shops.  There is a reception immediately following the concert - all are invited.

The program is as follows:

Sussex Celebration - Rosenhaus
Intermezzo from Vanessa for Orchestra   - Barber
Suite No. 1 for Cello Solo BWV1007 - Bach
L'Arlésienne Suite No. II - Bizet
Molly on the Shore - Grainger
Irish Tune from County Derry - Grainger
Pennywhistle Jig   - Mancini
