Finally went for tapas (I had been for the coffee when they first opened).  I have already posted about how well-designed the place is.  It's a cozy, inviting place for an evening of tapas and excellent wine.  Last night the place was full or almost full from 8:30 to 11:30 when I left.  Even though my friend and I shared a very cozy table with another couple whom we did not know, we still felt we had privacy to have our own conversation.  I think this must also be due to good design decisions.  The acoustics and the volume of the music seemed to be perfect for being able to hear the people you're with but not so loud you have to yell to be heard.
