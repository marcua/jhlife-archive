STUNNING THREE FAMILY TOWNHOUSE WITH PATIO AND GARDEN!!

This stunning three-family townhouse gem is situated in a most charming townhouse block. The house is flooded with sunlight, the front faces east and the back faces west towards an open patio with garden vista. This charming townhouse is near trains, shopping, airports, Flushing Meadows Park, Citi Field and more. This home is truly complete with the wonderful convenience of a one and possible two car parking garage. A brilliant residence with multiple options for anyone looking to create an income-producing space or an extravagant home.

Truly a grand home for grand living, encompassing approximately 2,682 sq. ft. of living space.

GARDEN LEVEL:
Lovely entrance with living room, two bedrooms, eat-in-kitchen and full bath which opens to the patio and garden.

SECOND FLOOR:
Open front terrace with entry to the living room, one bedroom, eat-in-kitchen and full bath, with access to porch, patio and garden.

THIRD FLOOR:
Elegant staircase with entry to the living room, two bedrooms, eat-in-kitchen and full bath with attractive skylight.

A brilliant residence with multiple options for anyone looking to create an income-producing space or an extravagant home.
Delivered vacant or with tenants in place.

CONTACT: MARLENE FLORES, CENTURY 21 BEST, 917-208-6709 or marleneflores25@yahoo.com
