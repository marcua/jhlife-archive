Todd,

I don't think you have been in JH long enough to remember the Dragon Seed but it was a Chinese/Polynesian restaurant on the corner of 86th Street and 37th Avenue. I believe the name of the restaurant that occupies its space is called  Listo Pollo.  It was one of the most popular places in all of Queens.  People came from near and far to eat in their beaded booths or have a tropical drink.  They actually had another Dragon Seed Restaurant further down 37th Avenue near Junction Blvd. The owner got to old and his children were the success stories of his  American dream and didn't want to run the restaurant anymore.  It closed sometime in the mid nineties.  I would trade twenty rotisserie chicken places ( We would still have a plenty) to get it back.
