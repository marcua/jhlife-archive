Neighborhood Chat / Jackson Heights Orchestra Spring Concert
« on: May 17, 2016, 02:26:41 PM »
Wednesday March 18 at 7:30
St. Marks Church
Concert is free - there is suggested donation of $10 with $5 for seniors and students.

Join the orchestra on their final concert of the season. It will be a delightful concert featuring Carol Sudhalter on flute, tenor and baritone saxophone. Jessica Ramos won the raffle at the JHBG dinner last September and will be leading the ensemble in one number.

The concert will feature classics, big band, and jazz. It should be a fun evening with some very familiar melodies. All are invited to the post-concert reception and meet the wonderful people who make this all happen.

As a reminder, St. Mark's runs a food pantry and donations of non-perishable food are welcomed.

Neighborhood Chat / Jackson Heights Orchestra Concert March 16
« on: March 04, 2016, 06:07:45 PM »
Jackson Heights Orchestra Concert
St Mark's Episcopal Church
Wed, March 16th, 2016, 7:30 PM

On the program will be works by Stanford, Bartolozzi, Hindemith, and Schubert. Come hear Barbara Oldham play Hindemith's Horn Concerto and a premier piece written by our local composer and member of the orchestra, Joseph Bartolozzi.

The concert is free, but there is a suggested donation of $10/5 at the door and $5 for seniors and students. Doors will open at 7:00. Make sure you get there early, our December concert was standing room only. Everyone is invited to the reception immediately following the concert in the social room.
