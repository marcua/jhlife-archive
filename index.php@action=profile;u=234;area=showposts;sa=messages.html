Neighborhood Chat / Re: Graffiti
« on: September 07, 2008, 02:49:46 PM »
I just wanted to say, I grew up in JHts and in the 80's graffiti was bad around the neighborhood.  Kids would "tag' there names everywhere, the side of buildings, in the lobby's of the building and even on sidewalks.  It was similar to the display you see on the # 7 train in LIC.  I have to say I knew a lot of those kids doing the graffit and they came from good homes and went to Catholic and private schools.  It really doesn't matter if the kid is from a broken home or not it usually is a kid acting out for attention.  I agree with a lot of the things Mr.C wrote and I just wanted to say I knew someone who went to Rikers for displaying his graffiti.  I don't know how many times you have to get caught to make you go to jail but, I do know its a crime.

Anyway, I just wanted to say JHts has come a long way and yes there still is a problem with the graffiti and it annoys me that this happens and right now there is graffiti on the side of my bldg and no matter how many times you clean it, they do it again.   

I hope they get caught and correct there problem.
