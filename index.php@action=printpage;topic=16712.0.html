Title: Reliable cat sitter in Jackson Heights
Post by: fumithecatlady on July 01, 2017, 03:20:45 PM
Getting ready for summer vacation and still looking for a reliable cat sitter for your feline friends?

NYC animal care & handling certified / experienced cat sitter in Jackson Heights. I make daily visit to feed your cats, play, keep them company, and maintain clean litter. My goal is to make sure your cat’s needs are met along with meeting the cat owner’s expectations.

1 hour minimum of fun, companionship and care.
-Clean bowls before feeding
-Change water during every visit
-Waste removal and tidy up area around litter box
-Sent text or email with photos for updates (I’m also a freelance photographer!)
Additional services include watering indoor plants and collecting mail

Proof of certification and references available upon request.

Please email fumithecatlady@gmail.com for additional information and for scheduling a Meet and Greet. Meet and Greet will be scheduled in your house to get to know you and your cats better. I look forward to meeting you and your kitties!
