Double Charged Sales Tax at Downtown Market
« on: May 28, 2016, 04:37:24 PM »
I just came from Downtown Market, where they double charged me for sales tax. I've attached a copy of the receipt. It shows where they marked off the second line of sales tax after I complained.

Given that this is a fully automated transaction--the sales clerk simply scanned the item and this was the total the computer system generated--it's hard not to believe that their systems are seriously flawed, at the very least.

I recommend everyone check their receipts from that store very carefully.
