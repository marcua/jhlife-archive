1st Queens Bike FOOD Tour
« on: August 10, 2015, 10:57:15 PM »
Queens Bike Initiative is launching a series of bike tours in our area.
Once a month, from now to early winter, we'll visit parks and stop to taste some of the best food in Queens (meaning: in the world) along the way. The first ride will happen Aug. 29. We'll go to Astoria, Ditmars and East Elmhurst. It's free and everyone is welcome to join!
Register here: https://www.facebook.com/events/868931326519300/868931333185966/


1st Queens Bike FOOD Tour

Join us in exploring some of Astoria's diverse and exciting food and drink destinations! We will meet at the NW corner of Travers Park at 10.30am. On this leisurely bike tour, we'll make a few stops at bakeries and cafes to pick up supplies for a break at Astoria Park. We'll bike and eat delicious food in Astoria, Ditmars and East Elmhurst, back in Jackson Heights at around 1pm.

While we're on it, please sign our petition for a bike lane grid connecting in Queens: http://www.qns.bike/petition
