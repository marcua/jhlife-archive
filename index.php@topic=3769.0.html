aside from sunset park, i cannot imagine a neighborhood with better mexican options.

my favorite, and one that draws visitors from all around the tri-state area (see any food-centric message board out there) is taqueria coatzingo on roosevelt between 76th and 77th. in house, i'd recommend just about any special of the day (if you have dietary restrictions, ask) and for delivery/take out anything with carnitas or al pastor is delicious. love the tortas and cemitas.

el poblano one block closer to the train station is also good...but their al pastor is iffy. i tend to like the stuff at sol de azteca on roosevelt closer to 80th a lot as well...

for a quick bite, the array of carts at roosevelt and 75th are all ace....as is the tamale lady on the southeast corner. none of these are particularly fancy and none serve americanized mexican, but the food is great at all.
