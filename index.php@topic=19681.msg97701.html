I have a few tips.  I went to the DMV in Whitestone on Yom Kippur and the place was packed with people with school age kids because  the schools were closed that day.  I waited over two hours to renew my license so  don't go on a day when the schools are closed.  I also went  in the fall before undocumented immigrants were able to get  driver's licenses so I am sure the place will be more crowded now. 

If  you want to get a REAL ID so you can board a plane come October 2020 make sure you have proper documentation.  I was unable to locate enough documents that morning and couldn't get a  REAL ID and  my driver's license displays "not for federal purposes".  During the holidays I was stopped by the police in CT because I had a break light  out and the officer quipped, "not for federal purposes you don't look like an illegal alien" and then laughed.  I think it is  going to be clear that anyone who looks like they are foreign born and doesn't have a REAL ID license will be suspected of being in the  country illegally without a REAL ID driver's license.
