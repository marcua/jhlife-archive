Title: Re: How's zipcar compared to other car rental options in JH area?
Post by: M7X7 on June 19, 2017, 01:40:59 PM
I recently signed up for Zipcar for similar reasons.

It's much more convenient but generally more expensive than renting from the airport. They are kept at the garage over the Rite Aid on 82nd and 37th. You walk in, tell them what car you reserved, and are out in less than 5 minutes. Gas and insurance are included. There is also the flexibility of renting for only an hour or two. On the negative side, you always have to return it to the same location, and if you're going for a long time (e.g. overnight) the hourly rate adds up to a significantly worse deal than you can usually get from regular rental places. I probably wouldn't use it for a road trip for those reasons.
