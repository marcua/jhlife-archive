Restaurants & Food / Re: Weekend bodega tacos, San Antonio Farm - 86th/37th
« on: April 11, 2018, 07:59:51 AM »
The tacos at the bodega are a real hidden neighborhood gem and better than most others in the neighborhood. Every time I have had they have had eight so different choices as well as great sides and really great salsas and sauces that taste homemade. Their food is thoughtful and the flavors make you want to eat few and order another. If anyone has watched the taco episode of Netflix's Ugly Delicious, their tacos hit three of the four points of a great taco place laid out early in the episode.
