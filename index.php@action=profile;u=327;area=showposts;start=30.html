Restaurants & Food / Re: Taste Good in Elmhurst. Gone?
« on: May 28, 2009, 11:13:42 PM »
Tomorrow is another day and Taste Good is not closed. Try the stuffed Tofu curry noodle, get the wide flat rice noodle, it absorbs the rich coconut curry brooth. The chunks of tofu are stuffed with minced pork, along with piece of stuffed eggplant and a long green pepper.A nice starter is the roti canai.  Get a small owl of their coconut rice, It is a dish by itself. Another good soup place is Lao Bei Fan, translates as old style dumpling house. Get the beef and hand drawn noodle soup, a food lovers delight fir under 5.00. They do the best fresh pan fried pork dumplings. 4 for 1.25, my kids live in them.

Neighborhood Chat / Re: Atlas Park Mall in Glendale near Trader Joes
« on: April 30, 2009, 11:57:40 AM »
I used to go to Atlas Park a lot when it first opened. My 2 kids went to  public elementary school in Forest Hills and it was a nice place to go in the warm weather. They have a the BEST  water fountain play area in all of Queens for young kids. There is a Johnny Rockets and a good movie theatre. There is plenty of street parking if you look. It is a strange place, very out of character for Queens but a lovely place to go when the weather is warm.
