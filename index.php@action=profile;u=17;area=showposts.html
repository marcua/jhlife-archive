Restaurants & Food / Re: Coffee shop on 77 street - lousy hot cup of tea
« on: May 17, 2010, 12:42:45 PM »
Interesting you mention that the water needs to be boiling.  As an experienced tea drinker, as you mentioned before, you would know that each tea needs a difference temp to reach it's full flavor.   For example, Black Teas  seep for 3-5 min. in 176-203 F and for Green Teas it depends if it is Chinese or Japanese. 158-180 F Early picking Chinese green teas should be infused for 2-3 minutes. Japanese Gyokuro should be infused for 1-2 minutes at 140-150 F.

I go to e77 not only for an excellent cup of coffee and an awesome espresso 77 bar, but also because the owners are always welcoming.  They are a community driven business who cares for their clients. 

Try taking the time to Julie or Afzul, I am sure you will come away with a different opinion. 

I, for one, am glad they are here!!!
