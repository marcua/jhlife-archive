Restaurants & Food / Re: Best Ecuadorian Food
« on: June 07, 2013, 01:04:46 PM »
You guys are so multicultural its a beautiful thang. My mama is Ecuadorian and I have to say there are a few spots but I would say the following are my go to spots.

Picada Azuaya on 37th and 85th I think - very good hole in the wall place (good food, although late night music is loud)
Pequeno Coffee Shop - This is on I think 86th and Roosevelt Ave is very very good. Its literally an Ecuadorian Diner.  I think that's why it might have not been mentioned but this might be the best place all around.  Interior design and wait staff are good.  Good for like a Sunday Lunch.
Barzola  on 92nd and 37th I think- Its a more uppity casual experience but also has good food.  Nice for a date or weekend thing.
