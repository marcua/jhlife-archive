Anzuelo Fino - Peruvian on Northern/86th http://www.eatingintranslation.com/2009/01/el-anzuelo-fino.html

Hi Iveta - How does it work?  Every month we pick a place in the area to go out to eat. The places are always authentic and sometimes adventurous, but the point of it is just to meet people and discover new things. It usually ends up being around 10 people, some are regulars, and every time new people show up. Most are from the area, but we have also had people from brooklyn, manhattan and jersey. We've never gone to a place where we've needed reservations, part of the discovery is that these gems are in our backyard and we can walk right in.
We will settle on a date by the start of the month, I'm thinking either the 7th or 21st. What works best for you?

So yes, consider yourself joined. Hopefully you can come and meet some people from the hood. I'll put in your vote for peruvian.
