Title: Farm Spot Winter Share Sign-ups
Post by: civita on November 14, 2011, 09:43:53 AM
Farm Spot is now signing up members for our Winter Share.

*Deadline for signing up is November 23*

click here to sign up: http://csa.farmigo.com/join/farmspot/winter2012

If you have any questions, email us at farmspot@gmail

WINTER SHARE BASIC INFORMATION
- The Winter Share has a total of 5 deliveries. Distributions will take place every 3 weeks, on Thursdays at St. Mark's Church 5:30pm - 8:15pm on December 15, January 5, January 26, February 16 and March 8.
- In addition to root vegetables, there will be some greens included in the share this year, which will be grown in-ground in the farm's greenhouses.  They hope to have greens in at least 4 of the 5 shares. 
- We ask each share to help out with distribution once during the Winter season (for a 2-hour shift).
 
WINTER VEGETABLE SHARE ($155) [from Golden Earthworm Organic Farm]
Approximately 15-18lbs of mixed root crops and greens which may include any of the following: Sweet Potatoes, Carrots, Beets, Potatoes, Watermelon Radish, Kale, Spinach & Arugula and maybe some other surprises...
 
WINTER APPLE SHARE ($23) [from Briermere Farms]
3lbs mixed apple varieties per distribution
 
WINTER CIDER SHARE ($20)
1/2 gallon fresh-pressed cider per distribution
