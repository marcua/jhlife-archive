** NYS Assembly Candidate Ari Espinal staff defacing doors in neighborhood **

My family and I live in the Montclair Gardens co-op (3535 75th Street) in Jackson Heights. We came home yesterday to XXs marked in pen next to our door. Registered democrats in our building have the same XX marks. Attached are photos. We have seen several political canvassers come and go from our building over the past few weeks. However, the fact that Ari's team-members have blatantly defaced our walls is unacceptable. We know the XXs came from Ari's campaign, because Ari's flyer was slipped under our door with a note written in the same pen color and handwriting as the defacement on our doors. We can only assume the XX marks are to aide Ari’s canvassers when they enter our building.

The timing of this incidence is particularly upsetting because our building just completed painting all interior walls, a project that lasted several months and cost tens of thousands of money (I know because I am on our co-op's Board).

We won’t give our vote to someone who disrespects our community and our homes!

I'm sending this message so you don't suffer the same fate and can be on the lookout for Ari's canvassers.
