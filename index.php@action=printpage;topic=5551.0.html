Title: EMERGING ARTISTS in Jackson Heights
Post by: balducci on November 17, 2010, 11:52:39 AM
Restaurants have been stepping up in showcasing and displaying works by local artists
NOVO LOUNGE (37th ave & 79th ST)
 has been having openings since February and the NEXT ONE IS THURSDAY NOV 18th from 5-9pm -
everyone is welcome - come out for a cocktail and make some new friends
artists can give their info to be featured at the restaurant inthe coming months- ask for Celeste
also check out there JAZZ MONDAYS with Jim Hershman Trio

Espresso 77 has openings and music and meetings for local artists too-

Lety's recently included a local artist in the renovated bakery, which has the best cakes in JH...

Elliott Bassman has an ongoing series at MARAM Pharmacy

and Carla Lobmier has a wonderful painting in the display window at ULTIMA FLORAL on 81st - south of 37th ave- check it out.

please visit my blog for more info and highlights: communitycommunicato.blogspot.com

we have alot of talent here across the arts and would like to include yours too!

see you on the avenue! Celeste
