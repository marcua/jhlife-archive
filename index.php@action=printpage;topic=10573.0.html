Title: Pre-Ballet for 3-6 year olds
Post by: jeanette on January 04, 2014, 11:19:59 PM
Mimi For Me Yoga is offering a once-weekly pre-ballet  class for the littlest dancers aged 3 to 6. Pre-ballet training introduces children to the basic requirements they must meet in order to learn the art of classical ballet. I'm the instructor. Sans barre, I focus on pointing, posture, spotting, turn-out, gracefulness, counting music, and French dance vocabulary. Methodology is inclusionary games, exercises, repetition, visual aids, and mimicry. The goal is to support and encourage children to step up to the barre with confidence and become enthusiastic dancers.
The 12-week course is being held on Fridays at 4:30 p.m. First class is January 10, 2014. Classes are small, instruction is hands-on, price is $175 ($14.50/per class).   To register, please contact the yoga studio at 718-505-YOGA (info below).
