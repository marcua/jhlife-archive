Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Neighborhood Chat / Re: Another one bites the dust!!!!!!!!!!!!
« on: February 12, 2011, 01:07:53 PM »
Europa Art Gallery was a great place to have in the neighborhood and I find it devastating that a butcher shop (of all things!) is supposedly going to open up after they're gone. I had the guys at Europa frame a few of my pictures. They did a beautiful job and gave me a bargain on the price while being super nice. The store manager David Fan, as well as the rest of the staff are the nicest customer service you could ask for. If only there was the right clientele in the area (artists and art lovers, where are you!), Europa would thrive. They were capable of getting beautiful artwork, if you found anything you liked in the catalagoue. Unfortunately, in order to try to cater to local tastes, their window display didn't really present the more sophisticated stuff you could find inside. It is a great loss for the neighborhood to bid farewell to these guys. I'm sure if a cheap store or a hispanic diner opens us, they will be very successful... Good night and good luck.
