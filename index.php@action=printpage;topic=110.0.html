Title: DSNY Leaf Collection Day - 12/1
Post by: toddg on November 27, 2007, 02:09:30 PM
In fall 2007, it will be mandatory (according to NYC's Yard Waste Composting Law) for NYC residents to set out their fall leaves in paper bags (or in unlined rigid containers) during the designated DSNY fall leaf collection period.

In Queens, Leaf Collection Day is December 1. 

For more information, please visit:
http://www.nyc.gov/html/nycwasteless/html/recycling/fall_leaf_program.shtml
