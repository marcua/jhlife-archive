As a kid, my parents would always take the family to Orchard Beach in the Bronx.  My dad would buy a large block of ice, one of those ice scraper thingies, and my mom would make a syrup from Tamarind seeds to pour over the scraped ice.  We'd head on over to the beach, and while my dad was preparing our Piraguas (snow cones), people would start lining up wanting to buy them.  He'd end up just giving them away to the kids.  That ice didn't last very long, and it certainly wasn't because it was melting under the hot sun!  We'd follow-up our beach outing with a side trip to City Island for dinner.

Favorites of mine as a kid:  Coney Island (for the rides, of course) and like Buddy, Seaside Heights.

Nowadays, we don't do the beach that often, but my last trip was to Jones Beach.  We're more the outdoor BBQ, State Park kind of folk.  And, while on the subject of Jones Beach, has anyone ever been to a concert there?
