Many of you have taking the time to critic me, for posting rentals in this forum. However, not everyone is luck to have purchase a Co-Op or a Condo apartment many years ago, and no longer has to pay a mortgage.  Or they move to Jackson Heights a long time ago and donot have to pay the rents that landlords are currently charging.

The reality of the rental market in Jackson Heights is that many people are being completely priced out and can no longer afford to live in this great neighborhood.

By posting my rentals in JHL, I try to give locals the opportunity to find out about upcoming apartments before I posted them to the mass on other online channels. I am proud to say that over the years, using JHL I have help a number of people find affordable, rentals and sometimes some great deals, in Jackson Heights.

Over the years, I have sold so many coops and condos in Queens that I am no longer interested in dealing with that segment of the real estate market. When I get calls from Coop's and Condo's owners interested in selling, I pass those calls to other well known, Broker's in Jackson Heights, without expecting a referral fee in return. I only focus on rentals and sales of houses and multifamily buildings.
