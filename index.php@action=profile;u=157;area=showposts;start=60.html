Restaurants & Food / Re: New pizza place in the hood!
« on: January 03, 2011, 08:35:42 PM »
I love pizza.  I haven't tried Louie's yet and am looking forward to eventually getting there.  Because I have a car I'll drive to Astoria for my two favorite pizza places: Rizzo on Steinway and Porto Bella in the small shopping plaza on Ditmars where the Matrix gym is.

I belonged to Matrix and would get a slice to "reward" myself after every work out.  Not the smartest strategy but they have delicious pizza and other dishes.

I would love to find a place right here in JHs that I liked as much as either of them.  Rosa is, for me, a bit too cheesy.  And the few on 37th Ave. are all okay but they don't knock my socks off.  Rizzo and Porto Bella definitely do.
