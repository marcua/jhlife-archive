Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Did you know that Minnesota is the land of 10,000 lakes?...exactly, WHO CARES!!  Whether you know that nonsense or not, it is always fun to brag at holiday parties about the stuff you know.  It is perfect for changing the subject and making co-workers feel inferior and/or uncomfortable.  Seriously though, Trivia Night at the Ready Penny Inn is happening tomorrow night, Wednesday, November 23rd!  Celebrate the beginning of Thanksgiving and the end of the work week by showing off your tremendous thinking and drinking abilities with your JH neighbors, as well as guests from across the river!

If you haven't experienced Trivia Night, we'd love to see you for the first time.  If you have experienced Trivia Night, come back and join us!  The RP is a great local with a fantastic bartender (Linda), great drinks, and you can order food from the neighborhood right to your stool or table!!  What's not to love?!!

The details:
Wednesday, Novermber 23, 2011
8:00pm
the Ready Penny Inn -- 73rd St btwn Broadway and 37th Ave
Prizes for 1st and 2nd place, as well as "Best Team Name".  And, of course, the lovely Linda will be there to offer drink specials for everyone!

See You There...
