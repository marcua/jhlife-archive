Post by: jher on April 17, 2008, 08:48:48 AM
I am a member of ZipCar.  They rent cars by the hour or by the day.  The cars are left at a local parking lot and after you order it online, you just go pick it up and put it back when you are finished with it.  I am posting this because the closest ZipCar lot to JH is at 47th street and Queens Blvd.  I have written to them about putting some cars in JH but so far nothing has happened.  It is a good service for someone without a car or if you occasionally need a large car for shopping or a delivery.  Please look at their site and ask them to put some cars in JH.  They have many different types of cars, from Minis, to BMWs and large Hondas and Fords.  Gas is included too.  (I have nothing to do with ZipCar other than I want some closer to where I live...on 35th and 74th. )

Here is a response I received from ZipCar.  It takes from 20-25 members to support a car in a given area and they keep track of the number of requests if you add your email at http://www.zipcar.com/notify-me (http://www.zipcar.com/notify-me)


"Thank you for your email. We are always looking for new areas that will benefit from our service. The best way to let us know your neighborhood is ready for Zipcar is to have your friends and neighbors add their address to our "notify me page" http://www.zipcar.com/notify-me. We use this information as a guide to place our next fleet of cars. Generally, it takes 20-25 members to support a car in a given area so spread the word! Enjoy the rest of your day.

If you have any questions please feel free to contact member services at info@zipcar.com or by phoning 1.866.494.7227.

If you have any other questions or concerns, please contact us at the links below.

Regards,
Darlene
Zipcar Member Services

members visit www.zipcar.com/help
or nonmembers www.zipcar.com/how

PS. Members are also able to submit web inquiries from the "Help" page via the "Contact Us" screen http://www.zipcar.com/help/contact "
