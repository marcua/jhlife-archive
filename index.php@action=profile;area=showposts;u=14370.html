Neighborhood Chat / Re: Time Warner Monopoly
« on: February 09, 2012, 12:37:44 PM »
It may be some work on your part, but if you get the whole building involved, then they should be able to allow other providers. We were all going back and forth for a year about getting fios, and finally the building made adjustments so we were eligible.

My building is offering Verizon Fios now, in addition to Time Warner ,and I switched right away! I used to have 2 cable boxes, 4 premium channels, internet and phone and was paying $187. I have the same package now, and I pay $132 woo! After 2 years, price goes up to $145. Must tell you, I love Fios. My internet is way faster and the picture looks clearer. The only thing I miss is NY1 because I used to watch that a lot to know what was going on in NYC, but eh saving $50 wins that battle.

Good luck! I hope you get to switch soon!
