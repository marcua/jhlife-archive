Hi folks,

I started working out with Kiki after I gave birth to both my girls. Years ago, I didn't imagine it was even possible to get into good shape after all that ordeal...now I know it is!!!!

Kiki Kwesi Morris comes all the way from Brooklyn to give us great, outdoors workouts in Travers Park!
It's a great community activity. We work out together, no issue if people are on different levels, and all sizes, shapes, and bodies are absolutely welcome!!! we bring our kids and they play in the park or join us, Kiki supervises them at times so it's a win-win situation for everyone!
