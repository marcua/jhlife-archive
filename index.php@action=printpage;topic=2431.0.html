Title: Re: I need a new chinese restaurant
Post by: meigui on March 28, 2009, 04:06:17 PM
Forget JH for anything other than "Take Out" style. Get yourself over to Elmhurst, it is considered one of NYC 5  Asian communities. Broadway Seafood, just past Whitney Ave, is traditional, with a HUGE dim sum. The whole stretch of Broadway starting from Elmhurst hospital all the way up to Queens Blvd. is a gold mine of various regional Chinese restaurants. Some have limited English but you can still get by. For the full experience go to the buffet at East on Albion Avenue and Queens Blvd. 8.99 during the week for lunch and you will be astounded.
