Title: Re: Tree disposal
Post by: Chuckster on December 22, 2007, 04:28:44 PM
We've been instructed by our superintendent to take the trees down to the curb by the designated garbage area.  Our super usually informs the residents ahead of time of the official pick-up day by the Sanitation Department for recycling purposes.  No bagged trees.

The Park's Department also holds an annual Mulchfest.  I believe Travers Park may be a designated location where people can drop off their trees for chipping.  More info here:

http://nycgovparks.org/services/mulchfest/mulchfest.html

This info is copied and pasted from another list:

The following are two options for recycling your holiday tree:

1. The NYC Dept of Sanitation is picking up trees curbside from Jan 3 through Jan 16 for
citywide composting. The compost will be used for city trees, parks, etc.

http://www.nyc.gov/html/dsny/html/pr2007/122407.shtml

2. Travers Park is participating in the NYC Parks Dept Mulchfest on Jan 5th and 6th from
10am to 2pm. And you have the option to take the mulch from your tree home with you.

http://nycgovparks.org/services/mulchfest/mulchfest.html#top

For more information, see the above links.
