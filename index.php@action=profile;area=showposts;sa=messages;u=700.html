It's either called Stone Computer or Stone PC on Northern Boulevard and 67th ish just on the other side of the BQE...
We brought our desktop unit, a HP Pavilion, to Stone last year after it died. (Note the techie term ). They could have told us that it was anything & we would have not known the difference. Instead, their initial diagnosis (given while we waited) was a power supply problem that would cost ~ $100 to repair. They said that they would check it further & let us know if anything more was involved. We called the next day & got a very happy surprise. The PC was already repaired & the cost was actually less than the estimate, around $80 or so. I don't know about their new equipment pricing but I do know where I'll go with the next problem.
