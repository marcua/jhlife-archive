Title: Free E-Recycling, Saturday 9/13
Post by: acasey103 on September 10, 2014, 09:19:48 AM
Assemblyman DenDekker will host a free e-recycling event on Saturday, September 13 from 10:00 AM – 4:00 PM at the Jackson Heights Shopping Center (77th St between 30th and 31st Avenues). Members of the community are invited to safely dispose of unwanted and/or broken electronics, including: working & non-working computers, monitors, printers, scanners, keyboards, mice, cables/cords, TVs, VCRs, DVD Players, phones, audio/visual equipment, cell phones, & PDAs.

   For more information, please contact Lower East Side Ecology Center at (212) 477-4022, www.lesecologycenter.org, or Assemblyman DenDekker’s District Office at (718) 457-0384.
