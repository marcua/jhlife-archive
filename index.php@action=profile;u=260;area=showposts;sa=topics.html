Community Groups, Activities & Events / Bake Sale! UNITED CAKES: JACKSON HEIGHTS KIDS FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE
« on: November 15, 2016, 03:38:56 PM »
UNITED CAKES: JACKSON HEIGHTS KIDS FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE
A Bake Sale to support our local immigrant communities

When: THIS WEEKEND! Saturday, November 19 and Sunday, November 20, 10 am – 3 pm
Where: Travers Park Play Street (78th between 34th Ave. and Northern Blvd.)

In the wake of a presidential election that resulted in a real threat to our predominantly
immigrant community, Jackson Heights kids and their families are organizing a
fundraising event to bring together and support all members of our neighborhood.

This weekend, we are gathering at Travers Park Play Street for UNITED CAKES:
JACKSON HEIGHTS KIDS FOR SOCIAL JUSTICE. This “bake-sale-plus”
will feature donated, homebaked goods for sale, plus other arts and awareness activities
to raise money for local charities supporting the many immigrant  families that make our
neighborhood vibrant, rich, and one of the most diverse in the  country.

All proceeds from the bake sale will benefit two outstanding local organizations that
serve and protect our immigrant population:

New Immigrant Community Empowerment (NICE), which protects and assists
immigrant laborers through direct outreach and public advocacy,

and

Chhaya CDC, which supports New York City’s South Asian community
through housing advocacy, community organizing, and civic engagement.

Please come out this weekend and support this worthy cause!

If you wish to provide baked goods or volunteer for the event, please DM JulesNYC here
and I will put you in touch with the organizers.
