Title: Chinese For Kids
Post by: shazza09 on September 12, 2018, 10:23:15 PM
All children in and around Jackson Heights (ages 2-7) are invited to come along to a free trial Mandarin class this Saturday morning.
We meet from 10-11:15am at the Community United Methodist Church, 81-10 35th Ave., in Room #4.
Your child will have lots of fun learning Mandarin with songs, moving to music, playing games and making crafts.
No prior knowledge of Mandarin is needed, so join us on Saturday.
Spaces are limited so please register your child/children, by emailing creativechinese1@gmail.com
