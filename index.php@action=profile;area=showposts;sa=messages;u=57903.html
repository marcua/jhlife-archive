Community Groups, Activities & Events / FESTIVAL DE LAS FLORES PARADE

THIS JULY 12TH THE FESTIVAL DE LAS FLORES PARADE IS COMING TO JACKSON HEIGHTS!

The parade is a celebration of the Colombian tradition of the "silleteros" and their traditional flower arrangements called "silletas".

The parade will travel down 37th avenue between 69th street and 86th street and will begin at 12pm and end at 2pm.

Come witness these beautiful medallion flower arrangements, dance groups performing their best routines, floats, and much much more.

HOPE TO SEE YOU THERE!

For more info; Like Us on Facebook @ Festival De Las Flores US, Follow Us on Instagram and Twitter @Festival_Flores.

www.festivaldelasfloresus.com

Hi everyone! The Festival de las Flores is a celebration of the Colombian "silleteros" who carry large medallion flower arrangements on their backs. The parade will include dance groups, floats decorated with many flowers, and of course the silleteros and their "silletas".

The parade will be this July 12th between 12pm and 2pm on 37th ave between 69th street and 86th street.

After the parade, we will continue the celebration at the Queens Botanical Garden where we will have food and craft vendors and special performances.

For more info and ticket purchases for this special event, visit www.festivaldelasfloresus.com or Visit Us on Facebook: Festival De Las Flores US
