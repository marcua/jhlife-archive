Neighborhood Chat / DiBlasio's Education Forum last night
« on: November 13, 2015, 07:01:47 AM »
 Mayor DiBlasio brought an entourage last night to PS69 and they were ready for the onslaught of questioning by residents.
He seemed genuine as did Carmen Farina who is a powerhouse of info. People were asked to make their questions tough
and they did. They were answered with courtesy, I thought, and a real interest in solutions. Overcrowding was a big issue
and one not easily solved. Time for parents to meet teachers to discuss their students education plans seemed an easier fix.
  Younger teachers expressed a new appreciation for the profession which may have been shown only to them as teachers
with more time in the system privately disagreed. Huge increase in UPK seats here was noted and all in favor of the reading
on grade level by 3rd grade mandate. Wished for more discussion of any sort about High Schools but maybe that happened
after we left.
  Overall, it was impressive that DiBlasio would make the effort and take the heat. Will remember this at voting time and
see what improvements have been made. We do have some great elected here right now and hope they stick around.
