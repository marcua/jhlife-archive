Restaurants & Food / Caviar
« on: March 13, 2017, 09:09:44 PM »
The delivery app, not the product (sorry). Just wanted to pass along my recommendation. I've used it twice and been pretty impressed. It's a little bit more expensive--they have their own delivery people and charge a small fee. But it gives us access to a whole wider set of good restaurants outside of the neighborhood.  They deliver to here from a ton of Astoria restaurants (Greek--including MP Taverna and Zenon, Italian, Vietnamese, gastropub, hipster comfort food, etc) and they just added JoJu and JoJu Bowl. They also have a few Jackson Heights restaurants, but there really isn't a need to use this service for those.

Anyway it's a well-selected (I hate the overuse of the word curate) group of restaurants, the app is well-designed and the two times I've used it the food showed up quickly, and before the estimate.
