Title: 10/11 Free Film Screening: The Naked Civil Servant
Post by: causidicus on October 08, 2011, 04:10:26 PM
Council Member Daniel Dromm invites you to celebrate--

NATIONAL COMING OUT DAY

With a Free Film Screening of

THE NAKED CIVIL SERVANT

Tuesday, October 11, 2011
7:00 PM

Jewish Center of Jackson Heights
3706 77th Street
Jackson Heights, NY 11372

John Hurt collected a Best Actor BAFTA for his flawless portrayal of gay icon Quentin Crisp, a flamboyant performer and pop-culture personality who railed against the rigid social mores of pre-World War II Britain, where homosexuality was illegal. John Rhys-Davies also stars in this bittersweet made-for-TV movie, which is based on Crisp's autobiography of the same name and caused considerable controversy when it first aired in 1975.

Running time: 77 minutes
