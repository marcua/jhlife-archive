We were disappointed that Fruitel Farms (the fruit stand on 37th and 78th) was not selling trees again this year as we got an awesome 7 foot tree for $40 there last year. So this year we went to a guy selling trees at the drug store (a Duane Reade?) on Northern and 85th. Not many trees over 6 feet and they range from $40-$60 depending on the type and quality. The tree we got is about 6 feet, great shape and fullness, with blue undertones. I would recommend checking them out. The vendor is Canadian and he was a hoot to buy from.

We bought a tree from outside the gas station on 90th and Roosevelt this morning. $20 for 6 foot. They have about three types of trees, I think Douglas Fir hold up the best with radiator heat.

The guys on 80th and 37th seemed kind of kooky. Not the French Canadian buskers you see in Manhattan and Astoria at all...we joked that they had a deal with the mob, if they could unload the truck load of trees, their next job would be unloading a truckload of iPods!! They know nothing about trees, but were fun to talk to. The French Canadians who run the City, usually camp out, make friends, and take their loot to busk and relax in Mexico after Christmas. I miss my old guys....
