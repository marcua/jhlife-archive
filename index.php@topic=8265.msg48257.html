Annual Towers Flea Market in Historic Jackson Heights, Saturday, May 11, 2013- 9AM to 4 PM
The Towers is having its annual flea market! The flea market is comprised of donations from current Towers residents and vendors. A diverse array of gently used, new, and vintage items including books, decorative items, bric-a-brac, linens, clothing, prints, gift ware, home décor, kitchenware, frames, estate items, glassware, jewelry, trinkets, crafts, curios, ...and much more.  In addition there are tables for children's items including clothing, toys, baby gear and child care paraphernalia.   Please join us Saturday, May 11, 9AM to 4 PM  at the Towers, on 34th Avenue between 80th & 81st Streets in Historic Jackson Heights [Queens, 11372].  Looking forward to seeing you there!
