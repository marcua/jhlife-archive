Restaurants & Food / Jax Inn - an appreciation
« on: September 14, 2018, 03:05:02 PM »
we're really lucky to have a wide array of terrific Asian and Latinx restaurants in this area. and the opening/near opening of places like the Queensboro and Eloise should offer some upscale diversion. but there's something to be said for a really good diner, and Jax Inn is one of the better ones. no, it's not instagram fodder, but morning to late night, the place offers terrific comfort food -- pancakes, burgers actually cooked the way they're ordered, parm platters that provide two meals for even a hearty eater -- and reliable delivery. my partner and i are both big fans.

Neighborhood Chat / thumbs up for InstaMed
« on: September 25, 2017, 03:25:49 PM »
i hate going to urgent care places as a rule, but i had a nasty slip and fall at Queens Center yesterday and decided to sleep on it before seeking an opinion. having sworn off City MD after two really lousy experiences, i gave this place, on Northern, a try. was seen quickly, treated pleasantly and given all the info i needed, by a doctor with good bedside manner. in and out in 45 minutes or so, versus 90 minutes to be shown to an exam room at City MD. highly recommend, if needed.
