One of the few downsides of living within the historic district boundaries is the spotty cell phone service. Since landmark buildings can't have cell phone antennas placed on them, the area is a bit of a dead zone for signals. I'm happy to get 2 bars when standing near a window inside my apartment (I have T-Mobile). For home, I'd highly recommend a land line or VoIP service via your cable provider.
