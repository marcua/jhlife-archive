Queens Health Care Fair for artists Nov 29 6-8:30pm

(1/1)

Shelby2:
Thursday, Nov 29 from 6-8:30pm

Please join Queens Council on the Arts for a Free Health Care Fair for Artists. Health Care Services providers and administrators will discuss various affordable programs and novel solutions available to visual and performing artists.  All artists and arts organizations are encouraged to attend.
 
The event will offer:
- An overview on affordable health programs available to artists in the NY metro area
- Invaluable information on novel and innovative health care options for artists
- Advice from artists enrolled in programs will offer their experience
- An individual consultation at the end of the panel presentation
- A networking opportunity
 
LOCATION:
Kew Gardens Community Center
80-02 Kew Gardens Road, Suite 202, Kew Gardens, NY 11415
 
REGISTRATION
Email your name, address telephone number and artistic discipline to register@queenscouncilarts.org or call Elise 718-647-3377 ext. 17.
 
Admission is Free. Registration is required.
Space is limited.
