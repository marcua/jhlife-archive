I've found that the best way to deal with things like this is in writing.  Return receipt requested. With copies to everyone.  It's much harder to dismiss a paper trail.

the other thing that usually works is a show of numbers: a petition signed by as many people on the block as you can get to sign and print their names, addresses etc.

Paper trails usually make bureaucrats pay attention.  it's a pain to do because it's time consuming but putting things in writing usually works.
