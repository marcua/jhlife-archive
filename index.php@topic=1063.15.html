Just got back from Timmy O's Frozen Custard in Corona.  Wow!  What an amazing treat.  The custard is a smooth as butter.  It is so rich and creamy.  Tim makes everything from scratch each day with natural ingredients (no high fructose corn syrup hurray!).  I had the yummy banana custard with pecans on top.  My fiance had vanilla custard with peanuts.  The owner is so nice and entertaining.  We wish him all the best.  Thanks for posting.  I would have never known about this place.
