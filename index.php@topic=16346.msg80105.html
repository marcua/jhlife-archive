Best way I can contribute here would be to remain as factual as I can. As a rule most brokers do not release the actual contract price , or are too vocal about listings under contract, because we are probably too busy holding our breath and praying hard that the deal comes to a close as intended  . Deals that pioneer the way of new pricing are truly fragile, as they have a greater margin of error to make it to the closing table.
