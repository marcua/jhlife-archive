This site is for pigeon LOVERS!!!

I've been having a real pigeon problem too - they nested on and around my A/C unit, and are SO loud.  not to mention the LOADS of pooh they got all over the window, outside wall, and unit. 

So, my super very nicely put a nail-studded board under the unit, which limited the nesting, but they still come. so this weekend I dedicated some serious time to cleaning the area.  I sprayed windex like mad, scrubbed everything down, and spent the rest of the day banging on the window every time one landed near it...  This morning, no birds cooing.  I know it's not a permanent fix, and I don't know if they're just scared because I spent the better part of a day pounding their former home, or the smell of the large amounts of windex has turned them off, but I'm hoping they decide to relocate.
