Reckless Drivers
« on: February 24, 2016, 05:51:45 PM »
Since moving to JH over 6 years ago, I've had to adjust the way I cross the road (in the pedestrian crosswalk). Even though the lights say I can walk, I'm constantly looking around for that one car whose driver doesn't want to wait for people walking. And I'm constantly fed up with pedestrians not crossing when they're able to, simply because they think they have to wait for a car that's making a turn as fast as they can.

Tonight my BF was almost hit by a young girl driving recklessly -- she made a turn into the crosswalk and only missed him because I pulled him back. I took down her license plate and called 311 to report to the Police, but what else can we do here?

I'm so sick and tired of reckless drivers. I'm sick of having to speed up my walking or jump out of the way of oncoming vehicles, even when it is MY turn to walk across the road.

Does anyone else have this problem?!
