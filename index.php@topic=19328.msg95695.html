This is not traditional police work. It's done undercover. Once a bust is made, the agent(s) have to be reassigned. First they make a bust some place, make a deal, and place the informant somewhere. Then step back. It's not a one step operation and it's dangerous work. Press, politicians, etc will only slow down progress, if any.
