Neighborhood Chat / Re: summer night noise
« on: June 28, 2008, 02:24:09 PM »
Street noise (Boom Cars, horns, huge trucks) can be really bad around here. It's normal to get some noise in a city but it becomes a real problem when those noises sound very close, when they're in your face. One of the reasons of that is that the streets are narrow here (not meant to get that kind of traffic), with six story buildings on each side. Acoustically speaking, it sounds twice as loud.
Anyway, our solution for this problem was to put soundproof windows in the bedroom. IT WORKS!
They're not cheap: $750 each. But for me $1500 it's worth it to be able to have a quiet room where you can sleep in peace. It's a great investment.
