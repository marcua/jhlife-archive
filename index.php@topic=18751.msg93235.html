Just wanted to chime in here to say we visited the new ERT, and it's truly excellent.

The menu is the same as the old location, and the food is fresh, delicious, and beautifully presented. They definitely need a sign and some things on the wall, but the new space is very sleek and spacious, lots of potential.

We had fantastic guacamole, the lovely kale and mango salad, and huevos rancheros. The prices are extremely reasonable, especially for such exceptionally high quality. And the service was friendly and attentive as always.

PLEASE GO! There was only one other occupied table when I was there, and it was prime brunch time. I'm worried they won't make it if word does not spread and people do not start visiting the new location. I will definitely be back soon, but spread the word and visit often.

ERT is really a neighborhood treasure, and we're so lucky to have it back so soon after we thought it was gone forever.
