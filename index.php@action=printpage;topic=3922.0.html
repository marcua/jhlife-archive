It's for a good cause!  Proceeds go to City Harvest.    
 
About the 4th Bin:
The 4th Bin is dedicated to educating businesses and consumers about the hazards of electronic waste (e-waste) and providing them with a simple and inexpensive means of rescue and disposal. Our goals are to rescue of all forms of e-waste, ethically and responsibly recycle, or reuse, the equipment and give back to our local communities.
