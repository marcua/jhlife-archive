Re: JH Post Office - Understaffed, Poorly Managed, or Both?
« Reply #5 on: October 23, 2016, 03:33:41 PM »
JHResident and dssjh are correct.

In my experience, the JH Post Office is one of the better ones in NYC, particularly after they installed the self-service machines. I'm usually in and out in a flash because of them. If I have a large package, I still use the machine - without issue - and walk straight up to the window and drop it off. 

My only complaint would be that they should have a separate line for picking up packages. Other than that, the regular line moves rather quickly. To be honest, I would say the vast majority of the time, it's usually the customers' fault for holding it up. When you go up to the window, your package(s) should be 100% ready and you should generally know what you want ahead of time.
