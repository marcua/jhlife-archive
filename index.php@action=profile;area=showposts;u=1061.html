Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Restaurants & Food / Re: New Mi Tierra Supermarket at 81st & Northern
« on: June 14, 2011, 07:27:14 PM »
So far, so good! What a pleasure to have a nabe supmkt. we can walk to, once again.

Produce is amazing - I hope people will buy some of their organics so they keep providing it - organic carrots were a good deal, as was organic celery.

Harvestland organic-style (but not USDA Organic certified) chicken - the only "organic" meat I saw but it's great to have that. Pieces and whole birds are available.

The tanks are for live fish - circular lists several items, like live Tilapia, etc.

And I agree, people are so thrilled to have a nabe supmkt. again, so their may be dancing in the aisles for awhile.
