So what does everybody think of the new yoga studio on Roosevelt and 79th Street?

I have been attending the Vinyasa Yoga classes and it has been a great experience. i feel more flexible and have learned a few things on clearing the mind. believe it or not I also feel like I walk taller.

There are other types of yoga as well. Like power yoga and gentle yoga. They also offer guided meditation and body works which is a more personal yoga with a smaller class.

Also being offered at the studio are different types of dance classes with an emphasis on fitness. They have salsa-licious, latin-licious and belly dance. I have seen the practitioners leaving after class and they are always laughing or have huge smiles on.

Let's not forget the kids classes. My niece and daughter are hooked. They really enjoy the whole experience, from the kids karma, to the creative acting, to the dance class. The kids are having nonstop fun.

So come on Jackson Heights join the fun. It has been great. The address is 79-05 Roosevelt Avenue on the 2nd floor.
