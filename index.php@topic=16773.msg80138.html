Quote from: Lilybell on July 20, 2017, 10:30:36 AM
They are planning for it to be 7 stories - is that allowed?  I thought there was a 6-floor limit in JH.

Per my quick research:

That lot is located well outside the historic district, so just ordinary zoning laws apply. That lot is zoned "C4-3", like the rest of the area, intended for medium density commercial with continuous storefronts. The maximum allowed floor area ratio is 3.4 (floor are ratio is the ratio of the floor area "excluding mechanical space, cellar space, floor space in open balconies, elevators or stair bulkheads" to the size of the lot, which in this case is 3442 sq ft). There are various ways this can be increased, like adding a public plaza, etc.

If it doesn't take up the whole lot, seven stories could be allowed.
