Title: Espresso 77 - Now Hiring!
Post by: julesnyc on August 25, 2008, 04:20:53 PM
I just posted the following ad on Craigslist - if anyone on JHLife is interested, we would love to talk with you.  Many of our summer staff are heading back to school, so we need folks for all kinds of shifts: mornings, evenings and weekends. Please also forward to friends who you think would be a great addition to our team!

Espresso 77, Jackson Heights' favorite independent espresso bar, is in need of new baristas and counter people for all shifts. We are looking for baristas with experience in creating handcrafted espresso drinks and a passion for the world of coffee. Latte at the push of a button? No. Pulling beautiful shots on a La Marzocco and finishing the drink with a rosette? Yes Yes Yes! Counter people should have outgoing personalities, strong experience in retail/restaurant customer service and an interest in coffee and in learning the art of espresso.

Come work for us - it's a cool coffee shop with interesting staff and customers in a great neighborhood! We are open to creating flexible schedules for moms and dads, actors and artists, students of all kinds. We offer competitive hourly pay based on experience and duties.

Please send your resume and a little note as to why we should chat further to work@espresso77.com.
Check us out on the web at www.espresso77.com.
