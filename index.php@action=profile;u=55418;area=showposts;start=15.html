Neighborhood Chat / Re: Anything better then time Warner internet?
« on: January 01, 2018, 10:26:27 AM »
I second trying to switch to RCN. That's what I have, and it's far from perfect, but I am able to pay for internet only and even after fees my bill is under $50/month (I think it's at 47 right now). RCN annoyances: occasionally there are random surcharges and rate increases—never move than $5 so i think they rely on the customer not even noticing, and then you have to decide whether it's worth it to complain. However, in most if not all cases I've gotten rid of the increase by just contacting customer service and telling them that I'll switch otherwise. The internet is occasionally slow but I think this is normal with any service, and I've found that it's generally reliable.

Restaurants & Food / Re: if you have own a store in JH - please ditch the credit minimum.
« on: December 26, 2017, 10:36:45 PM »
I do think that cashless businesses are (eventually) the way of the future, but also worth noting that those places often charge more than the standard card minimum for virtually all of their menu items. That's fine, but it's a different business model than some of the places in JH with credit/debit minimums. Said minimums are usually around $5, and you'd be hard pressed to go into a Sweetgreen and find anything for under $5 (rarely would anyone wait in that line for only a drink, and even if you did, I think they're close to $4).

Restaurants & Food / Re: Casa Del Chef Bistro in Woodside
« on: July 06, 2017, 10:23:07 AM »
As others have in the past, I feel compelled to revive this thread to say that I finally had dinner at Casa del Chef Bistro in Woodside, and it was so lovely! It was, sadly, not very crowded on a Friday night, so I thought it worth plugging this again- if you haven't been, truly worth it. We did the (reasonably priced) tasting menu, and everything was delicious, particularly the first course of ricotta gnocchi with greenmarket vegetables. Also had a perfectly seared white fish, and super flavorful steak (flat iron I think? would have to go back and check).

quaint and cozy interior, attentive service, and most importantly, excellent food.
