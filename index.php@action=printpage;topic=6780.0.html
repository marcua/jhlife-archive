Title: Compost your Jack-o-Lanterns at the Jackson Heights Greenmarket!
Post by: Markella on November 03, 2011, 04:14:38 PM
Bring your Halloween pumpkins in for composting to the Jackson Heights Greenmarket! Drop off times are from 9am-Noon. Also stop by the info tent and learn all about winter squash, pick up some new recipes and find out the weight of the Giant Pumpkin!

Jackson Heights Greenmarket
34th Ave at 77th St
8am - 3pm, Year-Round!
www.grownyc.org/jacksonheightsgreenmarket
