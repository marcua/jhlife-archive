I don't use an exact recipe. I just toss a handful of fresh peppers (serrano, habanero or jalapeno) into a Cuisinart food chopper/processor. I like to add a few drops of lime or lemon juice. I try to eat low sodium, so I don't add salt, but you can add salt.

You should google hot sauce recipes. There are probably better recipes than mine.
