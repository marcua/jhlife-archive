Hi All,

My dog ran out this morning at about 8:45am. My grandma was taking out the trash and must have left the gate open. This was on 99th Street and 37th ave.

Her name is ANNIE.  She is microchipped, but she was not wearing a collar. If you take her to a vet they should be able to contact me via the microchip scanner. She's a Westie/Maltese mix and is about 15-20 pounds. She has a white coat, but her top coat tends to get blonde. She just got a bath, so she's pretty clean, but you can see in the second picture what I mean by the blonde coat.

She was last seen at 10:00am on 37th avenue and 97th street in Corona. Running towards the Jackson Heights area.

If you have any information on her, even if you've just seen her running down the street - please give me a call at 917 579 2685. There is a $100 reward for her return!

Thanks so much in advance to anyone who can help.
