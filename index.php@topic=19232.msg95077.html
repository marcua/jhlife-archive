Aggressive homeless man on 37th Avenue
« on: August 05, 2019, 08:57:42 PM »
Today I noticed an aggressive homeless white man, about 6'2'' on 37th Ave.  He was walking up and down 37th Ave in the afternoon sort of kicking things around. He looked at me aggressively - sort of angry - when I was walking holding my girls' hands (ages 3 and 5). In the evening I saw him sitting in front of the liquor store on 76th and 37th Ave.  He had what appeared to be a pile of US Postal mail that he was going through and literally throwing the stuff on the floor after going through it.  He looked at me like he wanted to fight. He was looking at other folks the same way.   

What the hell is going on in this neighborhood?  I hope its not a harbinger for things to come.  Let's not kid ourselves, DiBlasio is probably the worst mayor we have ever had in this city.  He has done nothing to deal with this population.  The police are completely absent.  I suspect that he's going to hurt somebody at some point.

Call 311 and ask to have a street outreach team sent to the area.  Street Outreach is a service that is funded by the city but the services are provided by a number of non-profits that can offer healthcare including mental health services, drop-in centers, supported housing, etc.  The object is to get people off the streets, stabilized, and into housing.  They do a great job reaching out and convincing homeless people that there's an alternative and in fact better life off the street.
