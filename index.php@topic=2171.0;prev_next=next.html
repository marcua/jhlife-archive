La Flor - 53rd and Roosevelt - A must try!
« on: February 01, 2009, 11:35:23 AM »
This place is scary good!  I've been there about four times and have never been disappointed. It is French/Mexican.  Decor is more East Village than JH.  Try the chicken quesadillas and the chocolate brioche - or anything else on the menu.  Wonderful and right at the #7 stop (get off on the east end of the platform).
