as far as i know, it's not "kept out of sight," it's made to order, so nothing is laying around.

the bases are all different, so it's not like they just slap different ingredients on a standard pizza sauce/mozzarella canvas. some have that, some have a white-ish garlic sauce, some a sweeter sauce, some a different cheese, etc.

ordered delivery the other day and didn't have a menu, and at the time, they didn't have one online, so the nice young woman who answered the phone read each item off the pizza menu individually.

we ended up getting an "English bacon" (essentially a 'meat lovers' type thing) that was very good, though the bacon could have been a little crispier and a Colombian style with chorizo and sweet plaintain with more of a central american queso fresco, which i loved.

the menu has a number of veggie pizzas as well. definitely worth a try.
