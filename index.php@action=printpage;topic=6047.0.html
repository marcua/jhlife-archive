Title: Baby Hour at Curves
Post by: Curves on April 14, 2011, 12:42:12 PM
We know it's hard to find a sitter so when Dad or Grandma can't watch your
little one Curves is introducing "Baby Hour" during the week where you can bring
your child while you workout.
The hours are: Tuesday and Thursday 1:00pm to 2:00pm

April is our 6th Annual Food Drive, you can join with any membership for $39 and
pay no registration fee when you donate a bag of groceries to our food drive.

Curves is a women's facility featuring a complete full body workout combining
cardio and strength training to give the results you want. All with a trainer to
keep you motivated and we weigh and measure you once a month to track your
progress. You can travel to any Curves world wide when you're a member.

Curves Jackson Heights features "Zumba on the Circuit" where we've combined our
quick-fit circuit with the amazing Latin-inspired dance workout of Zumba.

Many insurance companies reimburse a gym membership to Curves. So be sure to
check with your insurance provider to see if you're entitled to a membership.
