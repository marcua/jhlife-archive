They're having some opening issues...went yesterday at 12 and some people sitting next to us got up and left because they had been there for awhile without so much as water. They were also told the kitchen wouldn't be ready for 20 minutes, even though restaurant said they were open. LOL

That said, our service was good. We did notice the 15% service charge; lame that they don't make it more obvious on the menu, at least that I could tell.

The restaurant interior is nicer than just about any other place in western JH. The prices are mostly fair...Desi Chinese is pretty limited (mostly fried and/or covered in thick sauces), but the stuff we had was generally pretty good (lollipop chicken, chow mein, fried rice, "sizzling" platter).  Better than "Talk of the Town" (which often takes ages to get any food even when empty), and way better than the sad gobi manchurian or chicken chili that sits out on the buffets on 74th street.
