News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

From Facebook DRUM - Desis Rising Up & Moving : The reports of raids today and our responsibilities:

The Jackson Heights reports have so far turned out to be the usual abuse meted out by police. Not ICE

Staten Island raid is confirmed

Bushwick was home arrests

Manhattan unconfirmed

Spreading such reports online is causing more damage than helping people. Vast majority of undocumented immigrants are not following Facebook posts and twitter feeds of activists.

We had folks (over a dozen throughout the day) go on location and talk to workers (street vendors, etc) in area. It ended up being a waste of time and valuable resources.

Rumors are spreading in name of "colleagues", "friends", "friend's mom" etc but never able to trace original report

Those of us dealing with impacted communities are seeing the damage being caused is spreading fear and paranoia. People stop going to work, sending their children to school, leaving their homes.

Social media is NOT the platform to publicize such reports. We will be putting out a guidance tomorrow on best practices for sharing such reports and the rare instances that social media should be used.
