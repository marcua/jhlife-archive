workshops For 1st-Time Homebuyers: Part 4: The Mortgage Process 

The Chhaya Community Development Corporation presents 4th session of a 5-part series designed to help first-time homebuyers learn about buying a home. Part 4 (12/1): The Mortgage Process discusses steps first-time homebuyers take in getting a mortgage; types of mortgages available; and the people/companies involved in the process.
