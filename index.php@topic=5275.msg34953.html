Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Jackson Heights proper runs from the BQE to Junction Blvd. And from Roosevelt Ave. to Northern Blvd.

The section just north of JH that is 11370 has always had an identity problem. Some have called it JH. The Post Officer has it's Trainsmeadow Station over there. Trainsmeadow does seem to be a designqtion that predates most if not all construction over there. The part of 11370 that is North of the Grand Central Expressway is sometimes called Astoria Heights. Riker's Island which technically is Bronx county uses 11370 as it's zip and calls the address there east Elmhurst, which most of the folks that live in 11370 seem to prefer.
