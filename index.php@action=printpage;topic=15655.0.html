Sign Up for Farm Spot Winter Veggie, Apple and Cider Shares!

Hi Potential Farm Spotters!

If you are interested in local, organic eating, you should consider joining Farm Spot!

There will be 4 winter deliveries, one each month in December, January, February and March. 


Please sign up as soon as possible, but no later than Wednesday November 30th!


Click here to sign up for Farm Spot Winter Shares!
http://csa.farmigo.com/join/farmspot/winter2017

Winter Organic Vegetable Share - 10 lb box (nearly 1/2 the size as previous years) x 4 deliveries = $90
Each box will contain 10+ pounds of the following crops: Sweet Potatoes, Potatoes, Watermelon Radish, Rutabaga, Carrots, Beets, Leeks, Winter Squash and possibly Broccoli, Kale and/or Cabbage.  Exact contents to be decided. 

Winter Apple Share - 4 lb bag x 4 deliveries = $31

Winter Cider Share - 1/2 gallon jug x 4 deliveries = $23

Winter Share Delivery Dates.
Delivery 1 - December 15
Delivery 2 - January 12
Delivery 3 - February 9
Delivery 4 - March 9



We hope you will all join us!!
