Bilingual Sprouts! Spanish immersion program for children!
« on: June 06, 2015, 09:05:03 AM »
Bilingual Sprouts is a Spanish immersion program for children 24 months to 5 years of age founded and taught by two New York State certified Early Childhood Bilingual and Art Education teachers. Through interactive activities such as Spanish Literature read-alouds, art, drama, music and movement, Bilingual Sprouts will immerse your child in a new language while having fun! We offer classes  Tuesday or  Friday and have 3 sessions on both days. Adult participation is required by one parent or caregiver.
Session 1 (2-3yrs old) 10:00am-10:45am Session 2 (4-5yrs old) 11:00am-11:45am
Session 3 ( 2-3yrs old) Tuesday 12:00-12:45 (4-5yrs old) Friday 12:00-12:45. Contact us about our Trial classes starting June 30th! At BilingualSprouts@gmail.com. We look forward to hearing from you !
