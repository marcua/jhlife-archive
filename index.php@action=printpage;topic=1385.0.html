Title: Re: 35-20 Leverich Street St opinions
Post by: C11106 on September 02, 2008, 05:23:20 PM
I lived there the summer of 2006 (subletting a room from the owner of an apartment).  The apartment was beautiful and the building was really quiet (I was on the 7th floor).  The only problem was that the elevator is very old and rickety and I'd often just ended up taking the stairs because the elevator took so long to come.

The neighborhood seemed nice and safe, although I wasn't walking around by myself too late at night.  I had my car there and I recall parking being a giant hassle.
