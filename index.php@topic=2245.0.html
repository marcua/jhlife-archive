This a long overdue topic for our elected officials to address.  Kudos to everybody involved in putting it together!

MTA's finances are in very bad shape.  The legacy of the Giuliani/Pataki years are that the MTA has mounting debt, an unfunded capital program, and a large operating deficit.  If we want to avoid a repeat of the downward spiral we saw in the 1970s, it is time to restore the agency to sound financial footing.  Approval of the Ravich Plan is absolutely essential, or the current round of service cuts and fare increases will just be the tip of the iceberg.

We shouldn't allow our elected officials to get away with saying that bridge tolls are intolerable without providing a fiscally and politically viable alternative.
