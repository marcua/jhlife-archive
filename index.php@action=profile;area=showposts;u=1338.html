Community Groups, Activities & Events / Re: Illegal Basement Apartments- A community forum
« on: May 13, 2009, 03:51:17 PM »

Yes, a lot of basements are certainly unsafe and pose grave health risks to tenants as well as firefighters if something goes wrong. However, current laws focus more on physical aspects rather than the health and safety issues. Loosening the regulations on physical requirements that could allow for easier legalization would certainly not mean throwing safety out of the window. Our reseach has showed that a high proportion of currently illegal basements in JH have sufficient means of egress, or could have it with limited construction work.
