How sad......... Zabb Queens IMHO one of the best Thai restaurants in the neighborhood.  I especially love the large selection of spicy crispy salads made with a variety of crispy fried meats such as duck, pork, chicken, catfish or shrimp topped with roasted peanuts and a sweet/sour/hot dressing.  The noodle dish called pad khee mao is also a favorite in our household which consists of wide rice noodle tossed in a flavorful spicy sauce and top with cilantro, basil and bird's eye peppers (hot..hot...hot).  They can adjust all orders to your heat tolerance.  BTW they have speedy delivery service as well.

Thanks jz23 for getting the word out.  I'll stop in this week for dinner and tell my friends to do the same.

I had lunch there about a month ago...they have a limited menu during the day (basically a few choices of noodle soup).  I had soup with duck and wide, handcut rice noodles  that were delicious.  They are fully focusing on "Esarn" cuisine from the north (or Issan, which is probably an alternative spelling as used above).

None of the typical curries and dry noodle dishes...mostly soups with a vinegar / chili / fish sauce base (based on what I tasted) and rice dishes.

FWIW, it always seems to be crowded in the evenings.
