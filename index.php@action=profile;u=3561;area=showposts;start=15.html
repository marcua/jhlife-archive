News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Neighborhood Chat / Espresso 77 is expanding and we need your help!
« on: July 30, 2015, 12:02:15 PM »
Dear Jackson Heights Friends and Neighbors.
We excited to announce that Espresso 77 will soon be renovated and will be expanding into the adjacent retail space!
Most importantly, this will mean more seating (hurray!), but it also gives us a chance to freshen up the storefront, add new items to our menu (draft beer, anyone?), and upgrade our air-conditioning and sound systems.

NOW - WE NEED YOUR HELP!
We have been saving our pennies in hopes that this opportunity would finally come our way, but we need a bigger budget to make it terrific.  We have set up an IndieGogo fundraising site (http://igg.me/at/espresso77/x/5521115), and we are asking for the financial support of our friends, neighbors and customers to help out with the work.  We've set up some great perks to show our appreciation - t-shirts, parties, free coffee - most of all, know that with your help, we are going to invest in the life of our community for years to come.

Thank you for the past 8 years and we look forward for many more years of coffee, art and music!
Afzal and Julie

Please stop by Espresso 77 from 6-10pm tonight for an reception to celebrate the opening of our newest gallery shows.  Come for snacks, drinks, community and great art!

Callicoon_Counter, photographs by Scott Hall
and
Farm Life, photographs by Kari Grimsby

Both shows feature gorgeous photography inspired by life in bucolic farm country, and our artists both have ties to Jackson Heights   Scott Hall is a Jackson Heights resident who has found elegant beauty in the objects that have come across the kitchen counter of an upstate farmhouse.  Kari Grimsby, who left Queens to work on farms and build a cheesemaking business in Western Pennsylvania, takes striking photographs of her family's life on their farm.

Restaurants & Food / Season's Greetings from Espresso 77!
« on: October 02, 2014, 11:59:07 PM »
Season's Greetings from Espresso 77!

Instead of adding you all to our Christmas card list and waiting until December to send you our Year in Review (think of all the photocopying!), we thought we would send you our holiday greetings now - with a few updates on what we are up to...

1.  We opened a second shop in Park Slope - it's even smaller than our JH store, so don't worry, we're not relocating! But please tell all your friends who are still living the Brooklyn dream to visit our location at 410 7th Avenue (bet 13th/14th) in the South Slope. We are having a reception for the opening of our new gallery show there on October 12th at 6pm- please join us!

2.  We have lots of new treats to offer - brownies, muffins, bar cookies - and many of them are vegan and/or gluten-free.  They are all delicious!

3.  We have a special birthday coming up - on December 7th, espresso 77 will be 7 years old!  Watch this space for more news about that...

4. As the colder weather rolls in, we have a new selection of deep rich craft beers and wines that will warm you up and bring cheer to chilly evenings. 

5.  We have a great event planned for the evening of Friday, October 24th and we hope you can join us.  We will be celebrating our music director Jonny Meyers' new CD, Somewhere You've Been, and also opening a gallery show, Speak Louder: new paintings by our own Afzal Hossain,   The party starts at 6pm - bring the kids, listen to some great music and enjoy everything that Jackson Heights' favorite coffee and wine bar has to offer.

Espresso 77
35-57 77th Street (Jackson Heights)
410 7th Avenue (Park Slope)

follow us on Facebook and Twitter (@espresso77)
