Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

A new online store has reently opened named Jackson Heights Studios, where the artist is locally and nationally known, Manuel Cordero. He makes Queens themed graphic designed t-shirts and also other designs/artwork/custom graphics made on American Apparel brand clothing.  The link to the store is http://jhartstudio.com.  On this landing page, you can either view Manuel Cordero's art work, bio, and accomplishments by clicking the "Artwork" link or go to the online store which is called the "Neighborhood Store."  These t-shirts are reasonably priced, especially since they are each handmade, screen printed on American Apparel t-shirts. 
First time customers can use the promotional code "JHSPromo78" at checkout to receive 20% off the entire order.

Jackson Heights Studios will also be having an Online Store Launch Party on June 4th at 7pm, in Jackson Heights at Terraza Cafe(www.terrazacafe.com).  There will be a fashion show of our clothing line which includes, men/women's t-shirts, dresses, hoodies, and other apparel, all inspired by Jackson Heights, Queens life.  Also there will be live performances by NYC Songwriter/singer Julianna Riccardi and recording artist Nancy Magarill.  Additionally, there will be a raffle at the party for an iPod Nano, other Apple Products, and free t-shirts. 

The site is pretty cool and is designed very well.


If you need any additional information, please let me know. I hope that you all can attend this event.  Please RSVP to mc@jhartstudio.com or call 917-330-4853, since space is limited at Terraza Cafe.

Thanks,
Michelle
