News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

New Parent / Breastfeeding Support Group
Second Tuesday of the month - next meeting November 12, 2019
10:00 – 11:30 am
$10
2nd Story Pilates & Yoga, 81-10 37th Ave, Jackson Heights, Queens NY
https://www.secondstorymovement.com/

Please join us for a support group for new parents! It can be isolating at home with a new baby, so many questions, conflicting advice, and sleep deprived to boot. Meet some neighbors and have your questions answered with compassion and experience to help navigate what works best for your family. We will have snacks!

As a postpartum and birth doula and certified breastfeeding counselor, Rachel Goldstein has helped countless NYC families with the transition to parenthood over the past decade. Please wear your baby as there is a flight of stairs to climb and no stroller parking. We can’t wait to meet you! All are welcome, you don’t have to be breastfeeding.

Please register here: https://clients.mindbodyonline.com/classic/ws?studioid=624865&stype=-8&sView=day&sLoc=0&sTrn=100000049. See you soon!

SAVE THE DATES: Upcoming meetings on Nov 12, Dec 10, Jan 14, Feb 11, Mar 10, Apr 14, May 12

https://postmodernchildbirth.com/services/lactation-support/
