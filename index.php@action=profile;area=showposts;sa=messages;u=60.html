Neighborhood Chat / Re: Garbage on street
« on: July 18, 2008, 02:30:23 PM »
This may have already been said but it seems to me that the street conditions in some sections of JH are a matter of compliance with the law and lax enforcement.  I went to the Dept. of Sanitation website and pretty quickly found that it is the law in NYC that every business sweep in front of its establishment- actually extending 18 inches into the street- at the beginning and end of every day. 

Why not simply ask all merchants in Jackson Heights to comply with the law?  Check out the DOS rules for yourself here-  http://home2.nyc.gov/html/dsny/downloads/pdf/rules/digest/digestprint.pdf . The section relating to business responsibilities are on the bottom of page five.
