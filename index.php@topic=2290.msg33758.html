We just returned from our first visit to Nixtamal and we are already planning what we are going to order the next time we go!

Everything was good - from the guacamole and freshly made chips to the tamales and then amazing chicken and shrimp tacos.  The shrimp tacos (which were available in place of the fish tacos on the menu) were made with fresh shrimp and a nice mango salsa.

Very clean and bright and the tortilla machine was entertaining for our daughter.  There is also a cart with fruit / ice cream bars outside the entrance - all natural and very tasty.

Most convenient if you have a car, but not terrible via the 7 train.

    I have eaten here 100 times in the past 6 years.  Usually we order tacos, enchiladas (mole), tamales, pozole, guacamole and horchata.
    The last time I was there I saw a trio of bowls going to another table, and I asked what they were.  They are 'parrillada molcajata' it is the stone bowl they use for quacamole, heated up over a flame, and filled with things to build your own tacos.  One bowl is really enough for two people to share. 
    YOU MUST TRY THIS!  Is is kind of like the fajita where you make your own tacos, but no sizzling smokey platter - but I think this is better!  This will likely be our go-to dish going forward..
