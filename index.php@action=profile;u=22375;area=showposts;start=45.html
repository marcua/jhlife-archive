Neighborhood Chat / Re: Cell service in JH?
« on: July 10, 2013, 02:23:57 PM »
I can't comment on JH cell service but I can say that I've had great success using an AT&T Microcell device to provide excellent cell service where there was practically none before. You can buy one from AT&T, although sometimes in the past they gave them to people who had a terrible signal. But I got two of them on eBay for about half price; they're also common on Craigslist. The key, in buying a used one, is to make sure the seller has deactivated it; if not you won't be able to activate it on your account. These units rely on your Internet service, so you'll need a broadband connection, but I assume you have that. Verizon has their own version as well.
