News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

I know that Community United Methodist Church Flea Market is completely sold out for this Saturday, July 11th. However, if you want to take a chance and stop by early on Saturday AM; wait to see if there are any Booth Spaces available, due to vendor(s) not showing up, you may be able to get a spot this way. But, I am not sure if there is already a waiting list for Booth Spaces, so here is my best advice for you now. Stop over to the Flea Market and introduce yourself to me; I am selling at (spot #3); nearby to the entrance to the Playground. I am more than happy to get you to the people who are running the Flea  Markets here; perhaps you will be able to secure a spot for the next event on July 18th. Again, I do not know about waiting lists for this event; how many people are already on that list, but surely worth for you to stop by on this Saturday and see what is what for July 18th; future events during the summer and early fall. By the by, you may be interested to find out about the Flea Market going on at IS230 Magnet School on Saturday, July 11th, and I believe that there may be some available Booth Spaces for this event; not sure about any outside/sidewalk spots, but several inside spots, I think are still available.
