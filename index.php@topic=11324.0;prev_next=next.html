I am using this website to gather local, doable, affordable Valentine's Day activities. Please post what you plan to do for Valentine's Day, what you've done before for a satisfying V-Day, and/or ideas for others to pursue. It can be a gift idea, an experience/activity, or doing something special for someone else that you think will make them happy.

For my own personal valentine's day event, I plan to visit Regal Heights Rehab. But not something one can do on short notice as you need medical clearance first.

For others I can tell you about my friend's tango class. very seductive. Azul Ibáñez Fernández and her partner are the instructors. People always leave that class very happy!!! It's $16 per person, 7:30 p.m. Fridays. Reservations are not required, but recommended. The location is Mimi For Me Yoga, 76-11 35th Avenue, 2nd Floor, ph: 718-505-9642. Azul also offers the class at 8:30 in Spanish.

Please share your ideas!
