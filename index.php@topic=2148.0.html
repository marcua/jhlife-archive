When is your first sign of Spring?
« on: January 21, 2009, 10:02:45 PM »
For me the hardest part of winter is when the holidays pass and it is bitterly cold and dark.  My first sign of Spring is when the days start to get longer.  We are now gaining about 8-9 minutes of daylight every week in the evening (the morning daylight change is slower this time of year).  I think I hate the darkness of winter more than anything else.  I actually like snow while it is falling but hate it afterwards because of the parking headaches it causes and the sloppiness of melting dirty snow.

Do you realize that the sun sets tomorrow at 5:00 PM which is 32 minutes later than when it set at 4:28 PM on December 14th and that on February 28th the sun will set at 5:45!  Spring is coming!  To me the glass is always half full!
