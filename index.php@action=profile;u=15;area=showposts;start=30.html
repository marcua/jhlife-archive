Real Estate & Home Improvement / Re: track lighting
« on: August 20, 2009, 01:52:44 PM »
The proper way to do it is to run a new line from the panel or at least from a junction box (a blanked off plate, that might be located somewhere in the room).

If it's never been upgraded though, you'd need to pull all new wires, everywhere. You probably have old cloth covered wiring rather than proper BX cables. Electrical code will not allow you to do this type of work mixing and matching old and new wires. It's also a big fire hazard.

But....people do it all the time.

If there's no ceiling fixture already, you can't get around having to dig into the plaster to pull a line from somewhere if you don't want hanging wires. It will certainly be more expensive than you probably imagine. You'll need to replaster that section of the wall, skim coat and repaint.
