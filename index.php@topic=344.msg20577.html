Quote from: madalyn on July 31, 2009, 11:43:09 AM
We used to have bunnies when I was a kid but they lived in a bunny house and yard outside.  I didn't think rabbits could be house broken (apartment broken).  Is it possible?

Where did you get them?

I adopted them from an animal rescue group, Long Island Rabbit Rescue (www.lirrg.org).  Bunnies are actually supposed to live inside.  The LIRRG site gives all the information. 

My bunnies use a litterbox and I usually have them run free through the house, although they do have a cage (it's a 4'x4' dog pen, actually).

They are great pets, are sweet, and less work because you never have to walk them outside.  They also are quiet.  If anyone is thinking of getting a bunny, I highly recommend adopting from LIRRG--there is a huge overpopulation problem with rabbits, and pet stores that sell them contribute to the problem.
