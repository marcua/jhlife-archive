Regarding H&R Block:  when I was a student (masters in accounting), I attempted to get a job there in November for their tax season, and they told me that their hiring was already done for the upcoming season.  As it's already March now, you are definitely too late.

It is a tough time--I am an accountant at a big 4 accounting firm, and we just had another big layoff last week. 

What I would recommend you do until you find a full-time job is to visit a temp agency (I know there is an agency called accountemps (accounting jobs only) in upstate NY, it may be in the NYC area as well.  There are so many temp agencies in NYC and with companies laying off so many people, they may need temporary help to cover day-to-day needs.  I've also heard that some companies are laying off their full-time help and hiring temps because it is cheaper.  If you find a good fit and things turn around a little, they may hire you on as full-time.  The temp agencies also love for temp jobs to turn to full-time, because they receive a placement fee from the company that will hire you.
