Hi Everyone!

We just want to introduce ourselves. We are a new pharmacy in the neighborhood, located at 33-13 Junction Blvd (near Northern Blvd.). We offer fast, friendly service and free delivery. 10% off on all purchases. 

We also specialize in pet medications, we compound and flavor liquid medication for your lovely companions. We will take our time to help your pet feel better. Nothing is too small or time consuming for us. You can just give our number to your vet. and the prescription will be ready when come to pick it up. No waiting and no hassell.

We recently just treated a 4-week old kitten for diarrhea with our compounded medication. The kitten was found on the street. Now she is fully recovered and have already gone to her new adopted home. The vet. actually had trouble locating a pharmacy because the dose was so small that no one else would take the time to make it.  And we made it happen!
 
Our price is very competitive, $ 3.99 for a month supply on most generic medications! We look forward to your visit.
