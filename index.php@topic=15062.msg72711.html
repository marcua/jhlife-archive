What about Pata Paplean in Elmhurst? Love the quirky decor, delicious bar snacks if you're hungry (although it is still very much a bar, unlike a place like UFC which is primarily a restaurant that has a good beer selection). Servers/bartenders are friendly, vibe is laid back...I'm not one for the Thai specialty cocktails (they seem fun but also way too sweet for me, but that's just a matter of personal preference), but I think it's a great place to go grab a beer and catch up with a friend. I haven't gone by myself, but I would feel comfortable doing so.

There's a number of places that actually put heavy drapes in front of the big plate glass windows out front. I'm not sure why people are saying you have to go over to Astoria (7 to the N) to find a decent. We live right next Woodside, which has decent bars with Irish pub food. A little further down is Sunnyside, which also has bars (though I understand my favorite one, Molly Blooms, has been renamed and renovated with a new menu coming soon.
