Neighborhood Chat / Spanish classes & Spanish baby music & story telling class winter registration !
« on: December 15, 2015, 11:40:04 PM »
Hello  Jh neighbors ! I hope your having an amazing holiday season and enjoying this unusual warm weather. I wanted take the time to thank all the families that have embraced and supported our program in the community. Our second semester has been a hit and we have seen such a growth in our students Spanish language development and are super excited that we will continue to be part of that growth.

I also wanted to remind families that Bilingual Sprouts winter semester registration is underway. Our winter semester will run for ten weeks from January 4th to March 14th at the Community United Methodist Church here in Jackson Heights. Please visit our website for more information about class schedule and tuition information at BilingualSprouts.com or you can email us at bilingualsprouts @ gmail.com.

Our program is for children 6 months to 7yrs of age.
Our Spanish baby music and story telling class for babies 6 months to 19 months is a program created to give children a multi-sensory experience while being immersed in the Spanish ​language. Our classes are taught through the use of verbal input (Visual and auditory), which includes original and traditional music, movement, puppetry and singing and storytelling.

Our 20 months to 7 years Spanish ​​class is a program created to give children a multi-sensory experience while being immersed in the Spanish language. Our class is taught in a full immersive format and our taught through the use of verbal input (Visual and auditory), which includes singing, literacy, puppetry, theater, role playing, art and exploration, as well as take home experiences to bring back and share with class and group work. Each experience purposely engaging the child in a multi- sensory learning process. Both teacher led and structured classes where we encourage exploration, discovery, socialization and creative thinking.​​

Who we are
Bilingual Sprouts is a Spanish immersion program taught and founded by my sister and I. We are both New York State Certified teachers in Early Childhood Bilingual and Art Education. We have created a program to foster the development of bilingualism and biliteracy in students from all language backgrounds. Through multi-sensory activities and research-based practices, students will experience Spanish through original and traditional songs, movement, singing, conversation, artistic exploration, dramatic arts, writing, reading exercises and group and or partner work. The learning will be extended to the home with weekly family or individual projects.

Our Mission
Bilingual Sprout's mission is to help committed families preserve and strengthen their heritage, grow in the Spanish language and understand the benefits bilingualism has to offer. For that reason we created our unique and tailored approach with fun filled multi-sensory, hands-on, interactive and engaging experiences so that students become willing participants of their own learning and gain a positive and strong connection with the Spanish language

Please contact me if you have any questions about our program and classes.

Community Groups, Activities & Events / Bilingual Sprouts presents Sproutlet Theater
« on: August 22, 2015, 09:55:41 AM »
Bilingual Sprouts is happy to announce the addition of their "Sproutlet Theater" program for children's ages 5-7, intended not only to motivate children to read but also to nurture a love for literature and theater. Throughout the program, children will listen, read, study and dramatize classic children's literature, expand their vocabulary and gain an understanding of plot, setting and character development. Each session will begin with several fun and interactive warm-up exercises designed to help children relax and develop confidence in public speaking and performance.  In addition, children's creativity will be encouraged as they  learn cooperative and team building skills while planning and designing simple sets, costumes and props.  At the end of the semester children will bring their piece of literature to life and perform for their families and friends.

Please contact us if you have any questions at Email: bilingualsprouts@gmail.com or call 212-731-4558. Or visit our Website: www.bilingualsprouts.com Facebook.com/bilingualsprouts
*Sproutlet Theater program will be taught in English.
 *Program will have 12 sessions and will meet once a week every Saturday from 12:00pm -1:00pm
*Program will run from September 19th through December 12th.
*Cost of program- $330
