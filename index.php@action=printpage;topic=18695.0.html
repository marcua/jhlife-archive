Title: Re: Three guys on Roosevelt & 79th St Update
Post by: am315 on February 05, 2019, 03:42:40 PM
I emailed P.O. Liebold on this last week. 79th St  falls under the sector he supervises. He replied to me while he was on vacation in California and we arranged to talk today while he was still away.

He said the Vice squad has an open investigation with this group and has arrested at least one of them on prostitution related charges. Apparently these guys were steering people to a location on 79th where prostitution was happening. They also busted a man and a woman in one of the houses on 79th St and accused them of running prostitution from there. In between all that they have fined these guys for minor infractions like loitering.

Bottom line is that they are on it. But it sounds a bit like whack-a-mole. He said the Vice folks continue to shoo them away and are continually trying to catch them actually breaking the law, which isn't easy as it sounds, I guess.

Anyhow, it wasn't the most satisfying response but I was pretty impressed the guy actually took the time during his vac to reach out. He said they hold a monthly community meeting and he'd text me with the info if people want to show up. It sounded like he could arrange to have one of the Vice people come and explain what they've been doing.
