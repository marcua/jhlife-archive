Cozy 1 bedroom Apartment with NO BOARD Approval - Just renovated with steel appliances, original hardwood flooring, granite counters and updated lighting

  Sublet OK after 1 year

  $470 maintenance covers all - Electric is extra

OK, so it is not Jackson Heights but if you are looking for a place in a very quiet place to live and probably Cheaper than renting...

The complex is known as Kew Terrace - very quiet Garden Style complex.  Two Blocks from Union Tpke and a 12 minute walk to Queens Blvd if you so chose to walk it and skip the Bus.  The area is family oriented full of kosher and mainstream spots within walking distance.  Two blocks from everyday conveniences, supermarket, pizza shop, cleaners, Spirits and wine store and so on.  If you are looking for a quiet and peaceful place that is probably cheaper to own then it is to rent then this may be the place for You.
