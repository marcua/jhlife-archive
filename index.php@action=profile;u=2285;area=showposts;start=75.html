Neighborhood Chat / Re: anyone have Metro PCS?
« on: November 30, 2010, 12:38:44 AM »
I made the switch from Verizon to Metro PCS back in March 2010 and I must say I have been pleased thus far.  For the money I pay, I really can't complain.  I have the Samsung Code, which is a smart phone that requires the $50/month plan.  Unlimited talk, text, & web.  It even includes free navigation and unlimited international texting which is cool.  I left Verizon because of the cost and the fact that you have to lock in with a 2 year contract.

So far, I have been to Orlando, San Diego, Los Angeles, Atlantic City, and Las Vegas with the phone and it worked well in all those places.  On the other hand, I took the phone to upstate NY (New Paltz) and I had zero reception there.  The are parts of Jersey where the phone gets very weak reception as well.  Bottom Line: If you are a city dweller, I say go for it.  With some carriers charing well over $100/month with data plans, I'm willing to sacrafice a little bit of coverage and pay my flat $50 monthly fee.

Over a 2 year period, I'm probably saving over $1,000 which is enough for me to take a nice vacation to the Caribbean! =)  Now that is change we can believe in!
