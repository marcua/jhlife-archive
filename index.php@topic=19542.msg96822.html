Hi all My Name s Dionisio Flores Owner at Laly's i haven't have a lot time to chat or post anything here all i want to said is thanks for all the support that we have get from all lovely people from around and fair away from Jackson heights, it is hard to keep everybody happy, but we always try our best to ,sometimes you may get confused about the concept, is very simple, the name may not be what the restaurant is ( that's what some people said ) we just love to cook  and everything we make is always made with love from a french fry to a big meal , we spend hours to make our mole poblano , we make our own dressings, sauces, soups,our french fries , and many many other thinks, thats a reason why our prices maybe a little high but if you compare other places around  you will see that lalys is just on average, fresh ingredients , homemade  products, that makes the difference.
I know this days we are lazy to pick up the phone and place a food delivery order, (siri or alexa does everything,) unfortunately the big companies like grubhub uber eats just to mention some of them they get a lot of commision that is one of the reasons we have to increase the prices on those platforms to get a little bit profit ( i'm sure many other restaurants do ) you can visit our website (lalysrestaurantny.com)or text LALYS to 33733  and place your order directly from there and  in partner with CHOWNOW  you will get exactly the same service that you get with grubhub and others but at our regular menu prices.
So  here we are doing what we like and making happy customers and metting new people  every day, all  i have to say is thanks for your support community and please let us know if you have any ideas, concerns or anything that  will help improve our restaurant everything is very welcome   info@lalysrestaurant.com all of the love for all of you  :)DIO

Like the previous commenter, I had one not-good experience at Laly's, which I have to admit made me think twice about returning. I ordered the lemon chicken plate, and the chicken was so gristly and tough and un-chewable that I asked the waitperson to take it back and give me anything else. They were very gracious about it (not all restaurants are) and brought me shrimp tacos, which were fine. I've since been back and been very happy. It's too bad, because nothing and nobody is always perfect, but one such experience can really turn you off to a restaurant. I would never order that dish again, I'm afraid. Oh and the other bit of feedback--try to make any background music you choose to play innocuous. I'm sometimes really bothered by blasting music I find unpleasant to endure during a meal. Overall, I'm very glad Laly's is there and seems to be doing well, and will definitely go back soon.
