Jackson Heights Life Photo Contest - Fall 2008

Welcome to the Second Jackson Heights Life Photo Contest!  We are looking for an excellent photograph to use on the banner of our website. See here for our first photo contest.

Here are the rules for our contest:

1. Three submissions per person only - show us your best work.
2. Your image should be in digital format, taken with at LEAST a 3mp camera. Scanned images must be HIGH quality.
3. Photos should not portray identifiable individuals.
4. A small version of your image should be posted to this thread with a brief description of what it is.
5. The contest starts immediately and will run until November 17, 2008. At that time the contest will be closed to new entries.
6. After the submission period is closed, we will vote on the submitted images.
7. In December, a winner will be announced and the graphics will go live on the homepage shortly thereafter.

Further details:
Your image will run for a period of weeks or months. It should be a picture that captures some aspect of Jackson Heights.  Horizonal composition is preferred -- we need a wide and narrow section of the photo to serve as the banner.  On the bottom right hand corner we'll add your name/username/link/email whatever you want so people can link or contact you. Obviously you are giving us permission to use your image on the Jackson Heights Life homepage by your submission.

FYI, there are two ways to post photos on this site.

The first way is to upload them to another site, like Flickr or Photobucket, and link to the image from here.  An explanation of how to do this is over here.

The second way is to attach a file directly to your post.  At the bottom of the page, click on "Additional Options," and then enter the filename in the box next to "Attach:".  Files must be smaller than 128 kB.

You may crop the photo so that it fits the approximate dimensions of the banner, but there's no need to do so.... we can always assist you with that later. 

Can't wait to see the submissions rolling in!

signed,
Moderators shelby2 and chuckster
and Calendar Editor toddg
