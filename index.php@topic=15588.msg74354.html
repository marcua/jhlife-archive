News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

My advice is to utilize this site for opportunities it can offer. Many online anonymous forums can sometimes fall into negativity, but I suspect if you ask around, you would find that many JH neighbors have met as a result of this board, or have found out about events or opportunities they might not have otherwise known about.

I know there is a team that is developing an initiative called Jackson Heights Creates.  They have a FB page at https://www.facebook.com/JacksonHeightsCreates . Also you could check out #jacksonheights tags on twitter and Instagram to see what sorts of creative activities people are posting about.

Also, last time I went to Addictive Boutique Winery, the owners mentioned to me that they had just had a very successful reading by a local author and they seemed interested in hosting other sorts of things like that.

And lastly, I do agree with you -- there is a wealth of talent in Jackson Heights.  Good luck with your endeavors!
