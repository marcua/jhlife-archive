Title: Titan Foods in Astoria
Post by: rachel on February 16, 2009, 11:21:56 PM
Today I went to an incredible Greek specialty store today in Astoria on 31st Street - Titan Foods!  This place has an amazing selection of olives, cheeses, freshly baked pastries, and more.  Surprisingly, I found it a lot less expensive than other specialty food stores I've been to.  Cheaper feta than the deli at Trade Fair and so much tastier! I made a delicious Greek salad for dinner tonight and I'm in heaven...

Title: Re: Titan Foods in Astoria
Post by: Griswold Contessa on February 27, 2009, 06:31:09 PM
Titan has the most wonderful selection of Greek sweets the tiny baklava are made with a mixture of walnuts and pistachios they are melt in your mouth good and they have at least 10 different kinds of feta cheese.  If you go there around Greek Easter the place is mobbed!!  If anyone is into marinated octopus they have simply the BEST it's incredibly tender and slightly tangy from the red wine vinegar and oregano.  This place is truly a gem.
