I went out various times during yesterday's blizzard, but didn't really encounter strong winds.  Maybe I just went out at the right times or during  lulls in the storm.  That said, my recollections from previous blizzards are of forceful and blinding winds that made it uncomfortable to walk even one block.  I remember being driven by the wind, feeling the snow hit my face and the burning sensation that followed.  It was actually pretty scary to walk through.  So looking at the big picture, while a snow day can be fun, the severity of a storm can be unpleasant and dangerous as well.
