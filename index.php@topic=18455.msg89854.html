Any local freelancers, homeworkers or entrepreneurs out there?

If so, we'd love to have you join us for our first Soloist Collective meeting for the Jackson Heights chapter on Wednesday 7th November from 9am-10.30am at Espresso 77 (sign up for attendance using the Eventbrite link below)
https://www.eventbrite.com/e/jackson-heights-soloist-collective-chapter-meeting-tickets-51797778550

The Collective is a community of Soloists (independent professionals/business owners, freelancers, entrepreneurs) committed to helping each other pursue successful solo careers. The group is intended for those who want help navigating their careers through online and offline peer support channels.

For more info, check out the main website here: https://www.soloistcollective.com

Looking forward to seeing you there! Feel free to contact me directly if you have any questions

Ok, a couple of things:
This is a separate chapter from Jackson Heights (each chapter is run independently).
I regularly attend the monthly Astoria morning meetups and although I haven't been able to attend the evening workshops, my personal experience is that this is one of the less salesy/pushy groups (as oppose to BNI for example) and more of a support network - which is one of the reasons why I like it.
The workshops are intended to give soloists (members) the opportunity to deliver a presentation related to their business. Aaron is a member. Attendance is completely optional - if anyone thinks it sounds like a sales presentation, they don't have to attend.
