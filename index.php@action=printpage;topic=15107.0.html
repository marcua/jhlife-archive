There is a unique light setup at 90th and Roosevelt. All traffic must turn right from 90th onto Roosevelt. It's a constant red arrow that turns to flashing yellow--and stays that way for about four, maybe five cars to pass through--then turns yellow for a second or two like a traditional light. It used to be a stop sign, but this gives drivers a chance to go when there are so many pedestrians.
