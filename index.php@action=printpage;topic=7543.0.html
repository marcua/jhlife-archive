Title: Re: Mamajuana Cafe - new in Woodside
Post by: rhydewithdis on July 23, 2012, 09:05:32 AM
FINALLY, Mamajuana's has shown a pulse! After catching Questlove of the Roots spin a DJ set at Studio Square we swung by Mamajuana Cafe in the hopes that we might stumble onto some food -- we were actually delighted to see that all of the windows/awnings were totally open to the outside and they had their parking lot cleared out of cars and placed tables and chairs and a DJ outside.

Very festive, party atmosphere, not too crowded, maybe 50-80 people. Most were out for post-Colombian Independence Day celebrations so the crowd was quite attractive. At around 10p they also started serving hookah's. Admittedly, the music was on the loud side, and since the speakers and DJ set up were all outside, I don't think the neighbors will tolerate this on a regular basis. He was playing mostly salsa, bachata, a little cumbia and reggaeton.

We tried the chicken empanadas which were good.

Anyway, while it's fun and lasts, I finally recommend stopping by.
