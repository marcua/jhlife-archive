Title: Dog Run Dogs in the Halloween Parade - Open House and Costume Contest at Dog Run
Post by: MarcusW on October 28, 2010, 02:20:09 PM
Dogs of Jackson Heights: Come one, come all to the annual Jackson Heights Halloween Parade!

Sunday, October 31, 2010, we will meet at 11:30am, on the corner of 89th Street and 37th Avenue, to march with our canine companions in the annual JH Halloween Parade, which kicks off at high noon! Costumes are strongly encouraged but not required! Party at the dog run to follow immediately after (around 2pm). A huge thanks to everyone who agreed to help with the party planning.

PLEASE RSVP so we know how many doggy bags to make!
RSVP Here: http://jhdogs.ning.com/events/jackson-heights-halloween (http://jhdogs.ning.com/events/jackson-heights-halloween)

OPEN HOUSE - Both the parade and Costume Contest/Party will be open to non-members! This means, if you haven't filled out an application or are still waiting for it to be processed, you can enjoy the day with the rest of us! This will be one of the ONLY days when food will be allowed in the dog run, so enjoy it while you can! Among the delectable treats, we'll have pizzelle and home-made oreo cookies, mulled apple cider and pumpkin muffins!

Anyone interested in helping plan the costume party is encouraged to e-mail John at: mailto:membership@jhcrew.org (http://mailto:membership@jhcrew.org)  with "Halloween Party" in the subject line. Linked below is a flyer; feel free to download, print and hang in your favorite hangout!

Happy Howl-O-Ween!
