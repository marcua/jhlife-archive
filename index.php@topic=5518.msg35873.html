Also keep in mind that it's not just about cleanliness. Inspection grades are also about safe food handling practices. Things like making sure perishables are stored at the proper temperatures; taking steps to ensure that there is no cross-contamination of utensils, food containers, preparation surfaces, etc. That being said, I also know it's really not that hard to get an "A" or at least a "B", so rest assured I will not be eating at any establishment that could only manage a "C"!!!
