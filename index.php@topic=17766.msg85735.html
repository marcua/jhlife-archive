73rd Street is dirtier for a reason. One the businesses are open 24/7 while 74th closes around 8pm. Two there are more grocery stores that sell food and produce more garbage. Three there are more restaurants that have to throw out food that expired. Four, the most controversial idea is the the city doesn’t want to install metered parking on the entire street. Metered parking would allow the street sweepers to clean the street on both sides of the street 6 days a week. In fact what we have is just dirty streets that don’t get cleaned every morning. Then there are commercial businesses that don’t have metered parking in front of the buildings. The coops don’t want metered parking because they want the block to be residential again and want to fight every attempt to adapt to living in a commercially zoned area. Also many of these free parking spots are hogged by the commercial businesses with their own delivery trucks. 74th has metered parking the entire length and also has residential dwellings. So there is your answer.
