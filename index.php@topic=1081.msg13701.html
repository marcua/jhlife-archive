DOT is responsible for ticketing. My advice is to pay forty bucks and get a New York State inspection. Assuming you value your time at more than $15 an hour, if you waste three hours on this you've already made a bad financial investment. Any time added to this is simply putting principle above practicality. If your boyfriend is quixotic by nature or retired with plenty of time to wage this campaign I don't think you'll be able to stop it.

On the other hand, the ticket should have the complaintent's name on it and that is the person who is ticketing your car. If it's the same person over and over again, perhaps you could get someone to talk to them.

I wish you luck and highly recommend that regardless the outcome you relax from the ensuing drama by visiting the big Korean spa over in Flushing.
