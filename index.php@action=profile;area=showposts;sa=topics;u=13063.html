Restaurants & Food / Bamboo House
« on: September 20, 2019, 07:10:28 AM »
I noticed a sign in the window of Bamboo House (opposite Foodtown), "Curry Chicken $4.99 + tax to go only".  I ordered and wasn't expecting very much for $4.99.  The chicken was a very large leg, lots of rice and some greens.  It was a large portion that was enough for dinner.  I was very pleased.  Naan to go is $1.00.  The wait time was 20 minutes.
