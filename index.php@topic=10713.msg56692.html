News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

My husband and I went over to the wine tasting on Saturday night at Addictive Boutique Winery, 3262 87th St (off Northern). We expected to stay only a few minutes, we ended up staying 90 minutes. This is a great place, decorated wonderfully with wooden shelves and table, very relaxed atmosphere. The owners were extremely nice and friendly. We tried four different wines from Spain and learned a lot. There were about 7 other people there and we made new friends and bought great wine. Needless to say I will be back for another wine tasting, our new weekend hangout.
