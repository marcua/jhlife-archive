Of the two Korean restaurants in the hood, which do people prefer: Hae Woon Dae vs. Chung Ki Wa?
Title: Re: Hae Woon Dae vs. Chung Ki Wa
Post by: iheartbigass on February 23, 2017, 07:17:06 PM
I think they are both equally mediocre and not worth the time/money. The quality leaves a lot to be desired and both are missing staple foods that I think are integral to a Korean meal. For example, neither of them serve mixed grain rice, something that I think an actual Korean person would find shocking (disclaimer - I do not speak for all Koreans).

As a testament to their similarity, on yelp 1 has 122 reviews averaging out to 3.5 stars and the other has 121 reviews averaging out to 3.5 stars.
