My favorite subject. Honors and SP honors either in 145 or 230 send a large percentage of kids on to select and specialized HS. My 14 year old goes to Baccalaureate School of Global Education or BSGE
http://www.bsge.org/, up there with Townsend Harris, Bard and Bx.
