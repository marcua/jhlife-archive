Neighborhood Chat / Re: NYPD Mobile Observation Tower
« on: November 30, 2011, 12:09:52 AM »
Elaborate?  Well I posted two incidents which took place in the last few weeks on 85th.  I also have further information from a police contact.

As to the posted crime statistics, often the police do not report incidents in their statistics or the crimes are against illegal immigrants who do not file complaints.   Some of you seem rather upset about a police tower.  Frankly this makes no sense to me especially in light of the crimes that have recently taken place.  Some of you appear to hate the police and some seem afraid to admit that there are crime issues in Jackson Heights. 

After the last mugging a couple of weeks ago (posted above) the police stated that gangs are coming to this neighborhood from Brooklyn to commit crimes mostly against illegal immigrants in the neighborhood because they know they are afraid to call the police.  However, the criminals have grown more bold and have branched out against others.  In particular, there have been gang members targeting people getting off the 82nd Street station.  They follow their victims to the side streets and rob them of cell phones and jewelry.  Cell phones are prized because they can be swiped clean and resold - much better than credit cards.  Car breakins have increased.  The police stated that they are seeing a resurgence of La Famiglia and Latin Kings. 

I think we need to watch out for each other rather than quibble about police towers.  A neighborhood watch might be a good idea.  Anyone have any good ideas about organizing one?
