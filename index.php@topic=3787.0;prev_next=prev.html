The price will include:

Full demolition of walls and ceilings. 

Leveling of your tub

Make all framing PLUM (re-frame if needed)

New Cement Board around tub area and greenback everywhere else

Repair and or Replace valves and shower body (Mixer)

Frame and partially enclose exposed risers

White reinforced thin set over current flooring for tiling

Tile walls from floor to ceilings.
