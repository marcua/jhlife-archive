News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Business Listings and Services Directory / Summer Camp Program at Rainbow Christian Preschool
« on: March 23, 2015, 02:38:47 PM »
SUMMER CAMP 2015

Low Rates for Registration will end by April 30!

Rainbow Christian Preschool at St. Jacobus Lutheran Church * 72-01 43rd Avenue, Woodside, NY * 718-335-3361

We are now accepting deposits for our Summer Program, which will run from Monday, July 6 through Friday, August 28, 2015.  You may register your child for a minimum attendance of any 3 consecutive weeks.  We will offer a theme-based, inter-disciplinary program that includes special events and trips.  Free breakfast, lunch and snacks will be provided, depending on your child's schedule.

You may choose from the following options to best suit your needs:

* Full time (8:30 am - 3:00 pm) - $200/week

* Extended hours (8:30 am - 6:30 pm) - $250/week

* Early arrival (from 7:30 am) is available for an additional $10/week.

All students will be charged an activity fee of $175.00

To ensure a place for your child in the program of your choice we will require a nonrefundable payment that includes both the activity fee and a one-week deposit.  This deposit pays for the last week the child attends our Summer Camp.  It cannot be applied to any other program.  The balance of the tuition will be due on the first day your child attends.  (This amount may be paid in two installments only if your child attends the full 8 weeks of our program.)  Please call to request a registration form soon.  Space is limited!

Questions? Call 718-335-3361.  Visit our website at www.rainbowchristian.com
