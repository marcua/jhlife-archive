News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Jackson Heights map artwork is finished
« on: October 07, 2009, 10:28:38 AM »
Dear friends,

I'm pleased to announce the artwork for the Jackson Heights 2009-2010 neighborhood map is finished! While I await approval on the sponsor's advertisements page, I'm wondering if you would mind taking a look at the inside of the map before it goes to print. I'm currently a staff of one and could use some help catching typos and any other minor mistakes. I also welcome criticism, though please don't let this post turn into a gripe session. This map is my love letter to the neighborhood where I'm choosing to raise my kids and I'm sure it won't please everyone. Just know that my intentions are good. I also apologize in advance to the few businesses I wasn't able to feature. There's a limited amount of space and I'm trying to cover the breadth of businesses we have in the neighborhood. Thanks again for all the wonderful suggestions that I got on this message board! It wouldn't have been the same without you all.

If you want to drop me a line and bring anything to my attention, I'm open to hearing from you: davidmheatley [at] gmail [dot] com.

Here's the link: http://davidheatley.com/images/JH_map_side2_LG.jpg

Looking forward to announcing where you can pick up your printed copies in about 2 weeks' time!
