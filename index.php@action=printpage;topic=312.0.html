Title: Brouhaha at Queens Central Blog
Post by: Chuckster on January 10, 2008, 01:33:56 PM
There's controversy brewing at the Queens Central blog regarding a comment posted on a rumor that Popeye's Chicken would be setting up shop in Forest Hills.  The rumor turned out to be false.  Nonetheless, someone decided to post a racially insensitive comment that has the blog's members debating the right or wrong of the post.  It seems that the OP is a friend of Steve's, the blog's proprietor, and Steve claims that his friend's post is a humorous and edgy attempt to mock racists.  Steve says that he will not delete the post, as some bloggers have requested.
