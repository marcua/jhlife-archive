April Workshop Announcement:

Yin Yoga:
A 3-hour workshop on the practice of yin yoga, also called yogic
acupuncture, or yinsight yoga. Yin yoga is the balancing side of our yang or
energetic/fiery practice. It is yoga for the connective tissue and joints.
During this workshop we will explore the anatomy of the joints and come to
an understanding of what connective tissue is and why it is important to
target in a yoga practice; define the concepts of yin and yang; practice
meditative techniques in preparation for a yin practice; discuss
applications for a home practice; and experience a full yin session. This
workshop is open to practitioners of all levels from beginner to advanced.

When: Sunday April 26, from 1-4pm.
Where: Garden School, 3rd floor music room
Cost is $40 and must be prepaid in advance (snailmail me a check to 86-10
34th Avenue, #523, Jackson Heights NY, 11372 or drop it off at one of my
classes - see website link below for times).
18 participants maximum.
