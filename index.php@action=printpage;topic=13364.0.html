Title: Re: Best Hamburger
Post by: Jeffsayyes on June 15, 2015, 05:28:06 PM
I bet they do a good burger at Cuckoo's Nest, they do everything good.


I can't be impartial bc I work with them, but the Momo Burger at Little Tibet is made with grilled momo meat as the pattie, topped with tomato, frizzled onions, homemade mayo, inside a buttered, grilled, steamed tingmo. Forget being ethnic, it's a good burger, period. It's not on the menu, you have to ask for it. There are a few other secret menu items too, the ones I know are the desserts - Tsampa cake which is pretty much like pie crust with ice cream on top, and then this balled dough with paneer, butter, and sugar.
