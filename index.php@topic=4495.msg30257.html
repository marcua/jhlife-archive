News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

It's time to vote!  Since I'm about to leave on a road trip for the weekend, I'm closing this contest 5 hours early... if you submit something between now and midnight tonight, I'll add it to the poll when I return on Monday.

Please keep in mind that some of the images have been severely compressed to fit into the website's upload restrictions.  Higher-quality versions of these photos are available, and will be used for the final banner.

Please also keep in mind that the final image we use will have approximately the same proportions as the banner we're currently using.  So any standard-sized photo will need to be cropped significantly.  But a thin slice of a squarish photo can still look great!  Once a winning photo is selected, we will work with the photographer to find the best way to crop the image for the banner.

Apologies to any contestants who don't like the names I've given to your photos... I'd be happy to edit them on Monday if you let me know that you'd like a change.

Voting will remain open for two weeks.  Good luck, everyone!
