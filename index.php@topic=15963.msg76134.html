There is already a thread on cable companies in a different section,  so review that thread before switching. You should be able to get good enough Internet from your cell phone provider to view your course work.  You might have to upgrade your service to an unlimited plan,  but if you get rid of your cable or Fios Internet, the extra expense would be offset. You may need to pay extra for tethering or Hotspot access if you have to use a laptop,  but you could also just buy a 4G USB modem for your laptop instead.  Something to consider, anyway.
