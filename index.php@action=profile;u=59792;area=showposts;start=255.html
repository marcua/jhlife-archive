Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

College Scholarships from the New York Conference of Italian-American State Legislators

Once again, the New York Conference of Italian-American State Legislators will award four scholarships (two academic and two athletic) at this year's Italian-American day in June. The four scholarships will be for $2,500 each and are available to residents of NYS that are current college students or high school seniors entering college.

If you or someone you know is interested in applying, please contact Assemblyman DenDekker's office at (718) 457-0384 or in person at 75-35 31st Ave, Suite 206B, East Elmhurst, for more information and to recieve a scholarship application.

Completed applications are due no later than Friday, April 8.
