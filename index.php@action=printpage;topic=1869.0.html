Title: THANKSGIVING DINNER WITH A FRENCH ACCENT
Post by: Griswold Contessa on November 20, 2008, 10:15:55 AM
   My favorite French Bistro Tournesol in Long Island City has sent me their Thanksgiving menu and it looks quite interesting.  If anyone is planning to go out for their Thanksgiving meal this year this may be the perfect choice.   The atmosphere is pure Parisian, the food wonderfully prepared along with an amazing eclectic wine list.   BON APPETIT !

TOURNESOL 50-12 Vernon Blvd. LIC, (718) 472-4355
