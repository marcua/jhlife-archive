Post by: E train on February 13, 2014, 01:39:39 PM
I think that film The Naked City(1948) should be added to the list. I haven't seen it in a while but I do remember a scene that I am sure was roughly in Jackson Heights.

The film itself is very good with a gritty location feel that is capped with an exciting chase finale on the Williamsburg Bridge. There are also some sequences on the subway which show some kind of pot bellied stoves near the turnstiles. Directed by Jules Dassin,written by Albert Maltz both in their final innings before the blacklist took them down.
