guys, I got all this good beer for Little Tibet (I'm the bev director). Focusing on American, hedge local, and ones that are probably familiar to South Asians. Also I love bottle conditioned.


We are keeping the prices low right now because we want people to get used to drinking here regularly - also it is an experiment to see if Tibetans are going to drink something other than budweiser and heneiken if it's the same price.

https://www.beermenus.com/places/19864-little-tibet

also feel free to come and just drink with friends. no need to order food.
