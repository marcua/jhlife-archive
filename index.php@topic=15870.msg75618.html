JHBG's graffiti-removal team, like all of our endeavors, is volunteer and does not work in the winter because graffiti removal involves spraying lots of water under high pressure to remove chemical solvents - something that winter weather is not conducive to doing.
The city has a program to remove graffiti, but I'm not sure of the extent of its work. May want to check with Councilman Dromm of Ferreras offices. Good luck.
