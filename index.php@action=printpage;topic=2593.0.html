Title: Electronics Recycling this Saturday
Post by: aandrews on April 14, 2009, 08:11:23 AM
Saturday, April 18 is Electronic Recycling Day at Travers Park.  Bring your old computers, TVs and VCRs from 10 am to 4 pm to 78th St. next to the park and the Lower East Side Ecology Center will recycle them.  For more information, visit www.lesecologycenter.org.

-Alexis Andrews
