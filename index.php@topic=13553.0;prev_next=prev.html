Her name is Maria Sroka and she sits with my dog each evening until I get home from work, sometimes quite late. (he has separation anxiety and while we probably overcompensate, she keeps him company. She walks him, feeds him, brushes him. If my husband and I have to go into work on weekends, she is most often available and will take care him until we can get home. She is loving, responsible, educated and cultured lady, who does pet sitting to bring in a little extra income, since she is retired. she always puts our dog first, and treats him with respect and care. She calls herself a pet nanny, and she is indeed. My dog loves her and we have grown very found of her too. We hope to see her after we move, but we hope that she can find a another family that will appreciate her as much as we have. She walks dogs, cat sits, takes car of birds and fish and waters plants. She can be reached at 718-898-0440. She has references besides ours but please feel free to send me a personal message if you want to know more about her.

Moving to Riverdale, for an easier commute on Metro North. Too many late mornings. With our long hours, my husband and I need a more reliable, easier way to get back and forth. If we were coming home at an earlier time, we'd probably not be making this decision.

Jackson Heights is a very lovely community, which got a bit dog-eared over the years. Gentrification is a definitely a mixed bag for any neighborhood, but hopefully it will bring with it better supermarkets, cleaner streets etc. for the people who live here. In my ten years here I have meet many wonderful people, which is JH's greatest strength. I will especially love the JH dog people, who create a micro-social community of their own.

Maria is one of the loveliest, kindest and most responsible pet sitters I've ever had. She has lived in Jackson Heights since the '50s. My dog adores her, and I am sorry we can't manage to have her continue walk/watch our baby. We hope to bring her up to our new apartment to watch him on long weekends when we can't take our dog with us. I can't recommend her enough. she is more than a sitter, she is a friend to the animals. Again, please feel free to reach out to me, for any questions. I'll be in JH for a few more months.
