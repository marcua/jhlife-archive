Ice Cream Truck Season is here again and I wonder if anybody has any idea about how to get the noise level controlled.  

There is one that sits diagonally in the intersection in front of Las Margarita's at 84th St pretty regularly and today spent about 25 minutes diagonally in front of La Nueva (Hello........Hello..........you know what it sounds like every couple of minutes.)

I sent an e-mail to our councilman and got a polite, seemingly interested response, and then wrote again to ask if police officers were actually supposed to ticket this behavior, but I never got a second response.

I thought the city had a rule about no stationary ice cream truck music.  Is anybody else bothered by the continuous jingle, or is it just me?
