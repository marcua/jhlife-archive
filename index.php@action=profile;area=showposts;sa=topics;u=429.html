Community Groups, Activities & Events / REVIEW OF TINY TADPOLES SWIMMING LESSONS AT GARDEN SCHOOL!
« on: July 20, 2015, 03:20:19 PM »
Tiny Tadpoles is a Red Cross certified infant aquatics class held at the Garden School’s outdoor pool on 79th street between Northern Boulevard and 34th Avenue in Jackson Heights.   Margaret Murray, an instructor with over 15 years of experience, leads ½ hour classes on Thursday afternoons and Saturday mornings over seven weeks. Classes are grouped based on age, with one class for children 6 months to 2 years, another for children 2 to 4 years, and one mixed age class for children 6 months to 4 years.

Margaret is warm and her classes aim to familiarize children with the water through music and activities.   There is a lifeguard present for the duration of the class.  In my child's class, Margaret led us in song while demonstrating movements such as bouncing, pulling, kicking, floating and blowing bubbles.  Using toys, Margaret encouraged the children to pull and reach.  Margaret also performed an exercise with each child herself with the parent close by to foster independence in the pool.

As the class approached 25 minutes it became very clear to us why the class is only 30 minutes long.  Attention spans had reached their max, as did each child’s tolerance for the chilly water. We closed with another song and quickly retreated to the sunny patio to warm up.

Overall the class moved quickly, kept the children entertained, and accommodated children of different readiness levels. By the end of the class my three-year-old son was practically leaping out of my arms to swim on his own.  We are definitely looking forward to the next class!

For more information contact Margaret at Tinytadpoles@hotmail.com

Here are some of the specifics:

$260 for seven classes.  Make-ups available.

All tadpoles must be accompanied in the pool by an adult.

All tadpoles must wear a swim diaper unless they are potty trained.

Classes will be rescheduled in the event of rain.
