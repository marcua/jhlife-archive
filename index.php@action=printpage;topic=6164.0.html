Title: 1st Merit Scholarship Exam for the Lower Division of Garden School on May 21st
Post by: taggie on May 15, 2011, 02:48:42 PM
 For the first time ever, the lower division of Garden School will offer a Merit Scholarship exam on May 21st, 2011 for students currently
enrolled in grades 1 through 4. See the Garden School website for details and application. The deadline for applying is this Wednesday, May 18Th by 5:00pm. Approximately 35 students in the Upper Division are already receiving some type of scholarship and this is a chance for the younger students to participate as well. Good luck everyone!

 PS- results will be quick. you will know by May 28Th
