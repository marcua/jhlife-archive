Real Estate & Home Improvement / Re: AptDeco?
« on: August 18, 2016, 05:38:48 PM »
I've sold many items on Apt Deco as well as Chairish and have good experiences with both.  Apt Deco is only local (I think) while Chairish will ship all over the country.  Chairish is more design oriented and also more expensive.  Both websites are easy to use.  I got a good price for all the items I sold minus a reasonable cut for the companies.  I liked the delivery people from Apt Deco, very professional and on time for every pickup.  I'd recommend both websites if you are looking to make some money off stuff you don't need anymore.
