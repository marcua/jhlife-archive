Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Just spoke to Steve Kulhanek, chair of CB 3, and he confirmed what several people have said - There is no need to sign up in advance to speak at tomorrow’s public hearing on the proposed redevelopment of the landmark site at 37th Avenue and 85 Street. Just show up.

BTW. I might have been in favor of this proposal if this was just filling in one low site in a continuos stretch of 5 and 6 story buildings on the avenue, but it is not. This will set a precedent for what will happen on seven other half-block and full-block stretches in the landmark district, including the Post Office.

Developers will jump on those opportunities. And that will be a huge change in a historic district created to protect a NYC garden city. And while everyone wants nice stores - the 40 new apartments is not going to bring in new businesses.

Len Maniace
President, Jackson Heights Beautification Group,
an environmental and civic organization
