Yes I also live at the Griswold and unfortunately we are electric only as well.  Both the Belvedere and the Griswold were designed by the same architect Joshua Tabatchnik and back in the late 30's he felt that a building with electric only would appeal to folks as being safer and cleaner.  I've worked as a professional chef in the past cooking on electric was something I absolutely hated even worse I had to give up my vintage 1950 mint green Chamber stove to move here!!!! In the end I was glad I made the decision to buy here at the Griswold it is not only a beautiful building but a great community to live in!  Recently we purchased a fabulous LG convection double oven and even though I still dislike the burners of a electric stove I've learn to make do.
