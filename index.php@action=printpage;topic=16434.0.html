Title: Historic Jackson Heights Weekend - June 10th and 11th
Post by: jhlifer on May 15, 2017, 10:35:49 AM
Come to the Jackson Heights Beautification Group's annual "Historic Jackson Heights Weekend"
Bring your friends, family, and neighbors
Events are located or meet at the Community United Methodist Church at 81-10 35th Avenue

Saturday, June 10th
  Exhibit of photos and ephemera: 10am to 2pm
  Slide shows: 10:45 and 12:15
  Garden Tour of up to 16 gardens from Noon to 4pm
     Tickets are $10 and available at Community Church beginning at 10am on June 10th
     Raindate for Garden Tour is June 17th

Sunday, June 11th
  Walking tour of the Historic District meets at Noon at the Community Church
  Tickets are $10
  You must reserve or purchase your ticket in advance due to limited capacity

Tickets can be purchased in advance at Espresso 77 beginning May 27th
You can contact the JHBG by calling 718 565 5344 or emailing info@jhbg.org
