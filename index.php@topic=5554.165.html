Updates from the Grow a Park campaign

 
Over $456,000 raised so far!
Dear friends, we have raised over $456,000 in pledges! We need a little more help to reach our goal of $500,000, please reach out to your friends and family, or consider raising your pledge. Let us know at pledges@jhgreen.org
 
As soon as we receive an announcement from the Deputy Mayor's office we will let you know. The news we have heard so far continues to be positive and we're hoping to hear something final very soon.
 
 
78th St Play Street
 
In addition to the Grow a Park campaign, most of you know we have worked to put together the 78th Street Play Street over the past three Summers, where a one block stretch of the street between 34th Avenue and Northern Boulevard is closed to traffic. Last year we enjoyed this car-free street in Jackson Heights for two entire months and this year we are planning to do it from July 1st to September 30th.
 
Along with our partners in the Jackson Heights Beautification Group we're hoping to make it the best one yet, but we need some help getting it off the ground this year.
 
 
Making the Play Street Better
 
In addition to offering more space for our kids to play, we have always wanted the Play Street to be the Public Plaza or Town Square of our neighborhood. We want this to be an oasis not only to families with young children but everyone else in our community - for people who want to get to know their neighbors and socialize, read a newspaper,  participate in a class, attend an event, or who just want to people-watch, relax and enjoy the diversity of our community in a public space where everyone feels welcome.
 
In order to achieve this, we want to make Play Street a neighborhood-wide effort. We will organize a Community Placemaking Workshop in the near future to brainstorm ideas and elicit more support, participation and ownership. We hope the Workshop will help us organize things better, and in a more inclusive way. We want us all take ownership of the space and are looking for your ideas for activities, amenities and program contributions. We'll announce the date, time and location very soon.
 
 
Shade and other amenities
 
We're looking for people who will be able to help us design some of the Play Street's infrastructure, specifically seating and shade. We had canopies last year which provided shade in some sections but they proved to be delicate and labor-intensive (they had to be set up and dismantled every day). We're exploring more dramatic and creative options for this year and we would love to have some help with this. If you are handy with tools or can help design a system of ropes and sails that would provide cover using very limited attachment points please do let us know so we can talk!
 
Thanks for all of your support for the neighborhood!

Dudley
The Jackson Heights Green Alliance
info@jhgreen.org
