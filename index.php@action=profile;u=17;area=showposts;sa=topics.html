Neighborhood Chat / JHELC and the greenspace NEEDS YOUR HELP!
« on: June 10, 2011, 04:12:27 PM »
Hello JHELC parents and community members:
 
WE NEED YOUR HELP!
 
Don't you love having a picnic in the grass on a warm summer day? JHELC is teaming up with Millamar Landscaping to create a  playspace that the children and community will enjoy even more! With your help we can say goodbye to the barren yard and hello to fresh cut grass.
 
 
JHELC  would also like to install 9 ceiling fans in the parish hall  to help circulate the fresh air and keep everyone cool and comfortable.  This project will help not only benefit our little friends at the preschool but all who use this space.
 
You can help us in several ways.
 
-- We are collecting change at the JHELC preschool in our Change for Change money drive.
 
--- You can also donate via our fundraiser website, http://jhelc.chipin.com/jhelc-play-yard-and-ceiling-fans.
 
---Spread the word.  Tell your friends and family about this special cause.
 
Our goal is to raise $5,000.00 by June 23rd.  Yes, we know its a short period of time, but when we work as a team we can do anything! If we meet our deadline, we can get started as soon as the children graduate.  Wouldn't it be wonderful if everyone returned to a great big green lawn!
 
Won't you please help us in this cause?
