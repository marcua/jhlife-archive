Title: Roosevelt Ave Street Crawl - this Sunday
Post by: Jeffsayyes on June 22, 2010, 09:58:30 PM
I posted this in the community board, but it should go in this food section too.

2nd Annual Roosevelt Ave Street Food Crawl this Sunday, June 27th starting at 1pm at the 74th street station. We will walk east into Elmhurst and Corona then end up in Spaghetti Park across the street from the Lemon Ice King of Corona.  The rules are only to not take a step off the pavement and discover some new, amazing food along the avenue.

It's hot, and the higher the temperature, the more of a frenzy Roosevelt Ave becomes. You gotta stay hydrated and healthy, so I just wrote a post about all the drinks available. http://iwantmorefood.com/2010/06/22/refrescos-on-roosevelt-ave-guide-respados-cholados-jugos-naturales-and-much-more/

I will have maps and guides to help you along. Bring your friends or make some here and we'll get groups together to go along and eat some tacos, tamales and whatever else you find. http://iwantmorefood.com/5-boro-pizza-tour/
