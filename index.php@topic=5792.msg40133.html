In order to keep the doors open Queens Pride House launched its SAVE QUEENS PRIDE HOUSE campaign.  Through a combination of austerity, volunteers, new funding, and fundraising we have pushed the viability of the center forward one year!  We exceeded our fundraising goal in 2010 and raised $12,000!  Our goal for 2011 is $20,000.
