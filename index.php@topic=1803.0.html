Private Viewing - The Roosevelt Terrace One Bedroom - Saturday, 11/15 1-2:30
Call in Advance for an Appointment

Located in The Historic Landmark District of Jackson Heights you will find one of the greatest co-ops, The Roosevelt Terrace, which consists of four modern buildings built in 1955, and specifically designed as co-ops. The buildings were built on a beautiful tree-lined street with each building having an outdoor catwalk hallway, which leads directly to all apartments on every floor and each apartment has its own private terrace. This charming gem is a renovated and newly painted one bedroom, one bathroom unit at approximately 800 square feet. Located on the fifth floor with an impressive refinished balcony, which runs the length of the apartment you will find a huge living room; dining area; kitchen with brand new appliances; three closets total, one which is a walk-in; new carpeting throughout; closed circuit TV camera in lobby; three in-door parking garages; two outdoor gated parking lots; two outdoor sitting areas; one enclosed gated playground, for residents only; storage room; bike room; one baby carriage room; evening security guard; newly refinished lobby; super and handyman on premises 24 hours a day; manicured gardens; newly refurbished elevators; porter on site daily; co-op board meetings held on premises; management on site; board president on site; separate service entrance; and a block from all shopping and a few blocks away from the 7 train on 82nd. You may also walk to the 74th Street/Roosevelt Avenue Station and access the E, F, R, G and V trains, and the bus depot.

ASKING: $179K, MAINTENANCE: $548, DOWNPAYMENT: 20%
MARLENE FLORES, SALES ASSOCIATE, CENTURY 21 BEST, 917-208-6709
