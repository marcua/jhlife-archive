Neighborhood Chat / Giving Tuesday is today! Think local, JHBG, for example
« on: December 03, 2019, 07:41:46 AM »
  Giving Tuesday is a way to help your favorite non-profits continue their work into the next year. So
many chances have happened in our world recently and it's not always top of mind to think of all that
local volunteer groups do to keep our neighborhood being a vital and beautiful place to live.

  The Jackson Heights Beautification Group has been working in the neighborhood for 30+ years.
The group sponsored over 100 events in the JH last year alone. Did you like the new stargazing
at Travers Park? The Summer Sundays concerts? The JH Orchestra? All the incredible gardening on
37th Ave, the Post Office and our schools? These are a sample of the reach of this all volunteer
group. Please donate at www.jhbg.org and better yet- our big celebration is this Monday Dec.9th
at the Queensboro and all are welcome. Tickets also available on the website.

   We hope to see you there!

Community Groups, Activities & Events / JH Cyclists speak out on the issues, a JHBG podcast in 2 parts
« on: October 03, 2019, 12:26:30 PM »
 Jackson Heights Beautification Group is presenting another in its series of local podcasts, this time on cycling here in the neighborhood. Locals Christine Furlong and Laura Newman, both avid cyclists and parents, are prominent activists for safety here in Jackson Heights and across the city.

Find out more about the pleasures and terrors about this ever growing method of transportation here in NYC. Learn realistic rules of the road and some surprising laws that we need to uphold. These passionate ladies have a lot to say and are truly building a cyclist community here in
Jackson Heights. Did you know MTA bus drivers can be your cycling friends? Oh, and find out about events like the Santa Ride which is one of a number of sponsored events in the local biking community.
