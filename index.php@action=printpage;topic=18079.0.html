Title: Free Reclaimed Windows
Post by: Palermo on July 30, 2018, 06:38:32 AM
Spanish Gardens has been renovating units after renters move out and have been throwing out window panes.  I grabbed five of them, one of which I used and one of which my friend used.  I have three which anyone can come and take for free.

The windows are 6 pane, old style looking, roughly 34" x 28" in size.  I don't know if they are 1920's originals, but they certainly don't look like anything recently produced. The wood is in relatively good condition, a bit flaky in some areas, and all of the glass is intact (each pane is about 9"x13" and is separated by about an inch of wood).  They have a brown paint on the exterior side and a white on the interior.  I wiped them down with alcohol so there are no issues of critters. 

I've seen these things run for about $25 at reclaim/junk shops and are good for any number of DIY projects.  They are free for the taking. They aren't light, but certainly can be handled by a single person.
