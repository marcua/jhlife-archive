I'd like to chime in and praise Ultima Florals on 37th as well.

High quality neighborhood business.

I had great success with Ultima.

I usually do telephone orders with them, and they have always come through for me.  The people there are friendly, professional and get the flowers out right on time, in perfect condition.  They are also extremely discreet when putting the special, personal messages on the cards that accompany the delivery. 

I have also seen special, custom arrangements done for weddings and other special events; beautifully arranged, stylish and interesting. Nice thing was, they didn't look too stiff or scary, "FTD arrangement-y". In addition, those particular arrangements looked like they had sourced special flowers and containers to met specific customer requests.

Seems like they will do anything for anybody, and definitely carry a selection stock goes far beyond the pedestrian selections found in the local delis, etc.

Give them a try, it's well worth it.
