And my definition of a good gym is one that offers group fitness classes. I can live with having to bring my own towel, shampoo, etc. but all the gyms in the neighborhood are geared towards people who don't need or want group fitness training.

Planet Fitness has "classes" for abs or "The Biggest Loser workout", but they are just supervised circuits. I'm sure there's value in that, but some people really are much more motivated to workout if they have to be a certain place, at a certain time, following a workout created by an instructor with some actual knowledge about the body and how to exercise, comparing yourself to others in the class.

JH has one of the BEST boot camps around: Kiki's group, aka Traditional Tribal Fitness, which meets EVERY Sat. and Sunday 8am and M, W, R at 6am.

The site is so-so, but here it is: http://traditionaltribalfitness.com/

1st class is free, then $20/class thereafter, or get a 10-punch card for $150. All ages and skill levels – it's super fun!
