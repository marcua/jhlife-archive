Title: Powerhouse Gym-LIC location opening soon on Northern/40th Ave
Post by: Shelby2 on March 25, 2018, 01:55:08 PM
It's been a source of frustration to me that we can't seem to get a really good gym to come to Jackson Heights (I have looked into Blink and Planet Fitness and opted to continue to going to NYSC in Astoria).

For those of you who are already traveling to a gym outside the neighborhood, you'll soon have another option in LIC on Northern Blvd, which is not that hard to get to by subway from Jackson Heights. I'm not sure if they will have parking.

This is an article from 2017 but they've been posting recently on instagram that the facility is almost ready to open.
