"The Notebook"

Another film that is surprisingly satisfying is this one.  The Notebook is a romantic drama that will definitely deliver to any romantic viewer.  An older man (Duke) reads a story from a notebook about a couple, Allie & Noah (duke is Noah). Noah's indescribable love for Allie who suffers from dementia is amazing and moving. Allie wrote their love story down in a notebook and gave it to him with instructions to read it to her on days she couldn't remember their relationship due to her disease.  With this in mind she believed that her memory would come back as Noah read her their love story.

The movie is subtle and unassuming.  The plot is clear and the actors where great.  Some critics didn't say much every person that have seen it will likely tell you how amazing and moving it was.  The story culminate3s with both of them dying at the end in the same bed (this was the part they could have improved).  See the movie, you will not be disappointed and if you are a softy like me you'll shed a couple...LOL

Starring


Ryan Gosling
Rachel McAdams
James Garner
Gena Rowlands
James Marsden
Joan Allen
