As a new resident of donner gardens I feel an obligation to post in this thread as well. Before I visited the apartment I ended up purchasing, I read the very negative threads on this forum and nearly didn't even go see it. I was pleasantly surprised when I visited the building to find that most of the nasty things said about the place were not accurate.

Donner Gardens is a working class coop full of working class people. There aren't the amenities of the famed garden coops but there are the basics and some extras: a laundry room that functions well, my garbage/recycling room is generally clean but occasionally people don't follow directions and put things where they don't belong, there's a nice little park at the side of the building, and there's a parking garage which seems to have many available spots(I'll soon be applying for access).  In my experience, maintenance does a very good job of maintaining the common areas. I have never seen any of the filthy conditions that were mentioned previously. However, maintenance is pretty much useless for things that happen in your apartment. If you have a problem you better have a lot of time on your hands to repeatedly call the office or they just won't come. I've had two issues where I called and called and they simply never showed up.

Although the policy is no pets for owners, the apartments still occupied by tenants from before the building went coop are allowed pets and many have dogs. I don't have any complaints about this except I think it's pretty unfair that the owners must deal with the inconveniences of pets(barking, poop on the sidewalks) but we cannot have pets of our own. I hope to campaign for amending this rule at the next board meeting.

On pests: I have heard whispers of some tenants having bed bugs but thank god I haven't had that problem. As a previous poster said, you can't really fault a building for that. As terrifying as it is, you could really get bed bugs anywhere. The building provides exterminators if you sign up. I have seen a dead cockroach in the garbage room so I've been super careful about keeping my place clean.

To conclude...
Positives: affordable purchase price and maintenance
The renovated apartments are really nice, some with great views
Enough amenities for comfort
Effective maintenance in common areas
Convenient to public transportation

Negatives: maintenance could be MUCH better handling apt issues
The pet policy is unfair to owners
