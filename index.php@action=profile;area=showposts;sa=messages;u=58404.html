News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

"Music for Yuletide"

A candlelit concert of works written to celebrate the season by Sammartini, Biber, Lalande, Dandrieu and others in an intimate concert setting in Jackson Heights.

The Queens Consort:

Claire Smith Bermingham, baroque violin
Margrét Hjaltested, baroque viola
Anneke Schaul - Yoder, baroque cello
Aya Hamada, harpsichord

Guest artist: Marina Fragoulis, baroque violin

Saturday, December 16,  2017 at 7 pm
St. Mark´s Church
33-50 82nd street, Jackson Height, Queens

This is a one hour concert with no intermission.

A festive reception after the concert in the Parish Hall with wine, food and good cheer.

Tickets at the door: $20/$15

For more information:  www.queensconsort.com

The Queens Consort is a professional early music ensemble playing on period instruments and based in the borough of Queens. Our mission is to bring engaging chamber music programs of the baroque period to our vibrant communities of Queens.

The Queens Consort is a fiscal project of Gotham Early Music Scene INC, a 501(c)3 corporation. Donations are tax deductible to the full extent of the law.
