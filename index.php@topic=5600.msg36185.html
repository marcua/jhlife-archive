This 900sf fourth floor renovated unit in the Berkeley building has great morning light and faces the quiet garden. The Kitchen features new floor to ceiling cabinetry, stainless steel counters and appliances while the Breakfast Alcove's corner window provides a sunny morning coffee spot. A separate Dining Room, Bedroom and sunken Living Room have retained the original plaster ceiling cove detail, picture rail molding, and oak floors with decorative mahogany inlay. New oak radiator enclosures with modern details have been added in addition to integrated bookshelves at the Living Room and Breakfast Alcove. The renovated Bathroom with its original deep tub and separate walk-in shower features new fixtures and updated tilework. The unique semi-circular master bedroom offers ample closet space.  A small office space provides privacy.  All original doors have been stripped and refinished and updated with new hardware.

This pet friendly co-op elevator building has a very healthy reserve fund and features an interior garden, updated security system, laundry in the basement, free bike storage room, rentable storage lockers, community room, two live-in superintendentâ€™s and a very attentive staff of porters.

Ideally located within the historic district, it is a quick walk to the main subway/bus hub at 74th Street/Roosevelt Avenue - a very easy 20-minute commute to midtown Manhattan and a 15 minute drive to LaGuardia airport. Post office, 24-hr grocery, fruit/vegetable purveyor, pharmacy, drycleaner, and bank are all within a 1.5 block radius.  Other neighborhood amenities include year round FarmSpot, Travers Park Farmer's Market, nearby Dog Run, FreshDirect delivery, Zipcar, Espresso 77, Starbucks and the 82nd Street shopping district.
