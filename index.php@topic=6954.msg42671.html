HI Jackson Heights Friends,

Just wanted to share from me and and my partner- because we're shocked and angry-- Please do not use Mexicana High Class Car service in Queens.  They have HORRIBLE HORRIBLE MEAN SERVICE.

It's Christmas Day, my sister, her two little kids, and my senior mom get into a cab.  The driver is rude, starts driving BEFORE the KIDS are buckled in.  My sister asks him to stop, and he is mouthy and mean.  He doesn't listen to my mom's directions.  (It's not a language barrier, because we are Spanish speakers).  When we arrive at our location, he over charges us TEN DOLLARS over the quote. My sister refuses to pay that much, especially after being treated so badly.  She then proceeds to call dispatch, who are just as rude and argumentative.  Finally after minutes on hold, they find the guy that gave her the quote, and they accept the original price.  The driver then says, "Get out of my car, you're wasting my time." (WHAT!?) She gets my mom and her kids out of the car, and as she is unloading CHRISTMAS PRESENTS out of the trunk, he starts to CLOSE THE TRUNK ON HER HANDS.  She stops him and confronts him, and he SHOOS HER AWAY.  It's horrible, misogynistic, and disgusting.  We live in Jackson Heights, and will NEVER use them again.  We encourage others to also not use their services.  Hopefully they will learn how to treat people better - especially a woman, her children, and her elderly mother.  On xmas day.  It disgusts me and makes me sad.

Please don't use their services.  I called to complain, and all they did was put me on hold.

Thanks all.
