Restaurants & Food / Re: Recommended cookbooks for Chinese/South Asian cuisine
« on: August 25, 2013, 03:42:06 PM »
Just came across this post.  These books have always served me well:

The Modern Art of Chinese Cooking by Barbara Tropp is a good start.
Any of Eileen Yin-Fei Lo's books:  The DimSum Cookbook, The Chinese Way, The Chinese Kitchen
The Wisdom of The Chinese Kitchen by Grace Young
Asian Noodles by Nina Simonds
Chinese Cooking: The Classical Techniques Made Easy by Norman Weinstein
And for something a little different: The Classic Cuisine Of Vietman by Bach Ngo and Gloris Zimmerman
Authentic Vietnamese Cooking: Food from a Family Table by Corinne Trang and Christopher Hirsheime
Taste Vietnam - The Morning Glory Cookbook by Trinh Diem Vy
