Restaurants & Food / Re: what happened to La Gloria? The bar/lounge on Northern?
« on: September 26, 2010, 09:38:32 PM »
Went there this morning for Brunch.

Really nice looking space, very well decorated. Has a great ambiance, and they have outdoor seating!

Food was excellent and reasonably priced. Cuban sandwich was one of the better ones I've had. Too often what people claim to sell as a Cuban is something entirely different. This is pretty authentic. Mangu (mashed plantains) good too.

Looking forward to checking out the bar at night for some Meringue/Salsa.
There's a lack of decent night spots here, this looks like a good option.
