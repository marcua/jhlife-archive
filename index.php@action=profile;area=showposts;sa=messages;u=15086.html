Neighborhood Chat / Excluded Workers Fund
« on: September 13, 2021, 10:56:11 AM »
For undocumented workers, financial assistance is available via NYS for residents who:
-Have lived in New York State since before March 27, 2020 and continue to live in the state
-Lost at least 50% of their income income between February 2020 and April 2021 due to COVID-19 for reasons like losing their job, losing hours at their job, or being unable to work because of illness from the virus
-Did not receive unemployment insurance
-Earned less than $26,208 in the 12 months before April 2021
-Had a person who was the major source of income in their household die or became unable to work because of illness from the virus

For further info. and list of orgs. helping to access this assistance, visit—
https://jhimmigrantsolidarity.org/excluded-worker-fund-application/
