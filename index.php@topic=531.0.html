Jackson Heights Art Club

Club Gallery
33-50 82nd Street
St. Mark's Church (lower level)
Jackson Heights, NY

ABOUT US[/b]
Since its beginning in 1948, the Club has been committed to furthering the knowledge and enjoyment of visual arts among the people of Jackson Heights and adjacent communities in western Queens.  We accomplish these objectives by maintaining an art studio and teaching facility which offers classes in all media and by presenting a year-round calendar of visual arts events including indoor and outdoor art exhibits and painting demonstrations.



EVENTS

1.  OUTDOOR ART SHOW

In the Spring and Fall, The Jackson Heights Art Club holds its Outdoor Art Show called "on the fence" off PS69 located on 37th Avenue between 77th and 78th Streets in Jackson Heights.  For two weekends each season from 10:00 AM until 5:00 PM, members and nonmembers are invited to exhibit their works for a small fee.  The exhibit features both framed and unframed paintings in oil, watercolor, acrylic, pastel, mixed media and photography.  The event is open to the public free of charge.
 

2. ARTIST DEMONSTRATIONS

About 4 times a year, the Jackson Heights Art Club presents a demonstration by a guest artist at the Club Gallery located at 33-50 82nd Street, Jackson Heights.  The demonstration is open to the public free of charge.



3.  ART INSTRUCTION

The Jackson Heights Art Club offers watercolor, oil, acrylic, pastel and drawing classes for both adults and children's at the Club Gallery located at 33-50 82nd Street in Jackson Heights.

Adult Classes

There are classes throughout the weekdays and are day, or evening, depending on the medium.  The classes are 8 week sessions and cost $80 for members and $100 for nonmembers.  The Club also offers some specialty 4 weeks classes.

Kids Classes

Multiple children's classes are offered on Saturday and Sunday at various times.  An 8 week class is only $55.



The Club currently has more than 100 members and meets at St. Mark's Church located at 33-50 82nd Street in Jackson Heights, NY.

Membership in our Club is open to artists and non-artists at an annual fee of $60.  Whether you are a working artist, an aspiring artist or someone who enjoys the art of others.  The Jackson Heights Art Club is the place where you can satisfy your urge to create, meet others in the artistic community, and learn and grow.  Watch on the Jackson Heights Life calendar for more information on our full schedule of classes, demonstrations, exhibits and other upcoming events!
