Typically, stick to the Tub but in a Studio you can do it and not worry about the "kids & tub" rule of thumb. If you are doing a walk in showe stall...stay away from those plastic prefab crap, unless you are buying the fancier one with multiple jets, radio and special lighting.  Instead, Build one with smaller 4" x 4" or smaller on the floors and larger on the walls.  Maybe make a "Drama wall" on the water closet wall.

As a Broker I can tell You...Bathrooms and Kitchens sell your apartment, it's not rocket science, but if your bathroom is older, try to do a complete Gut-Rehab so you can get a squarewall and ceiling.
