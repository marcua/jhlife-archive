Brown or yellowish water is caused by rust in the water. The variation in shades is a matter of chemical oxidation in the iron and by concentrations of the rust (iron) in the water.   Most likely, you are not going to get sick from NYC's finest.  Really - in some places, even a car smacking a hydrant can cause your water to get weird.  

If you notice the water is discolored turn it off.  Grab a stop watch and run it again. Wait 2 minutes, fill a glass up.
If the water is clear then your plumbing is probably the problem.  Ask the Board to call a plumber.  Now, If the water is discolored after running for two minutes, then the problem is probably coming from a distribution point.  Call 311 FAST!!
