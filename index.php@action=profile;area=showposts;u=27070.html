News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Community Groups, Activities & Events / FREE Energy Efficiency Workshop Orientation - Chhaya CDC
« on: January 02, 2014, 12:08:37 PM »
Chhaya CDC, a local non-profit organization, will conduct a free information session for Queens homeowners seeking to reduce their utility bills, make their homes more comfortable, and do something positive for the environment.
 
Chhaya helps homeowners apply for free home energy assessments and incentives for energy upgrade work. At this info session we will explain what an energy assessment is, how much energy our homes waste, and what you can do about it. We will go over programs that provide incentives for homeowners to "green" their homes, and help you apply. There are grants that provide up to $25,000 for home energy upgrades!
 
You can RSVP to 718-478-3848, extension 15.

Tuesday, January 7, 2014
5:00 PM - 7:00 PM
 
at
Chhaya CDC
37-43 77th Street, 2nd Floor
Jackson Heights, NY 11372

Chhaya CDC is launching its Fall ESOL Class beginning September 16th, 2013. We will be conducting registrations on Thursday September 5th & Friday the 6th from 10 AM – 2 PM.
