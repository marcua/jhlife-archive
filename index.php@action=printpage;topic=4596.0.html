Title: Re: Daily News doing article on dog park - Photographer today at 5:30 - bring dog!
Post by: MarcusW on May 12, 2010, 04:16:45 PM
This was something that was decided on at our last meeting, and voted on by all attending to approve as a part of the overall park rules list - although this, like many of the rules we have, are certainly subject to change. Part of the concern was that many have been in dog parks with children with very inattentive parents (reading, texting, on the phone, talking with friends) and seen children running around essentially unsupervised, sometimes engaging with large dogs in potentially dangerous ways. However, your point is certainly a valid one.

We fully expect to refine and customize our rules for our park (since it's not a city-run dog park, we can change our own rules when we deem necessary) and if this is something you feel strongly about, we should certainly open up for discussion. We don't have any future rules meetings planned, but I'm sure that soon after we open the park, we will absolutely do so to adjust our park rules to fit our park.  We'll continue to make sure everyone signed up for the website (http://jhdogs.ning.com (http://jhdogs.ning.com)) knows about upcoming meetings, and have an opportunity to raise any concerns ahead of time if they will not be able to attend.
