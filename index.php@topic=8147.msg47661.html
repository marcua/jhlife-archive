New iteration of Italian Farms
« Reply #8 on: March 04, 2013, 07:42:15 PM »
I stopped into the new and improved Italian Farms today on 37th near 82nd and was delighted. The space is bigger, and the owners have stocked more Italian product and even put in two cute little cafe tables near the front. They were beaming as locals crowded in to wish them well and get the advertized free cup of coffee. There was real excitement in the air.....people  bitch on this blog about one thing or another, but now we have a Polish deli, a Russian deli, and a really fine new Italian market within a few blocks, not to mention the stock at Lemon Farms and the new Two Brothers as well as old stand-bys, Growing Farm, and Kim's. If you don't eat well in this neighborhood, you are really not paying attention....
