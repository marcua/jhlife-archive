Book Culture is now open in LIC
« on: December 08, 2017, 11:35:48 AM »


Book Culture, an independent bookstore with three locations in the Upper West Side/Morningside Heights area, just opened its fourth bookstore—this one in Long Island City, at 26-09 Jackson Ave (right on the way home for lots of us in JH). According to their newsletter, they will be open (tentatively) till the "early evening" today, with regular hours starting tomorrow:

Monday - Friday: 9am - 10pm
Saturday: 10am - 8pm
Sunday: 10am - 7pm

Queens now has three independent English-language bookstores, up from zero (by my count) just five years ago:
  • Astoria Bookshop (31-29 31st St, Astoria)
  • Kew & Willow Books (81-63 Lefferts Blvd, Kew Gardens—weekend hours only)
  • Book Culture (26-09 Jackson Ave, LIC)
(For used books, there's also the tiny Turn the Page Again, at 39-15 Bell Blvd in Bayside.)

Yes, we still need one in Jackson Heights (to complement Libreria Barco De Papel, which is great)... but let's keep these all open for starters! (Astoria Bookshop, for one, lets you order almost any book to your door right from their website... something that's nice to do if you're ever tempted to order online—you can still shop local. https://www.astoriabookshop.com/)
