Community Groups, Activities & Events / Free Childrens Sunday School program at the Community United Methodist Church
« on: January 27, 2012, 10:26:36 PM »
Interested in introducing your child to Bible stories and other fun Christian activities? Join us on Sunday mornings at Community United Methodist Church here in Jackson Heights, for a free Sunday School program for ages Pre-K through 6th grade. We meet each Sunday morning from 9 to 11:15 to learn Bible lessons, sing songs, create arts and crafts, and participate in dramatic play. Older children also attend the first portion of our Sunday morning service. Snacks and prayer time close out our time together. Teachers are provided for several age-based classes. To learn more, contact the Reverend Delois Davis at 718-446-0690, ext. 106. You can register your child in Room 7 at the church (Community Church at 81-10 37th Avenue) on Sunday mornings.
http://www.1churchnyc.com/
