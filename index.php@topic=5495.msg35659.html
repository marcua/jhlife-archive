Thus far all the documents relating to the resale of the unit have been delivered to the buyers attorney and she is satisfied with the financials, and all relevant information.

The building is kept extremely clean at all times, the building manager is quite efficient, and so is the legal assistant of the company.

I didn't want to reply to this as you said you wanted UTD reviews. I agree anyone will paint a pretty picture but until you've lived there you don't know. I have Nothing postive to say about Stephen Hall. I've lived there years ago and it looks the same now. They do not have a staff adequate enough for two buildings. I haven't heard anything good about their management company. I actually heard how bad they were to their renters. I also know if you have an apt facing the front the noise is horrific. The traffic and bus pollution is disgusting. The windows get so dirty and cannot be kept opened. There is also no yard/garden to enjoy. The laundry room is in one building and if you live in the other you have to go down the front stairs down the block up the stairs and into the basement of the other building. The basement is also very creepy. Nobody can make your decision for you. You have to weigh the pros/cons and see what works for you.
