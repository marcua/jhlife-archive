Neighborhood Chat / Great, Inexpensive Massage Found!
« on: December 26, 2010, 04:51:36 PM »
I've been in need of a massage for awhile, and I've been passing this place and meaning to stop in.  The blizzard altered my plans for today and I found myself nearby with some free time.  

The name of the place is "Magic Spa N.Y. Inc. and it's located right near the 61st Street 7 train stop in Woodside.  I believe the most prominent sign from the outside says "Chinese Body Work".  There are curtains on the door so that you can't see in, but I never see anyone walk in there and I think it's because they are not well-known.

They advertise "walk-ins welcome", so I walked right in and asked for a 60-minute massage.  I don't think they speak English very well over there, but I had no problem communicating my basic request.  I was led into a small curtained area.  The place is no-frills, but was clean and quiet.  I did not hear any background noise during the entire massage, despite the lack of real walls.  

My massage therapist was a man named George.  He put on some relaxing music, and showed me the timer to confirm the 60 minutes I requested.  The massage was very relaxing.  At times I felt the pressure was a little bit much, but I chose not to say anything because I think he was working out some major knots I had in my shoulders and lower back.  

The best part--the price:  $48 for an hour-long massage.  Their menu shows that you can do a 15-minute for as little as $18 and it goes up to $92 for a 2-hour long.  They also offer acupuncture, hot stone massage and foot reflexology.  I will definitely be back!

Magic Spa NY Inc.
61-15 Woodside Avenue
Woodside, NY 11377
718-424-8081
