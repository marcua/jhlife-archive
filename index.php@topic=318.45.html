Got take-out tonight from Don Alex, the new spot on 37th between 84-85. Delicious! The beans and rice were flecked with pimentos; the chicken was moist and flavorful; and the hot sauce was delicious. Fast and friendly service. They are new so will try and spread the word. Slightly more expensive than the fast-food chicken places but not by much. And with all the small touches adding up, it feels like you're getting a less greasy, mass-produced chicken dinner. The flan looked good, too (that's for next time.....)
