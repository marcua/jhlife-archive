News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Tonight's Wine Tasting
« Reply #26 on: November 06, 2015, 11:52:20 AM »
Introducing LUCATONI of Chile at tonight's wine tasting.

Dear neighbors,

Every now and then we come across certain wines that leave us in awe. This week, we tasted a series of LUCATONI wines from Chile and became obsessed with not just the wines, but the label itself. Carlos Iglesias is our good friend and also a wine importer. After his son Luca Antonio was born last year, Carlos looked for an opportunity to dedicate a wine to his son. Carlos captured a perfect moment of Luca Antonio tipping his little toes and looking outside the window. He decided to memorialize that image onto his wine label, and name this series of wine LUCATONI. See picture below. Please join Carlos tonight as he is pouring these very personal wine.

Pour time: 6:30 to 9:30
