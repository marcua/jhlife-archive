Sappori D'Ischia is great. Featuring food from the island of Ischia off the coast of Naples (near Capri), it's definitely worth a visit. Trattoria L'Incontro is also another great find (order the specials - filet mingon ravioli!).

Just went Manducatis in LIC (13-27 Jackson Ave). It's been there since 1977. From the outside it doesn't look like much, but once you walk inside it's like stepping into a different world. Multiple stone fireplaces, a vintage piano flanked by bottles of wine (the wine list is fabulous and overwhelming). The waiter was able to suggest something excellent within our price range.
