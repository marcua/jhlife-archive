Both Doctor Guidera (female) and Hate (male, pronounced Hah-Tay, though the other way would make for a great supervillain) are great for pediatrics, and they're at 7053 Broadway, phone number 718-426-6655. Dr. Halac is the old guard in that office, I've never met him though.

For dentistry I've loved Dr. Shapiro at Symphony Dental on Northern Blvd., with his Sunday hours, thorough approach, and honesty (I found him after a dentist in Manhattan scheduled two cavity fillings for me that smelled fishy... Dr. Shapiro properly recognized the areas as mere gumline plaque and scraped them off). However he broke his hand late last year and opted to retire instead. The office is still open with replacement dentists under his supervision, but I haven't seen any of them yet. I plan to though.
