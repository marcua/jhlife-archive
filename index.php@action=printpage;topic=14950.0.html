Title: Historic Jackson Heights Weekend - June 11th and 12th
Post by: jhlifer on June 06, 2016, 09:41:05 PM
The Jackson Heights Beautification Group's 26th annual "Historic Jackson Heights Weekend" takes place this Saturday and Sunday.

Note: the gardens are ONLY OPEN ON Saturday.  Also, we are short one walking tour guide, so if you are interested, you MUST reserve your ticket by Saturday.

Saturday, June 11th  (Rain date for Garden Tour: June 18th  -  all other events rain or shine)

Slide Lecture on the History of Jackson Heights
       At 10:45am and 12:15pm at the Community Church, 81-10 35th Avenue; FREE

Exhibition of Vintage Photos/Memorabilia
   From 10:00am to 2:00pm at the Community Church, 81-10 35th Avenue; FREE
   View original photographs, marketing brochures and newsletters that document
   the early history of Jackson Heights.

A Self-Guided Tour of Private Interior Gardens
From 12 noon to 4:00pm.  These park-like private gardens, surrounded by 1920s-era
garden apartments, are only open to the public once a year.  Visit up to fifteen gardens at
your own pace, following a map included in your ticket; Tickets can be purchased in
advance or in front of the Community Church, 81-10 35th Ave, on Saturday morning; $10

Sunday, June 12th

Escorted Walking Tours of the Historic District  (Rain or shine)
   At 12 noon. Tickets must be purchased or reserved in advance.  The tours will meet in
   front of Community Church, the southwestern corner of 82nd Street and 35th Avenue; $10
     

Special Ticket Pricing - Save Money by Attending Both Tours!
Tickets are $10 for one tour, $15 for both tours
Tickets may be purchased in advance beginning May 26th at:
Espresso 77  35-57 77th Street
Beaudoin Realty Group 78-27 37 Avenue – Suite 5 Second Floor – Weekdays 11-5

For ticket information and directions leave a message at (718) 565-5344
