Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Double Parking on 73St & 37th Ave.
« on: May 21, 2018, 04:48:11 PM »
It appears that the NYPD is starting to take action about the terrible congestion on 73rd St. and along 37th Avenue between 72nd and 74th Sts. On Sunday afternoon, I saw a uniformed officer walking along those streets shooing away double parked cars. There were no fewer than eight on 73rd alone. He also was at the intersection of 73rd St. and 37th Ave. monitoring drivers who block the box.

The idea that anyone will ban out of state plates from parking is insane. We have a constitution that prohibits discrimination against residents of other states who want to engage in commerce. The parking ban for commercial vehicles is only for overnight vehicles. We are addressing daytime double parking. The only solution is either to close the stores (or prohibit new stores) or create more metered parking. There are blocks along 37th Ave between 69th Street and 73rd where there are no meters. Half of 73rd Street including commercial properties don’t have meters. You can park a car next the subway entrance for 5 lines on 73rd and not have to move it for one week. This is a waste of public resources.
