Tonight is a regular 115th Precinct Council Meeting.
The last one (last month) the big topic was the Salvation Army Church Block Party. The event was fun however, there was a major issues that impacted the residents in the area. This month I suspect a major discussion will be the recent events including gangs, attacks and neighborhood safety.

The leader of the Precinct is an appointed Inspector and he attends. Also other officers who are involved with youth services, gangs and other issues. There are also representatives from the Community Board and the various elected officials. First there are reports from the Precinct and the residents who attend become involved with the topic. I am sure there will be lots of discussion tonight. I also feel that this will be important meeting to attend.

The Townhall on the 26th, will also be a place to discuss the recent issues (i.e.gangs). There will be some review of the issues and events. There will also be some reporting of actions taken and plans that will be put into place.

The Precinct Meeting is always a good one to attend. I highly recommend attending tonight and on an ongoing basis.

The 115th Precinct Council Meeting summary:

The Police reviewed the situation. They mentioned that there are several gangs in the surrounding area. There were members of one gang harassing a young man in the park. They thought he was a member of another gang. At this point there was nothing to report regarding any gang membership for the young man who was stabbed.

The plan going forward is to increase patrols around Travers park as well other other sites in the area using the gang unit, the drug unit, other special units and regular patrols. Some of these patrols will be in plain clothes to be unidentifiable but they will be there.

There will another meeting next month.
