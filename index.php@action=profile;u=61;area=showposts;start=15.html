Neighborhood Chat / Re: Coming to live in Jackson Heights
« on: November 27, 2007, 09:04:46 AM »
Hi Dzollo,

I guess if you're a young person who is moving to the area, it isn't so bad. I will be honest with you that it is quite noisy particularly during the weekends especially around Roosevelt Ave. It is good if you are looking for inexpensive yet delicious food from different regions of South and Central America (definitely a big help if you're on a budget). Seeing that you speak Spanish, it is a plus since there is a large Spanish-speaking community. The area is mainly made of a blue-collar families and is relatively a safe area but I would be careful to not wander around too late at night (I think that goes for anywhere in general).

Hope you enjoy your stay in JH!
