Title: How to Start Your Own Home Yoga Practice:
Time: 3hrs - from 1-4pm
Date: Sunday, September 27th
Place: The Garden School, 3rd floor music room.
Whoâ€™s it for? Beginners, Intermediates, Advanced - (please - no absolute beginners ie: brand new to yoga)

Workshop Description: There are many benefits of a yoga practice - greater strength and flexibility, peace of mind, emotional well-being, a little mental clarity, a little less mental chattering, self-awareness, and a greater sense of spirituality. But, as with any worthwhile practice, in order to reap the benefits, you have to practice, practice, practice. A home practice, whether it's daily or three times a week is a wonderful goal but it also can be an intimidating one. What poses or asanas should I choose? What sequence or sequences? What will help me with my back? What will help me with my hips? What time of day should I practice and for how long? Do I need music? Should I change every day or do the same practice? And ... where do I start?  In this 3-hour workshop we will: map out what you need and what you need to do in order to succeed; conduct a benefit/barrier analysis; learn how to sequence your own yoga practice; learn about different kinds of home practices; and learn three 15-20 minute sequences to help you get started. Class size will be limited to 22.
