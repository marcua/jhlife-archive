Restaurants & Food / Farm Spot CSA Sign-ups for 2020 Season
« on: March 31, 2020, 11:01:32 PM »
Hello neighbors,

Farm Spot CSA is now open for sign-ups for the 2020 season.

Sign up here: https://csa.farmigo.com/join/farmspot/summer2020
 
The season runs from June 4th to November 12th, 2020 (total of 25 weeks).

Farm Spot has been Jackson Heights' CSA since 2006. If you're not familiar with CSA (Community Supported Agriculture), basically it is purchasing a share in a local farm. Farm Spot is partnered with Golden Earthworm Organic Farm, located in Jamesport, Long Island. Each member pays before the beginning of the season for a share of the harvest, and in return receives a box of vegetables (and optional fruit) once a week, to be picked up at St. Mark's Church on Thursdays between 5:30-8:15pm. CSA supports small, family-owned farms and boosts the local economy. Members receive fresh, seasonal, organic produce directly from the farm (no middle men).

For more info visit our website: www.farmspot.org
For questions, get in touch at: farmspot@gmail.com

Best wishes,
Sonya Gropman

#4 FARMBRIDGE FROZEN VEGETABLE SHARE

To sign up for #4, a frozen share, please follow this link:
http://thefarmbridgeshares.csasignup.com/members/types
(please scroll down to the "Jackson Heights Share"

The frozen share will receive a total of 35 items spread out over the four deliveries. The share consists of frozen items which are either individually quick frozen (IQF) or pureed fruits/vegetables. The IQF process freezes each kernel of corn, each berry, which not only maximizes flavor, but allows you to parcel out what you need (without having to hack off a chunk from a solid block), and put the rest of the package back in the freezer. We freeze at the peak of freshness, often hours after the farmer’s harvest.
