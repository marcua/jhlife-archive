News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

From http://www.mta.info/busco/advisories.htm

Quote
Queens

Q101R (Q100) CUSTOMERS
EFFECTIVE SUNDAY, APRIL 6, 2008

The Q101R route between Long Island City and Rikers Island will be re-labeled the Q100 route.

There will be no changes in service or bus stop locations.

Other MTA Bus routes being re-labeled in April:
Q9A will be re-labeled Q89, effective 4/7/08
Q19A will be re-labeled Q69, effective 4/20/08
Q19B will be re-labeled Q49, effective 4/20/08

Q10 CUSTOMERS
EFFECTIVE SUNDAY, APRIL 6, 2008

In response to increased ridership, the hours of the Q10 Limited-stop service will be expanded.

Sunday service will be added, and will operate from approximately 10:30 am to 7:00 pm.

Weekday hours of service will be extended to operate from approximately 6:30 am to 9:30 pm.

Saturday hours of service will be extended to operate from approximately 6:30 am to 7:00 pm.

There will be no change to the Q10 Limited travel path or bus stops.

Q10 local service will continue to operate at all times and serve all bus stops.

Q9A (Q89) CUSTOMERS
EFFECTIVE MONDAY, APRIL 7, 2008

The Q9A route between Jamaica and South Jamaica via Merrick Boulevard and Linden Boulevard will be re-labeled the Q89 route.

There will be no changes in service or bus stop locations.

Other MTA Bus routes being re-labeled in April:
Q19A will be re-labeled Q69, effective 4/20/08
Q19B will be re-labeled Q49, effective 4/20/08
Q101R will be re-labeled Q100, effective 4/6/08

QM18 CUSTOMERS
EFFECTIVE MONDAY, APRIL 7, 2008

To better serve the South Ozone Park community, the travel path of the QM18 in South Ozone Park will be revised.

The QM18 travel path will be revised to serve 135th Avenue between Lefferts Boulevard and 130th Street, and 130th Street at North Conduit Avenue, instead of using Lefferts Boulevard south of 135th Avenue.

This change will discontinue service to the stop on Lefferts Boulevard at 150th Avenue. Customers may use the stop on Lefferts Boulevard at 135th Avenue instead, or transfer to/from the Q10 (transfer is free with a MetroCard), which will continue to serve the stop.

New stops on the revised travel path:
Westbound Pick-up Stops
(to Midtown, Manhattan)    Eastbound Drop-off Stops
(from Midtown, Manhattan)
130 St & N Conduit Av    135 Av & 122 Pl
135 Av & 129 St    135 Av & 126 St
135 Av & 127 St    135 Av & 129 St
135 Av & 123 St    130 St & N Conduit Av

QM16 and QM17 CUSTOMERS
EFFECTIVE MONDAY, APRIL 7, 2008

To avoid duplication of bus stops and reduce delays, the QM16 and QM17 bus stops on Cross Bay Boulevard at 157th Avenue will be discontinued in both directions. Customers may use the QM15 at the nearby bus stop on 157th Avenue at 92nd Street, which is a half-block west of Cross Bay Boulevard.

Customers may also continue to use the QM16 and QM17 at the bus stop on Cross Bay Boulevard at 159th Avenue. The QM15 serves the same bus stops in Manhattan as the QM16 and QM17.

Effective April 7, 2008, the QM15 will operate Weekdays to Manhattan from 5:45 AM to 6:00 PM, and to Queens from 7:59 AM to 11:15 PM; and on Saturdays to Manhattan from 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and to Queens from 7:44 AM to 6:44 PM.

Q67 West Bound Customers

Effective immediately and until further notice, the Q67 stop on Borden Avenue at the far side of Van Dam Street has been relocated due to construction. The new stop is located on Borden Avenue, center of the block between 31st place and 31st street

QM16, QM17, Q21, Q53 CUSTOMERS
EFFECTIVE MONDAY, OCTOBER 8, 2007
UNTIL FURTHER NOTICE

MTA Bridges & Tunnels will be closing the northbound ramp to the Cross Bay Bridge from Cross Bay Parkway (Beach 94th Street). The buses will travel via Beach 98th Street to Beach Channel Drive, using the Beach Channel Drive approach to the bridge.

QM16

Passengers to Manhattan using the stop at Rockaway Beach Blvd and Beach 96th Street can board at the following stop:

Rockaway Beach Blvd and Beach 100th Street

Southbound buses will continue on normal route.

QM17

Southbound buses will continue on normal route.

Q21 Q53

Passengers using the bus stops between Beach 98th Street and Beach 94th Street can board at the following stops:

Rockaway Beach Blvd and Beach 100th Street

Rockaway Beach Blvd and Beach 101st Street

Southbound buses will continue on normal route.
