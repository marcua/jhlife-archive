Title: Re: This 2bed/2bath apartment is priced in the $700s....
Post by: agentarmen on June 04, 2019, 05:06:54 PM
It is not unusual for developers to use stock kitchens and rental grade bath finishes to complete projects in order to get a certificate of Occupancy and pass on upgrade project to a purchaser (which is very well the case here). Bigger issue with this one is that the envelope in which they squeezed  a 2 bed 2 bath layout into , would likely more comfortably fit one bed with a good sized windowed kitchen. Since an architectural compromise must have had to be made , they clearly have compromised a functional kitchen. Even if you add an island, you still may have a ventilation issue.
