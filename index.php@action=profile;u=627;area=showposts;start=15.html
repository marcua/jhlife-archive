Restaurants & Food / Re: What do you eat for breakfast?
« on: September 22, 2009, 08:14:31 PM »
On Saturday and Sunday I go to the Colombian bakery on 37th between 80th and 81st, south side of the street near Jahn's.  It has the best coffee in the neighborhood and really good bread that is still warm if you go in there around 7:30 or 8:00 am.  Get the brown bread with the raisins or the lighter bread with guava (I think) jelly inside and some white sugar on top.  Great inexpensive food and very friendly ladies behind the counter even if you don't speak Spanish, like me.
