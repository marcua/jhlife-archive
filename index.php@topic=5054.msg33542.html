Ishwar Center Vegetarian Cooking Classes
« on: August 04, 2010, 12:43:39 AM »
Welcome!

We teach how to cook various vegetarian foods by expert Indian lady cook. More and more people are becoming vegetarian for health reasons. We teach not only pure vegetarian cooking, but many delicious varieties which you and your family would love.

All those who are into health, nutrition and wish to learn a huge variety of pure vegetarian foods will find this the best place to learn. A hands on session with practical demonstration will make things much easy to learn. You get to taste every dish that is made.

Ayurveda says that food is medicine, and the right kind of food is the best remedy for all maladies. Nature has provided humans great nutrititive foods. Eating the right kind of foods, with the right kind of spices works wonders on the human body. You can learn it all, in our weekly teaching sessions.

Homely atmosphere, expert lady cook will teach you every detailed aspect of vegetarian cooking, and also some special desserts, which you will not find anywhere else. Sonali Gupta was featured on Indian National Television as an expert cook. She has already taught many New Yorkers her cooking skills.

Now, she is starting her regular weekly cooking classes again from Sunday, August 8th 2010 from 2pm to 3:30 pm. Please call or sign up for this great opportunity to learn pure vegetarian cooking classes. Classes are held in a private house 1st floor in Northern Boulevard, East Elmhurst. Convenient location in good neighborhood with good transportation around and street parking.

The vegetarian classes are a part of services being offered by the Ishwar Center, a spiritual organization.
 

The class fee is $20 per person. Couples get a discount and will pay only $30 for two. Please bring cash only.


Call: 718-429-1892. E mail: rakesh_ishwar@yahoo.com. Or join us on meet up. website:www.ishwarcenter.org.
