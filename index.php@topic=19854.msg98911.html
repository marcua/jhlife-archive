In case anyone is still looking, I ordered some homemade masks from this place in Brooklyn and got them within a week. I wish they used a thicker fabric for the cotton layers, but they do have a non-woven polypropylene middle layer, which I’ve read is a good filter-type fabric. I’m still very much in the exploring and experimenting phase with masks but thought I’d share since these are at least nicely made and mail right away.
