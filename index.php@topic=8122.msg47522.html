Re: Joju Vietnamese sandwiches, Elmhurst
« Reply #6 on: September 23, 2013, 06:29:53 PM »
I've been there a couple of times.

Their eponymous bahn mi -  the JoJu - a classic with extra pork..... is outstanding. 8/10
Their lemongrass chicken is tasty   7/10
The pork belly is pretty damn fatty.  5/10

Cafe sua da is very good, made with cafe du monde  7/10 ( not _quite_ thick and bittersweet enough for me, but WAY better than the local vietnamese place in the mall  - pho bac, who served me an insipid cafe sua da. haven't tried the pho bang place in same mall.)

Bread is crunchy with a little give like all good french baguettes should be. They don't stint on the pickled carrots or radishes or cucumbers.

I'm a bit of a bahn mi  addict. Their classic would stand up to any in chinatown and a lot in LA.

They're really nice & friendly and have a loyalty card.

Highly recommended overall.
