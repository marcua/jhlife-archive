I've been to Tanger a number of times.  It doesn't pretend to be a fake village like Woodbury Commons does and it is less crowded.  The stores are pretty much the same.  I only find bargains when the already reduced items are on sale.  The Pottery Barn outlet for instance is not that cheap unless they are trying to clear stuff out.  I've bought really, really cheap tables, chairs curtains, etc., but only when they hit clearance prices.  Still, if you want to hit a number of different stores at once, and you have a day to kill, it's a pretty good place.  Before going, you might want to check and see if any online coupons are available.

Everyone I know that's been to Tanger has visited the one in Riverhead.  I didn't even know there was one in Deer Park.  Maybe the others didn't know as well. 

So I visited the Tanger factory outlet in Deer Park.  In fairly light traffic, it takes about an hour from here.  No tolls. 

The place is brand new and the anchor stores have only been there for a year.  I would say about a quarter of the retail space has not yet been occupied.  Most of the activity was in the Christmas Tree Shop.  In all the other stores I went into (Banana Republic, Ann Taylor, Kenneth Cole etc.) there were maybe 2 or 3 other shoppers besides me.  No fights for the dressing rooms.  No lines to pay.  No crowded parking lots.  I even got additional discounts when I asked in two places.  I think they are a little desperate for business!

All in all, it's very easy to get to and if you cannot deal with crowds when shopping, this is definitely your place!!   

http://www.tangeroutlet.com/deerpark

Just wondering if anyone frequently drives to Long Island to shop, and what stores are better out there than the ones we have here.  And by better, I guess I mean more pleasant to shop in, better stocked, better customer service, etc.

And a related question: is the Century 21 in Westbury any good?

As someone who grew up in Nassau County, I can say the stores in the Carle Place/Westbury area are really good. There's pretty much any store you can think of. I've only been to the Century 21 in Westbury once, and it was pretty good -- and much less of a nightmare to deal with than the one downtown. There's also a Kohl's right near there too. And if you need any plants or garden stuff, I highly recommend Hicks Nursery which is also in Westbury (I believe).

As for the outlets in Suffolk county -- it's been years since I went to any of them. I was never impressed with them. I you really want good outlet shopping, then make a day (or weekend) trip to Pennsylvania -- either the Crossings Outlets in Tannersville (right by the Poconos) or the Tanger and Rockvale outlets in Lancaster PA.
