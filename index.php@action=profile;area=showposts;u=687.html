Restaurants & Food / Re: Dosa Place
« on: September 14, 2008, 10:48:32 AM »
I think the latest incarnation of Dosa Diner is as good as the previous ones.  I have to restrain myself from getting take-out from there too often, but it's very inexpensive and delicious.  For about $6, you can get a dosa that comes with some sauces--plenty to fill me up.  I've found the service to be friendly and fast.  I've only been in once when there was a peformance, but on weekdays, it's pretty quiet.  Nothing weird about it--just yummy dosas!  I recently took a friend from out of town here, and he thought it was some of the best food he had in NYC.

Neighborhood Chat / Noise from exhaust fans
« on: September 13, 2008, 05:19:25 PM »
I'm wondering if any of you know whether there is something that can be done about the noise from business exhaust fans.  I live in a building next to the block of businesses on 37th between 86th and 87th (Tomo sushi, Cafe La Nueva, Listo...El Pollo).  While I appreciate the smell of baked goods from La Nueva early in the morning, my bedroom overlooks Listo El Pollo, and along with the other restaurants, the 24/7 noise from exhaust fans is driving me mad.  I've read about the noise-blocking windows; however, I rent, and I don't think my landlord would be amenable to paying for such expensive windows.  Would 311 be able to help this?  I can't imagine that all businesses in NYC use such incessantly loud fans on the roof.  Are there noise regulations that might apply?  Thanks for any help on this.
