Title: Storytelling Home Concert on Sunday, 9/30 - a few seats left
Post by: nancygoldman on September 15, 2018, 08:22:50 AM
Everyone,

We're launching the second season of "Come Over For Coffee," a series of storytelling home concerts to be held on Sundays at 6:30pm on 9/30, 10/28 and 11/18. It's a great way to meet others from the neighborhood while being entertained and enjoying some home cooking.

If you've never been to a storytelling home concert, it takes place in the living room with about 25 people sitting around (without a campfire), sipping beverages, and listening to 3-4 performers tell stories (usually non-fiction, but sometimes folktales). We have a sofa, chairs, meditation cushions, and pillows. We're featuring Refreshments by Connie (aka My Mom). Her homemade noodle pudding, potato omelettes and chocolate chip cake are still being talked about. Oh, and heads up, we have a cat (Milo, he's very sweet) and bird.

We're asking for $15 donations to pay the performers but it's just a suggestion.

Please email me at heightshub@gmail.com for reservations and our address. Seating is limited.

Nancy Goldman
