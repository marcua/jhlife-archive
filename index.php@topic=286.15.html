Very interesting list Chuckster!!!

About 10-12 years ago my parents were on a cruise, and the only way to communicate with passengers on the ship was via fax, which the ship was able to recieve when they were at port.  I thought it would be fun to send them a "Happy Anniversary!" fax, which would be delivered to their cabin while they were docked at some exotic port.

So I went up and down 37th Ave & Roosevelt through the 70's and 80's in search of a place that would send an international fax. 

I had never sent an international fax before, but with literally dozens of places in my neighborhood claiming to send faxes, and clearly catering to an international crowd, I didn't think I would have a problem.  I walked into place after place.  They always looked really surprised to see me.  And the fax machine was broken.  In every place.  Well, in some places the person who worked the fax machine was out sick.  I walked into dozens and dozens of storefronts... many of them named on your list. 

It was then that I realized there were things going on in storefronts here that were not what they seemed....
