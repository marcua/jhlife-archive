Community Groups, Activities & Events / Training on Public Benefits assistance and volunteer opportunity
« on: December 02, 2010, 04:02:36 PM »
The Jackson Heights-Elmhurst Kehillah - a local community based organization the provides social services and helps fight poverty - is looking to expand its public benefits access service (currently the organization helps people access or solve problems with public benefits from 10am - 1pm on Thursday's at the Jackson Heights location, and from 10am  - 1pm on Tuesday's at the Lefrak City location).  We are seeking bilingual volunteers who will receive extensive training on benefits assistance (this presents a good opportunity to get into a new line of work!)

The volunteers will be trained through the Community Service Society's ACES program (http://www.cssny.org/services/aces_vita_financial_coaching_corps/).  They will receive training in Medicaid, Food Stamps, SSI, Public Assistance, housing, and many other government benefit programs. After successfully completing the training course, the volunteer must provide 4 to 6 hours per week of volunteer service at the Kehillah, helping local residents access much needed benefits.

For more information, email anat@anatgerstein.com
