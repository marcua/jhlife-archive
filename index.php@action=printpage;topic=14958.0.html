Title: Re: PS 212/Ps 69 question
Post by: 718mom on June 09, 2016, 08:39:36 PM
My children have been in PS 69 since kindergarten.  My daughter is in honors, there are 32 kids in her class.  My son is not in honors, there are 28 kids in his class. Teachers in honors are usually awesome, non- honors teachers are hit or miss.  PS 69 scores are dropping because teachers there are teaching the lessons out of the teacher guides and they are not really teaching the standards.  The male gym teacher really can't stand children.  The administration can be really unhelpful when you desperately want to help your child.  There are some fantastic educators in that building and some duds.  Overall, I am extremely satisfied with my daughter's experience and I am sickened by my son's experience.
