Hair salons/stylists
« on: October 29, 2015, 02:04:35 PM »
I've searched, but didn't see an earlier thread on this subject.

I'm looking for a hair stylist that really understands hair. Someone who looks at your hair texture, volume, and thickness before you are sent back for a shampoo. Someone who understands you have to look at how the hair falls on a head before deciding on the type of cut to achieve the look a client wants.

I have thick, wavy hair, and so far the hair cuts I've gotten in the neighborhood haven't been great. I suppose if I blow dry my hair straight all the time they'd be fine, but since I'm more interested in wearing my hair with it's natural wave/curl, I've ended up with cuts that don't take in to account the amount of hair on my head and its volume.

Any recommendations in the neighborhood? I'd much prefer to stay local for my hair cuts. Plus, my stylist in my old neighborhood left the salon.
