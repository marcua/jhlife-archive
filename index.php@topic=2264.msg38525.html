I don't recall who sent me this joke.  It was about a year ago and I recall that I translated it then.  I emailed it to myself and I just read it again about a week ago.  I did my best in translating it.  I think it lost a little bit (maybe because I edited it into a PG13 from an X rated version)

During a court hearing in a small town somewhere a lawyer calls a witness, a very old lady from the neighborhood.  The attorney was concerned that the jury would think the witness would be too old to be sound of mind so for his 1st question he decided to ask a very simple question so as to not throw the old lady off.  Lawyer looks at her and asks, "Mrs. Brown; Do you know who I am?
 
The old lady squints a bit and answers, "Yes, of course I know You..You are Danny, Mrs. Rubin's son.  You really turned out to be a real disappointment to your poor mother.  You are a manipulative man, constantly lying and stealing from people.  You cheat on your wife and have been known to be pretentious, pompous and abusive.  Yes, I know you Mr. Rubin...ever since You were a baby" 
 
Perplexed, The lawyer, attempting to downplay the unexpected and embarrassing answer quickly looks around and points and ask...Mrs. Brown, do you know the defense lawyer?  Calmly, she positions her glasses and answers...ah, yes, That's little Mickey,  that's Mrs. Garcia's son.  Boy, he is much like you but in addition he is an idiot.  The kid has always been lazy since he was a kid and it only takes a couple drinks to temporarily  get him out of the closet...unfortunately he has an alcohol problem.
 
Mickey has always had problems maintaining a relation.  He cheats on his wife with 3 different women...One of them your wife Danny - You remember right?  Yes, I know him - his mom is not very proud of him neither.  A deep silence comes upon the court room and the judge nervously calls both attorneys to the bench and says to them, "if any of you two son-of-bitches ask this old bat if she knows me?, I will send both to the electric chair"!!
