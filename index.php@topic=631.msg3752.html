I found a really good "Bakery" in Astoria
« on: March 21, 2008, 11:18:34 AM »
I found a really good bakery called Martha's Country Bakery on Ditmars in Astoria. It has all types of pastries from cakes to cup cakes that are similar to the Magnolia famous cup cakes except with a variety of flavors.  The bakery is open late and has seating so you can enjoy a beverage as well as a pastry.  I know its not JHts but, its close enough and I thought I would share.  :smiley6600:
