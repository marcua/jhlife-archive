Title: Discoveries and re-discoveries
Post by: toddg on September 07, 2013, 10:05:07 PM
My family has been in a bit of a restaurant rut, eating too often from old favorites (Pio Pio, Nanking, Delhi Heights, Arunee, Thai Son, etc.).  We realized we needed a bit of a change, so we've been trying to mix it up a bit more.  In the process, we've had some wonderful meals at new places and old ones that we used to enjoy but haven't tried in a long while.


We've particularly enjoyed:
Phayul (37-65 74th St) - Himalayan/Tibetan
Taste of Lahore (73-10 Northern Blvd.) - Pakistani
Dosa Delight (35-66 73rd St) - South Indian Vegetarian
Himalayan Yak (72-20 Roosevelt Ave) - Himalayan/Tibetan

The food at all four was excellent.

The first three are not particularly about service and atmosphere.  That didn't bother me, but they're not necessarily places to take your guests from out of town.

Himalayan Yak, on the other hand, had a very festive atmosphere.  On a Saturday night, it was packed, with several groups (6-8) apparently visiting from outside the neighborhood.  There was some fantastic live jazz (2 guitars + drum), which I was told is there every Thursday, Friday, and Saturday night.

Has anybody else found any overlooked gems recently?
