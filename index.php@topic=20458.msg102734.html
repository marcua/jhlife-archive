Optometrist recommendation?

Anyone recommend an optometrist? Would like someone who actually spends time doing an eye test and isn't in a rush (or trying to hawk me their overpriced glasses). 

I went to NY vision center on 77 by Roosevelt a couple of years ago, and while they were nice people doing good work, they had a huge line of people waiting, I felt rushed, and the tests were rudimentary (e.g. no tonometry, not microscopic examination of the eye)  for someone who was a new patient - I don't think they got my baseline at all for a middle-aged person with long-term vision problems.

Don't care about buying glasses (I buy online).  Happy to pay for a better suite of tests and a prescription.

e.g. the optometrist  should have access to most of these tools....

My recommendation is to go to an Ophthalmologist and get the tests done correctly. I go to NYU on 41st St and 2nd Ave and their staff is very nice and professional and they have state of the art test equipment. A refraction is $55, and your health insurance may cover most of the exam cost if you get a referral from your primary physician.
