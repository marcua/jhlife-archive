Title: "You Are Now the Owner of This Suitcase" returns for 1 night, this Friday in JH
Post by: itsit on May 02, 2016, 07:34:26 PM
  Jackson Heights Beautification Group is proud to bring back to Jackson Heights for just one day You Are Now the Owner of This Suitcase.

  This Friday, May 6 at 7 PM they will perform back in Jackson Heights at PS 149, 93-11 34th Ave.
 
Theatre 167 is ending its second run of You Are Now the Owner of This Suitcase in Manhattan and working with the Jackson Heights Beautification Group and PS 149 they are returning home for just 2 performances. If you did not see it back in 2011, you have a another chance.This is an original play written about Jackson Heights by people who live in Jackson Heights. We are limited to only 99 tickets, so reserve your ticket now at www.theatre167.org as soon as possible. Tickets are $10. There will be some tickets at the door.
