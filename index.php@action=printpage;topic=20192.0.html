I saw on Queens Liberation Project's Instagram page that there's a Coats for Queens drive starting today. None of their 5 drop-off bins are in Jackson Heights, but thought I'd share the info anyway. Hopefully there'll be some drop-off locations in our neighborhood eventually, through QLP or other orgs.

Here's a link to the Instagram post: https://www.instagram.com/p/CGd16nhlzof/

Sunnyside: Sugar Room - 4421 Queens Blvd
LIC: AT&T LIC - 4632 Vernon Blvd
LIC: Bella Via - 4746 Vernon Blvd
Astoria: First Reformed Church of Astoria - 2726 12th St
Queens Village: Hollis Deli - 20602 Hollis Ave

I did see one comment that a person couldn't find one of the bins today, so it might be worth calling the location(s) to confirm the bin is there before hauling your coats there. I'm guessing you can also email QLP at queensliberationproject@protonmail.com to confirm.
