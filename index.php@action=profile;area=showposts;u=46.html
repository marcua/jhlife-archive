Real Estate & Home Improvement / Re: Anybody have feedback on the Wilshire? 74th street.
« on: October 25, 2012, 08:16:05 PM »
You asked this so long ago you probably don't need to know anymore, but just in case... I rented a studio there for 3 years, moved out in 2011.  The building is in a great location and they have a big laundry room with lots of machines.  The studio I was renting was not renovated and the kitchen was falling apart.  I had to have the exterminator spray every month to help with the roaches. I often thought the reason I had so many roaches was because there were so many gaps & openings in the kitchen where they could get in, so it's possible that the apartments in better condition didn't have as big of a problem.
I never had any issues with the school being across the street.  It was very easy to go vote!  But I wasn't usually home during the day on weekdays, and my apartment faced the interior rather than the street. 
I probably would have lived there longer but I wanted more space and I was tired of the roaches.
