Hey everyone, first time here.

This past Monday or Tuesday around 7 PM I saw a big arrest outside with maybe 10 cop cars, some of them real heavy duty, and later an ambulance came. I heard a huge commotion outside but ignored it first, then all of the police cars came and I looked out at it. They grabbed some guy -- looked like a teenager -- and muscled him into the police car. It was a huge scene out on the street, but no one I asked had any idea what had happened. Someone said it was a shooting but I didn't hear gunshots, so I doubt it.

I'm pretty sure I've seen this guy and his group of friends hanging out on these blocks in the middle of the night, shouting and yelling nasty stuff at a girl upstairs inside a building. I'm not positive they're the same people but it looked like them.

Anyway, I was just wondering if anyone knew what happened.
