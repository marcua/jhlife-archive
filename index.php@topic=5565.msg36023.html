    ~~~      Free Self-Defense Classes for Boys, ages 8-14       ~~~      Free Self-Defense Classes for Boys, ages 8-14   ~~~       Free Self-Defense Classes for Boys, ages 8-14    ~~~      Self-defense class for boys and teens teaches violence avoidance and detection, equipping boys with the tools to prevent violent confrontations and how to deal with the threat of physical violence.

Curriculae: Violence avoidance, using talking to de-escalate a situation, techniques to deal with physical confrontation (shock and stop). The techniques will be practical street defense moves (based on krav maga).
 
Time investment: Total 8 classes - Two 50 mins classes a week (weds 5.30p & fri 6.30p), for 4 weeks
Student must be able to attend both classes a week, it is not an either-or option.
 
2011 Class Dates (8 classes):
Weds 12th Jan @ 5:30-6:20p
Fri 14th Jan @ 6:30-7:20p
Weds 19th Jan @ 5:30-6:20p
Fri 21th Jan @ 6:30-7:20p
Weds 26th Jan @ 5:30-6:20p
Fri 28th Jan @ 6:30-7:20p
Weds 2nd Feb @ 5:30-6:20p
Fri 4th Feb @ 6:30-7:20p
 
Cost:
None / Free
 
Min/Max enrollment: 6-12 students.
Enrollment is on a first come, first serve basis. If minimum is not met, the class will be cancelled. When maximum is met, class will be closed.
To RSVP, see website: www.mkdkarate.com for contact information and/or email contact form.
 
Requirement: boys ages 8-14
 
Location: 72-26 Roosevelt Ave, 2nd Floor, Jackson Heights NY 11372
 
Notes: - Class is for boys only and taught by a male instructor, Sensei Orlando.
- Registration by a parent/legal guardian will be required for the child to participate in the class. This will be done at the first class Weds 1/12.
- Any child who isn't registered by a parent or legal guardian will not be allowed to participate.

http://www.mkdkarate.com/self-defense.html
