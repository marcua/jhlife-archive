Title: Jackson Heights Extinction Rebellion at the Queensboro Sunday 3/8
Post by: lisajarnot on March 01, 2020, 11:06:38 PM
Jackson Heights Extinction Rebellion Neighborhood Group will be meeting at the Queensboro next Sunday March 8th from 6 to 8 pm.  We are preparing for Earth Week and have been asked by Extinction Rebellion NYC to round up 100 volunteers for a big arrest action on 4/23.  It will be a historic day (likely the biggest civil disobedience action in the city's history).  We are putting pressure on Chase Bank to divest from fossil fuels, and on the City Council to act on its Climate Emergency Declaration from last year. If you can't get arrested, there are plenty of support roles, and there will also be a non-arrest action to participate in.  Please come out to learn more!
