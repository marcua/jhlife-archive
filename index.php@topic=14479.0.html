Sometimes I think it's because the apartment is an investment property, and the people living there don't give a hoot about cleaning up for the landlord to sell the apartment. I was very interested in apartment where the owners were subletting, and some of the photos on the listing were pretty questionable.

As a former renter of a 3 bedroom apartment who sought roommates on Craigslist, getting a decent picture of a 10 X 11 room is actually hard to do. I lucked out at some point with a roommate who was a photographer, and before she moved out I asked as a favor if she wouldn't mind taking a couple photos of the other two bedrooms. She brought out the wide angle lens and got up on a step ladder to take those photos, and she knew what the best time of day for the light was going to be. (Just think how many crappy photos you have on your phone or in photo albums of people with their face in shadow)
