JUST RELEASED: Our exclusive 4th Quarter 2017 Elliman Reports for the Brooklyn, Queens & Riverdale Markets

Dear Friends,
 
It is with great pleasure that I share with you the just-released fourth quarter 2017 “Elliman Reports” for the Brooklyn, Queens and Riverdale sales markets, the leading resource on the state of these markets.
 
Our market reports are produced in conjunction with Miller Samuel to provide you and your clients with the most comprehensive and objective market insight available.

The Brooklyn sales market continued its long-term brisk conditions with rising prices, more sales and less inventory – remaining the strongest borough overall.
 
Queens continued to show the same pattern of record prices but saw sales slip and inventory edge higher.
 
In Riverdale, which includes the areas of Fieldston, Hudson Hill, North Riverdale and Spuyten Duyvil, we continue to see a chronic shortage of inventory which is holding back supply.

As far as the tax law goes, it didn’t seem to have much impact unlike Manhattan as the market paused last fall. The sales declines in Queens and Riverdale began to decline earlier in 2017 and therefore seem related to low inventory and affordability.
