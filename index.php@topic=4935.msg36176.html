Just walked by last night and noticed that it is closed!

This became one of my favorite spots in the neighborhood...very friendly staff who are proud of their food and encourage you to try the many great items on the menu. Some memorable meals were the Beef Rendang, spicy Mie Goreng with seafood, Spicy Coconut Chicken or Salmon, different flavors of grilled corn, Indonesian spicy sour vegetable soup and Malaysian Pancake just to name a few...So much flavor with great seasonings and VERY affordable. A dish that comes with their delicious and light fried rice/white rice/fries and salad runs you only $6-$9(for the Salmon).

Oh and not to mention their delicious bubble milk tea (especially Taro and Mango)...best bubble tea I've had here or Elmhurst.

On the bright side, the sign did say that they are relocating, but provided no hint of where.

Hope that they re-open soon and close by. Unfortunate, but there is still Java Village and Mie Jakarta in Elmhurst that are both very good and Bromo Satay House and Upi Jaya (have yet to try them).
