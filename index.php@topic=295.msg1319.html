Queens eatery stabbing wounds worker

The Daily News
BY ETHAN ROUEN
DAILY NEWS POLICE BUREAU
Saturday, January 5th 2008, 4:00 AM

A Queens restaurant employee stabbed a co-worker several times in the stomach Friday night, critically injuring the 37-year-old victim, police said.

The attacker, 23, was taken into custody soon after he knifed his colleague at Pio Pio on Northern Blvd. in Jackson Heights at 7:25 p.m., cops said.

The victim was rushed to Elmhurst Hospital Center for treatment. The suspect's name was withheld pending charges against him.
