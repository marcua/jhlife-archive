After reading great things about it, I finally went to Juquila on 83rd st,a few days ago. Having been to Mexico about 10 times, I will tell you it's one of the best, AUTHENTIC Mexican restaurants in NYC. All the tortillas tasted like they are made fresh. Everything tasted incredibly fresh, and flavorful. And there are items on the menu you don't see in most mexican restaurants.

Casa Enrique in LIC, also very, very good, but can be quite a 'scene' on busy nites.

I just tried the new spot at the corner of 83rd and Northern, El Maguey, and found it a lovely experience. The decor is folklorico without being cutesy, and the service was very attentive. The food was flavorful and nicely prepared. The guacamole was really yummy and the mole dishes particularly noteworthy. The mole isn't overly sweet as is often the case.

A really nice addition to the neighborhood! They have outdoor seating for when the weather turns....the owner told us that they are adding new dishes to differentiate themselves from other Mexican places in the area. Worth a try for sure!
