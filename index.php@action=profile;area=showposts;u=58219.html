Real Estate & Home Improvement / Re: Carlton house
« on: December 15, 2015, 01:08:29 PM »
Thanks Minimal4me. Will talk to William.

To be honest, the prices in JH have gone up too fast, but now that I've purchased, I need that to continue. My wife and I have been searching and started with LIC and Astoria. Quickly realized we were late to the party and were priced out. I hear Sunnyside is also pretty frothy (at least Astoria and LIC have stuff to do), but I'm not a fan.

So the next "neighborhood" place was JH historic district. Stuff is getting snatched up so fast in JH - it's crazy.  We were outbid a couple of times. From what I understand Carlton House is a very well run building, good tenants, underground parking, and nicely renovated units.

IMO, the only downside of CH is the hike to the train. Fine if you work in midtown, but if you're down in FiDi, it's almost an hour door-to-door. Ultimately, CH had all the right stuff and the walk to the train is our tradeoff.
