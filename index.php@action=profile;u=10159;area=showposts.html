Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Business Listings and Services Directory / $5 Haircut
« on: April 04, 2013, 08:50:11 AM »
Almost 400,000 New Yorkers are out of work. That's an unemployment rate of 9.1% in New York City.
 
With so many people looking for work, it is important to look your best when interviewing.
 
But how are you supposed to look good when funds are limited?
 
Charles' Hair Stylist in Jackson Heights has been serving the community for over 55 years and is here to help.
 
If you are unemployed and need your hair cut and styled, let us take care of you. Also - If you know someone who is not working and could use a cut, forward this information to them.
 
For only $5 (gratuity not included) you can get your hair cut by a professional stylist so you can look good in your next interview.
 
 
What's the fine print?
 
1 – Prices are as follows:
Dry cut for short & medium hair - $5.02 + tax = $5.25
Dry cut for long hair - $7.18 + tax = $7.50
Wash, Cut, Style for short & medium hair - $11.24 + tax = $11.75
Wash, Cut, Style for long hair - $13.88 + tax = $14.50
 
2 – You must make an appointment
 
3 – In order to receive the special price you must be currently unemployed and must present the Interview Special certificate. You may obtain a certificate by emailing your current resume to info@CharlesHairStylist.com and include Interview Special in the subject line. Resumes will be used for no purpose other than to verify current employment status.
 
For more information about the salon – visit us at www.CharlesHairStylist.com
