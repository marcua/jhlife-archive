Re: Juquila Mexican Restaurant on 83rd Street
« Reply #3 on: March 07, 2016, 10:54:27 PM »
Tried this place out for the first time tonight and came away impressed!

The mole poblano was excellent and unlike many places in the area, the chicken tasted fresh. Others may reheat it, giving it a gamey flavor. Seemed like it was made to order. Also had the barbacoa de chivo, which was tasty and was a huge portion.

Great ambiance to the place with nice decor and leftover details from the former Meson Asturias restaurant that used to be in this space. For a place that is nice like this, it was a pleasant surprise to see prices in line with the Coatzingo's and Tulcingo's of the neighborhood. Recommend eating inside for the best experience - they also give you a nice bean dip to go along with the fresh chips and three sauces when you order your main dishes - green hot sauce, red hot sauce - had excellent flavor and the tamer guac that you get at some of the local taco joints, but with better flavor.
