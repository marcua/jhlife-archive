Dear Artist:
If you live and/or work in Queens, this is for you.  QMAD, Queens Media Arts Development, is launching the initiative A-Lab (Artists Lab), a monthly meetings to foster creativity, social networking, and assistance sessions to compete in the market place.  The event is FREE, but you must RSVP (Include Name, Contact Address, and Discipline): A-Lab@qmad.org.

NOTE: This program has been specifically created for Queens based Artists.  If you are not, pass the word to your friends and colleagues.

Hey there!

I went to the first meeting and wanted to share it with you guys.  The person who will be running the meetings is named Hector Canonge.  He is a contemporary, conceptual artists and quite sharp.  I think he is capable of doing a lot for this community (especially because he is familiar with our needs, being a Queens artist himself).

We basically brainstormed for about two hours and talk about our needs and "Wish List".  We discussed everything from exhibition opportunities, to money, grants, collaborative projects, performance art, publishing a magazine, renting studio spaces and forming a community.

We are going to start with a list serve, a blog and stay connected via the internet. There will be a lot more to do and discuss over the next meetings. I see the importance of this opportunity tied to the idea that it's a lot easier to  progress when you have a non for profit organization behind you, as your "Umbrella" organization.  This way you have more access to resources and sometimes - less bureaucracy.  It seems like QMAD has some tools to help us out as a community and as individual artists, and also-They are eager to help.

I recommend for everyone to join and try to attend the next meeting.

We are so lucky that the meetings take place here, in JH, and that we have our JH arts group too. These two  complement each other beautifully.   I believe that in a year or two, this community will be thriving!!!

Good night everybody and hope to see ye on Sunday!

A-Lab
Wednesday, December 17, 7 - 9 PM
Jackson Heights Jewish Center, 77th St. @ 37th Ave.

QMAD, Queens Media Arts Development, newest initiative for artists living/working in Queens. The A-Lab was created to forge stronger ties among local artists, foster creativity, prompt dialogue, encourage social networking, and provide information assistance sessions to compete in the market place. Through monthly meetings, starting in November 2008, the first sessions will serve to evaluate the needs, presence, and interest of local artists working in any medium.  The gathering of information based on participants input and collaboration will help develop further programs designed specifically to meet those needs.  With upcoming creative workshops and brainstorming sessions, starting in January 2009, the A-Lab will collectively put an exhibition (for Summer 2009) reflecting on issues and themes discussed by the participants.

In addition, the A-Lab highly encourages and supports social activities to take place in local pubs, clubs and restaurants in Queens.  The convergence of the Arts and Community Building is another important component of the A-Lab as members can propose performances, shows, music gatherings, poetry readings, projections, public interventions, and more, in and around the various neighborhoods of the borough.  Our motto â€œQueens is the world, letâ€™s perform in itâ€¦â€
