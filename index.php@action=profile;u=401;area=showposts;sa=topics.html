Neighborhood Chat / FIOS TV...NOT IN JACKSON HEIGHTS
« on: April 14, 2014, 10:59:18 AM »
After reading on this site that FIOS TV was now available in Jackson Heights, I phoned Verizon to inquire about FIOS in JH.  They assured me that FIOS TV was available for my coop building.  Upon further investigation (and many phone calls), I discovered that FIOS TV is not available here.  Verizon has cut a deal with Direct TV to provide service to Jackson Heights.  Unfortunately, most coops will not allow those unsightly satellite dishes.  Guess we're stuck with the terrible (and pricey) Time Warner.

Restaurants & Food / Terrific Pisco Bar on Northern
« on: August 08, 2011, 04:13:21 PM »
Last week I checked out the new Pisco (a Peruvian liquor made from grapes) Bar, Amaru, on Northern.  The coolest, sexiest bar in Jackson Heights.  A beautiful marble bar and tasty tapas--the Pisco-based drinks are all worth trying.  A fine addition to JH.  If this bar was in NYC's Meatpacking District, it would be packed.  Stop by soon and show your support.  According to TIMEOUT magazine, Amaru stocks "sixteen different kinds of the fermented grape spirit, categorized by single varietals and blended versions."
