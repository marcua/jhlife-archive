Title: Summer Programs at PianoVerse
Post by: PianoVerse on May 27, 2011, 07:35:04 PM
This summer, PianoVerse will be having two summer programs that encourage children to explore their creativity through music and art.

Our July Piano Explorations program will take place from July 12-29, and include AM and PM sessions.
July Piano Explorations is designed for beginner pianists who wish to propel their music and piano skill forward through diverse study of piano and be a part of something musical, creative, fun and productive this summer. These classes will take place from Tuesdays through Fridays, for 3 weeks.

You can view more information about the July program here: http://www.pianoverse.com/july-about.html

Our August Music & Art Program will run from August 1-12 (Session I) and August 15-26 (Session II), with classes offered at 9:30AM-12:30PM and 1PM-4PM
Join PianoVerse in a specialized program that fuses music and art. Students will have the opportunity to explore their creativity and create original pieces like painting sound images, building instruments, learning how to write original songs and more. Classes take place Monday through Friday, for 2 weeks.

You can view more information about the August program here: http://www.pianoverse.com/august-about.html
