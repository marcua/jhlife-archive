I second Mexicana at 718-446-8700. They've never let me down. As said, there are a few similarly named limo companies in the area, I think there's a thread about it on here.
I've never used Uber & there are no Yellow cabs here, so I tend to use livery cars. I don't see green cabs driving around as much. I would guess about $35.00.
You can get to JFK in 20 minutes if there's no traffic. There's always traffic. I would leave at least an hour for travel.
