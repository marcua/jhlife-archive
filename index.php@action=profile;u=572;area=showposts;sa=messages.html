Neighborhood Chat / Re: Car Alarms, anybody?
« on: August 17, 2008, 12:07:46 PM »
Hi DC!

Totally with you on this one. Happy to see people reacting to this. I feel like there's a general numbness about car alarms going off around me and that I'm the only one to even hear it...
Here's what I do: go on this site:http://www.noiseoff.org/caralarms.shtml#action
and you can print a flyer that you put on the car windshield (Ok. this is a big contradiction for somebody who is also annoyed with flyers on car windshield).
This flyer nicely tells the car owner that car alarms "disturbs our daily lives" and lists alternatives.
It's in English only but what are you gonna do.

I put one on the same car several times and I think it helped... Basically, it's a good feeling to DO something about it; try it.
