Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

I imagine most of us in Jackson Heights received a "Happy Holidays from NYSS Hiram Monserrate".   In his wishes he says, "My staff an I are always ready to help you, with any matter of importance, so please don't hesitate to contact or visit my District Office."  I cannot bring myself to write the entire beginning of the note but he also says, I for one, am grateful to have earned another chance to serve as your voice in Albany."

I want to scream!  He and 9 other cowards in the Senate have caused a lot of pain, suffering and delay for full equality of all New Yorkers!  I plan to call him on this!   Help me,  where is the YES vote and helping to move others to say YES last Wednesday?

I will send back his holiday greetings!  All those who feel that he is not representing them should do the same.
