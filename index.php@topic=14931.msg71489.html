In my experience from quiet to noisy -> Freidrich, LG, GE, Frigidaire.

Through wall ACs can be installed in the landmark district on secondary and rear walls with some strict guidelines for waterproofing and finished look from the outside.  If you want the through-wall AC unit on the street side it would need to be approved at a public hearing (yikes!)
