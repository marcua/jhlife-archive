The Donner Garden complex on 81st and 82nd Sts between 34th and Northern has had parking for non-residents for years. However this year the City passed a rule (or at least Donner said they did) that no non-residents could use the area. This has been disastrous for many who've had their vehicles there for at least 15 years.

So, yes, Donner Garden has a garage and now it seems they will have spaces if you buy there.

Terrace View also has a garage but I'm sure there is a waiting list (79-10 34th Ave).

Good luck,
