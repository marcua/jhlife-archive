Title: Zagat Restaurant Survey
Post by: toddg on March 31, 2009, 12:05:17 AM
Voting is now open for the 2010 Zagat Restaurant Survey... visit http://www.zagat.com/survey to register and add your ratings.

In recent years, the survey has had spotty coverage of our area.  In 2009, it listed six restaurants in JH (Afghan Kebab House, Delhi Palace, Jackson Diner, Jackson Hole, Pio Pio, and Tierras), three in Woodside (La Flor, Sapori D'Ischia, and Sripraphai), and a few others around the area.  It has far more extensive listings for Forest Hills and Astoria.  By voting, you can help local restaurants make the cut.  Plus, you get a free copy of the book when it's published!
