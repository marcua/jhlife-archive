first takeout experience tonight:

El Toum appetizer platter - hummus, baba, tabbouleh, falafel. really top notch across the board. the dips were both super well spiced, the baba had a good hit of garlic. falafel very tasty, maybe a bit more crunch-to-soft-inside than i normaly like, but fresh  tasting and made with care.

chicken shwarma platter - also very good.  juicy chicken, generous portion, rice had some personality. salad was, well, salad. a little bummed that they didn't have the pickles/turnips that were supposed to be part and parcel, but maybe next time.
