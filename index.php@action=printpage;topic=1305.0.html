Title: Re: Flushing food stops
Post by: Griswold Girl on September 02, 2008, 10:25:13 PM
#6 on the NY Times List, Yipin Chinese Cuisine, is such a tasty cute little place.  It is a family run business with mom and dad there mostly and their son who gladly translates and answers any questions.  Everything is made fresh daily and when they run out, they run out.  So get there early.  We had yummy dumplings and a fresh chive pancake with two drinks.  Our total was $7.50 for everything and we were stuffed.  Very clean, comfortable and friendly.  Will absolutely go back next time I am in Flushing.
