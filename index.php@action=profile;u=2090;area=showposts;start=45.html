Restaurants & Food / La Triangolo
« on: November 16, 2013, 03:31:54 PM »
We went to La Triangolo.  Superb.  It's a new place with an old world charm.  The owner Mario has paid a lot of attention to detail.  Very warm, cozy, and comfortable feel to the place.  They pride themselves on the food being homemade.  They make their own pasta & sauces.  The owner makes the house wine.  His wife is the pastry chef and makes all the breads and desserts.  No meats of fish are frozen.  All fresh.  Slightly pricey but worth it.  A nice treat.  Make reservations if you go on weekend evenings.
