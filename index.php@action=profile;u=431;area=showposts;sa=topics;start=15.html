We have teachers with extraordinary experience with their own musical careers and education on staff:

Seunghee Han-Director;

 SeungHee is a professional jazz vocalist, who is a graduate of Berklee College of Music and holds a masters degree in jazz voice from New England Conservatory. With over 6 years of Music Together teaching, she gives group/private piano and voice lessons and she was a former faculty member of the Brooklyn Conservatory of Music in teaching voice from teens to adults, while a member of the New York Singing Teachers Association, Inc. She also performs her music in jazz groups around town, with latest album 'Waiting' released in 2008. Check out www.Seung-Hee.com.

Diane DiCroce-

Diane has been a registered Music TogetherÂ® teacher in Jackson Heights/Woodside for the last 3 years, and has participated as a mom in Music Together classes with her daughter Grace since 2004. A Musical Theatre singer/actor/dancer by trade, she was last seen on Broadway in 'Les Miserables' and toured the USA,Canada and East Asia with 'Les Miserables','42nd Street' and 'My Fair Lady'. She received her Master's Degree in Musical Theatre from the Boston Conservatory. Formerly a full-time faculty member at the University of Central Florida, Diane is now a Professor of Musical Theatre at Pace University and teaches voice privately. Diane, husband Ron and daughter Grace happily, musically reside in Jackson Heights, NY!

Melissa Stylianou;

Melissa is an accomplished jazz vocalist, recording artist, and educator, originally from Toronto, Canada. Here in NYC, she has performed at venues such as Birdland, Jazz Standard and Carnegie Hall. She can also be seen on Canadian television on the hit kids' TV show "Foursquare". An experienced educator, Melissa has taught at National Music Camp, Humber College and University of Toronto and teaches voice privately. She is a graduate of Ryerson Theatre School in Toronto with a degree in Acting.
