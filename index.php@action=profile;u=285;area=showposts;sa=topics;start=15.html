Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

celebration on April 20th from 12-4pm. Come see what they are doing with your

Jackson Heights Beautification Group.

Neighborhood Chat / The Daffodils Have Arrived!!! Happy Spring!!
« on: April 06, 2013, 10:17:03 AM »
  Watch the median strip of 34th avenue bloom this week with beautiful yellows daffodils. It's the whole stretch! through our neighborhood from 90th St. down to 69th st. Talk a walk this week and enjoy them as we finally
warm up. If you are so inclined, take some photographs and enter them into the citywide contest of 2013 daffodil pics*. They planted bulbs in the community gardens on 69th st and 90th st and infront of IS145 and PS149 too!

 The people we have to thank for this flora are a collection of community members and local school groups
(JHBG- big thanks!, the NYC Grow Truck, White Castle community outreach, Garden School Key Club Members, PS149,IS230, IS245, PS212, Renaissance School and some home schoolers). Data was collected on the location
of the bulbs and students are invited to check on the survival rates of their plantings. And we need to thank the
sponsors citywide of this project who donated the bulbs- NYers4Parks- as they planted the seed - actually the
bulbs- to start this project of renewal and regeneration after 9/11. Many thanks!

musicians in a concert beginning at 7:00. Let's come out and hear them play in this exciting effort to add
another dimension to our quality of life here in Jackson Heights. Applause given in advance for all the 25
or so musicians who became part of the orchestra and to the Jackson Heights Beautification Group as they
once again provided sponsorship for this local endeavor. Please support them as well as Ms. Glunt and the
musicians for making it all happen! For more info, check out the JHBG.org website. 

 Concert is this Wednesday, Dec.12th at the Community Church, corner of 81st and 35th Avenue, at 7:00 pm
and there is a suggested donation of $10.00 with a wine/cheese reception to follow. Everyone is welcome.
