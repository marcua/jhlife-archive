Great American Art   

Using slides, museum lecturer Joan Hessekiel explores and discusses the changing taste as well as love of country found in American design, paintings, sculpture and decorative art currently housed in the American Wing at the Metropolitan Museum of Art.

Queens Library - Jackson Heights Branch
