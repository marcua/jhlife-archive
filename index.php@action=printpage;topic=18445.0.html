Title: Chinese Classes For Adults
Post by: shazza09 on October 26, 2018, 02:16:43 PM
If you have always wanted to learn Chinese or started and soon gave up, this class is for you.
We meet every Sunday morning from 10-11:15am in Jackson Heights.
Location is: Community United Methodist Church, 81-10 35th Avenue, Jackson Heights.
Anyone can join us and the classes are particularly useful for complete beginners.
All materials are provided and the focus is on having fun while learning the Chinese language.
The classes are engaging and enjoyable, so you will learn quickly!
Please email me if you are interested in coming along to a free trial class this Sunday. (creativechinese1@gmail.com)
