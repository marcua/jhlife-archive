Eddie's Sweet Shop
« on: March 01, 2016, 06:41:43 AM »
It's not in the JH or even within walking distance, but I figured it would be fair for here as Jackson Heighters (or Jackson Heightonians?) have a reasonably likely chance of coming across it.  Maybe a thread can be devoted to non-JH eateries?

Anyway, Eddie's Sweet Shop (originally Witt's Ice Cream) opened the same year Virginia McLaurin was born (1909) and it would be worth going there at least once just to experience it before it turns into a starbucks/bank/99 cent/pharmacy/et cetera.
https://www.flickr.com/photos/8095451@N08/sets/72157623511583600/

My chocolate egg cream was old fashioned and perfect.  My wife's cappuccino was topped with freshly made cream.  We'll be back for the ice cream when it's not so cold out.

We went there before hopping into the Cinemart, another older style place right next door.   If you are someone who can do without stadium seating, over the top surround sound and thick crowds of folks who text on their smartphones during the film, this place may be for you.  Cheap tickets (9 bucks) and free popcorn to boot.   

Located in Forest Hills, on Metropolitan by Ascan.
