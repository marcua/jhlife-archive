I just wanted to share a tip with all the long-time Time Warner Cable customers out there. If, like me, your bills were approaching (or maybe even exceeding) $200 per month for the bundled service of cable+internet+phone, you can call Spectrum and ask to be switched to the Spectrum triple-play package and save yourself about $65 per month for the first year, and then about $20 per month when the introductory price expires. (There's a slight difference in the channels you receive, but I never really watched DIY or Logo anyway.) My bill just went from $187 to $124, and the internet speed is 30% faster, too.
