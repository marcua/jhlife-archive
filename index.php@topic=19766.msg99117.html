Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

We were lucky enough to be away for the winter.  Our original plan was to return to JH on or around April 1st.  But - Since the news at that time was so sad, we decided to visit my sister in NC.  After a few weeks there we decided that we are basically treading water, and we can do that at home in JH. 
Since arriving, I have been happy and relieved to see basically everyone wearing masks. The shopping has been pretty good, and overall it feels like ‘there’s no place like Home’. 
While we were away we were missing a lot of the fantastic food here!  Since arriving we have been cooking daily, with the exception of a Delivery From : Ayata (Thai), Tacos Morelos (Mexican) and Asian Taste 86 (Indonesian)
Everyone here is so lucky to have easy access to incredibly delicious food! 

If you ever leave JH, you will miss it!   
Please give our small local restaurants business during this difficult time
