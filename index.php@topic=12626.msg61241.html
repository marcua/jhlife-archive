Let Us All Praise Terraza 7

And not that it needs any praising.  We all know how good it is.  Been there a lot recently and, as always, amazed at the consistent excellence of the music.  And the drinks.  And the vibe in general.  Great 70's influence fusion jazz band last night--not entirely unlike Weather Report--and I was a little disappointed to find the place wasn't jammed packed, being a Friday and all.  In other words, come out and support this great club, a jewel in our neighborhood.  It would be a shame to see it go away (not that there's any talk of that).

Praise is well deserved. I was there on Friday night. John Benitez the bass player, is one of the best in the city and also runs the open mic Sunday jazz jam session. I think one of the reasons that it was not crowded for the jazz fusion group is people seem to prefer music with a stronger Latin tinge, or a more danceable groove. While I may appreciate Chick Corea type changes and complexities as much as the next jazz guy, I don't think it's that easily accessible and not altogether danceable. Very cool stuff though.  Best venue in Queens I think.  It's our answer to Brooklyn's Barbez ot the Bronx's An Beal Bacht.
