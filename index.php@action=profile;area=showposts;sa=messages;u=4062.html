Neighborhood Chat / Re: TWC internet problems in JH - they are trying to avoid fixing it
« on: September 10, 2016, 05:27:37 PM »
Jackson Res, as an alternative to switching to fios, you might want to have a tech come over and switch your modem. I had that done today after my internet had been out since Wednesday AM. The tech knew immediately what my problem was before he even got upstairs, and told me they remotely upgraded all the Arris modems and they've been having troubles since. He set up my modem and activated the router in the new modem so I don't need a separate wireless router anymore. I was getting at most 17 mb/s, and now I'm getting over 100, as advertised. On Thursday I was pricing fios packages, and now I find myself in the strange position of being pro TWC.
I hope this helps.
