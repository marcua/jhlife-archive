1) Mixed use property

COMMUNITY FACILITY   824 sq. ft.
COMMERCIAL   8,965 sq. ft.

There is really nothing to be done.  The site is zoned as C1-1 commercial in an R7-1 residential district.  This has a particular meaning under the law, and the owners can build something as large as the law allows as-of-right.  If they're not asking for permission to do something outside these limits, we have no recourse. 

As a longer-term strategy, the community could decide that the existing zoning in the whole neighborhood needs to be changed.  But I don't think you'll find an easy consensus on that.  (For example, my preference would be for more housing over retail within a block or two of our transit hubs, and less development of the car-dependent areas north of Northern Blvd.).
