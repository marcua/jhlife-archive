Yes, And...Kids Improv! is a brand new SUMMER CAMP teaching kids ages 6~14 the amazing art of Improv!!

What is improv?
Improv is a type of performance theatre where NOTHING is scripted or rehearsed and EVERYTHING is made up on the spot, never to be performed the same again!

Why improv?
Improv helps to build strong listening and memory skills. It also helps to build self confidence through trust building and teamwork. Plus, you're laughing, being silly, and having fun the entire time!

Spend your summer playing fun theatre games, be silly, pretend to be a moose, an alien, or a tiger!
Just be prepared to laugh a ton!
Make new virtual friends!
Learn short-form improv and perform in a virtual improv showcase!

Who is it for?
Offered to kids ages 6 - 14 (no prior theatre or improv experience necessary)
Shy, outgoing, introvert, or extrovert! It’s for EVERYONE!
