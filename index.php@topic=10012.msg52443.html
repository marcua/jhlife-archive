News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

I know much of this thread is tongue-in-cheek, but who remembers how just a few years ago this board was over the moon because of a rumor about Le Gamin considering setting up shop here? (For those who don't remember, there was a time when their food truck parked at the farmers' market and did a brisk business.)

Back then, no one accused such an addition to the neighborhood of being the ugly side of gentrification.

Speaking of new eateries, 37th Avenue is getting a new take-out Lebanese place (in the space that used to be the eyeglass store and then was subdivided). I talked to one of the guys and told him how we didn't have a good shawarma/falafel place. He replied, "I know, that's why we're here!"
