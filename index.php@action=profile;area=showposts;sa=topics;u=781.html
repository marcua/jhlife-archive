Business Listings and Services Directory / Affordable Acupuncture Clinic
« on: September 16, 2008, 10:13:16 PM »
My name is Samina Quraishi and I am a licensed acupuncturist. I run a community acupuncture clinic, called Queens Acupuncture, located in Astoria. Below is a little blurb that explains a little about my clinic which is different than most other acupuncture clinics. I am trying to keep rates affordable and build a sustainable practice that allows me to treat patients in need of help with whatever they have going on--stress, pain, migraines, infertility, anxiety, depression...the list goes on. Feel free to send me an email or call me for a free consultation.

Queens Acupuncture is a community acupuncture clinic in the diverse neighborhood of Astoria, NY. Our sliding scale is $20 - $40, plus a $10 paperwork fee. It is for you to decide what you can afford to pay. The purpose of our sliding scale is to separate the issues of money and treatment; we want you to come in often enough to really get better and stay better! We understand that everyone's situation is different, and our primary goal is to make acupuncture available to you as often as you need it.

Our aim is to make acupuncture and wellness resources accessible to people at all income levels and cultural backgrounds. We are a part of a movement to create social change in American health care called Community Supported Acupuncture (CSA), a model that empowers patients, builds community and breaks down class divisions.
