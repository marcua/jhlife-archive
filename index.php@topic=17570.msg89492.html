Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Hi gang, I wanted to throw out some updates on the music we're going to feature at The Queensboro over the next couple of weeks!


Tomorrow, Thursday the 11th at 8PM, The Evan Schwam Trio will be playing a couple of sets of funky grooves and soulful ballads.

Evan is a NYC-born saxophonist/multi-reedist who lives in Jackson Heights. He's performed and recorded with many musical giants and he leads groups all over the world when he's not teaching music. The members of the trio are all alumni of NEA Jazz Master Chico Hamilton's Euphoria Band.

Needless to say we're excited about this one!


Then next week, on Thursday the 18th at 8PM we're delighted to host the Goussy Célestin Jazz Trio!



An evening of Jazz standards, blues, with some Caribbean sensibilities. Laid back but with some spice! with Goussy Célestin- Keyboards/vocals, AJ VanSuetendael on upright bass, and Dan Aran on drums.


And finally, don't forget to remind all teachers to come our DoE Happy Hour on Fridays. We are offering discounted drinks to all Department of Education employees from 2:30PM to 7PM!

See you at the Q!
