A Colombian-Venezuelan Musical Event: Llanos Orientales Colombovenezolano

Wednesday, January 16, 2008
The group, Momentos, presents a night dedicated to Colombian-Venezualean music of the Llanos (http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Llanos): its culture, music and history.

There will be live music with special guests, videos, speakers talking about the Llano - its region, culture, music and history. Open mic.

Special Guests:
- Andrea Tierra- Melodia Verde - www.andreatierra.com
- Edmar Castaneda and his harp - www.edmarcastaneda.com
- Ramiro Sandoval, Colombian actor - won best actor in Salvador - www.redyaccion.com/Nov_04/ramiro_sandoval.htm
