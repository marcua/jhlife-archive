Stopped in earlier today for my first JuJu bagel.  Some initial thoughts on my first visit:

+Plain bagel with veggie CC was good.  Much better than Slim Bagels! (Sorry Slim) =)  
+Def the best tasting bagel in the neighborhood.
+The decor was very nice and plenty of seating in the small space.
+Service was very good.
+This will def be my bagel venue going forward.
+Prices were very modest.

-Not sure the conventional toaster that they have is going to do the trick.  Hopefully they invest in a commercial toaster. lol
-I know they have only been open for a few days, but I didn't get the impression that the guy working the counter was going to be able to handle a wave of people (especially with aformentioned toaster).
-Not sure I am a big fan of the curtain they have separating the front space from the kitchen.  I'm a transparency kind of guy, so I would prefer if the curtain was removed... though I must say that even the curtain matches the rest of the decor very nicely.
