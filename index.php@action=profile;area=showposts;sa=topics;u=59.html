Restaurants & Food / best salads
« on: March 05, 2008, 07:59:48 PM »
So, as much as I love to wander the world of food by dining around our wonderful neighborhood, I miss the range of great salad spots (and other healthy food options) from my former neighborhood (the Upper East Side). I've lived in JH about 3.5 years and, still, I'm just sort of wandering around lost when it comes to finding a great salad. I do a lot of cooking, but, from time to time, really just want to sit down and be served a dazzler of a salad. So...your favorite local salads or other healthy-ish restaurant options? I've had an ok salad here and there from Mark Twain; good avocado salads at Pio Pio; a sad sad salad at Jahn's (canned mushrooms just does not a salad make!); a decent side salad from Happy Kitchen; and, well, not many others that are truly memorable for either good or bad reasons.
