Post by: Crabby_Appleton on March 03, 2016, 04:00:01 PM
I TNR'ed almost all of the cats behind my building two blocks away from you. I'm assuming you made sure yours don't already have the tell-tale ear tip, right? Chicken baby food works like a charm. But you need to make sure that there's only chicken and water in the food or they can get really sick. They can't resist it. Either that, or get some roast chicken to put in the trap.
