Park(ing) Day is coming to Jackson Heights
« on: September 08, 2009, 09:04:28 AM »
 
I thought I'd let you know that I will be hosting a Park(ing) Day spot on 37th Ave and 78th St, in front of the Aqua Clara Laundrymat    for Park(ing) Day on Friday September 18th.
Here is a link to Park(ing) Day, which is a national event being organized here in New York by Transportation Alternatives.
 
http://parkingdaynyc.org/about  Be sure to watch the video, to see how fun it is.
 
In a nutshell, we will take over a parking space to highlight the fact that many public spaces go to parking, when they could be used for parks. Ours, will have games for kids, music, a free photobooth, and hopefully shade and turf.
 
I welcome any volunteers to man the spot, (shifts between 10am and 6pm) as well as any donations of tall plants, tarp or tenting (for shade), turf, indoor/outdoor carpet- for ground cover, or anything else you might think would make the spot more fun. Thanks already, to Len and his various groups for offering help/donations for the event.

On Sept. 18th there will be over 60 spots across the boroughs, and I'm so pleased to be hosting one in our community. I will alert local media for a piece about the dire need for public space in our area.
 
Feel free to simply stop by, rest and relax, and visit with your neighbors, as well as chat up whoever will listen about the need for parks in our community. I am acting on behalf of the Queens Committee of Transportation Alternatives.
Thanks,
Cristina Furlong
