hi katie

this topic gets posted every year or so....mostly by folks who are new to jackson heights.

every year (or two), laguardia needs to do runway maintenance, and planes get rerouted to take off over our paths, rather than land. it happens for two, three, maybe four weekends every summer, year in year out. nothing we can do about it.

i've lived here 15 years and gotten sort of used to it. but just to warn you, this will probably be the state of affairs tomorrow as well.

just imagine if you lived in, say, college point, where this is the noise they get every day!
