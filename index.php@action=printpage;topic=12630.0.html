Title: Re: Soundproofing windows
Post by: Superclam on November 17, 2014, 08:40:13 AM
I can't vouch for any soundproof windows, but what about when you want to have the windows open? Street noise is a classic NYC problem that I can't imagine will go away anytime soon.
Enforcement is very difficult, it's such a temporary thing. Jerks stop at the light with their bad music blaring, but they're gone in a minute or 2. Calling 311 wouldn't do a thing. The most I've ever seen is a cop yelling at a car to lower their music. I'm sure it was raised 2 blocks later.
I use the fan function on my A/C unit as a white noise generator; there are lots of similar devices out there. My noise woes come from noisy upstairs neighbors.
Good luck with the new place and welcome to the neighborhood!
