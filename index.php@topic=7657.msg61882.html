Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Thanks JH Orchestra and Jackson Heights Beautification Group
« Reply #21 on: December 12, 2014, 08:20:38 AM »
Wednesday evening's concert at Community Church was simply wonderful. The setting was beautiful and the music was a true delight! We were happy to see such a full house on the first magical, snowy evening of the year. As neighbors entered and were seated, it was great to see our fine, little community taking advantage of the season's opener under the direction of JH resident, Patricia Glunt.

From the resounding tenor solos of Ulises Solano, who made his Lincoln Center debut in 2011, to a short visit from Borough President, Melinda Katz, all was great. To our neighbors who were not able to make it on Wednesday, be sure to pencil in the next concert date, when it comes up. You will be just as pleased and proud as we were, to have such quality music right here in our neighborhood.

To see photos, or listen to a short video of the event, be sure to friend the Jackson Heights Beautification Group on facebook.

Thanks Ed, Nuala, Len, Rodrigo...all the volunteers,  and the wonderful and talented musicians who provided not only a great musical offering, but a superb evening in the neighborhood.
