Title: 1924 Aerial City Photos
Post by: toddg on January 28, 2010, 10:26:36 AM
Coolest map since Google Maps:

NYC has issued an online map browser that uses aerial photos of the city taken in 1924.

Here's what Jackson Heights looked like in 1924 (http://gis.nyc.gov/doitt/nycitymap/index.jsp?z=6&p=1016673,213164&c=orthos1924)

Here's F. Scott Fitzgerald's "Valley of Ashes" (http://gis.nyc.gov/doitt/nycitymap/index.jsp?z=6&p=1026229,213087&c=orthos1924) (now Flushing Meadows Corona Park)
