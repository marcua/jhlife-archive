Title: Queens Arts Connections for artists and cultural groups, March 22
Post by: Shelby2 on February 06, 2008, 05:28:08 PM
QUEENS ARTS CONNECTIONS
Saturday, March 22, 2008, 2:00 - 6:00 p.m.
Queens Museum of Art, Flushing Meadows Corona Park, Flushing
A networking event for artists and cultural groups pooling the resources of two dozen art services organizations. Each organization will provide material and answer questions on their services.

Highlights of the day include:
-Presentations on the New York Foundation of the Arts Source database and Instructions on How to Become a Teaching Artist.

-Curators from the Queens Museum of Art and other Queens arts organizations will provide one-on-one portfolio reviews for selected visual and performing artists.

-Registrants will be offered the opportunity to participate in a slide/reel/poetry slam during which peers will offer feedback on their work in a public setting.

-There will be a live performance by a Queens-based artist.

-The day will conclude with an informal networking reception.

SUBMISSION PROCESS
To submit your portfolio (visual or performing arts) for critique please a CD or DVD clearly labeled with the name of the artist and contact information to:

Queens Arts Connections Submissions
c/o Prerana Reddy
Queens Museum of Art
New York City Building
Flushing Meadows Corona Park
Queens, NY 11368

Submissions should be limited to 10 images or 3-5 minutes of performance. Only a limited number of submissions will be discussed at the workshop. A submission is not required for registration.

REGISTRATION
Admission is free. Registration is required. Space is limited.

To register, please email your name, address, telephone number, and artistic discipline to register@queenscouncilarts.org
