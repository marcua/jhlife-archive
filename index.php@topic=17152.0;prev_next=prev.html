Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Air train information meeting tonight.
« on: May 29, 2019, 08:44:08 AM »
So the Port Authority wants to spend $1.5 BILLION for a train to take people from LaGuardia Airport to Willet Point.
It sounds like a billion dollar boondoggle to me.  Come out and learn about the Port Authority's "preferred proposal" and what power we have to comment on it and maybe change it for a sensible way to LaGuardia.

We need more information about a one-seat subway ride to LaGuardia.
We need more information about ferry service to LaGuardia.
We need more information about improved bus service to LaGuardia.

The Air Train as proposed by Port Authority only hurts our neighborhood.  We will never use it.  We will suffer through the construction.  We will lose parkland.  We will lose access to Flushing Bay.  We will not receive better subway services, better bus service or better ferry service.  We want a sensible way to LaGuardia.

Come out tomorrow night an hear about other options and learn how you can have your voice heard.

AirTrain Community Forum

Wednesday, May 29th, 6:30-8:30pm

37-65 89th St, Jackson Heights

SPACE IS LIMITED, RSVP HERE
WHEN: Wednesday, May 29th; 6:30-8:30pm
WHERE: 37-65 89th St. Note that there has been a location change.
RSVP: https://airtraincommunityforumjh.eventbrite.com

Join residents, local community organizations, and citywide partners for a forum on the proposed LaGuardia AirTrain to Willets Point. The forum will include a panel discussion about alternatives and impacts, and a workshop on the environmental review process. Learn more about the potential impacts of the AirTrain and acquire the necessary tools to be involved in the review process.

The forum is organized by the SensibleWaytoLGA coalition fighting for the best route to LGA for all New Yorkers. Download a flyer in English and Spanish to help spread the word!

Be sure to RSVP. The event is free, but space is limited!
