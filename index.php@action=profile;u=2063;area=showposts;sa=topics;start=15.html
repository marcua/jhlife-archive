Community Groups, Activities & Events / Gentle Stretch Yoga
« on: April 17, 2015, 01:22:21 PM »
GENTLE STRETCH YOGA> Sundays at 11:45am -1:00pm w/ Mike

Yoga is excellent for nourishing the mind and body throughout the life span and at times we'll find that we need something challenging yet gentle to keep us young in mind, body and spirit.
Gentle movement encourages better circulation, while weight bearing standing poses are a safe way to help build bone density. Awaken areas of the body that have been inactive, soothe aching joints, and strengthen muscles, while you calm your mind with this enjoyable practice. Any level of practitioner can benefit from this class, which consists of stretches, movement and postures that calm the sympathetic nervous system; helpful inde-stressing mind and body.

Community Groups, Activities & Events / PRE-NATAL YOGA GROUP
« on: February 27, 2015, 07:05:11 PM »
New group forming for Pre-Natal Yoga classes!
Saturdays at 2-3pm February 28th at your little yoga studio; Mimi For Me Yoga at 76-11 37th Ave, Ste 205

Our two instructors are amazing Mommy-Yoginis, and offer a fabulous, refreshing, and safe practice to prepare mind and body for the big day.  Here's our little blurb on what to expect.  If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us.

Best Wishes to all Mommys for a happy and Healthy pregnancy.  Namaste~

*Pre-Natal Yoga: Yes it’s true, it's never too early to connect and bond.
Yoga helps better connect moms-to-be and their soon-to-arrive babies, while assiting with a better transition into the months of pregnancy and preparation for the big day!  You’ll practice postures that work on the reproductive organs and pelvis to allow for a smoother pregnancy and an easier more focused and enjoyable child birth. While concentrated breathing (prananyama) is a powerful way to ensure an abundant supply of oxygen is reaching the fetus, while infusing you with some extra “life force” as well! A little meditation offers a heightened state of awareness, a greater bonding with your baby-to-be.   Lastly, but certainly not ahhhh...., deep relaxation learn it now, because you’ll need it later! The effects of a yoga practice during pregnancy can make for a smoother pregnancy, relatively easier childbirth and a quicker restoration of body post labor.
