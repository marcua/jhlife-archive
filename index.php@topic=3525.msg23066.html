Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

A quick Google search took me to the Le Gamin website. There I found owner Robert Arbor's contact information.

I wrote:

Monsieur Arbor:

I have heard many rumors that you are considering opening a Le Gamin bistro in Jackson Heights. I want to assure you that there are many, many people here in the area who would love to welcome your artistry to our neighborhood.

We have enjoyed the fare from your truck at the Jackson Heights weekend markets, and only hope that you will proceed with your plans to honor us with your artistry here.

And he replied:

Bonjour david
Thank you so much for your message and support . Yes it is indeed a strong possibity that Le gamin could open in JH as I am looking for a space but at this time nothing as been sign . I shall keep everyone updated on the progress .. But will bring the truck on Sunday until then ..
A bientot

THIS BODES VERY WELL!!
