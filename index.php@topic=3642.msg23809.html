Idling trucks and ambulance
« on: November 12, 2009, 12:37:18 PM »
What do you think about this problem:

There's a sick person in my building and they get picked up daily for treatments, I guess, and then dropped off. In the meantime, the ambulance is left idling, sometimes (like this morning) for up to 40 minutes, twice a day. This has been going on for at least since spring.

I really feel for my neighbor and I understand all their suffering and difficulties that come from being seriously ill. However, is it necessary for the ambulance to be left idling for sooooo looooong twice a day, even when there's no one in it?

It's noisy as hell, I can't get much work done, and I'm breathing burned gases because it parks right under my window.

What can I do to resolve this situation? Do I have any recourse at all?

How about delivery trucks that are left idling while they unload?

THanks a bunch!
