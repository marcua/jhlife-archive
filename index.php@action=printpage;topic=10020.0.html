Title: Re: New wine bar on Northern/82nd: Bocaito
Post by: Dkaplan on March 23, 2014, 04:36:31 PM
I was elated when I saw this opening up so close to me and I must say, my expectations were met and then some. 
The place has great ambiance and decor.  The bartenders definitely know what they are doing, I'm looking forward to trying some of their signature cocktails.  I got an Old Fashioned which was done very well.  My girlfriend was pleased with the wine she ordered and their overall selection. 

The food we tried was excellent.  We had the empanaditas from the appetizer menu, these were great.  We also ordered the seafood/chicken/chorizo Paella which comes with a lobster claw in the center.  I especially enjoyed the chorizo in the Paella.

Overall this is a great addition to the neighborhood.  If you haven't yet, check it out!!
