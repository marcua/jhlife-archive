News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Neighborhood Chat / JHBG's Halloween Parade for All - Special Year
« on: October 30, 2014, 08:00:17 PM »
 This is a great year to come to the JH HALLOWEEN parade and show solidarity for our neighborhood, especially the
folks that were burnt out of their businesses last year. There are three Grand Marshals for the parade all from the
Bruson Building fire and we can show them, and our children, how much we support our neighbors when they are
in need.
 Queens Pride, Armondos and Franks are the honorees (don't have the names at hand). Brought to you by the
Jackson Heights Beautification Group (JHBG) and local business as sponsors.The parade has a 5:00pm start time
on 37th Avenue all the way from 89th street to the Halloween bag give-away in front of P.S.69 all on 37th Avenue.
Two local school bands, Scouts, Dance troops and lot's more. It's a celebration and the whole neighborhood is
very welcome.
