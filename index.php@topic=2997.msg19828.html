Le Gamin Comes to Jackson Heights this Sunday!
« on: June 27, 2009, 10:12:02 AM »
Last week my husband and I were in Park Slope and met the owner of Le Gamin, a French
restaurant in the East Village and in Prospect Heights, Brooklyn. He has a
mobile truck that he drives to the Union Square farmer's market and the
farmer's market in Park Slope, Brooklyn every week. We told him that we had
a thriving farmer's market in Jackson Heights as well as a play street on
Sundays where families are known to linger and work up an appetite. After
some lively discussion, and some delicious french fries, they have agreed to
bring their truck this Sunday to Jackson Heights!!

If you have a penchant for croissants, croque monsieurs, crepes and crave
vendor variety beyond Mister Softy, please come on Sunday and buy some
french food! They even have ice cream and Nutella.

Check out their menu:

http://legamin.com/LG_MOBI_MENU.html

-Braden & Sarah
