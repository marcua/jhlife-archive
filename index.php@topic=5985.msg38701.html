Salt & Fat, new restaurant in Sunnyside
« on: March 25, 2011, 09:03:31 PM »
It just opened tonight, mostly small plate with 3 larger main dishes. The food was great and interesting. Salt & Fat, 41-16 Queens Blvd. Sunnyside, (718-433-3702). They are only opened for dinner right now, and are waiting for their liquor license. Waitresses and busboy were super nice, they gave us popcorn cooked in bacon fat!

here's a link to their menu: http://saltandfatny.com/html/menu_dinner.html
