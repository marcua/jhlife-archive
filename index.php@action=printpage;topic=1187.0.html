Title: What do you eat for breakfast?
Post by: Chuckster on June 30, 2008, 01:40:38 PM
I just read an interesting article in Time Magazine entitled Chicken for Breakfast (http://www.time.com/time/magazine/article/0,9171,1818200,00.html).  Prompted by spotting a McDonalds offering a chicken sandwich for breakfast, the writer asks the question of "...why we accept certain food at certain times."  There's an accompanying video whereby he tries the sandwich at breakfast time even though his brain is craving what he considers a more traditional breakfast food such as eggs.

As a kid in Ecuador, I remember adults eating just assorted fresh baked breads/rolls with real butter and accompanied by a cup of coffee.  Nowadays, when family visits us in the States, they crave soups and heartier meals and sort of frown on the egg thing.  So, what do you guys eat for breakfast?  I'm a cereal person.
