"Fanciest, classiest, best restaurant in Jackson Heights"?  That is a very open ended question that can only have subjective answers.  Fancy, classy and best are in the eye of the beholder.

But, it is still a fun question.  My two cents:

I agree with Uncle Peters.  It is a nice place to sit and have dinner.  I think they really care about your dining experience and I think they do a nice job.

La Portena.  Another place that is pleasant to sit in and have dinner.  And as a matter of personal preference, I like the Argentine steakhouse experience.  Love the mixed grill with a nice glass of wine.

A place I would add that hasn't been mentioned is Meson Asturias (83rd street half a block south of Roosevelt).  I must admit I haven't been there in a very long time so I hope it hasn't slipped.  But it was always my personal favorite and would be the place I would go if I was seeking out the "fanciest, classiest, best place in Jackson Heights."

It would be fun to hear what you decide on and how it worked out.
