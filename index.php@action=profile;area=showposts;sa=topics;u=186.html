Neighborhood Chat / Rogue private garbage truck on 37th Ave
« on: February 05, 2015, 01:33:37 PM »
One night in early October I was walking down 37th Ave when a garbage truck was doing its runs down the same street. It had a private name (i.e. not city) and numerous guys popped in & out wearing t-shirts and umbro-type shorts. They picked up garbage at Jackson Diner and Subzi Mandi, among other businesses. The reason they caught my attention was they accelerated though red lights (not ones changing from yellow to red but RED LIGHTS) *and* each time they stopped to do a pickup the truck parked diagonally, totally blocking traffic from behind. I purposely avoided crossing the street while they were around.

The name was printed on the side of the truck tho I don't remember it. Anyone witnessed or familiar with the rogue garbage truck?
