Title: ASPCA Mobile Spay Neuter Clinic
Post by: ajf160 on March 28, 2011, 02:21:39 PM
On Saturday, April 23, the ASPCA Mobile Spay Neuter Clinic will be parked at the corner of 34th Avenue and 92nd Street.  It is suggested that people arrive at the location before 7:00 A.M. The ASPCA serves an average of 20-25 animals per day on a first-come, first-served basis. 
For more info, please visit http://www.aspca.org/aspca-nyc/mobileclinic/

ASPCA Mobile Spay Neuter Clinic is being brought to the neighborhood at the request of Assemblymember Michael DenDekker and Senator Jose Peralta.
