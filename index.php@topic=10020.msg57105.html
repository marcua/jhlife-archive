Hi all, new to this board, have been in Jackson Heights for about a year now. I went to Bocaito for dinner last week and it was really lovely. My friend and I sat outside in the back garden space and shared several dishes tapas style. The ceviche mixto, pandebono with crab meat fondue, and yuca gratinada were especially good. The service wasn't especially quick but our server was more than competent and friendly, and in any case we weren't in any hurry (the dishes and drinks were coming out a bit slowly, but that wasn't the server's fault). I'll definitely be back!

Just wanted to get a read on people regarding Bocaito and brunch, as I had an interesting experience there.

The good:  black bean soup was amazing.

The strange:  their 'traditional' eggs benedict came with cinnamon raison english muffins.  I inquired the waitress about it and she believed it was a mistake.  I hope so, but it seems kind of a rookie error.

The not so good:  20 dollars for brunch seems a bit high.  So does 15 dollars for kids brunch.

I'm happy it's here. And i look forward to trying their non brunch entrees.  And i'll give their brunch another shot, but the price point seems a bit high.
