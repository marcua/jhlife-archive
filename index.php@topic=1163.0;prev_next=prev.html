The original Jackson Dinner was a small silver bullet dinner - free standing - on 74th street next to Pizza Boy. The building was built around it. It was the basic dinner - burgers, eggs, sandwiches, etc.

Then it converted to the Indian style food. Finally, as it's following became larger and the space opened up, it moved to the middle of the block where it is now.
