Hi EJ,

Welcome to the neighborhood !

JH does have a vibrant and active gay population, even outside of the bars on Roosevelt Ave. which at the moment you are too young for.

In addition to the yahoo group mentioned, and Queens Pridehouse, There's also the Lesbian Gay Democratic Club of Queens that meets monthly at St. Mark's Church. Additionally there's a monthly dinner for LGBTQ members of the community that meets at a different restaurant each month.

These groups do tend to attract a slightly older crowd (late twenties / early thirties and up) If you're looking for something a bit closer to your age you may have an easier time in Astoria. CHeck out OUTastoria.com for info on their social events, and maybe even the rainbow Center in Astoria.

And of course there's the Queens Pride Parade which takes place right here in early June !

Hope that helps !
