Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Neighborhood Chat / Re: Hawks and other raptors around Jackson Heights

The Merlin was around in January and was identified by a guy i know who was doing the kestrel count for the city (he also does central park birdwalks)
The kestrels like the top of roosevelt terrace and later in the day the school on 87th st and 37th ave. They have been around for a few years but they don't nest in either building ....the red tailed hawks i see in spring and fall... The only nest i know of is the one in the Unisphere at Flushing Meadows but that is too far away ....there is also a Cooper's Hawk that likes to sit on buildings between 37th & roosevelt near the pigeons by the 7 train. I have never seen a peregrine in the neighborhood.....mike
