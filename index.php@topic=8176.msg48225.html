Let me offer some clarity

There are 11 stores within the Trade Fair chain, all owned by Farid Jabber, with corporate offices on 30th Avenue in Astoria.

To my knowledge all 11 stores have a picket line

I have visually seen picket lines at both Jackson Heights locations, 75th Street & 89th Street at the same time

Additionally on a trek out to Astoria, passed 2 locations with a picket line

These picket lines seem to be formed by the workers from the current locations supplemented by union workers from other chains such as Stop & Shop.

Please note, Trade Fair has now replaced all of these workers, and at this point seems to have no intention of allowing their now former employees back onto the job.

Trade Fair is a bad neighbor in almost every community they "serve", simple violation searches clearly show their disregard for laws and community, such as illegal air conditioning units, illegal signage, illegal stoop line uses, illegal storage, blocked sidewalks, blocked exits, as well as agricultural and labor issues.

The owner simply pays the fines as a cost of doing business, in the manner he wants to, but not the manner that complies with the law.
