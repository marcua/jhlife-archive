Hello neighbors,

I'm a Jackson Heights resident since 2014, and I'd like to introduce my business - LUXE Windows & Doors. We sell genuine German Rehau tilt and turn windows and doors.

Unlike most American windows, the European tilt and turn kind operate with a handle. Turn the handle 90° and the window swings open into the room; turn it 180° and the window tilts in. (Here is a link (https://99percentinvisible.org/article/tilt-turn-ingenious-three-one-window-security-breezes-egress/) to a 99% Invisible article about tilt and turn windows.)

LUXE windows are very high quality, fully customizable (for those of you concerned with landmark requirements), and competitively priced with brands you'd recognize like Pella. What makes our windows stand out is that they're all Energy Star Compliant, and all our profiles come standard with Low-E glass, super spacers, multiple locking points, and a unique u-shaped steel bar that reinforces the entire frame — all of which lower your energy bills and offer unparalleled security.

LUXE windows are also simple to install and easy to clean.

There are more details on our website, livewithluxe.com (https://www.livewithluxe.com/) — but please note that since we launched relatively recently, the installation guide and testimonial sections are still under construction.

We have a mini showroom in Bushwick, Brooklyn, but I'd be happy to visit you locally with an example of our windows.

If you or any of your neighbors, friends, or relatives are looking for new windows, please think of LUXE (https://www.livewithluxe.com)!
