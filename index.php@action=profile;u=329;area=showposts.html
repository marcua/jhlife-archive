News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

THE JACKSON HEIGHTS ART CLUB
2009 Fall Outdoor Art Exhibit


WHEN:
Saturday, October 17th and Sunday, October 18th

FROM:
9:00 am to 5:00 pm on both days

WHERE:
On the fence of P.S. 69 on 37TH Avenue, between 77th & 78th Streets, Jackson Heights, NY 11372

HOW MUCH:
Members of the JHAC - $30.00
Non-member artists - $50.00
For a space approximately 12 feet long by 6 feet high

THINGS YOU NEED TO KNOW:

  • Only original works may be entered, including oils, watercolors, acrylics, pastels, and mixed media. You may also enter prints of your own original work.

  • Artists or their representatives must be present with their works during all show hours.

  • Artists with a limited number of pieces are free to share space with another artist.

  • Your work must be in place each day by 10:30 am.

  • No projecting fence racks are allowed and no show articles are permitted at the curb. Browse boxes and portfolios must be placed alongside of fence.

  • Artists are responsible to remove all strings, wires, hooks, and name plates from the fence at the end of the day. Please remove all rubbish from your area.

  • We suggest you bring a chair, string, â€œSâ€ or drapery hooks or clamps to hang your paintings if you do not have racks. Also bring drop cloths or plastic covers to protect against sudden rain.

  • Place numbers on the paintings you wish to have judged. If you wish to have all of your work judged, it is not necessary to put numbers on your paintings. Judging is for ribbons only.

  • PLEASE COMPLETE AND RETURN THE APPLICATION WITH PAYMENT BY SEPTEMBER 30, 2009. (Application is attached below).

For further information call Edwin Rivera at 718-397-1301 or Judi Chandon at 718-706-0669.

Please follow JHAC's activities on its Facebook Group page. Thanks.

Community Groups, Activities & Events / JHAC July & August 2009 Summer Workshops
« on: June 25, 2009, 12:59:16 PM »

THE JACKSON HEIGHTS ART CLUB
JULY & AUGUST SUMMER WORKSHOPS

Pay as you go $15. per session

No Experience necessary.  Coffee & tea.  Come join us for some inspiration, creativity & community.  Classes are limited to 15 students per class.  Come early to secure a seat or call to reserve one.  Call 718-426-9821, leave your name and phone number and a reservation will be made in your name.

Classes start Tuesday, July 7th and run through Thursday, August 27th, 2009


  • Every Tuesday Afternoon -- 1-3 pm
    Oil & Acrylic:  "Explore your creative side with Eric March.  This class will cover a new topic in oil painting every week: one-shot painting, light effects, color mixing, drawing the head, working from life and more.  A great way to get started with oil painting or to refine your skills.  There will be an additional $5 modeling fee for life painting classes."

  • Every Tuesday Evening -- 7-9 pm  - and  - Thursday Morning -- 10-12 noon
    Drawing & Watercolor:  Need some inspiration, drama or serenity?  Find focus balance and value with instructor Caryn Gerega.  She'll cover all the essentials with each class, starting with a lesson in drawing and moving forward into watercolor.

  • See Below for Children's Schedule
    Children's Classes:      Out of school and need to keep your child's mind active?  Despina Argyros, our children's class instructor is up to the task and does it with grace.  Various painting and craft activities are on the agenda!

Children's Schedule & Pricing

Session 1:  July 9, 16, 23 & 30 -- from 1-3pm

Session 2:  August 6, 13, 20 & 27 -- from 1-3pm

Cost of each session is $50.

On Facebook?  Join JHAC's Facebook group page.

Activities at The Jackson Heights Art Club are sponsored in part by grants from the New York State Department of Parks, Recreation and Historic Preservation, the New York State Education Department, the New York City Department of Cultural Affairs and Councilmember Helen Sears.
