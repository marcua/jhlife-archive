Has any other Time Warner Customers been getting piss poor service lately? Primarily in the evenings? My issues started June 28th - and by now I have a call/notes log long enough to circle queens. I've been making myself very vocal to get the issue resolve. I live on 81st and the issue just destroys Time Warner's fastest speed service.

My ping times skyrocket into the 300-400 range and bandwidth drops significantly until around midnight, then speeds are normal. It starts going to shit around 8:30PM each evening. Even on weekends, when you'd think 'peak hours' would slow it down sooner than 8:30, I get full speed service (300Mpbs) until about 8:30, then BOOM down to between 8-12Mpbs and horrible latency.

Trying to see if anyone else has been getting similar performance in the area. Thanks!

I've been having similar issues with 300/20 since February.  I'm near 81st as well.  It gets fixed for a while, then it starts again within a month or two. 

Have you tried twc.cotp@twcable.com?  (Office of the President, Executive Customer Care Team.) They can usually get things unstuck but unfortunately, fixes don't last all that long.  They have some serious issues with both their infrastructure and available bandwidth in the neighborhood and seem unwilling or unable to address it.

The level 3 support people are good (direct number 866-506-0218), but once these issues are referred to their network group they end up in limbo with no response.

I wish we could get FIOS, but there's no fiber passing most of our streets.  When Verizon ripped up 37th Avenue near 81st I saw they were burying some fiber there, but when I asked they said it wasn't for FIOS, only phone service. 

Last but not least, complain, complain, complain!   The NY State Attorney General's office was interested in whether or not New Yorkers were actually getting the bandwidth we're paying for. 

http://ag.ny.gov/are-you-getting-internet-speeds-you-are-paying?wssl=1

FCC Complaint Form:

http://consumercomplaints.fcc.gov/hc/en-us/requests/new?ticket_form_id=38824
