Title: Dunningham Triangle Holiday Decoration and Tree Lighting Ceremony
Post by: 82nd Street Partnership on December 07, 2017, 04:54:26 PM
The 82nd Street Partnership, in partnership with Elmhurst Hospital and Community Board 4, will be hosting a Holiday Decoration and Tree Lighting Ceremony at Dunningham Triangle Park at 4:30 pm on Friday, December 8th.  The event is free and will feature snacks and refreshments courtesy of Duane Reade and local businesses Pancito and Delicias Colombianas. 

Parents with children 5-and-under are invited to bring the kids to the park at 11 am to take part in decorating the smaller trees in the park!   

Dunningham Triangle is located at the intersection of 82nd St and Baxter Avenue.
