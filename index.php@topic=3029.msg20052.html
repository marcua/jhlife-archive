 My neighbors and myself don't have a "formal" group, but everyone watches out for everyone else. Whether it is noise or vandalism, there is always power in numbers.  This is especially true when making a complaint to the 115th. We make sure that when there is a problem everyone calls and complains about it. We're pretty relentless and we've gotten some attention as a result. I also have found that when people know that everyone is watching---they shy away and go to the next location. We communicate by word of mouth or telephone. Know your neighbors well!
