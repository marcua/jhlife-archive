I spent a surreal time in Belfast before the first peace initiative was signed. In a hotel whose restaurant had been relocated to the roof level. Swell place, 17th floor. Rubber duck in the bath tub, chocolates, fresh fruit and a complimentary bottle of wine!

And then towards dusk, the helicopters started. My first thought was Bruce Springsteen (or was it Billy Joel?). I looked out the window and there they were, stationary over an area in the distance. All night. Yep, all night.

Thank heavens for the Walkman (it was long ago) to lull me to sleep.

Breakfast in the sky was fun.

Later I found that I had booked myself into the hotel that was in the Guinness book of records for having the most bomb attacks and was still functioning. And yes, it is still there, and still wonderful.

I suppose this is off topic, but thought I'd share.
