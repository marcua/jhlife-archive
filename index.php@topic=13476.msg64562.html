I'm not exactly sure which local store -- beyond maybe one women's shop on 37th Avenue (by the liquor store I think) -- would possibly lose customers to a Banana Republic factory store. And even that women's shop is questionable as the clothing there seems to be for a decidedly mature customer.

We don't have great clothing stores in this neighborhood. Want a dress that's 100% polyester, ill fitting, and sure to pill up in two washings? Looking for horrific patterns? You got it.

One good thing I'd say about ABC Outlet clothing selection is that if you browse frequently, you can sometimes find good stuff. I still have Danskin capri workout pants I spotted at there, and am routinely saddened that I lost a second pair I left in a gym bag on the train. I'll buy some of the light sleeping shorts there and an after shower mumu. But I haven't found anything decent clothes to wear outside the house wise there is years.

On another note, I visited the new location of ABC on Junction the other day. What a difference! Two floors, with home stuff upstairs. I imagine it's only a matter of time before they add racks and pack the first floor just as badly as it was on 82nd (and on Greenpoint), but since I mostly pick up a couple home items there, it was paradise.
