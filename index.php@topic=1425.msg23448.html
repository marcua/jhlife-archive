I was looking for a place to buy a couple of months ago and wanted to get some background on my closing costs.  I did a Google search and the best website I found also happens to be a blog run by a local attorney that someone else had mentioned, Sunil Agarwal.  The link is

http://skalaw.blogspot.com/ 

Very interesting reading and something that I have referred back to several times.  When I did find a couple of places, this guy's name kept coming up again.  The agent I was using gave me a list of preferred attorneys and his name was on it.  When I asked him about Mr. Agarwal, he said that he was "great and wonderful to work with.  I didn't take the place so I didn't use him.  Then I found another place and that agent also told me to use him.  So I did and I was very satisfied.  He is friendly and returns phone calls and emails and knows what he is doing.  I am very happy.
