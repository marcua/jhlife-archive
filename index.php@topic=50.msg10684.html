Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Hello,

I'm Luis and I was Born in Jacoby Hospital, "circa 1968", in the "boogie Down Bx".  :rockon: Moved early on to Puerto Rico and returned to NYC at the age of 10.  Went to Samuel Gompers HS and then part time to John Jay College while working for the Trump Org.  Quit college, left the job and went on my own for while.  After I started making a NORMAL wage I left the Bronx.  I moved to Ozone Pk and bought my 1st home in Howard Beach soon after.  After a divorce I ended up living in JH for a couple of years.  It was a strange time in my life but Jackson Heights is one of those places that make a mark in ones life.  Good or bad, a mark nevertheless and in my case a bit of the bad :coolsmiley: and lots of the good . 

I found the "mentally challenged"    creatures here at JHL to be legitimate and caring and while I live in Holliswood at this time I do make part of my income in JH and spend about 2 or 3 hours in the neighborhood daily.  I am an environmental remediation consultant by trade and I have been able to tie in all things real estate and construction into what I do today.  I think I found a home here,  locally.  Who would have thought? :smitten:
