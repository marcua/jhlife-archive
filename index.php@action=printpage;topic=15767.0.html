Do You Fear What I Fear?

Climate change?  Zika?  The opioid epidemic?  No.  But, truth be told, 2016 has seen me morph into a bona fide political junkie.

From CNN to Meet the Press
SNL to PBS....
From MSNBC to Fox
To election day and the ballot box.

There was Cooper and Maddow, O'Reilly and Hayes
To guide me through this political maze.
Ivanka, Bernie, Christie and Mitt
And dweebish John Kasich who just wouldn't quit.
There was Priebus, POTUS, Podesta and Pence
Pundits and polling where little made sense.
Conflicts of interest and countless attacks--
An Emoluments Clause, emails and hacks.
Tic Tacs and tweets and no chance to escape
From an eleven-year-old Access Hollywood tape!
And Clinton and Kaine left singin' the blues--
"OMFG!  How the hell did we lose?"

'Tis the season to be jolly?
Fa la la la la, la la la laaaaaaaaaaaaah!!!

I wish everyone in Jackson Heights a wonderful holiday.  I'll probably be spending mine with Anderson Cooper.
