I forgot to write in to say that I tasted this ice cream a couple of months ago.  It was delicious -- but not life changing.  Basically, it's very good gourmet ice cream, but no better than the ice cream at Grom a block away. 

Somehow the wonderful novelty of getting gourmet ice cream from a truck lost its magic when I discovered that the ice cream was hand-scooped, not custard-style served from a machine.  I don't know why this should be -- hand-scooped ice cream is more environmentally efficient and doesn't require the truck to idle.
