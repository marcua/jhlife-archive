It is amazing...We had two or three Tamarindo trees in the back of the house (en El patio de atras) in Bayamon PR.  We just grabbed them, took them off their shell and threw it inside a jar Then added water and sugar.  We shook the crap out of it and BAM.  Jugo de tamarindo...  I didn't know what is was to pay for Naranjas (we called it chinas), tamarindo, aguacate, guayaba and even algodon (cotton) and the mangos would literally fall off the trees and rot since each tree would give hundreds of them.  We had, mango paso, mango pina, mango comun and so on.  It's amazing what a tropical environment does for fruits, plants and medicine.
