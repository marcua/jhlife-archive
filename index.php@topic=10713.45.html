Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

A shout out to the owners of additive boutique.  Although I've bought wine here in the past, I went in for a tasting this evening and finally met with the owners. It's  great to meet with people who are knowledgeable about the product they sell.... A very rare find in Jackson Heights indeed. They are Jackson Heights centric, which is good for us, but also informative about what they sell.  I've had coffee and quiche in the morning  at their place across the street and was also impressed by the help they hire.The people who they hire are a far cry from what you'd expect. They are both communicative and engaging.   Thank you Patrick and Francisco. Happy to have in in my neighborhood
