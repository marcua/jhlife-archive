La Gloria on 86th and Northern is awesome.  A little noisy with the DJ, but at least his tunes are good.  Great atmosphere as well.

Also La Pequena Columbiana on Northern and 83rd and Northern is great as well.  Only catch is that they end breakfast at Noon.


Otherwise it's just the diners.  Jax Inn probably has the best and is the cleanest.  I have a fondness for Jackson House though.  The staff can't be beat.

I have to say that both the food and the service at Jax Inn is very hot-or-miss. I'd made a habit of going there every Friday with my parents this past summer for lunch, and while the burger platters and huge omelets were consistently fine, ordering almost anything else off the menu usually ended up in disappointment. As for service, some waiters were very attentive to our needs (almost too much so) while others virtually disappeared after taking our orders, leaving most of the work to the busboys or other staff. But I certainly can't argue about the cleanliness of the establishment.
