News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Email blast by Councilmember Daniel Dromm today:

"I am working very closely with the School Construction Authority (SCA) to build a new school in our neighborhood by acquiring the old White Castle Headquarters property located at 69-01 34th Avenue in Jackson Heights.  (See the photo above.)
 
Since I was elected to office in 2009, it has been my priority to add new school seats to our district.  I believe this old White Castle site would be a great location.
 
I am proud to have suggested the site to the SCA!
 
Now, it looks like we may have a deal but your input is needed.
 
The proposal will be discussed tonight, Tuesday, May 5, at 7 PM at the Community Board 3 (CB3) joint Education and Land Use Committee Hearing held at CB3's district office at 82-11 37 Avenue, 6th Floor. Space is limited.
 
There will be another presentation at the monthly CB3 Hearing at 7:45 PM on Thursday, May 21 at the Louis Armstrong Middle School, 32-02 Junction Boulevard.
 
I am very proud of this accomplishment and ask you join me in support of this proposal.
 
I look forward to seeing you at the Hearings."
