Title: Re: floor refinishing
Post by: Minimal4me on May 22, 2019, 07:19:52 AM
In our last home the contractor had to do one room at a time because we were already living-in. There were two rooms that needed it: bedroom and living room. The guys who did it actually moved all the furniture from one room to the other and back again, doing the whole job over the course of a long weekend. When they were finished, they put all the furniture back where it belonged.

Richard at USA Floors: 646-739-7535

Title: Re: floor refinishing
Post by: Fensterschauen on August 03, 2019, 10:18:03 AM
Does anyone have other floor refinisher recos?

Agree with the group: move all items from one room to the next. The guys you hire will likely do it, but I would 100% be onsite when they move items back into the room to avoid scratches. Also a good opportunity to chuck needless items. And to get away for the weekend.

If you have area rugs, ensure you get carpet protector as the new finish, even when dry, might be newly adhesive.
