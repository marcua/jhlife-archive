There's a 3-acre corn maze in Floral Park (near Long Island)  that I'll be trying this Sat.  You have the option of doing it in daylight or in the dark with a flashlight!  There's always the climbing wall at Chelsea Piers if you're really looking for something active.  If you want something slightly less challenging, they have a driving range where you can practice your golf swing and watch your ball fly up and out into the river (well, until it hits the net) from the third floor.
