Title: Abdullah Sweets and Food
Post by: Jeffsayyes on March 24, 2014, 11:32:57 AM
just wanted to give a quick shout out to Abdullah Sweets - it's a new place on 37th Ave between 73 and 74th streets = across from Rite Aide. Good assortment, nice people, good flavors. This has been a few different owners, and these seem like the most dedicated yet. Also, they are more than happy to talk to you about the food and whatever else. I should have listened more as my Halwa Puri breakfast doesn't come with any thing else than the sweet and bread and the woman seemed to be hinting that the extra thing would be $2 more. I should have gotten the extra thing like chickpeas... (kababish got me hooked on halwa puri)


It's really close to my place, so I've been eating there a lot.
