I would stay away from Window-Fix.  We hired them to fix our double hung windows.  They could not fix them.  They took our money.  They literally came to my home over 15 times because their work product was poor.  They did some work on one window and they did such a bad job that the window cannot be opened because the panes will literally fall into the apartment.  I am now in the market to replace all of my windows.  By the way, Window-Fix quoted three different prices for new windows that had a huge range, i.e., approximately $2,000 difference.  The prices kept going up.  I spoke to different people and there was no consistency.  I am not suggesting that they are dishonest.  I am suggesting that if you are looking to repair windows, stay away from them.  I am also suggesting that the workers have vastly different opinions that I found troubling. This was a nightmare experience.
