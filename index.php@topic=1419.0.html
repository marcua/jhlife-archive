On Roosevelt Ave. from 78th st. to 80th street there will be several people who proposition you for hookers and/or illegal social security numbers.

The hookers are located in houses just about a block away from these locations.  Just look for the guys on the corner to escort someone to a house or parking lot behind a house.  It's pretty easy to figure out where they work.  Or even better yet just ask them where you can have sex with a hooker.  They will be glad to show you the way!

Just don't get with the hookers because STD's and AIDS really sucks to have!  You could also easily pick up bedbugs in those beds!   

Even more disturbing is that this all occurs right down the block from an elementary school that has been zoned right next to a stripper club and a tattoo shop that sell bongs and marajuana... ehem... sorry i meant... "tobacco" paraphenelia", a liquor store, and a sex shop!

AWESOME!  Drop off the kids at P.S. 69 (no joke this is the school right next to all this stuff) and grab a pipe and get down at the strip club and have some after hours session with a hooker!  Geez Jackson Heights is the place to be! *sarcasm* :laugh:

9/11 memorial is coming up again.  Are we gonna watch on TV as somebody reads off a list of names like 200 long ringing a monotonous bell after it or are we gonna do something about somebody selling social security numbers on the corner of our own block?   

This summer innocent people have been shot, banks (citibank)  and restaurants (mario's?)  have had violent incidents.  The police were out in force with their big trailer and uniforms everywhere, however even though we ALL see the guys soliciting us for illegal social security numbers and hookers somehow these guys have consistently beat the cops and escaped!  Maybe beat cops aren't enough.  Maybe we need a DETECTIVE on the case. 
After all I beat the guys that solicit me and you for hookers and illegal social security numbers don't solicit uniformed cops!

As a Community Board member, I can tell you many times our hands are tied.

With liquor licenses the board vote is a mere 33% of the State's decision

With the tall buildings, if zoning allows it, the owner does not even have to seek approval, they are "as of right"

The strip club in question has personally been a thorn in my side, it is technically a cabaret, the issue is the board can decide on liquor and cabaret license for the establishment, but the NYPD club unit must enforce the nudity, and prostitution.

A cabaret license in no way gives a person automatic rights to take their top off.

Romantico's, i know this is what has been referred to, is a major oversight, NYPD knows, but they don't act, and their cabaret license is the same as that of Atlantis, food and drink, with dancing.  The difference is that in Atlantis, which goes by the book, their is "exotic dancing" at times, but clothed to some extent.

As for the sex shop, there must be a community outcry, honestly, there are rule for liquor licensing near schools, and places of worship.

We must band together to find a way to remove the "London Boutique", people just have to have the nerve to step forward.

Too many people here are afraid, I go shooting my mouth off everywhere, because I fight for my neighborhood, and I get in trouble for it sometimes. 

I sound like a politician, but there is progress that has been made, and there needs to be more.

People have to band together, that is why I have been fighting for many years for Jackson Heights.
