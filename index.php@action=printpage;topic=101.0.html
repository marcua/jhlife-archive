Title: Affordable working space for artists in Queens - Discussion Dec. 3
Post by: Shelby2 on November 26, 2007, 03:56:28 PM
One of the most pressing issues facing Queens artists today is affordable workspace.

In response to this, Queens Council on the Arts (QCA) is pleased to bring together artists, arts professionals, government agency representatives and community activists for an informed discussion.  Three specific case studies will be presented by Taryn Drongowski, Sheila Lewandowski and Loukas Skipitaris for review and commentary by panelists Caron Atlas, Sarah Belleas, Susan Chin and Irving Poy.

Join us for Creative Space Dialogue, a panel forum that will take place on:

Monday, December 3, 2007
6:00 - 8:30 pm
