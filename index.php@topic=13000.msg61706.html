Luckily we don't really have the illegal clothing donation box problem much in Jackson Heights.  The two that I have seen recently were on 86th St. between 34th and Northern, and a few in Woodside outside a tire shop on 63rd St. near 35th Ave.

I also saw one on 31st Ave as I was heading from JH into Woodside.  That one had apparently been closed down by the city, as it had orange signs (from the city) pasted over the opening.  I'm pretty sure that any collection box on a sidewalk is illegal. 

I do understand that having them makes it easier when you're cleaning out your closets.  What about bringing your items to the Greenmarket on Sunday instead for textile recycling?  http://www.grownyc.org/clothing
