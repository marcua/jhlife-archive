Title: Re: A look inside the new condo building on 81st St. near Roosevelt
Post by: am315 on July 09, 2018, 03:37:09 PM
These are sponsor-owned apartments that have been on the market for some time. The parking spaces are not included with the price. They may be sold separately either outright or be rented out monthly.

Buyer beware: The reason the maintenance is so low is the sponsors are really just guessing what the building's monthly expenses are. The sponsor puts together an estimated budget, which rarely matches real operating expenses. I once bought a sponsor apartment and the so-called "budget" barely covered expenses. Ultimately, we had to raise the maintenance 30 percent to cover our costs.

Another thing to consider: This is new construction. An engineer friend of mine says 90% of newly constructed buildings have leaks in walls and ceilings and very poor quality finishes. And, indeed, that sponsor one I bought had tons of leaks.
