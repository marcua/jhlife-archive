News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

The New Visions Democratic Club was created to provide a forum for the community to gather to discuss issues impacting all of us, politically and communally, nationally and locally.

Meetings are held the second Thursday of each month (except for the summer) at 8PM.
Place:  The Jewish Center of Jackson Heights
           Corner of 77th Street and 37th Avenue (Near Met Supermarket).

The New Visions Democratic Club will meet tonight, May 13, 2010....


Tonight's meeting of the New Visions Democratic Club will be quite exciting.

We'll hear from NYS Comptroller Thomas DiNapoli who is running for reelection.  He'll speak about his recent report on the contributions of immigrants to NYC's economy and will answer questions from the audience.  The comptroller will also have staff available to help you find out if you are owed any money from any state agency that you might have forgotten about or didn't know was due to you.

We'll also hear from Bryan Pu Folkes and Francisco Moya who are running for the 39th Assembly seat vacated by Jose Peralta's election to the NYS Assembly.

Next month petitioning will begin for all of the above mentioned positions and for the 13th Senate seat and for our district leaders and state committee members.  Poll workers will be given their applications at the June meeting.

So, we hope to see everyone tonight at the Jewish Center of Jackson Heights, 37-06 77th Street at 8 PM.
