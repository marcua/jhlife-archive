Community Groups, Activities & Events / Bilingual Sprouts fall registration!
« on: July 30, 2015, 06:19:33 PM »
Bilingual Sprouts is a mommy(daddy/caregiver) and me Spanish immersion program for children ages 2 to 7 in the heart of Jackson Heights founded and taught by two sisters, mommies and New York State certified Early Childhood Bilingual and Art Education teachers. Using an early childhood Spanish curriculum that strongly emphasizes and facilitates adult involvement, Bilingual Sprouts provides developmentally appropriate activities that are engaging, interactive and hands-on. Through read-alouds, art, games, music and movement Bilingual Sprouts will immerse your child in a new language while having fun!

The great news is FALL REGISTRATION HAS STARTED ! Please see our schedule below or visit our website.

FALL '15 SEMESTER will run with additional classes on SATURDAY'S and a new addition to the program for older children ages 6-7(parent/caregiver participation welcomed but not required)! From September 15th through December 12th.

Business Listings and Services Directory / Bilingual Sprouts interactive Spanish immersion program
« on: June 30, 2015, 06:20:54 PM »
Bilingual Sprouts is a Spanish immersion program for children 24 months to 5 years of age right here in the heart of Jackson Heights, located at the Community Methodist Church. Founded and taught by two New York State certified Early Childhood Bilingual and Art Education teachers. Through interactive activities such as Spanish Literature read-alouds, art, drama, music and movement, Bilingual Sprouts will immerse your child in a new language while having fun!

Our summer classes started today! We offer classes on Tuesday or Friday mornings. Session 1 starts from 10:00 am to 10:45 age group 2 to 3. session 2: 11:00 to 11:45 age group 4 to 5. Spot are still available! Contact us at bilingualsprouts@gmail.com or visit our website at bilingualsprouts.com.
