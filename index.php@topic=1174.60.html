As stated by Bryan in post #51 above, 73rd and 74th Streets will be receiving supplemental street cleaning as part of the New York City Clean Streets Program.  From what I've read so far, this additional help will be initially financed by the City and is scheduled to begin this November.  The program will continue for two years with a focus on having participants in the program ultimately be able to maintain cleaner streets on their own.  This all means that we have some cleaner streets to look forward to this Fall!

"The goal of the program is to build the capacity of selected partner organizations so that they are capable of independently financing and managing their sanitation programs at the close of the two-year initiative."

You can read more about how the program works here:

New York City Clean Streets Program
