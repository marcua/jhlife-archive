I wish people on this blog simply said how they how feel about something that they find egregious.  If it annoys you, it's annoying. And if it's illegal, it's illegal.  Don't let anyone make you feel small or petty because you object to something that simply should not be happening. I live on 37th Avenue at a light.  When my husband was home on disability after major surgery, "Mr. Softee" would sit idling under our window for ninety minutes at a time, playing those blasted tunes ("HELLO!"), then circle the clock and do it again.  I was at my wits end because my husband simply could not rest.  I reported "Mr. Softee" time and time again to 311.  Believe me these guys know they're annoying and they don't care about you and your peace of mind.  They know that they're violating the law. And they know that they're going to get away with it. This is not the stuff of childhood memories, it's thumbing their noses at  the people who have to listen to these inane jingles, sometimes past ten o'clock at night.

If you want to be a positive person, let us know when you see someone doing something truly positive. (like someone consistently keeping the area in front of their store neat and tidy.)  But you have a right to be annoyed and frustrated, especially since these situations are so rarely dealt with effectively.
