Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

to the question of why they're doing maintenance, runways have it far rougher than roads, which get maintained on a regular basis -- and the workers can't exactly just slap down a metal plate as a temporary fix.

and, having lived here for decades now, and experienced this on and off since almost day one, i can vouch that it sucks every now and then. considering how close we are to LGA, i'm surprised we get the blast as infrequently as we do.

for advice on how to handle it, perhaps ask someone in one of the surrounding neighborhoods that deal with the noise 90-plus percent of the year, year in, year out. that's not sarcasm -- maybe there are sites like this one in the other neighborhoods impacted by LGA.
