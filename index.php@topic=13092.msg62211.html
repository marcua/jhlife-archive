Taste of Lahore on Northern - AVOID!
« on: April 03, 2015, 11:05:14 AM »
Last night, shortly after 9pm, one of their employees came into our building and rang the actual door buzzer of EVERY single apartment. 

Our buzzer went off and my husband got up to investigate.  He did not open the door, but watched through the peephole and saw this guy ringing every other buzzer on our floor, dropping menus when they didn't answer.  I wanted to confront the guy, but my husband wasn't into that idea.  So after this loser did our floor, he went upstairs to the rest. (We could hear the buzzers in the nearby upstairs apartments too).

Who does this??    Aren't the piles of menus of menus in lobbies and menus shoved under doors enough - but this place has to disturb every single resident that was home last night? 

Wasn't interested in going there before, am certainly going to speak up about it now.  Who wants to order from somewhere that will harass your neighbors? 

Good job, Taste of Lahore!
