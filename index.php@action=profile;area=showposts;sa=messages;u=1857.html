Restaurants & Food / East Star - Good new Chinese on Northern and 81st.
« on: January 18, 2010, 02:59:33 PM »
I've now eaten twice from East Star - a new Chinese takeout place on Northern (80-24), and it is excellent.  The menu is standard Chinese fare, which is fine with us.  We aren't very adventurous when it comes to Chinese food.  The place is run by very friendly obviously Chinese guys and the food is great.  Wonton soup is rich and meaty and the wontons are perfect.  Mooshu pork probably the best I've ever had.  Tons of meat and very pungent and delicious, and comes with heaps of pancakes.  The Beef with Chinese veggies was a bit bland, but the meat was tender and the veggies were fresh and crisp.  All in all excellent, and at 2 meals worth of food for $19, the price can't be beat!  Free delivery, too.  Try this new addition to our neighborhood. 718-478-3088.
