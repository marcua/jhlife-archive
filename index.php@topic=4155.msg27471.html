Don't be scared - Elmhurst has gotten alot better over the years.  But as you already know, JH is very close to Manhattan and a ton of hospitals can be found there.  I gave birth at St. Vincent's downtown (which will now be changing to a branch of Beth Israel I believe).  Even if you don't have a car, a cab ride is not bad - maybe $25 or so.  NYU has a great birth center but is generally very full.  If you are interested in a good midwife, I can provide you with a name.  Good luck to you and your wife.  JH is a great place for kids, lots of activities going on.
