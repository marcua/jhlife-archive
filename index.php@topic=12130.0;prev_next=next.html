Know a good neighbor in Jackson Heights?
« on: June 09, 2017, 02:08:28 PM »
Jackson Heights is a neighborhood of good neighbors. and we want YOUR help in recognizing them when JHBG bestows its Good Neighbor Awards this fall.

Good Neighbor nominees are people or businesses that stand out for significant contributions to making Jackson Heights a better place. Past award winners include a food pantry, civic leaders, and people who work with our kids, such as educators, and coaches from a local youth group. Business winners include community-minded stores that have made landmark-worthy renovations, support the arts and fill an important niche in JH.

IMPORTANT: In addition to the name of your nominee, we need you to send us a way to reach them, preferably an email address. Also, please include your name (we will get back to you at your email address.) PLEASE SEND YOUR NOMINEES TO US BY JUNE 30 AT info@jhbg.

We will soon settle on a date for the Good Neighbor Award gala. We are now looking at either Sept 23 or Oct 14., Saturday evenings. Thank you.
