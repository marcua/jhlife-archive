Neighborhood Chat / Why did you choose Jackson Heights?
« on: July 19, 2010, 12:59:26 PM »
Our neighborhood is just fantastic in my opinion and I guess you do too or you wouldn't be here!!! 

If you had to describe Jackson Heights in terms of the type of people it attracts, it's level of affordability, amenities, school options and transportation, how would you describe it? What else makes it great?

I chose Jackson Heights because I was seeking affordability, ease of transportation to and from midtown Manhattan, as well as access to highways and the airport. I also wanted quiet streets and safety at all hours, regardless of the season. In my opinion,

- Jackson Heights is one of the few NYC neighborhoods that still offers "family friendly" amenities to middle income people.
- Except for the small population of African Americans and black Caribbeans, our residents are diverse in age, culture and ethnicity.
- The transportation options are second-to-none, considering the hub at 74th & Broadway, the highways and proximity to the airport. Also, having the LIRR at Woodside is just great.
- The restaurants are relatively inexpensive and the food is awesome.
- It's "GREEN" (passers by wouldn't know because our green spaces are behind our buildings but what other neighborhood has what we have?)
- IT'S SAFE.

I don't have any school-aged children but I would be interested in hearing your opinion of the public elementary and middle schools in the neighborhood. If you're not favorable towards public schools is it because of your feelings about public schools in general? the NYCDOE? or public schools in Jackson Heights, specifically?

THANKS and have a great day

Neighborhood Chat / Fire on 76th near Northern / Illegal Fireworks
« on: July 05, 2010, 11:35:04 AM »
Last night around 1AM a fire was burning out of control in what looked like a driveway between an apartment bldg and the Queens County Bank parking lot on 76th Street near Northern Blvd. The heat and smoke were pretty intense so I called 911 and my bf flagged a police car. Other ppl must have been calling since moments later the fire crews showed up and put out the fire.

I just wanted to say that at least two groups in the vicinity of the fire were shooting off fireworks.

For your own safety and those around you, lay off the illegal fireworks, please
With cars parked on our residential streets, fireworks could set off an explosion and injure or even kill lots of people.
