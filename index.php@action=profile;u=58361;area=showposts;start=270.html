Real Estate & Home Improvement / Re: Advice on buying a mattress in NYC
« on: August 29, 2016, 07:08:51 PM »
Here's what you do.  Go to Macy's and pick out your dream mattress.  Their salespeople can be very helpful.  When you know the name of the exact mattress you want.......go to Sleepy's to purchase.  Just tell them you want the "Stearns and Foster/Dreamaway" (or whatever the silly name is) and they will sell you the same Stearns and Foster mattress from their line.  It will have a different name attached to the brand you desire.  Don't be alarmed.  Each mattress seller has separate names attached to their products to make it difficult for the consumer to comparison shop.  The Sleepy's price should be cheaper than even the Macy's sale price.  I've done this twice in the past and it has worked out great.
