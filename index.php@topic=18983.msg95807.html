We went this morning. We have definitely been sleeping on this place. Had beautiful huevos rancheros and french toast. I tasted the french toast and it seemed like challah french toast... It seemed like a really good version... I was going through my mind's library and wondering if Mexicans make something like challah... Do they use a cemita bread? hmmm, my parents would really like this place... I asked the server and then she goes and asks the head guy. He comes out and says that he used to work at Fred's at Barneys and they use the same exact ingredients as they do! And he does salads similar to them, all fresh made etc etc. He also said they are waiting for the liquor license soon and hopes it picks up then but damn, this place is a gem.


And the owner actually mentioned JHL and he thought people might be avoiding it because someone said they haven't even been inspected yet. I felt bad for him and said they were stupid (sorry, but the letter grade means very little). The place is spotless. He's been waiting for them to come!
