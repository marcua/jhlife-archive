Neighborhood Chat / Re: Official DOT plans for 34th Avenue
« on: November 05, 2021, 04:32:20 PM »
The greatest factor in whether one is an open street/ linear park supporter is how someone feels about who gets to use and have access to public space.  If you think that cars, by default, have the right to an enormous amount of public space, then it's likely you will not support the OS.  If you think that public space can and should be used for things other than car transportation and car storage, then you will likely support and enjoy the OS.  I live right on 34th and I think the OS is the greatest.  The most vehement anti-OS person lives almost two blocks from the OS and has driveway.  The fact is that there are about 40 people who hate it and are very vocal about it.  The OS is demonstrably popular and is heavily used by wide swaths of the community.  Clearly there are people who hate it, but it's also pretty clear that an enormous amount of people like and use it.
