News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Community Groups, Activities & Events / Lunar New Year Celebration- THIS SATURDAY!
« on: February 26, 2015, 02:18:19 PM »
On behalf of Council Member Dromm & Staff, we wish you a New Year filled with success!
 
We are excited to be hosting our fifth annual Lunar New Year festival & parade in Elmhurst, Queens. We hope that you and your family will join us in making this year’s celebration successful. This year’s event will be held this Saturday, February 28th at St. James Episcopal Church (84-07 Broadway)
 
Since starting this event in 2010, our Lunar New Year celebration has grown from a small festivity of performances in front of C.C. Moore Park to this year’s festival & parade with dragon dancers, drummers and community members. This year we are also hosting several Lunar New Year themed Art & Crafts workshops for kids! Last year’s event had an attendance of over 200 guests and we anticipate another great turnout this year.
 
We hope that you can join us on Saturday, February 28th. We will gather at St. James Episcopal Church  at noon (11:00 a.m. for kids’ activities!) to celebrate with traditional drumming and dance, ending with a parade to CC Moore park at the end of the program. We hope that you will share this post & join us to celebrate Lunar New Year in the wonderful community of Elmhurst, Queens!
 
Please feel free to contact our office if you have any questions.
 
Thank you!
