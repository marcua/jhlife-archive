Free bilingual PUPPET SHOW, followed by workshop!
« on: June 20, 2017, 10:39:58 PM »
"PEDRO ANIMAL FALLS IN LOVE / PEDRO ANIMAL SE ENAMORA"

A bilingual puppet show in Spanish and English. Un espectáculo bilingüe de títeres. For children aged 3-9, para niños de 3-9 años.

WHERE: Jewish Center of Jackson Heights, 37-06 77th Street, Jackson Heights.

WHEN: Saturday, July 1st, 2017

2:00 -- Show begins. FREE entry, no tickets needed. Entrada GRATIS, no se necesita boletos. (45 minutos)

3:00 -- Puppet-making workshop, taller de títeres. Make split-pin puppets based on characters from "Pedro Animal." (2 horas) $10 in advance, pago por adelantado / $15 at the door, a la entrada. Buy tickets for the workshop, compra boletos para el taller: https://www.eventbrite.com/e/puppet-making-workshop-taller-de-titeres-tickets-35470941541

PEDRO ANIMAL FALLS IN LOVE: This comical folktale from the Dominican Republic tells the story of a simple, fun-loving young man who is crazy in love with a neighborhood girl. This humorous, interactive puppet play integrates audiences into the story in order to help Pedro win the hand of his true love.

PEDRO ANIMAL SE ENAMORA: Esta comedia folklórica de la República Dominicana cuenta la historia de un joven humilde y cariñoso que se enamora locamente de una joven en el vecindario. Con un sentido del humor y aspecto interactivo, esta obra de títeres logra integrar a la audiencia con el desarrollo de la historia para poder ayudar a Pedro a ganar el corazón de su amor verdadero.

INFO: Show is a production of Teatro SEA, presented by Firefly.

QUESTIONS / PREGUNTAS: FireflyPuppets@yahoo.com

EVENT INFORMATION: https://www.facebook.com/events/1599574053399928/

FIREFLY PUPPETS PAGE: https://www.facebook.com/fireflypuppets/
