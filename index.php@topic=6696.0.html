THE MANCHESTER TWO BEDROOM/TWO BATHROOM SURROUNDED
BY THE BEAUTIFUL HISTORIC LANDMARK DISTRICT OF JACKSON HEIGHTS
 
This renovated two bedroom two bathroom co-op apartment is presently set up as a three bedroom, and it is located in the immaculate prewar Manchester building. The foyer can easily be transformed into a dining room, sitting room or office as you then proceed into the huge living room. The very generous eat-in kitchen has been renovated and has its endless possibilities. There are lovely hardwood floors throughout, and ample closets. The bedrooms are quite appealing. The western, southern and northern exposures flood the apartment with sunlight. This well-run property is not only pet friendly and convenient to everything, but it has a garage with a short waiting list and a super that lives on-site. In addition, the laundry, storage and bike room is located in the basement for your convenience. Jackson Heights has been named among the most desirable neighborhoods to live in with Zagat rated restaurants, great shopping, weekend farmers market, close to major transportation and approximately a 25 minute subway ride to Grand Central Station. This truly is a great Jackson Heights opportunity!
 
ASKING: $459K, MAINTENANCE: $1,000, DOWNPAYMENT: 20%
 
EXCLUSIVELY LISTED BY: MARLENE FLORES, REALTOR, 917-208-6709
