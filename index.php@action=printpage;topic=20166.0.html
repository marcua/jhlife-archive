Title: Volunteer Needed to Paint Murals on Restaurants Barricades
Post by: 82nd Street Partnership on September 30, 2020, 06:04:35 PM
Wonderful Family and Friends Activity.

We will be helping Emoji Burger, Just Made For U, and Strawberry Yogurt add some art and fun to the their recently installed barricades.  The murals have been designed by local artists Annabelle Popa. 

THURSDAY OCTOBER 1ST | 4-6 PM
MEETING LOCATION: EMOJI BURGER
80-07 37TH AVE.

-No experience Necessary
-Ages 8 + & family clusters welcomed (under 16 must be accompanied by an adult)
-Materials and Masks will be provided.
Wearing of Mask at all times required.

For more information, please contact the 82nd Street Partnership at info@82ndstreet.org or at 718-335-9421.
