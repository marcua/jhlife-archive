I think the best thing to do is buy the equipment yourself and pay a company to maintain it.  That way your co-op gets all the profit.  It will cost you more money up front but then you get all the profit long term.  Be careful if you sign any contracts with laundry companies as they keep the majority of profits and if you get stuck paying the laundry room electricity you may end up losing money each month if you don't do your math correctly.  In addition, laundry companies usually make you agree that if they provide service, you ban laundry units from being used in individual apartments as it is competition for them.  Good luck.
