Restaurants & Food / Thai Thai Grocery
« on: March 07, 2017, 11:13:49 AM »
For those who haven't been, the Thai grocery on 77th and Woodside across from Ayada is an incredibly well-stocked little shop with lots of hard-to-find ingredients for southeast Asian cooking. Their frozen coconut milk and chile pastes have become pantry staples of mine. The proprietor is also super nice and accommodating. She helped me find a few things the last two times I was in there and we chatted about neighborhood news for a few minutes. They even have a kaffir lime plant!
