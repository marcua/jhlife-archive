Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

The Berkeley Gardens are people friendly.  The children enjoy them, run, and perhaps scream as all children do.  Is it frequent and annoying, no.  Are people becoming tolerant of the fact that residents are spending more time in the gardens.  Yes.  Has it been difficult for some to hear noise.  Yes.  Have children learned how to respect the plantings, and run around them. Yes.  Are there picnic tables and benches. Yes.  Are there dogs allowed.  Not sure, but I haven't seen any.  Does my son play ball.  Yes.  Does it bother anyone.  Maybe.  Am I worried.  No.  Are BBQ's allowed.  Not yet.

Is the community of The Berkeley becoming tolerant of those young and older and the use of the gardens.  Yes.  Are we all working together along with the magnificent board of directors to make it one of the most attractive and inviting buildings in The Historic Landmark District of Jackson Heights.  Yes.
