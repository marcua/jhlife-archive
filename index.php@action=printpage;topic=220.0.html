From WNBC.com:
Queens Bar Shooting Leaves Woman Dead, Man Injured
POSTED: 5:56 am EST December 17, 2007
UPDATED: 9:22 am EST December 17, 2007

NEW YORK -- A woman was killed and a man was injured after two men entered a Queens bar and fired several shots on Monday.

Police said two men entered the Tulcingo Cafe on Roosevelt Avenue in Jackson Heights early Monday morning and opened fire. The woman was shot in the back and the man hit in the arm. Police said the two suspects fled in a dark-colored vehicle.

Police said both victims were taken to Elmhurst General Hospital, where the 24-year-old woman died. The man remains in stable condition.
