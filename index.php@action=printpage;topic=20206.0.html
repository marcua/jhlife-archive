Title: Re: Stolen Packages
Post by: AndyJH on October 20, 2020, 12:33:16 PM
Sorry you're going through this. Seems like a very common problem during the pandemic. My building (rental) has also had a problem with stolen packages. It was really bad earlier on in the pandemic, but seems to have decreased a bit as of late (knock on wood). No actions taken by management though, other than putting up a sign threatening prosecution...LOL. Like that would ever happen.

I jokingly toyed with the idea of camping out in the mail area and checking IDs when people come to pick up their packages.

My personal solution has been to use an Amazon locker or UPS Access Point when applicable (NOT the UPS store, which is a MESS), or run to the mail area the second I get a delivery confirmation text. I realize that's not always possible if you're away at work, but my industry is still not back yet so I have lots of time to obsessively stalk my delivery tracking info.

Hope you find something that works for you--
