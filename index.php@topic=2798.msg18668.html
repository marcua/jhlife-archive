That is the problem with going to a shop for the 1st time. They will try to sell you maintenance/repairs
that your car may not need. One time I was 2 days away from my inspection expiring,went into a shop in Brooklyn after work and the guy asked me if I have ever been here before,I said no...he started the inspection and after a few minutes told me that I needed a tune up,otherwise it wouldn't pass inspection. At the time my van had 165,000 miles,9 yrs old. Cost me $480.00+ the state inspection fee,at the time I had no choice. The van was running a bit sluggish though at the time when I brought it in.

Did you know that the machines they use for the state inspection cost over $150,000? Garages make no money on inspections. The repair item that is most pushed by mechanics...replacing shocks. I overheard my mechanic once tell a guy who had his car up on a lift.."Don't think about the money,think about your safety."
