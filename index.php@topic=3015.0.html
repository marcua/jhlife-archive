Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Jackson Heights Neighborhood Transportation Study
« on: June 30, 2009, 01:10:04 PM »
If you are concerned about:
- Walking across Roosevelt Ave safely
- Traffic Congestion on 37th Avenue and side streets
- Noise from idling trucks
- Speeding cars on residential streets
- Slow buses
- Pollution from trucks and airplanes
- Horn honking !!!
- and many other transportation-related concerns

You can do something about it.

I wanted everyone to know about a very important community workshop
coming up that will have a huge impact on transportation in Jackson Heights .
The Dept of Transportation is beginning a major $1.5 million study of
transportation in Jackson Heights.

The first phase of the study will focus on the area of east-west
(69th Street-82nd Street) and north-south (Northern Blvd- Broadway).
A key focus of the study is to improve pedestrian access
to the mass transit hub at 74th/Roosevelt.
The study will include the implementation of short-term and long-term
transportation solutions.

The first Community Workshop for the study will be held on Thursday,
July 16th at 6:30pm at the White Castle Headquarters 69-01 34th Avenue
in Jackson Heights. Please attend. I think it is critical that we have the local
community participate in the discussion.

Mark your calendars:
Thursday, July 16th 6:30pm
Jackson Heights Neighborhood Transportation Study
Community Workshop
White Castle Headquarters
69-01 34th Avenue, Jackson Heights

I hope to see you there.
