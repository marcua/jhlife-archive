Title: Holiday Market?
Post by: sdgmg on November 01, 2011, 10:21:08 AM
I know it's early, but would anyone out there be interested in participating in or helping put together a holiday market, to be held a week or so before the winter holidays, somewhere in the neighborhood?
Things like baked goods, jams, salsas, candies, etc. are great- nicely packaged, locally-made treats. Or anyone who knits, sews, or does crafty things, basically anything you can think of that would be interesting for people to buy or give as gifts.
Last winter I helped organize a market in a small coffee shop (that donated table space) in Brooklyn and sold candies and cookies. Whatever we didn't sell, we swapped or bartered at the end, so it was great for everyone.
We would just need a place, a little publicity, and of course, vendors.
Please email me if interested (saramaarkel@gmail.com), it would be great to do something Queens-based!
Thanks,
Sara
