Neighborhood Chat / Re: Knife Sharpening
« on: September 15, 2015, 09:41:50 PM »
I took a couple Wusthof knives to Boris today and it was pretty ugly. I learned a life lesson, I think: if you have fancy knives, then bring them to the fancy knife sharpener.  Whatever tool Boris used went all up and down the side of the knife in random, burnt-looking patterns. The edges of the blades might have been sharp, but had burrs and were generally uneven.

I wouldn't say they are ruined because I am not a fancy chef and they will still cut things, but it was not the experience I was expecting. (I was expecting to be like "OMG, I can't wait to go home and chop things!") Instead I am a little bummed...
