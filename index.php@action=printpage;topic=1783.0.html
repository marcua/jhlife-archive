Boris Aronson, best remembered for the Harold Prince musicals "Cabaret," "Follies," and "A Little Night Music," was one of the American theater's most innovative and versatile stage designers. He created sets and costumes for hundreds of productions including the Yiddish Art Theater, the Group Theater and Broadway.

Jim Steere has designed sets for off-Broadway, regional theater, summer stock and dance (Alvin Ailey American Dance Theater and Hartford Ballet). He has worked as a scenic artist on such films as "The Wiz", Ragtime", and the Academy Award winning "Dances with Wolves".
