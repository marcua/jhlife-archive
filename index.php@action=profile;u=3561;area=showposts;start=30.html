Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Neighborhood Chat / Re: Any people bothered by noisy children in JH cafes?
« on: December 18, 2010, 01:29:02 PM »
Hi Inverness,
Julie here from Espresso 77 - just wanted to reply to your thread.
We find that the majority of our customers with children have well-behaved if energetic youngsters and they do a good job of monitoring the kids.  If you are in the shop and there is some really egregious behavior going on, please mention it to our staff and we will see what we can do to calm things down.
Mostly we have a mix of kids and adults and there are many times of the day when there are few to no kids.  Please come by one evening and have a cappuccino or a glass of wine - from around 7pm onwards, it's pretty calm unless we have music or another special event going on.
Happy Holidays!
