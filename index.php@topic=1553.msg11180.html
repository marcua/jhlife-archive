Before I call 311 (Tree Issue)
« on: September 22, 2008, 10:02:33 AM »
hello neighbors,

before i call 311 for the first time i was wondering if anyone here has any experience with over grown wild trees?  my next door neighbor has a tree that started growing right next to his garage.  at first (approx 10 years ago), it was a tiny tree that did no harm.  nowday, the tree trunk is doubled the size of an aluminum garbage can and the height is well into the 2nd floor of the houses. 

my main question and concern is that the tree is now clogging the gutters of my house and garage.  i trimmed the branches that hung over my property line with a professional grade branch remover/cutter that i had to buy just for this matter.  on top of clogged gutters, i notice certain areas on my backyard is has lifted due to the growing roots. 

on a normal circumstance, i will ring on the doorbell of my neighbor and address my concern but the landlord does not live on the property.  the tenants that do, hardly speak english we hardly see each other.  to be honest, i have no idea who and how the landlord looks like. 

i know i can call 311 and address this issue but i am sure that they will tell me to contact the property owner since its on private grounds.  what are my options?

also, i want to add that i love trees and anything that will promote our environment but in this particular circumstance. the tree is becoming a hardzard and inconvenience.  imagine have clogged gutters, leaves on everything you own, consist sweeping, worrying about falling branches etc...


thanks for the help
