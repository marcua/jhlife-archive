Kate would like to know if anyone or everyone would like to do a meet-up at one of the local coffee shops and discuss whether there's enough interest for a group class or if individual instruction would be the way to go. Or just if you have any questions. Espresso 77 is closing for renovation, but there's Juju's at one end (76th & 37th) or Addictive at another (87th & Northern) or if anyone has a suggestion anywhere in between.
As for qualifications, Kate has been practicing Yang style Tai Chi since 1996 and has been teaching, both classes at a school in Manhattan and privately in home, for the last 14 years.

Just by way of caveat, Kate stresses that what she teaches is not a "follow along" class. You don't show up and do what the teacher in front of the class does. She will teach you the form, a few moves at a time, building a foundation that moves forward in a progressive manner.   She does not teach a "senior" version but teaches to each individual's ability and range of movement.
