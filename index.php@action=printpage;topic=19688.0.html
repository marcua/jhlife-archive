Title: Re: NYS Plastic Bag Ban
Post by: ZzzzzZzzzzz on March 05, 2020, 12:53:34 PM
The issue isn't people who reuse these bags as garbage bags.  It's the people that do not, of which there are many thousands more.  If you do not monetize the cost of the plastic bag in terms of it's role in public pollution, clogging drains, getting caught in trees, harming the environment, etc., then the result is what we have today. 

Plastic bags are a genius invention.  I love them.  But more people don't consider the cost than do, and therefore if you want a plastic bag in the future, you need to pay for it.
