Chipotle has pioneered the improvement of fast food and is widely seen as one of the reasons that McDonalds is experiencing difficulties.

There's a good summary of what Ells and others had to say here: http://www.eater.com/2015/6/15/8783757/highlights-from-the-second-annual-welcome-conference

Probably the best message to come out of the conference was Danny Meyer's assertion that being right is less important than being generous. That idea will be part of staff training for the restaurant that I'm opening this fall in Jackson Heights.
