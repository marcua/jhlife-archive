Met a Manhattan friend at Jacx last Thursday.  Both of us had tasty Israeli/Middle Eastern meals from Taim, except their bottled lemonade was too sweet. A big empty barn of unoccupied tables except one other couple with the vendors on two sides and on a mezzanine (Japanese).  Recorded music both too loud and hardly discernible except as blaring sound; asked the wait staff cleaning tables to turn it down to no effect.  Will not make a trip there again, but OK if happen to be in area.
