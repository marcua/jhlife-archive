First Tuesdays is a literary reading series that meets on the first Tuesday of the month from September through June at Espresso 77. Peter Marra will be the first reader for the 2019-2020 series. An open mic will precede his reading. Sign up starts at 6:30 and the reading starts around 7 or 7:15. The calendar event is here: http://www.jacksonheightslife.com/community/index.php?topic=19095.0 (http://www.jacksonheightslife.com/community/index.php?topic=19095.0). For more information about First Tuesdays, you can visit the website—which is under maintenance—here: http://www.firsttuesdays.net (http://www.firsttuesdays.net). If you'd like to be on the mailing list, you can sign up here: http://eepurl.com/txwmz (http://eepurl.com/txwmz).
