Title: World Listening Day Event - Wednesday, July 18
Post by: nancygoldman on July 14, 2018, 11:08:49 AM
Wednesday, July 18th is World Listening Day. To mark this occasion, we invite you to experience and honor the power of listening by joining us in a sound walk. A soundwalk is any excursion whose purpose is listening to the environment. During our soundwalk, we will stroll through some typical Jackson Heights streets, and focus our attention on what we hear as we go. By remaining silent during our walk, we allow our sense of hearing to come to the forefront of our experience.
 
We will meet at 7:00pm at a nearby Jackson Heights location, do a brief “warm-up” exercise to focus our listening skills, and review our walk plan. Then, we’ll walk together for 20-30 minutes. We’ll regroup afterwards to share what the experience was like.
 
Want to join us? Need more information? Please email heightshub@gmail.com.
 
For more information about World Listening Day, please follow this link:
