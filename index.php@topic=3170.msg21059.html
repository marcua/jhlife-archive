Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Hi JH Lifers,

I'm starting to finalize the contents of the 2009 Jackson Heights map. This year I'm aiming to have more of a comprehensive list of businesses for the neighborhood. Last year I was beholden to a sponsor who had specific businesses they wanted to feature. This year, the sponsorship is strictly by local JH businesses. One critique I received of last year's map was that it wasn't diverse enough, so I'm asking for suggestions before this goes to print. Feel free to forward the request to friends and relatives and have them drop me a line too.

I'd love to hear from as many people as possible about what you'd like to see included. Please email me directly if you see something missing from my list below. I have room for at least 10 more businesses, probably more.

My email is davidmheatley [at] gmail  dot com. Please look first to see if I've included it before you email. Thanks in advance for helping to make this the most comprehensive list of JH businesses in existence.

Sincerely,
David
