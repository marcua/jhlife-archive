Title: Take a Free Photo With Santa!
Post by: acasey103 on December 14, 2016, 04:54:20 PM
Assemblyman DenDekker invites you to meet and take a photo with Santa Claus! Santa Claus will be at Assemblyman DenDekker’s office at 75-35 31st Ave, Suite 206B, East Elmhurst from Thursday, December 15 – Sunday, December 18. He will be available on Thursday and Friday, 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM and 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM, and on Saturday and Sunday from 10:00 AM - 12:00 Noon and 2:00 PM - 5:00 PM. All are welcome at this fun and free holiday event! Please bring your own camera.

If you have any questions, please contact Assemblyman DenDekker’s office at (718) 457-0384.
