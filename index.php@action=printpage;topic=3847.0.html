Title: New Year's Yoga Workshop followed by veg.dinner and party w/open dancing. 01/02
Post by: yogainqueens on December 21, 2009, 12:24:24 AM
New Year's Yoga Workshop January 2nd, 5:30pm.
Start the 2010 Year by practicing yoga!
Salute the passing year with breath and movement, honoring all you have been and done in 2009. Create space for the gifts of the coming year! We will have an open-level vinyasa class appropriate for all levels of practitioners, followed by an informal meditation where we will set our intentions for the New Year. Stay on to enjoy some healthy vegetarian food and chai and meet other members, friends and teachers of Creative Vibrations Yoga Studio. We plan to have a few of our musician friends swing by and to have an open music/dancing jam. Workshop fee is $12. http://www.creativevibrationsstudio.com/
