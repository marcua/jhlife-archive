Almondine,

One of the most common night-singers around here is the Mockingbird but is sings a wide and wonderful reportory of songs.  It's one of my favorites!

Lately, pre-dawn, I have been hearing a loud song which, as it turns out from Googling bird songs, is a Cardinal.  It's usually two or three whistles on an upscale - like a guy whistling to call someone, and then a series of faster whistles that may be going upscale or downscale.

If you Google cardinals and birdsong, you can see if that's it.  They have a few different tunes too so see if you can find several.

Here's a link to a site that has lots of birds and their songs:

http://www.math.sunysb.edu/~tony/birds/index.html
