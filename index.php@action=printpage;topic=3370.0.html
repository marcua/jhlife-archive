Title: Animal Shelters in all 5 boroughs or no?
Post by: Chuckster on September 21, 2009, 05:44:37 PM
A winning day for homeless animals throughout New York City, as animal activists win a legal battle that will force the City to open 24 hour animal shelters in all 5 boroughs.  The victory does not come without criticism, as is apparent from the comments posted by New York Daily News readers.  Some commentators believe that tax dollars should be allocated to homeless people rather than animals.  By the way, according to the article, the City plans to appeal the decision.
