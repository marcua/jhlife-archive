Frisby Chicken
« on: May 09, 2008, 01:13:20 PM »
I went to Fribee on 84th and Northern for the third time recently and it is pretty good if you like fried chicken.  The chicken and menu is similar to KFC.  The sides are slightly different with a Latin flavor.  In addition to corn on the cob and fries they have boiled red potatoes, maduros, rice, arepa and probably a few other items.  The prices are also similar to KFC (maybe slightly higher).  It is a large store with plenty of seating and is clean and they usually have somebody working the floor getting you extra napkins etc.  From what I understand this store is a chain in Colombia.
