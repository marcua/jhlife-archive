Neighborhood Chat / Re: Short Survey to help out a student
« on: June 11, 2008, 03:34:09 PM »
Caucasian female, 50+. Lived here about 5 years. Usually work in Manhattan. I have a car and I use the subway. I own a 1 bedroom coop. Since i moved here two cafes moved in and a number of businesses have gone out. I see more and more young white people. Real estate doubled since I moved here. When I first came here the subway renovation wasn't finished and now they've finally cleaned up the horrible litter around the subway. I wish they would clean up the gum.
Honking of car horns can get bad. Parking pretty bad sometimes. It would be nice if there was a park, for those of us who don't have gardens. Or at least a nice safe way to ride a bike to flushing meadows or astoria park. Sometimes I feel a little trapped here, surrounded by highways. But it really is a super-convenient neighborhood to live in.
