Title: Farm Spot CSA Winter 2015 Share sign-ups are now open!
Post by: civita on October 13, 2014, 11:08:01 PM
Farm Spot, the Jackson Heights CSA (community supported agriculture), is now accepting members for our Winter 2015 season. This year, we are partnering with a new-to-us farm (Norwich Meadows Farm) for a winter share. There are 3 types of share - Vegetables + Meat; Vegetables + Dairy; and Vegetables. All the shares will include added value items, such as locally grown flour or dried beans, and preserved foods in glass jars.

What: There will be 6 shares delivered every 3 weeks.
Where: Pick up is at St. Mark's Church on 82nd St./34th Avenue 
When: Thursdays, 5:30-8:15pm - Dec. 18, Jan. 8, Jan. 29, Feb. 19, March 12, April 2

For a detailed description of the shares: http://farmspot.org/winter-share-types/

For pricing: http://farmspot.org/pricing/

To SIGN UP: http://farmspot.org/winter-sign-up/

Questions can be sent to farmspot@gmail.com
