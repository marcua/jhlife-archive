UPS Store - long lines
« on: June 01, 2020, 05:20:07 PM »
If you're thinking of going to the UPS Store, be prepared for long lines. I got in line at 10:15 am today on the sidewalk outside the store, thinking I might wait 10 to 15 minutes, but ended up not getting to the front of the line til 45 minutes later at 11 am.

I mentioned the long lines to the guy at the shop and he said it's like that every day. He said by the time he arrives at 9:20 am, the line is already halfway up the block. Perhaps afternoons is a better time to go, but maybe not. It could be like this all the time.

Just fair warning to set aside more time than you think you need to ship a package.

Quote from: hum@njukebox1 on June 04, 2020, 09:46:02 PM
Any ideas as to the reason for the surge in business for UPS?  Just curious.

My theory is that for a few months people were staying home as directed and only going out for the items they truly needed, such as groceries. Many people did a lot more online shopping. Amazon extended its return period by at least a month, and other retailers such as Zappos always have a one year return period.

So now that people are feeling more comfortable doing non-essential errands, they are doing all their returns that had piled up during the stay at home order.
