KIDS MUSIC TOGETHER in Jackson Heights/Woodside/Sunnyside is having Spring registration now. SPRING semester starts on 3/31 for 10 weeks with TRIANGLE collection.- every semester there is a new set of music to sing and enjoy! Please don't miss out on this great experience of 45-minutes of PURE FUN with your child every week!

Music Together is an internationally recognized early childhood music program for infants, toddlers, preschoolers and the adults who love them! Since 1987, it pioneered the concept of a research-based, developmentally appropriate, early childhood music curriculum that strongly emphasizes and facilitates adult involvement.

WE HAVE LOWERED THE FEETO HELP EVERYONE TO CONTINUE/START THIS EXTRAORDINARY MUSICAL EXPERIENCE WITH/FOR YOUR CHILD. The tuition is $220 for 10 weeks. Additional class/sibling (over 6 months) fee is $95. Sibling under 6 months attends free of charge with registered child for one semester. The tuition includes 10 sessions+2CDs+a songbook+Parent guide DVD for new families. We also allow 2 makeups when you miss your registered class.

Check out our Spring schedule below and NOTE that we are offering a few new classes for more inquiries including expanded schedules for Wednesday and Friday in JH and a 3's and up class. There are classes from Tuesday through Sunday this Spring. We have teachers with extraordinary experience with their own musical careers and education on staff:
