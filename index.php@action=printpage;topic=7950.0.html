Post by: Beech Court on December 02, 2012, 12:27:02 PM
Just a couple of my own observations:

When the place became Due Fratelli (formerly Giulietta) it seemed to be run by a pair of due fratelli which is Italian for two brothers. They started off with a bang. Several months later the name changed to Due Frankie and I didn't see the "fratelli" anymore. An Indian guy was now there all the time. The name change was reminiscent of the Earle Theater becoming the Eagle. I think the guys tried to sell or lease the business.

Things seemed to go down hill. Then the "fratelli" were back and things sort of picked up again but maybe not quite to where it had been. A little while later the guys were again.

The "fratelli" by the way I believe are Russians, and run another pizzeria on Queens Blvd. in Forest Hills.
