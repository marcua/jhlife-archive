Title: Dog reunited with owners
Post by: Chuckster on April 14, 2010, 12:22:48 PM
Around last week or so, there was a massive search for a little lost dog named Bruno the Brussels.  The search extended to social networking sites like Twitter and Facebook.  Footwork campaigns were also launched with people distributing fliers around the neighborhood.  The efforts paid off, as Bruno was able to be reunited with his owners, Jeff Simmons and Alfonso Quiroz.
 
The New York Times, City Room Blog has a nice follow-up today on the story of Bruno the Brussels.

A Little Dog Touches Off a Big Search (http://cityroom.blogs.nytimes.com/2010/04/14/a-little-dog-touches-off-a-big-search/)
