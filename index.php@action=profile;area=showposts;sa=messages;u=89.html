Neighborhood Chat / Re: Jackson Heights - Corona BID
« on: July 09, 2014, 12:03:58 PM »
FYI

The paperwork be circulated to the business owners are Letters of Support, if a business owner signs it, they support the BID, if they do not, they are not in support.

As seen in the video, there is not a "NO" vote, that is because the process set forth by NYC only solicits Letters of Support, and there must be a certain percentage of "supporters" to make an application for a BID extension.
