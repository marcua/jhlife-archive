Title: Jewish Folk Songs of the World House Concert, 12/29
Post by: toddg on November 29, 2007, 11:28:04 PM
Saturday, 29 December, 7:30 pm.

Jewish Folk Songs of the World House Concert featuring Mark Levy singing in English, Yiddish, Russian, Hebrew, and Ladino. Families with children welcome. Cats in residence. Location: Jackson Heights, Queens, NYC. Reservations required. $10 members. $13 nonmembers. $6 kids & full-time students. Call Eileen for info at 718-672-6399.
Sponsored by the Folk Music Society of New York. (Source (http://www.minstrelrecords.com/fmsny/#calendar))
