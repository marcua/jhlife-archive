Neighborhood Chat / Our Beautiful Trees
« on: March 31, 2011, 05:06:45 PM »
Dear Neighbors,

It breaks my heart to see how many of our lovely trees are festooned with those horrible plastic shopping bags.  They often stay twisted into the branches for years before they fall away. 

I'm trying to start a small movement here be appealing to everybody to please, if you see bags or other plastic pieces floating around in the wind, pick them up and put them firmly in the trash.

I do it and I've seen a few others  do it but the bags seem to be everywhere and they're easily carried by the wind into the high, inaccessible branches of trees.  We need an army of bag-snaggers to solve this problem.

Thanks!
