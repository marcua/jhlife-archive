Title: St Pats for All Parade this Sunday in Sunnyside- JHBG invites you to march!
Post by: itsit on March 04, 2016, 08:53:22 AM
  You are all invited to join the Jackson Heights Beautification Group as they march for the first time in the
St. Pats for All parade. Let's show the folks in Woodside that we as neighbors in JH share the same spirit of
inclusivity they have shown. At the same time we can support this spirited parade. http://www.stpatsforall.com/
All ages are welcome and the meet up is at 43rd Street and Skillman Ave (1 block off Queens Blvd.) in
Sunnyside along the main street with stores and restaurants. Speakers start at 1:00 are loads of
politicians, Colm McCann is a grand marshall this year and there are always some surprises. Actual
marching begins at 2:00 and is over within the hour. The fabulous Donovans with its legendary burgers
is a Irish restaurant at the end of the parade route. Look for the JHBG green banner and join in.

  As the parade organizers say: Whether Irish by birth, heritage or simply affection.... all welcome!
