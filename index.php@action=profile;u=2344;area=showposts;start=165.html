Neighborhood Chat / Re: Street Parking for residents

I usually move my car once a week for street cleaning (I don't use it for commuting anymore, just for out of town trips and shopping at Target, etc.).  I live on a Monday / Tuesday block so I'll go out and move it at 8:30, drive around till I hopefully find a spot, and if not, just sit in the car till 10.  I used to go to Starbucks up on Northern, but unless you get back by 9:40 or so all the spots will be gone and you'll have to move your car the next day.  It can be done, you just have to be strategic about it.  And watch out for driveways! There's someone who lives on the West side of 75th St just south of 34th who has a very small, very un-noticeable curb cut.  I once made the mistake of parking there and got yelled at - fortunately before I got towed!

Restaurants & Food / Re: Lemon Farm mini Gourmet market on 78th/37th
« on: January 17, 2013, 03:26:52 PM »
Lemon Farm has replaced Trade Fair and Met for me for many of my staples.  The prices are usually on par or better than the other grocery stores, and the fruit & veg prices are better than at the green grocer down the street.  I'm usually able to find everything that I need there.  A few things (especially dairy products - they never seem to have cream/half & half) have me going elsewhere but Lemon Farm is usually my first choice.  The fresh coffee beans are quite good, too, and much cheaper than what I'm used to paying at Whole Foods.  This is a VERY welcome addition to the neighborhood, and I'd like to see them stick around for a while! :rockon:
