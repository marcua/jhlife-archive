Real Estate & Home Improvement / Re: Terrace View
« on: August 02, 2010, 10:17:26 AM »
I've shopped around in the Terrace View several times but never pulled the trigger.  Agreed that the building is very nice and the exterior including the facade and green areas are kept up very well.  The terraces are a great touch and the fact that it has parking and a full-time doorman are very nice as well.

A few issues I personally had:
1) Far walk from the subway -- at least 10 city blocks, it would probably take 10-15 mins to walk to the 74th st. station.  And a far enough walk from the main shopping areas that it would be annoying to lug 4-5 heavy grocery bags for 10 mins.
2) The hallways were a bit dismal looking to me and not anywhere nearly as nice as the exterior would have you believe them to be.
3) The maintenance charges are very high.  Maybe proportionately they are acceptable given the extras available, at ~$1 sq. ft. it is still a big nut every month.
4) There seemed to be an usually large number of apartments for sale.  Earlier this summer I went by to see one apartment that I had an appointment for, and somehow I ended up seeing four other apartments one after the other that were also being shown.  It was a bit off putting to see so many places for sale at once and made me second guess if there is something up with the financial state.  I haven't seen the financials so that is just speculation on my part.

These were all reasons that I personally decided not to purchase into this building.
