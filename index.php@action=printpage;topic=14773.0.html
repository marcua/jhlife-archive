Title: DenDekker to Host Free Smoke Alarm Event
Post by: acasey103 on April 28, 2016, 11:41:11 AM
Assemblyman DenDekker has partnered with the Fire Department of New York and the American Red Cross to provide free smoke/carbon monoxide alarms and installation appointments on Sunday, May 1 from 11:00 AM – 1:00 PM. Individuals can sign up for free installation of smoke alarms - there will be no limit to how many smoke alarms can be installed. Additionally, there will be 100 smoke alarms available to take home that day without installation sign-up. The 100 smoke alarms will be limited to one per household and distributed on a first come first serve basis.

For more information, please contact Assemblyman DenDekker’s District Office at (718) 457-0384.
