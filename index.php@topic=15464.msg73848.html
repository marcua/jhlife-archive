Big drop in homicides in the 115 in recent years. Pretty sure last year's total of one was the lowest ever. None so far this year.

Quote from: Shelby2 on October 06, 2016, 07:18:28 PM
One thing to remember is that we have a few more local news sources lately that are reporting things that may have previously not been highlighted for us.

Yeah, and that's a good sign. The more unusual something is, the bigger deal it is in the news. So paradoxically, when crime goes down, you hear about it more, and people understandably get the impression it's a growing problem. But actually, it's when it becomes too routine to report on that you're really in trouble.
