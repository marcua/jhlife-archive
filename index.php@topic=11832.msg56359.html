getting trash cans on street corners
« on: April 25, 2014, 08:14:28 AM »
This may be the least interesting public lobbying effort but I'm trying to figure out who I should contact and figure someone on JHL probably knows.

I like to walk my dog on 34th and 35th but for reasons that escape me, there is not a single public trash can on any street corner where I can dump a poop bag. Not even near Travers park, where you'd think, as a public space, there would be something.

I end up carrying the bag all the way home. My guess is that there would be a lot less dog poop and litter if there were places for people to deposit it.

Is this something the JH Beautification Group could do? Is this a Sanitation Dept issue? Local rep? Is there some community resistance to trash cans by the apartments and co-ops on those streets?
