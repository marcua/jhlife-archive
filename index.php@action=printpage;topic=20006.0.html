Title: "Moving Forward" 2050 Regional Transportation Plan
Post by: toddg on June 18, 2020, 05:51:55 PM
The New York Metropoltian Transportation Council (https://www.nymtc.org/), the federally-designated Metropolitan Planning Organization (https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Metropolitan_planning_organization) for our region, is launching the development of its next Regional Transportation Plan with a horizon year of 2050.  This next Plan is titled "Moving Forward, Your Region Connected." 

They've opened up a "community engagement platform" for getting input on this early stage of plan development through a series of online exercises.  Check it out here:
https://www.nymtc.org/Planmovingforward 

You'll also see information on a series of virtual forums they'll be holding next month.  Check it out.
