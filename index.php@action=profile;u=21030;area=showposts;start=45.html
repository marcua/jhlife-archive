You're not going to find fresh beans in Jackson Heights.  The one time I bought beans at Espresso 77  they were older than my Grammar School tie (read that as probably older than you).  Same goes for Lemon Tree and the alleged 'supermarkets'.

I've been buying from Intelligentsia in Chicago (https://www.intelligentsiacoffee.com) for about 8- 10  years now.
Buy just enough, based on your use pattern to justify the shipping ($5 for 6 12oz bags). 

You might find this link helpful:  http://www.talkaboutcoffee.com/coffee-storage.html


If you're not into web ordering, they have a cafe/retail shop near Macy's in Manhattan (I think 7th Ave and 36th).

Just curious, what grinder(s) and brewer(s)/steeper(s),  infuser(s) are you using?
