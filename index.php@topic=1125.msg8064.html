They forgot one:

RECESSIONS
Because everyone's freaking out over the gloomy news, they're staying home at night and plowing cash into savings accounts instead of spending.  The reduced spending causes demand for products and services to drop (this is what causes the recession in the first place).  When demand drops, merchants have to lower their prices to stay competitive.  Merchants who sell essential items don't react nearly as much as those who sell luxury goods and services.  Now's the time to buy those luxury vacation packages, sports cars, and fine cigars.  Yes, invest in leisure while you watch the world BURN!
