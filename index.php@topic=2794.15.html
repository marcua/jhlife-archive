I am  a foodie and I agree that J Hts Cafes with ambience  are few and far between.SOMEONE OPEN ONE!!!!!!PLEASE, OR HOW ABOUT A WINE BAR...  Jardi ( tapas bar) on Norther 86/87 is very good and nice atmosphere,  La Flor on Roosevelt /54th is great food and cutesy, Sripaphai ( the best Thai outside of Bangkok, I know I lived there!) on 65th/39th Ave off Roosevelt is great. Just tried Uncle Peters on Northern and 83rd (I think) nice atmosphere huge portions.  Pio Pio on Northern is great decor and roast chicken.  For the vegetarians there salad is enormous and filled with avocado and various veggies. Limoncello on 82/83rd and Baxter off Roosevelt is good food but decor looks like someone's took the drapes from someone's living room.  Owner is charming though and the food is good. I guess Novo is the hippest ( if you can believe it)  place in the neighborhood. They had a trio last night and the food is decent.  It has a bar where you can get a drink and finally they stop the incessant meringue music.  Okay someone  listen up - French Bisto, Mediterranean Osteria, a Pannini, Salad & Pasta Restaurant, A pub with a decent hamburger......it's time......

We went to Urubamba for Father's Day dinner on Sunday. It was excellent. For those who don't know, it's located at the corner of 37th Avenue and 86th (or 87th?) Street. It's Peruvian, the atmosphere was very nice. My wife thought the dishes were too salty, but I disagreed. The only down side was that it took a long time to get our food, granted they looked extremely busy for a Sunday evening. The appetizer sampler plate was excellent. It had fried calamari, ceviche, a chicken tamale and beef/olive/cilantro stuffed potatoes. My wife had the paella, which she said was very good. I had the Skirt Steak (enormous) with rice and Peruvian beans. Also very good.

We also had a pitcher of the Peruvian spiced punch. Also excellent.

The ambiance was the best I've experienced, next to Jardi, in the neighborhood so far.

Highly recommended. I hope the slow service was just a fluke. I'll be back here for sure.
