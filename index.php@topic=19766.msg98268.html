Table Wine is open for pickup and delivery and has a good system in place - order by phone - 718 478 9463 - and either have delivered or pick up from their shop. If picking up, you don't enter the store, just get their attention, give your name and they leave your purchase on a bench outside. You must pay by credit card over the phone, so they do not come in direct contact with customers.

I also noticed American Chicken, Emoji Burger, Just Made 4 You, and K&L Deli open. More and more restaurants are closed.

There were fewer vendors at the farmers market today as well.

I'm definitely torn between staying home and going out in public to support our local businesses. I generally don't use delivery and feel stranger about doing so now. If you are vulnerable, then I think delivery or relying on neighbors makes sense. Don't hesitate to reach out to people you know for help if you need it!
