News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

1st ever Earth Day Celebration at JHSCRAPS on April 20th, noon - 4pm
« on: April 11, 2013, 10:41:19 PM »
 JH SCRAPS, our new community composting center, is holding it's first Earth Day
celebration on April 20th from 12-4pm. Come see what they are doing with your
food scraps that are collected on SATURDAYS (11-1) at Travers Park. There will
be worm bins, seed bomb making for guerilla gardening and a place to make
recycled pots from newspaper for your new seedlings. See the projects this group
is working on to make this spot our local environmental center.
 The site is located in from the corner of 35th Avenue and 69th street* and slightly
under the highway. Look for the JHSCRAPS banner. Sponsored by the wonderful
Jackson Heights Beautification Group.
