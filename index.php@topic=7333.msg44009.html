After reading several posts recommending Aranda plumbing, I recently contracted them to do work in our apartment.  Unfortunately, our experience was not as favorable as others have posted. 

We ended up paying for work which will have to be redone by others.  For instance, the plumber took down our bathroom door stripping the screw holes in the process.  The door was rehung by drilling new holes but now the door no longer closes as the hinges are now out of alignment. The door was taken down and rehung while I was at the hardware store purchasing materials the plumber should have brought with him.  I could have told them that removing the pin is the simplest way to take down a door.

The big kicker was that not only did the plumber not own up to his mistake but the office has refused to return any of my phone calls the last two weeks to discuss the matter.  After a visit from the super to inspect the overall plumbing job, it also turns out their sink installations were not in accordance with the NYC Plumbing Code.

Other issues included paying for long periods of time to "locate and retrieve" tools from the truck (and personal residence) as well as paying to watch the apprentice learn the trade on my dime. 

Please learn from my mistake and do your homework before blindly hiring a plumber recommended on a website.  I am sure Aranda Plumbing has done plenty of professional installations but sadly not in my case and would NOT recommend them at all.
