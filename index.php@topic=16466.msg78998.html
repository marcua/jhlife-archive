Juju's is a one-man band, and the quality of the food he cooks is very good. He obviously sells far more than just bagels and spreads--the "bagel shop" name is not accurate in terms of what he actually offers. He probably thought it would be a good draw, so that's the name he chose. But it doesn't reflect the scope of the place. I often go for lunch on the weekends, and usually get an omelet or panini. I'm very critical and fussy about food, and I really like the place.

He just opened up for dinner and I hope people will support him. He has a Lebanese menu and a Mediterranean menu--a type of food I love and that is lacking in Jackson Heights, with the exception of Plaka, which is a bit out of the way. I'm really excited about this.
