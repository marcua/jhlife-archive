Dear friends and neighbors, when we decided to open The Queensboro, we agreed to make a restaurant that was for the community by the community.
 
We listened to what our neighbors want in a local restaurant, and committed our combined four decades of experience in the industry to create a place where people in Jackson Heights and surrounding neighborhoods could come for delicious food and drinks and friendly service, as well as for community events and special occasions.
 
We would love to hear from you, let us know how we’re doing! (https://forms.gle/uCMmEBcqgEMEdSaSA)
 
We hope you’ll take one or two minutes to share your honest thoughts about The Queensboro so that we can continue to improve, grow and best serve the community. Please feel free to tell us what you love and what you think we can do better. Your responses are anonymous and your information will not be shared or distributed.

Thank you in advance for your input and help!
