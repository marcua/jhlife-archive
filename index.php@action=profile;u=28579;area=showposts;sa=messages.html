Ok Guys,

I dropped by Blink today and I'll share what I learned.

1) They will open September

2) If you sign up and pay $1 today, you save the start up fee of
  a) $99 for Blue membership (Access to all locations expect Noho)
  b) $49 Gray membership (Single club access)

4) Annual fee is $39 for Blue, $29 for Gray
