Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

A friend requested that I post the following information regarding a protest rally scheduled for tomorrow, Sunday, June 14th.  I also cross-posted this event in the Community Groups, Activities & Events board.

JUNE 12, 2009
FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE
Contact: NYSYD President Matthew Silverstein, (718)644-0791, President@nysyd.net
VOTERS TO RALLY IN HIRAM MONSERRATE'S DISTRICT TO EXPRESS OUTRAGE AT GOP COUP OF STATE SENATE

Will Call Upon Monserrate, Elected as a Democrat, Not to Give Up Control of State Senate to Republican Party

The NYS Young Democrats and hundreds of local voters will rally in Senator Hiram Monserrate's Queens district on Sunday, June 14 at 1 p.m. to express their outrage at the GOP-manufactured coup that has brought state and city government to a grinding halt and disenfranchised the millions of New York voters who elected a Democratic majority to the State Senate for the first time in four decades.

They will also call upon Senator Monserrate to preserve the will of the voters of New York State by supporting the duly elected Democratic Majority of the State Senate.

Besides mobilizing Democratic voters in Senator Monserrate's district through a fervent telephone and Internet campaign, the rally organizers are inviting all New Yorkers supporting the duly elected Democratic State Senate Majority to attend the rally.


WHEN:  Sunday, June 14 at 1:00 P.M.

WHERE:  78th Street between Northern Blvd. and 34th Ave., Jackson Heights, Queens, NY, 11372

WHAT:  The NYS Young Democrats and hundreds of local voters will rally in Senator Hiram Monserrate's Queens district to express their outrage at this week's GOP coup of the New York State Senate.

THIS EVENT HAS BEEN POSTPONED.  SEE POSTPONEMENT NOTICE BELOW.
