Hi all,

I met with St. Mark's over the past weekend and confirmed space availability starting on 2/12.  Yay!!
Below are preliminary details for the class.  I will post the information in other forum sections as well, once I finalize all the details.  Please definitely feel free to give me feedbacks so we can add this to our community.

Location:  St. Mark's Episcopal Church, 3350 82nd Street
Day/Time: 3PM, every Saturday starting 2/12
Cost:       $15/class, or $50/4classes (participants 65+ years of age will get 2/12 class for free.  I definitely want to provide discounts but need to figure out participation numbers first)

Instructor: Zhou, Fuxin
(Brief intro.  I'm meeting with him to put together a better intro)
Zhou shifu (shifu = Chinese for instructor/master) is a long time practitioner of Taichi, and has been teaching since 2005.  He is a certified instructor by the Chinese Wushu Association with numerous gold medals and first place awards in Chinese regional and national competitions.  Since moving to Jackson Heights in the fall of 2010, he had practiced & taught me in Travers Park on Saturdays.  Many other practitioners there have observed him and provided great feedbacks on his form and knowledge of the art.

I hope the class can provide another healthy activity to our colorful community.
