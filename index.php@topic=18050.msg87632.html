IT’S OPEN

Went in a few days ago.

Location: 40-37 75 St  on the south side of broadway.

Less a gastropub (and by less, I mean "ZERO"),

..... more an incredibly loud sports bar. (6 giant tvs all playing sports, bright neon/lights).

Drinks list is all InBev, no  (zero) craft beer. Wines and spirits pretty generic/mass market too. Some funky cocktails, but nothing you couldn't get in the neighborhood already.

Not sure if they have food yet…..(apparently they have popcorn)

Very convenient for stumbling in/out of the Roosevelt ave station.

Don’t think anyone has to worry about gentrification with this one…..
