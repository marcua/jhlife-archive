Listen, Washington Square Park is a pleasant (and beautiful) oasis in the midst of some of the fanciest real estate in NYC. I often go to hear the excellent busking and experience the fun vibe.

Often, I'm approached by upfront dealers who offer to sell me drugs...beckoning with "Don't be bashful!".

And always I encounter homeless folks there.

Do I think Washington Square Park should be closed down... and turned into a wide highway? Absolutely not.

A bit of perspective here folks. 

What the plaza needs is GREEN LANDSCAPING to make it pleasant. 

Then we can all be proud of it.

And learn how to share it. Just like Washington Square Park is shared by all.

Now that's actually not as hard as we think.  Many communities started gardens in bombed out vacant lots in the LES.
These gardens are now little paradises in the LES.

They didn't wait for anyone...Not DOT or Bloomberg or their mothers to do it.

They simply made beauty... where previously there was only abandonment.

What I'm saying is that bitching is easy but meaningless. 

Creating something special from desolation, now that's meaningful.

Folks who get together and do that...are worthy of respect.
