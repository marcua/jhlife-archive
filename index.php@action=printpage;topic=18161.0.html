Post by: MrPlaza on August 27, 2018, 02:10:32 PM
The building is undeniably out of character for the neighborhood, but as an avid movie goer, I for one am intrigued by another theater option. Especially one that's on my way home from work.

That being said, UA Kaufman is literally only a mile directly north of this location. Understanding that Kaufman more so serves Astoria and northern LIC, they are extremely close to one another. Enough that there will likely be cannibalization. Seems like an odd business decision on Regal's part.

Nevertheless, once this is built, I'll probably start going here over UA Kaufman, unless I'm craving Chela & Garnacha or Arepa Cafe. It's a straighter shot on the 7-train, and I'm fond of a few of the restaurants on the surrounding blocks (SoleLuna, Turkish Grill, etc.).
