Friendly low key place & service. Food is fresh & made with care. Don’t seem to have a liquor license yet. Like that it’s not too “sit down-y.” Got the fried calamari & it was quite tasty, not at all rubbery or greasy. It’s a welcome addition & I’m looking forward to many more cozy meals there!
