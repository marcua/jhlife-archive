Title: Espresso 77 is expanding and we need your help!
Post by: espresso 77 on July 30, 2015, 12:02:15 PM
Dear Jackson Heights Friends and Neighbors.
We excited to announce that Espresso 77 will soon be renovated and will be expanding into the adjacent retail space!
Most importantly, this will mean more seating (hurray!), but it also gives us a chance to freshen up the storefront, add new items to our menu (draft beer, anyone?), and upgrade our air-conditioning and sound systems.

NOW - WE NEED YOUR HELP!
We have been saving our pennies in hopes that this opportunity would finally come our way, but we need a bigger budget to make it terrific.  We have set up an IndieGogo fundraising site (http://igg.me/at/espresso77/x/5521115 (http://igg.me/at/espresso77/x/5521115)), and we are asking for the financial support of our friends, neighbors and customers to help out with the work.  We've set up some great perks to show our appreciation - t-shirts, parties, free coffee - most of all, know that with your help, we are going to invest in the life of our community for years to come.

Thank you for the past 8 years and we look forward for many more years of coffee, art and music!
Afzal and Julie
