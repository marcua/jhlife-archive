Fillings are good at K&L as is service. Their bread is a big MEH (only white flour-y  bland stale-ish heros, forgettable buns and so-so bagels). Guyz; get great bread and you would win my service daily!

 If Juju's is open their bagels are great and sandwiches are solid (and their whole-wheat croissant makes a kick-ass breakfast sandwich), but service is entirely dependent on how on top of things Sammy is - if you get anyone else behind the counter.....prepare to........wait.

I just had a fantastic Cemita from El Rico tinto, but they only open at 830....unsure if that is early enough for you?

In all cases, calling ahead and ordering the sandwich to pick up is a better option than arriving to a big line if on the way to work......
