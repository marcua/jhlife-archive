Title: Espresso 77 is Renovating - but first, Music and Specials this weekend!
Post by: espresso 77 on January 09, 2016, 06:06:35 PM
Hello Jackson Heights!
Starting this Monday, January 11th, Espresso 77 will be closed for the construction of our new expanded space.  We expect to re-open in about a month and will post here once we have a re-opening date set.

BUT FIRST - Saturday and Sunday have exciting things going on!

Tonight, we've got our last live music show before the renovation - Mike Tedesco will be bringing his fun mix of jazz, pop and soft rock to the mic at 8:30pm; get there early to grab a seat.  We've got all of our drinks on special tonight - all beers are $5 and all wines are $6 a glass - we'd rather have you enjoy them than put all those bottles into storage!

Sunday, we will be open for our regular hours - drink specials will continue and we'll have pastries and sandwiches on special as well.  Don't forget to stock up on coffee beans too!

Thanks to everyone for all of your support - we are excited about bringing you a bigger and better e77!

Julie and Afzal
