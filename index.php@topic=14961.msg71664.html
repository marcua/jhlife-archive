About a month and a half ago some basket case turned up on the porch across the street from my building. He's usually angry and drunk by 9:00 AM (often earlier), shouts obscenities at people as they walk by, got into my super's face because he didn't like his t-shirt, screams that this is his block, makes threats to people as they walk by, made threats against my neighbor's sister, and attacked two people with his cane two days in a row (not sure why he has a cane, given that he is in his 30s and seems to walk fine). He usually disappears for a few hours in the afternoon/evening but is usually back on the porch by 9PM. The good thing is that he usually fall down drunk by then and can't hurt anyone.

The police have been called several times but he just goes into the house and doesn't answer the door but comes out after they leave. The first time he attacked someone the police talked to him and someone in the house a couple of doors down but didn't make an arrest for whatever reason.

This guy is clearly not right and something needs to be done before he decides to get stabby or shooty. The police seemingly can't do anything. My building's board has been going through security tapes but has yet to find anything useful. I've been trying to find some social services office that might be able to help with this but keep hitting dead ends. Anyone have any ideas?
