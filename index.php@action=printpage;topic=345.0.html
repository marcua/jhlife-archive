Crime fighter to bring justice to Queens (http://www.nydailynews.com/ny_local/queens/2008/01/17/2008-01-17_crime_fighter_to_bring_justice_to_queens.html)

BY JESS WISLOSKI
DAILY NEWS STAFF WRITER

Thursday, January 17th 2008, 4:00 AM

For 26 years, he has fought crime and struggled to bring justice to Brooklyn's streets.

Now Deputy Inspector John Lavelle, the new commanding officer of the 115th Precinct, hopes to bring peace of mind to the residents of Jackson Heights, East Elmhurst and North Corona.

His biggest challenge might be topping the precinct's impressive 2007 record, which saw an overall drop in serious crimes of 10.2%.
