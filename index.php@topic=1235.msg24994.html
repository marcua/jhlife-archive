There aren't many places that are licensed to care for children under 2, and there's a definite shortage in the neighborhood.  I think Rainbowland (718-803-1728) may cover this age group.  My son attended Adventureland (on 58th Street in Woodside, 718-274-4769), which was excellent.  I've heard that there are also smaller in-home group daycare arrangements available in JH, but don't know anything about these.

You may also want to ask your question at the JHFamilies listserv, http://groups.yahoo.com/group/jhfamilies/ ... there's a very active community of parents over there who are very helpful with this type of question.
