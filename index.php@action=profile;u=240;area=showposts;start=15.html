Restaurants & Food / Re: Where's the Ambiance?
« on: June 01, 2009, 04:59:38 PM »
delhi palace has the worst service ever. i recall at least two occasions when the people i was dining with and i had to remind the wait staff multiple (like 5x) times to give us our check. they were also slow to refill our water glasses as well. i am going to stand by delhi heights for its ambience, great service, and yummy food. i haven't been to jackson diner in at least a year so i'm not sure what it's like these days, but the food was always good, though not great. ambience is obviously lacking there (cafeteria style set up). i still need to get my butt to mehfil. and i've had take out from deshi biryani, which was really flavorful. the decor from the outside looks nice and homey. i also like rice ave. for ambience and food. some might say armando's is good for ambience (if you like the sort of old world style), though i haven't been there in years because it's simply overpriced for the neighborhood. what about tibetan yak? it looks cute from the outside and i've also heard good things about the food (if you know how and what to order).

no, there are no super cutesy cafes or bistros here (yet?), but that's not to say we don't have a lot of good options.
