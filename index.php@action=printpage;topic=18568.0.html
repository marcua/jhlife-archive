From the business
Specialties

Queens Beerhouse is specialized in German cuisine. Our menu is inspired by famous German restaurants in Munich features a selection of the most authentic German food brought to Queens, NY. Our beer selection features imported German beers to create the Oktoberfest feeling throughout the whole year.
History

Established in 2018.

Queens Beerhouse was founded in 2018 by an experienced owner who has been in the German restaurant business for a long time.
