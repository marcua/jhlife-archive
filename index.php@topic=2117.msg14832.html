Julissa Ferreras for City Council District 21 (special election is 2/24/09)
« on: January 17, 2009, 10:44:02 AM »
Ron Hayduk and I are planning a special, fun-filled neighborhood event and we hope you'll come and enjoy it!

Julissa Ferreras, a candidate for City Council, District 21 is the reason for the early evening party.  The details are as follows:

  • Date: Saturday, January 24, 2009
  • Time: 5 - 7 p.m.
  • Place: Law Offices of Bryan Pu-Folkes, 37-43 77th Street (bet. 37th Ave. and Roosevelt Ave.)
  • What: Julissa Ferreras For City Council Fundraiser
  • Dinner provided by Jackson Diner, Natives and Uncle Peter's
  • Drinks provided by the Law Offices of Bryan Pu-Folkes

To RSVP e-mail bryan@pufolkeslaw. com or call 718-205-5353.

A minimum of $10 per person (free for kids under eight years old).  Below are the top 10 reasons why I strongly encourage you to come to Julissa's fundraising party:

1.   Julissa is smart, affable, responsible and a doer!

2.  Ron Hayduk and I strongly believe that Julissa is the most qualified candidate in the race.

3.  Julissa grew up in Jackson Heights and Corona and has been a tireless advocate for our community.  See for yourself:  www.julissaferreras.com.

4.  The two most important City Council seats for Jackson Heights are the 25th (currently held by Helen Sears) and the 21st (formerly held by Hiram Monserrate) - regardless of where in the neighborhood we live, the government officials who represent us in those seats make decisions that directly impact our quality of life.

5.  Julissa has concrete proposals to ensure, among other things, greater funding for our schools, cleaner and safer streets, and a healthier retail mix along our commercial corridors.

6.  You get to disicuss your views directly and personally with Julissa, the next City Council member from the 21st Council District, what are our communities need and priorities.

7.  It's our tax dollars that pay their salaries.  Let's start off the new year by helping to elect the most qualified candidate and post-election let's work closely with them to ensure our neighborhood receives the resources and services we need and deserve!

8.  Knowing Julissa for over 10 years we know that once elected she will be actively in touch with you and make sure our collective needs are in fact her office's priorities.

9.  You get to hang out at a party with great friends and food! 

10.  It will have been 4 days after the President's Inauguration and we'll all be thinking "Yes We Can!"

Julissa is the only candidate with support from 14 current NYC Council Members, Assemblyman Jose Peralta and many, many from the community. Come one, come all! 

To RSVP e-mail bryan@pufolkeslaw. com or call 718-205-5353.

Working together, we will ensure effective and responsible leadership in government and bring many positive and tangible changes to our neighborhoods and lives!

Sincerely,
Bryan Pu-Folkes
