Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Burger was really delicious. I wish they had salads and more drinks other than shakes (too fattening, I'd never have one). Since they are owned by the same people as Just Made 4 You, maybe they could work it so that you could order food from both places. That happened in my old neighborhood. There were two restaurants side by side owned by the same people. And you could order off either menu or from both menus. They eventually combined into one larger place (one side became a bar/lounge). Anyway I'm a big fan of Just Made 4 You, and though I enjoyed my turkey burger at emoji very much, on a regular basis I eat more salads and veggies than burgers. It would be ideal if I could order a salad AND a burger, and maybe split each with my husband. I just don't tend to eat a burger and fries for a meal: too carb and fat heavy. And so far emoji has no veggies except fries. That said, they seem to be doing good business with lots of takeout.  I salute them! As I said, the burger was yummy with the aioli, avocado, etc, they added to it.
