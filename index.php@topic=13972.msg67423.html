Sign Up For Farm Spot Organic CSA Winter Shares!!
« on: November 11, 2015, 11:24:54 PM »
Are you interested in trying out Farm Spot, Jackson Heights’ Community Supported Agriculture (CSA) Program?

There are two separate time frames for Farm Spot Winter Shares running straight through the Winter!  You can sign up for both or just one.


Pick up your shares on Thursdays between the hours of 5:30 and 8:15 p.m. at St. Mark’s Church at 33-50 82nd Street.

1. Golden Earthworm Organic Winter Share & Briermere Apple/Cider shares (non-organic) runs from December 3rd through January 7th and includes three distributions (12/3, 12/17 & 1/7)

2. Norwich Meadows Organic Farm Shares (Meat, Ovo-Lacto & Vegan Shares) runs from Feb 18th through May 12th  (3/10, 3/31, 4/21 and 5/12)



Golden Earthworm Winter Shares (3 distributions)

CERTIFIED ORGANIC root vegetables and storage crops like Potatoes, Sweet Potatoes, Watermelon Radish, Carrots, Beets, Rutabaga, Winter Squash, and possibly Broccoli, Kale and Cabbage.  Exact contents may change.

Cider and Apple Share from Briermere Farms that is not organic. 



Shares Types (three deliveries each)

Golden Earthworm Root Vegetable Share $96

Briermere Cider Share  $15

Briermere Apple Share $15.75



Click HERE http://csa.farmigo.com/join/farmspot/winter2016 to sign up for Golden Earthworm and Briermere Winter shares!



Norwich Meadows Farms Organic Winter Shares (5 Distributions)



Meat Share

Some examples of what you will find in your share include: Yogurt, Meat (ground beef) or Chicken, Spring Garlic, Spinach, Dry Beans, Onions, Eggs, Carrots, Potatoes.



Ovo/Lacto Share

Some examples of what you will get from this distribution include: Yogurt, Eggs, Garlic, Beets, Potatoes, Cheese, Spinach, Radishes, Granola, Dry Beans, Turnips, Cabbage.



Vegan Share

Some examples of what you will find in your share include: Carrots, Maple Syrup, Flour, Carrots, Beets, Shallots, Potatoes, Spring Onion, Spring Garlic, Dry Beans.



All shares are $265.00 and amounts are adjusted based on the cost of each item.



Distribution Dates for Norwich Farms- Feb 18th,  March 10th, March 31st, April 21st and May 12th

 

Click here http://csa.farmigo.com/join/farmspot/winter2016to sign up for an Organic Norwich Farm Share!



Any questions?  Email us at farmspot@gmail.com
