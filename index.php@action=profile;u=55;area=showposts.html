Neighborhood Chat / Broken Glasses Scam Alert
« on: January 20, 2009, 09:50:06 AM »
As I was exiting the 74th St.-Roosevelt Ave. station yesterday I got bumped by a man. It was about 5:30, right in the middle of rush hour, and I had just got to the top of the stairs at the main entrance when the it happened. I saw the man approach, he was full head taller than me, and I am six foot, so he was hard to miss. As I saw him I shifted to my right to avoid a collision. He shifted to his left though and we bumped. This was not the typical shoulder-to-shoulder passing, but rather his arm dragged across my chest. At the same time I heard something fall. I looked down and noticed a pair of eye glasses. I retrieved the glasses and handed them to the man and turned to exit the station. The man grabbed my shoulder before I could get through the turnstyle and told me I broke his glasses. He showed the glasses to me, they were a well-worn pair of Duane Reade style reading glasses, not prescription, and indeed they were cracked. I apologized for the incident and pushed through the turnstyle, hoping the man wouldn't leave the station, or that the NYPD would be in their usual holding-up-the-wall position. Neither proved true. The man exited the turnstyles and grabbed me a second time, before I could leave through the doors. He told me he was just on his way to work and had no insurance in an attempt to appeal to my guilt. When that didn't work and as I tried to leave again the man changed his tone and threatened me. I shrugged off his threats and left. The man did not follow me outside but it was an anxiety filled walk home nevertheless.

The dropped eyeglasses, or some such variant, is a classic scam. I wanted everyone to be aware of my experience in case he tries this stunt in the future.

Here is a link to a New Yorker article profiling a similar scam. http://www.newyorker.com/archive/2006/05/15/060515ta_talk_mcgrath

Neighborhood Chat / Re: Christmas Trees
« on: December 09, 2008, 08:44:14 AM »
We were disappointed that Fruitel Farms (the fruit stand on 37th and 78th) was not selling trees again this year as we got an awesome 7 foot tree for $40 there last year. So this year we went to a guy selling trees at the drug store (a Duane Reade?) on Northern and 85th. Not many trees over 6 feet and they range from $40-$60 depending on the type and quality. The tree we got is about 6 feet, great shape and fullness, with blue undertones. I would recommend checking them out. The vendor is Canadian and he was a hoot to buy from.
