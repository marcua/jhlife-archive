I am sorry I didn't hear about any such event. I have written to the Congressman about Healthcare and my concerns.
There is no room for such negativism that distracts from the issue. The healthcare needs of our citizens are so important that the debate should not be diluted by irrelevant things.

The healthcare debate needs to bring to light the points in the bills that are so large and unreadable. We need to consider necessary changes but not attempt to remake an entire industry in one swoop. Now I understand that there is going to be a government investigation into the finances of the insurance industry. This is a distraction from the real points. People need medical coverage, the medical/legal aspects must be reviewed to protect patients and providers but not continue the horrors of tort law, and finally, we must not diminish our healthcare to the kind that is imploding in Canada, Massachusetts, California and other areas of the USA.

This is such a violate aspect of our lives we must take the road carefully and without rancor.
