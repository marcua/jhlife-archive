This solution sanctions/encourages double parking.

Double parking is more than just a nuisance. Emergency vehicles are slowed. DPing causes accidents, especially near intersections. It can make access to a fire hydrant impossible. Ppl/kids/animals not in the crosswalk are put in double danger. And what happens to bike lanes?

One ways also contribute to air pollution as drivers who are trying to park legitimately are forced to circle twice the distance.

Enforcement is the only really good option for a number of reasons, but it won't happen.
