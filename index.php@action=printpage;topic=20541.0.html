Title: Re: where to donate books?
Post by: voelteer on May 19, 2021, 10:49:22 AM
Confirming that Housing Works Bookstore Cafe accepts most media donations (usually no magazines, yearbooks and the like, though) -- AND it's a super organization! Because of the pandemic, obviously, smaller in-person donations (a couple boxes) in MH aren't happening now. However, according to the website, "We are still doing complimentary pick-ups for large high-quality donations/collections." The email for further info: donations@housingworksbookstore.org.
