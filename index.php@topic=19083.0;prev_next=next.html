I know this thread is old but with all the newer places opening,  I thought I'd revive a good thing. Had dinner here on Saturday night after being away from the place for awhile.  Such a pleasant and welcoming atmosphere and absolutely delicious food.  There were several specials being offered  but I was feeling down and dirty so I ordered a fried calamari ( best in the hood IMHO) and a veal marsala which was very nicely prepared.... not tough or overcooked.  Mojitos  were excellent as was the wine. Such a soothing environment (N0 blaring TV) and the service was attentive and unintrusive. One of the nicest places in JH.
