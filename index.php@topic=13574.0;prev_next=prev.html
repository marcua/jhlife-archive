What a beautiful cat!

I want to point out, in case anyone is still not aware of it, that cats with FIV can live nice, long lives if they remain indoors and are well cared for.  Such a diagnosis is by no means a death sentence and it does not mean that the cat will necessarily show any signs of illness.  FIV is not contagious to humans and can only be communicated to another cat via a deep bite.

If you have a friendly cat that might benefit from a companion, this sweetie may be just the one.   I understand he is friendly to other cats but Dotley can confirm this, if you're interested.
