Title: Yoga Class followed by Wine Tasting January 23rd, friday 7:30pm
Post by: yogainqueens on January 21, 2010, 11:23:46 PM
Location: Creative Vibrations Yoga Studio
22-55 31st street 2nd floor Suite 200 Astoria NY 11105 www.yogaastoria.com 347-257-7768

Start your weekend chilled & relaxed, join us this Friday Night for a delightfully unpredictable, dynamic and creative dynamic Vinyasa Flow yoga class with Aliki!

The only requirement for this class is a playful spirit.
Be prepared to explore and have fun.

This class is open to both beginner and intermediate levels but remains physically challenging and spiritually motivating.

Through posture, breath and unique relaxing techniques you'll end your practice with the linking of breath and body, preparing you for a state of deep relaxation. You'll begin to gain a deeper awareness of your body and develop higher levels of strength, focus and flexibility nourishing both body and mind. Always being guided by your comfort level, modifications are introduced for challenging poses so that even beginner students can work comfortably at their own pace.

The class is followed by 15-30 minutes wine tasting to unwind from the long week and allow us to relax and enjoy the health benefits of yoga and wine.
