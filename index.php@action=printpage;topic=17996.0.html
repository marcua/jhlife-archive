Title: Jackson Heights Kitten Adoption Week at Petco 7/7/18 - 7/14/18
Post by: lisco01 on July 07, 2018, 05:55:15 PM
Sponsored by the Petco Foundation, Feral Furbiscuits is hosting it's quarterly Jackson Heights kitten adoption event.  All the kittens/cats available for adoption were rescued right here in Jackson Heights, by Feral Furbiscuits (a local 501(c)(3)). 

9 kittens are available (more details here - https://drive.google.com/file/d/1xbNHcXPXjnUOkBhk2Mt1ykw2H31UUs87/view?usp=sharing)
2 adult cats are available (1 tabby female, 1-year old "Precious" and 1 special needs male tabby, 1 - year old "Chubby Chai-Chai").

Keep up with us on Instagram - https://www.instagram.com/feralfurbiscuits/
