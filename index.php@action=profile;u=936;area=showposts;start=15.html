Restaurants & Food / Re: Bars, Pubs, and Nightlife in JH
« on: August 10, 2009, 03:37:59 PM »
I am probably much closer to Cassidy's than most of you but in my opinion, it is worth the walk for beer and for the food. I've always found it to be friendly and welcoming. In fact, when I moved here in October after a long and yucky day unloading the moving truck by myself, I went there and was instantly welcomed. When I said I was new to the neighborhood, the bartender introduced me to everyone and everyone was very friendly. Having lived in a lot of different places, it was most of the nicest welcomes I have gotten to a new neighborhood. People gave me advice about different.
I now go there frequently with my husband and (sometimes) our 11-year-old. When we do bring our daughter, they are always really nice to her and remember that she likes Shirley Temples The food is really good, the special burger is fantastic and they do their own steak fries. During football season, they had a wings (25 cents) and Coors Light special ($7/pitcher).
And their draft beer selection is better than most in the neighborhood. They just added Franziskaner Hefeweizen which is delightful and much cheaper than most other places.
