I often come home to JH about midnight and often notice the office furniture lined up along the street. I  mentioned this at a community meeting where the local police precinct was present a few weeks ago at the Jewish Center (Nov?).  They suggested I call 311 when the furniture was out on the street.

A week or so later, I did call 311 who patched me through to 911 as they thought I meant the office furniture might be stolen goods, 911 said they couldn't do anything unless I hung around for an indeterminate amount of time until a patrol car showed up.   They could not tell me how long it would take.  I declined the suggestion to stand on a not so desirable street corner at that time of night unless they could give me a time.

As I was walking home, I approached a patrol car on 37 ave and mentioned the furniture and how the police captain suggested I contact the police when I noticed the furniture was out. They said they would drive by and check it out.

I called the police station directly and left several messages for the community liaison person the next 3 times I noticed the furniture over the next few weeks and again noticed them out the other night.

I wanted to take some photos of the furniture and the truck but didn't feel it was safe as I was by myself and it was late.

I'm going to contact Dromm's office the next time.
