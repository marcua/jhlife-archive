Community Trade and Classifieds / New Student Openings with Amazing Local Piano Teacher!
« on: May 17, 2010, 12:53:34 PM »
Experienced piano teacher with amazing patience and passion for her work and her students currently has openings for new students at her home studio in Astoria!

Kristy supports her students as they master classical music technique, theory, history, and artistic expression in a personalized way that allows them to thrive as budding pianists, while steeping them in the traditions of master composers and performers and challenging them to grow as talented instrumentalists. She organizes bi-yearly recitals for her students to celebrate their accomplishments in a comfortable, fun performance setting, while also allowing them to explore the art of performing and becoming comfortable and confident presenting their artistic accomplishments in public.

These many skills that her students develop are useful not just in artistic endeavors but in many aspects of life, and the parents of her students often find that their children have more overall confidence and a sense of accomplishment through their studies with Kristy.

Among her many accomplishments, Kristy has been playing piano for over 20 years, received a B.A. in music with honors and magna cum laude from Moravian College; won Moravian's concerto competition as an undergraduate; was selected to participate in master classes with renowned pianists Gary Graffman and Robert Taub; and has worked for many years developing the programs of several major performing arts organizations throughout the northeast, from New York City Opera to the Philadelphia Orchestra.

Learning an instrument develops many qualities beyond the music: cognitive and presentation skills, dexterity and coordination, artistic sensibility, persistence and dedication, and even a basic understanding of physics that make musicians among the best students in their schools and professionals in their fields.

It's never too late to learn! Give your child or yourself the gift of expression through the art of music!

Call or email for more information and to find out about Kristy's reasonable rates or scheduling lessons in your own home: 718-606-1536; kristybredin@yahoo.com.

Hello Friends in Jackson Heights!

My name is Kristy Bredin, and I am a Holistic Health Counselor and Herbal Medicine Apprentice bringing health and well being to members of the community.

As a Holistic Health Counselor, I work with you to create your unique diet and lifestyle for balance, optimal health, and happiness. Unlike other nutritionists, Holistic Health Counselors look at the whole person when it comes to nutritional and health needs and have a broad understanding of health and dietary theories--from Macrobiotics to the Atkins diet--and how the food we eat, activities we engage in, and people we surround ourselves with are all interconnected.

A few years ago I was lost. I had minor health issues that were plaguing me, and for which no doctor could help me. I had lost all passion for my work and didn't know what I wanted to do with my life. I had just gotten out of a long-term relationship, and I had a large amount of credit card debt that I couldn't deal with. But when I started working with a Holistic Health Counselor my entire being changed--I was able to focus and create more time for myself, my mood improved immensely, my renewed creativity and focus on making my own meals helped me save money, my chronic pains went away, and eventually the severe acne that had plagued me for years disappeared. The food I was eating was creating these brilliant, healthy new cells and thoughts, and the lifestyle changes I made gave me my direction. I became a health counselor so I could to share this amazing experience with the world!
