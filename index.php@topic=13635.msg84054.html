Gotta love the name!  Sounds like they make noodles laced with hashish.  I want some.

Well, maybe don't get your hopes too high regarding extra special ingredients! As you might guess, the restaurant's name is more the result of some rather literal translating. By way of even more literal explanation, the main characters on the awning headline that they serve "pleasant happy cow meat noodle," and the chef/owner acquired the nickname "Stony" somewhere along the course of his career, so "Happy Stony Noodle" it is and, with or without any added spice, some excellent Taiwanese food (beef noodle soup also being a classic dish from Taiwan). It's most def worth the short jaunt to Elmhurst!
