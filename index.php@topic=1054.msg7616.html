Summer apparel

This may be a bit of a naive question, but here goes. Since moving to J.H. a year ago I've been impressed by how most everyone seems to tolerate and get along with people from different countries and religions. Certain cultures may stand out from block to block, but overall no one group seems to dominate. Still, I wonder how beneath the surface different people think of each other.
The heat wave had me thinking about this again. Walk down 74th street and you pass women wearing everything from full hijab to miniskirt and halter top. Do any of you know if there have ever been, ummm, tensions about this? I know this is a political can of worms, but I'm really just interested in how individuals deal with clashing standards of modesty.
