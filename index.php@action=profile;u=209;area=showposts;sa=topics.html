Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Community Groups, Activities & Events / FREE Arts Talk & Collage/Found Object Workshop this Sunday, 1-3pm
« on: April 11, 2018, 09:28:28 PM »
Join Jackson Heights Creates for a FREE Arts Talk & Collage/Found Object Workshop

Featuring artists Junko Yamada and Nancy Gesimondo!

Linda Ganjian, the curator of the popular Art Talks, will lead a Q&A with each artist, followed by an artist-led collage and found object workshop.

ABOUT THE TWO FEATURED ARTISTS:
Junko Yamada applies modern subject matter and lively colors to a traditional art form, creating beautiful collages from rice paper.

Nancy Gesimondo, a found object artist, creates striking assemblages of natural materials, such as shells, feathers, seedpods, and crystals.

WHEN: Sunday, April 15 from 1-3pm
WHERE: The Renaissance School (theater room, 2nd floor) at 35-59 81st Street, Jackson Heights
WHO: All ages welcome

Jackon Heights Creates is a neighborhood arts alliance that promotes artists and connects them with the community.

Co-sponsors: Jackson Heights Beautification Group and Citizens Committee
