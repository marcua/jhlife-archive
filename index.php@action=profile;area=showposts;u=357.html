Hi everyone, and all interested in yoga, and /or Pilates:

I just received an email from a member (Laura) who asked that I visit this site (I had no idea!) as I am a yoga/Pialtes instructor, and hold practice sessions on the weekends in the heart of Jackson Heights!    Whoala!  The great little studio is on 75th st, between Roosevelt and 37th avenues at Mambo Bravo Studios : 37-40 75th street, Jackson Heights,NY 11372.  Soooo very convenient to the #7, E,F, V. 

Yes, the class is very beginner friendly, though I have some seasoned folks in there as well.  I mentioned "great little"  studio, and that's the way we want it, as it allows for more personal attentiion.  Average participants in a class is between 8-10, and we prefer no more than 12 people as a limit.

9:00 am start on Sat, and Sun mornings.  Cost is at $14.00 per drop in class, (space available) or if you prefer to save (and who doesn't?!) packages can average between $10-$12 per session.  Where else at this price can you get the attention needed to continue a safe, and fruitful practice?   Small class attention by a certified professional; lovely and clean hardwood-floored studio; and a fantabulously great location in queens! AWESOME! 

Feel free to contact me (Mimi) direct at 917-561-5501.  Or if you don't get a chance to call, just be there next weekend.  Bring your mat, comfortable clothes and your lovely self!
Till then, make it a good week. 

Namaste - Mimi
