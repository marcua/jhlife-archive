Suggestions to fix the problem:
1. Revert routes and number of flights to the as they were before Jan. 2013.
2. LaGuardia, Port Authority and the FAA should create a website where flight route information for each day is immediately accessible.
3. In case flights are reverted for weather or maintenance, the reason should be posted on the website.
4. Maintenance schedule and planned route changes should be posted with at least one month in advance.
5. Noise monitors, paid by the FAA, LaGuardia and Port Authority should be installed every 5 blocks around JH. Noise records should be posted online immediately.
6. Flight routes above residential areas, schools and hospitals should not used between 10pm and 9am.
7. A complete environmental impact should be done to record current noise situation.

These are just a few basic steps.

If none of these works, a nice option: close LaGuardia Airport and turn it into LaGuardia Park.
