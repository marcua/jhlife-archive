Title: St. Joan of Arc School - 1st Annual Cocktail Party Fundraiser @ the Queensboro
Post by: PK1 on November 05, 2019, 11:13:04 PM
The SJA Parent Association is having its first annual cocktail party fundraiser at The Queensboro on Monday, December 2nd from 6:30-8:30pm.

Join us for nice night out in the neighborhood for a good cause!  The evening will include food, drinks, live music, raffles & a silent auction.

Tickets can be purchased in advance via the following link: https://www.eventbrite.com/e/sja-cocktails-for-a-cause-tickets-79790687093
