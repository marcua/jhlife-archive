Jackson Heights Tennis Club- All Welcome
« on: November 22, 2015, 07:03:33 AM »
Hey guys
For many, many years residents of Jackson Heights have been playing tennis at Travers Park. All are welcome, just bring a racquet and an open mind. All skill levels play here, we all learned one time or another. I've been playing for over a month now, during most mornings, it's a lot of fun, can be competitive and is a good workout. I have seen my progression over the month and I have been getting better day by day! We are a regular group of 10-20 members, ages range from 16-80, all different backgrounds, look for a gentleman named Don Sergio (far right), he's been playing at this park for 25+ years, used to play with Arthur Ashe. There's lots of great advice and encouragement. It's free, fun and great place to make new friends in the neighborhood. My name is Mike, come by and say hi, my yellow lab is usually with me watching us play:)
