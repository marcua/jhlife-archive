Title: Hurricane Sandy Relief at Jackson Heights Greenmarket
Post by: Greenmarket on November 15, 2012, 01:07:15 PM
Once again, this Sunday, November 18th, Jackson Heights Greenmarket will be hosting a Donate-A-Bag promotion.  From 8:00-1:00, we will be accepting bags of hardy fruit and vegetables (i.e. apples, potatoes, carrots, winter squash), greens, bread and baked goods to donate to soup kitchens cooking for Hurricane Sandy victims.

We had a fantastic response last week and filled 17 HUGE BAGS with donated produce.  THANK YOU!  Please come out and show your support again this week.
