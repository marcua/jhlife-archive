Real Estate & Home Improvement / Re: Broker protocol?
« on: July 07, 2017, 06:18:40 PM »
Gardner, it's a loaded question but I as a professional will try to condense it into a short paragraph:
- Are you "locked in with an agent" - absent an exclusive contract NO

In terms of agency - two things must be present for a buyer's agent to be eligible for commissions paid by the SELLER.

1. Procuring cause - agent must be the reason you found and wanted the property and be clearly able to demonstrate it. (If buyer engages listing broker directly and sees property through listing broker without any other involvement, makes this difficult to demonstrate)

2. Value of service - agent must be able to help your deal, not get in the way of it. 


Shelby2 shared a good thread, I contributed a single paragraph to it I recall.

I am happy to consult you on the process. My specialty however is marketing properties for folks needing to sell their homes, therefore I rarely take on buyer clients.
