Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Community Groups, Activities & Events / Re: Scrabble Club?
« on: November 03, 2010, 03:57:06 PM »
Small Update.....

I got in contact with one of the previous organizers of the Scrabble Club in the church.  She stopped running the club after about 10 years when membership declined.  I don't mind trying to get the club going again, but I'd like to know how many people are interested in joining.  I would also need a few people willing to manage the club.

The Scrabble Club used to meet on the first Saturdays of the Month and charged $5/person for the day to pay for the rental of the room (12-4), coffee and cookies.  We'll see what needs to be changed if we start it up again.
