Title: First Annual JHBG Winter Holiday Market - Sunday 12/8 at the Greenmarket 10-2
Post by: jhlifer on December 05, 2019, 11:51:19 AM
Come out to the Jackson Heights Greenmarket this Sunday, December 8th from 10am to 2pm to visit the first annual JHBG WINTER HOLIDAY MARKET.   Find the best in local artisanal and boutique shopping.

This pop-up will be located at the IS 145 yard on 79th Street, mid-way between 34th Avenue and Northern Boulevard
The Market was originally scheduled for last Sunday the 1st, but was rescheduled to the 8th because of rain.  This link will bring you to the JHBG's site for more information.   See you there.
