I don't know if Utrecht sells product or not, but I heard that they will do NYC-area deliveries free with a purchase of $100 or more.  Valentine Packaging in Maspeth has or had the same policy so perhaps other local suppliers would do so as well.  So if you find a NYC based supplier, call and ask about local delivery.

If you can wait for delivery but just don't want to pay big $$ for it, try one of the places that offers flat rate shipping, like ASWexpress.com (outside NY so no NY state tax to pay) or Artist and Craftsman in Brooklyn.

Good luck.
