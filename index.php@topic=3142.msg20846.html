Eviction notice at restaurant El Punto del Sabor
« on: August 04, 2009, 07:43:55 PM »
On my way home this evening, I noticed an eviction notice on El Punto del Sabor on 37th Ave between 84th and 85th. It was dated 8/4 and since the plaintiff was the landlord, I assume it's for non-payment of rent.

Frankly, I am hoping this place closes down. Our building has been having problems with their garbage forever: for one, they never clean the sidewalk after their garbage leaks (which is daily), so now it stinks and the sidewalk is disgusting. Then, this past winter they had fallen behind on paying their waste management company so garbage collecting was interrupted -- during which period they STORED their trash in the back! They are just totally incompetent and really not a good neighbor.

Have you seen any other businesses closing down (or being evicted) lately in the neighborhood?
