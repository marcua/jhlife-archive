Sometimes around the holidays the papers do not include coupons. I also noticed I get alot more when I have the paper delivered. The flier packs (the plastic little bags they put in front of homes) usually do have coupons. I find the best are in Newsday and the NY times.  You can also go to the grocery store websites and have the coupons electronically added to your  shoppers card. Riteaid does this as well. I also will go out and buy more newspapers if the coupons are really good. When you are shopping look around (especially by the diary sections) they have coupon dispensers (waldbaums especially) I've been using them for years and have saved a bundle. I wait for the item to go on sale and pile up my coupons. The cashiers Love me  :2funny:
