Title: Addictive Wine and Dine Fest at La Gloria
Post by: ABWinery on December 02, 2014, 10:59:49 AM
Dear Community,

Occasionally we partner with different restaurants in Jackson Heights to bring you the ultimate wines and foods pairings. This time, we are excited to partner with La Gloria.  On December 10th,  we are excited to bring you a five course dinner to pair with five different wines. We tailored each course to match specifically with each wine. The cost will be $50 per person. This is a privately held  event. It will be hosted at La Gloria. The restaurant wil be booked for us only. Seats are limited.  If you would like to attend, please email us at info@abwinery.com for more details. Reasons of doing this kind of event is to bring our customers together, bring our community together, to support small business, to support our community and grow Jackson Heights.
