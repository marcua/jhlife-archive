Neighborhood Chat / Re: Pharmacy open during Covid-19 crisis - 7 days per week and free delivery
« on: April 28, 2020, 09:28:26 PM »
Hi Brian,

While we love seeing members of the community in our pharmacy, during this crisis I urge community members to only go out when necessary and to use delivery services when available. 

For deliveries we can go from 61st to 111th Street and from Astoria Boulevard to 51st Avenue.  We can usually do same day delivery if we can get the order together early enough.  If not, it'll be delivered the next day.

We do carry masks, both the 3-ply surgical masks as well as KN95s.  As of today, I only have the surgical masks in stock.  I expect the KN95s to arrive this week.  We are in stock of gloves, thermometers, sanitizer, and alcohol at this moment as well.  Oximeters are also on order for this week.

While the governor has stated that he will sign an executive order to allow Covid-19 testing to be performed at pharmacies, he has not done so as of yet.  There has been no guidance or information provided to pharmacies either.  I check multiple times a day for the governor's executive order and I invite everyone else to so as well by going to this website:  https://www.governor.ny.gov/keywords/executive-order

In regards to pharmacies selling over the counter testing to customers, I will say this; to my knowledge, none of these tests are FDA approved or FDA EUA approved.  So, although they are available, their reliability is questionable.  As the saying goes, "Buyer beware".  Our pharmacy has opted to not offer these questionable tests to the public.
