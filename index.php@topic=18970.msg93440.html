While I don't much like Planet Fitness, it provides a reasonable value for the very little that it costs, and from your description of Blink, jaxheightzforall, I suspect you'd be happier at PF, especially if you can use it when it's least crowded. The place is pretty clean and equipment is in good working order. Issues are crowding, which is often terrible, and gym etiquette, which is non-existent. People monopolize a machine for long periods, and everyone is sitting on exercise equipment while browsing (or talking on) their phones. There's a timed workout area upstairs but it's useless. Nobody pays attention to the lights indicating time to change of machines. Staff provides no direction on any of this. On the other hand, people are pretty nice if you ask them to work in, and most diligently clean machines after use. I suppose there is gym etiquette, just not the etiquette that I would prefer. Again, a good deal for the price, and kudos to PF for democratizing fitness.
