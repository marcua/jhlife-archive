News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Re-Connect: Jackson Heights
Wednesday March 19th 6-8pm
@Everest Hair Salon, 74-17 37th Ave

We had fun at the Queens Museum, now let's re-connect to see how we can bring it all back to the neighborhood were we live and love. Come help us plan our next big event, collectively we can go further. Bring a friend, family, or neighbor.

What kind of awesome art-sharing event would you like to see happen in Jackson Heights?

Art & The Commons
artCommons Organizing Team
http://www.artcommons.org

*ArtCommons, local art-sharing project*

- - - - - - - -


Reminder: First Art-Sharing Cycle Ends March 15th
Time for returns, extensions, or new reservations!

It's been 3 months since the first art-shares. Participants, both artists and borrowers, please connect to arrange artwork return or renewal (upto 3 more months). Feel free to make arrangements to return it at our next event on March 19th and share your stories with us!

New reservations?
1) Sign up to Jackson Heights artCommons. http://www.artcommons.org/#join
2)  Browse for new artwork at Jackson Heights artcommons website http://jacksonheights.artcommons.org 
3) Email us the name of the artwork to reserve-pilot@artcommons.org and we will connect you with the artist.


Reserve early to arrange a pick-up at the March 19th event!


http://jacksonheights.artcommons.org
