Neighborhood Chat / Dear Newly Elected....
« on: November 03, 2009, 03:24:16 PM »
By tomorrow we will know who our borough president and new council member will be.  I thought it might be nice to make a succinct list of pressing issues and/or concerns that we as Jackson Heights residents have (basically a synthesis of this site).  We could then email or mail a letter to them in person in the near future.


My major concerns are:

1)  Trash problem and dirty unsightly sidewalks
2)  Lack of park space
            a) Create fund to purchase more land, not just improve the little public space that we do have
            b)  consider permanently closing down areas to make them more pedestrian friendly and green.
3)  Zoning and landmarks - I feel more areas of the neighborhood need to protected before it is too late

Other opinions?
