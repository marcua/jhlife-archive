News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Neighborhood Chat / Re: Compost collection alternatives?

I have heard JH scraps is going to reopen soon.
The jackson-Moore cemetery garden has food scrap drop off on Saturday mornings now.
Smiling Hogshead in LIC is open for food scrap drop-off for an as-you-can sliding scale donation on the honor system. It is open Dawn to dusk everyday. The drop off is on the corner of Skillman and Pearson place.
There are 2 groups in sunnyside: sunnyside scraps and a new group on 45th street.
Sunny scraps i think operates on sunday mornings and the 45th street group is Saturday mornings.
If you check #saveourcompost on Instagram there are probably more. Many more small groups are reopening often now despite the DSNY de-funding.

Community Groups, Activities & Events / Smiling Hogshead Ranch's 2nd Annual Harvest Festival
« on: September 11, 2017, 06:07:01 PM »
Come celebrate the end of the 2017 growing season at our fun-for-all-ages fall street festival and fundraiser benefiting Smiling Hogshead Ranch, with live entertainment, activities, workshops, food/drink, and a silent auction!

Official entertainment line-up to be announced soon. https://www.facebook.com/events/1833144683665381

 If you have not heard about our urban farm in Long Island City, Queens, Smiling Hogshead Ranch is an all volunteer-run garden and community space that strives to inspire individuals through education, action and community. We are located on Skillman Ave near 49th Avenue in the industrial section of LIC.

If you or someone you know would be interested in contributing products or services as an event sponsor, please send an email to events[at]smilinghogsheadranch.org today! Thanks!
