I'm looking for anything from snacks to lunch in the vicinity of 75th and 37th (can walk a few blocks in either direction) that is very healthy - meaning not very salty, fatty or high in calories.

All I can think of so far is:

sashimi from Happy Kitchen
fresh sliced fruit from the fruit place across from Met Food
Greek yogurt from Met, Trade Fair or Italian Market
organic string cheese and soy chips from the Health Food store
$1 frozen yogurt from the salad bar place on 82nd St. in Elmhurst (is it really healthy?)

Any other ideas?
Has anyone tried the chicken avocado salad from East 21?  IS there a Chinese place that sells brown rice and steamed vegetables with no sauce?
