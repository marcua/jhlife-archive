Title: Origami meetup -- Second Saturday of the Month
Post by: SamInNY on November 08, 2011, 09:58:59 AM
Our second Origami meet-up is on Saturday, November 12th at Juju's, at 3:15.

We will bring basic paper, some books, and our own projects. We are prepared to teach a few models to beginners who want to try it out.

Bring your own (paper, books, projects) if you have them, and a favorite model to share with the rest of us.

We're likely to get visitors from the Manhattan group this time, as we are now part of the OMG (Origami Meet-up Group) family, which also includes AWOL (After Work Origami Lovers), WTF (Weekly Tuesday Folders), CFO (Complex Folding Origamists), and now FAQs (Folding Addicts in Queens).
