My favorite fried chickens in the neighborhood are Broadway Bakery on Case St (near 90th St, south of Roosevelt). They call it Broaster.

I really can't speak for the other items, and I probably never will, but the chicken is very good in my opinion. I went the first week, so it was all clean. Hopefully it is still running as well.
