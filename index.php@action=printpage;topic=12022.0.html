Title: Re: 10,000 square foot art supply store opens in Sunnyside
Post by: Shelby2 on March 01, 2015, 12:38:15 AM
This store will do free delivery to Jackson Heights if you spend $250 or more.  For orders under $250 the charge is $20 for curbside delivery.

Here are the details:
http://www.artistcraftsman.com/nyc-delivery

New York Delivery Service
A&C is pleased to offer delivery service from our NYC stores.  Here are some specifics, but please call your local A&C store in the New York area for complete details and ordering information. 
Free Delivery on orders over $250 within 5 miles of our store
Orders under $250:
Less than 5 miles from store $20
5-25 miles from store $40
25-50 miles from store $75
Over 50 miles from store $2.00 per mile
Orders with any item that is over 75 lbs. or is 48” x 96” will require a second person and be an additional $20
Free deliveries on all clay orders over 500 lbs.
All deliveries are curbside
Customer No Show = No Refund of initial delivery fee and additional redelivery fee as follows:
Within 25 miles of store: $25
Within 25-50 miles of store: $50
After 50 miles: $2.00 per mile
 
We offer FREE deliveries to “Preferred or Commercial” accounts – please talk to a Store Manager for details.
 
48 Hour guarantee for all deliveries:
Example – If you order something Tuesday you will receive it by Thursday
No deliveries after 5 pm
No weekend deliveries at this time
