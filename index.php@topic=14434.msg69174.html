Owl in Jackson Heights?
« on: February 20, 2016, 11:47:18 AM »
I live in Southridge 1.  For much of last night I heard a bird calling that sounded an awful lot like a Great Horned Owl (listen at https://www.allaboutbirds.org/guide/Great_Horned_Owl/sounds, then the "Adult Song Juvenile Call" in the "calls" section).  It was a fairly low-pitched hoo hoo-hoo hoo hoo repeated dozens of times from maybe 2am to about 6am.  It sounded pretty close-by (and I face an interior courtyard).  And then later in the morning, after the other birds had started up, I heard the same call farther away.

Has anyone else seen or heard an owl recently, or perhaps this time last year?

Kristin Graves
