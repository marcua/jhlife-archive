I strongly second Matrix Gym.  It is about $55 for a month by month.  I pay a little less because I have been a member for
2 1/2 years.  If you continue your membership without a break your monthly price will never go up.  I was a member of NYSC in Astoria and when I quit 2 /12 years ago my monthly cost was above $80 and going up every year.  I also used to pay another $10-$20 a month for municipal parking.  Matrix has free parking  but the only catch is can you get a parking space when you return to JH.

Count me as another happy Matrix-goer.  It does depend on what the person looks for in a gym, and I miss the New York Sports Clubs I used to go to in other neighborhoods.  After moving here, I used to drive to the NYSC in Astoria, but paying to park in the municipal lot and the ever-increasing NYSC fees got to me.  The free parking at Matrix makes a big difference - if you have a car (and can park back in JH afterwards).  Still seems funny to me to drive to the gym, but until something else opens up nearby, it works for me.  There are also NYSC locations convenient by subway, as Shelby2 mentioned.
