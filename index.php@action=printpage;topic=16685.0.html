Good morning,
I said I wouldn't write to much on this list about my run for City Council...but a couple of neighbors have stopped recently and asked about it, and how they could help.

If you're a registered democrat in any district you can help me petition, host a meet and greet, or contribute to the campaign- https://nycvot.es/2rUbPeD The campaign finance board matches contributions 6:1.

I decided to run when Council Member Ferreras-Copeland bowed out two weeks ago.
The challengers, Hiram Monserrate and Francisco Moya are both career politicians. Monserrate having done some time in federal prison for fraud involving misused $100,000. He also has a domestic violence charge.
People love Hiram in the district, and the resounding call I hear on the streets is that he deserves a second chance.

I was also dismayed by the fact that Assemblyman Moya would drop the important work he does in Albany to run locally.

And, there's the sad situation of only 12 women in City Council...
I don't want to write too many policy initiatives here, but you can read about me and my concerns for the district at cristinafurlong2017.com . I also have fb, Cristina Furlong for District 21 and Twitter- D21Cristina

I'll run on an agenda of rebuilding the safety, housing security, and quality education within the district. I'll be a council person directly involved in the community.

The district only includes 81- 94th St, between Roosevelt and 37th- then expands to LGA, Flushing Meadows Corona Park and LeFrak City above Junction Blvd.

Please write me, if you're interested in learning more or would like to help out.
Thanks.
