Neighborhood Chat / Re: JH Historic District Fire Safety
« on: June 25, 2017, 07:30:48 PM »
Here are the legal requirements for residential apartment buildings with three or more units.
Source:http://www.nyc.gov/html/fdny/pdf/safety/fire_safety_education/2010_02/07_residential_apartment_fire_safety_english.pdf
The owner is required by law to:
o post the fire safety plan on the inside of every apartment front door.
o post the fire safety plan in a conspicuous space in the common area.
o distribute a copy to each dwelling unit in the building.
o provide a copy to new tenants at the time of the lease.
o re-distribute the fire safety plan annually during fire prevention week.

As far as I can tell, the only thing the Fillmore is not doing is redistributing the fire safety plan during fire prevention week. Most fire safety guidelines are apartment-based, not building based (as seen above).
