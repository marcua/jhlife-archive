The $2 dance is somewhat of a cultural thing. Many immigrant workers from Latin America leave their families behind. The men who come here live a rather lonely solitary life. The $2 dance is way for these men who miss their wives to experience some form of touch and intimacy. By and large it's just a dance, of course in some cases it goes further than that and costs more than $2.
