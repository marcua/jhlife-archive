Neighborhood Chat / Re: LaGuardia Central Terminal Overhaul

I may be wrong, but the 240% increase refers to added space for "flight operations," per the Gov's press release, which I take to mean taxiing and such. Will that in turn allow for more flights? Probably. Will the many years of construction lead to more take-offs over JH? Will every other Saturday turn into every Saturday, turn into Saturdays, Wednesdays, Thursdays, and Fridays? Who knows. Is NextGen and increased demand for air travel making the noise problem worse already? Sure seems like it. Does the noise upset people? Will more noise upset more people to organize and speak out? Most certainly. Will elected officials promise to do something? Will technological solutions be considered? You can be sure of it. Will the NYTimes write about the noise problem, pushing real estate prices down? Could happen. Should I put my love affair with JH on hold and stay another 5 years in my rent stabilized dump in Williamsburg? Or do I invest in some earplugs? Trying to decide!

Neighborhood Chat / Re: LaGuardia Central Terminal Overhaul

My wife and I love Jackson Heights and want to live here. After many months of searching for an apartment, we finally found a place we like. In fact, we're considering bidding on this place today. Now we're having serious misgivings. We did the garden tour on Saturday and were stunned at how many and how loud the planes were. "Look how beautiful and peac-RRRRRRRRREEEEEEEEEEEEEEEE... Um, what was that I was saying!?!"
Seriously, does anyone have an informed idea of how bad this problem is now and what it's going to be like for the next 5, 10 years? We're in love with the neighborhood, but we're more than a little freaked out about the noise. Time to look for a neighborhood outside the flight path? :-(
