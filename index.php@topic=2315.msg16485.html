Quote from: Shelby2 on February 23, 2009, 12:51:11 AM
I believe that the crime that you witnessed would be classified as a felonious assault. 

not likely felony assault. Assault in NY is only a felony if some kind of weapon is involved (even improvised), if serious physical injury is caused, or if victim injured in course of the commission of some other felony.  Gang assault also requires serious physical injury.

"Serious physical injury" means physical injury which creates a substantial risk of death, or which causes death or serious and protracted disfigurement, protracted impairment of health or protracted loss or impairment of the function of any bodily organ.

this may have been a robbery which is a felony, but is not felony assault. But a group of people beating up a victim without a weapon is a misdemeanor unless serious physical injury is caused.
