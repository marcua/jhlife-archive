News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Community Groups, Activities & Events / JHBG's new podcast with JH Orchestra founder and leader Pat Glunt
« on: March 04, 2020, 12:27:21 PM »
 For those who have attended the fantastic local concerts of the Jackson Heights Orchestra, you are already familiar with its founder and leader, Pat Glunt. She is extraordinary and wears many hats in this operation including being the conductor, the impresario and host, along with commissioning a number of composers work, both local and international. If you don't know her yet, give a listen to this podcast* and you will get to know this charming personality and be under her spell like the rest of us. Learn the backstory to how and why we are able to have this kind of a quality operation here in our little neighborhood and what it takes to keep all parts working. Pat Glunt and the JHO is this lastest installment of JHBG's podcast series.
 
       *   https://soundcloud.com/user-570401025/jhbg-pat-glunt/s-FREVF

 ALSO- The next JHO concert is on March 11th at St Marks Church [/b]on 34th Ave and 82nd street. Arias by Tosca, Aida and Turandot will be featured along with a soloist from Sunnyside. More details on the JHBG website.
