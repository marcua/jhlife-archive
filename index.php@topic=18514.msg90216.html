News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

JHBG's 30th Anniversary Fundraiser
« on: November 19, 2018, 12:59:34 AM »
You and your family are invited to Jackson Heights Beautification Group's 30th Anniversary Holiday Fundraiser on Monday, December 3 from 6 to 10 PM at the new Queensboro restaurant.  We have so much going on before the end of the year, but to pay for all of these events we need the continued support of our neighbors like you.  Please buy a ticket for our fundraiser and bring a friend, neighbor or family member.   If you can't make it, you can still make a contribution on our website www.JHBG.org or mail a check to JHBG at PO Box 720253, Jackson Heights, NY 11372.

As you know, the Jackson Heights Beautification Group brings the neighborhood:

JH Orchestra

JH Halloween Parade

JH Dog Run

JH Gardening each Saturday at 19 spaces

JH Composting Center

JH Summer Sunday Concerts

JH Historic Weekend Garden Tours

JH Holiday Lighting of the Post Office

JH Graffiti Removal

JH Breakfast with the Principal

JH Newsletter – The Views

JH Post Office Project

JH Tree Care on 37th Avenue

JH Student Art Competition and

JH Debates and Townhalls

We still have tickets left for tomorrows JHBG 30th Anniversary Fundraiser.  Come meet over 100 of your neighbors and celebrate our great community.

We will have live music from the Paul Trust Jazz band, a photo exhibit of JH Photos, JH artist Jonny Goldstein will be capturing the event, we will have great food and drink from the Queensboro and a chance to discuss the future of JH with our neighbors.

Buy your tickets at the door or at  www.JHBG.org/donate and help support our efforts to make JH better every day.
