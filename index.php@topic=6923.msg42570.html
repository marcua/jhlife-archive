Lost bird found, how to find owner?
« on: December 12, 2011, 07:44:48 PM »
We found a white bird, possibly a pigeon, on a window sill this afternoon. Very tame, apparently tired.

It has a ring on its leg with a number. Mark Marrone of Parrots of the World told us there is no central registry and no way to find the owner using the Internet, for example. He said the only way to get the bird back to its owner was to keep it for two weeks, feed it and let it get strong, and then it will fly away itself home.

Anyone lost a bird like that? Anyone with any ideas about what to do?
