It turns out that on the show DEXTER, which contained a lot of movie blood, the special fx team came up with their own concoction for fake blood. Also a glass salter shaker filled with 70% isopropyl alcohol and a few piece of clove with cotton stuffed in the opening is recommended by a guy in some south east asian country. If it works in a hot, muggy, jungle country, I'll take it.
