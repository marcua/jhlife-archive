Real Estate & Home Improvement / Re: UNFCU(United Nations Fed Credit union) Mortgages - closing speed/customer svc?
« on: December 10, 2014, 10:23:55 PM »
I guess this comes a bit late but for the sake of sharing the info I'll drop couple lines about this topic. I would say the process of getting a mortgage is satisfactory, they can prequalify within 24-48 hours and the deal is usually done within 2 months. Rates are good, closing fees around 2.5k.

There's always a catch unfortunately. Like most credit Unions, they only provide services to a specific group of people. In this case: united nations employees.
