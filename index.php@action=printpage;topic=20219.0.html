Post by: itsit on October 27, 2020, 11:35:16 AM
 The past couple of years, no one in our extended family wanted to cook much. We have ordered a turkey, side and trimmings from Whole Foods and economically heated up Thanksgiving dinner for 10-12 people for about $100/125 dollars. Added our own desserts and fyi, both years we had leftovers. We pick up in Williamsburg because of easier parking there but Bryant Park and Union Square both are accessible from JH. It's just another option if you can't find anything you like here or might not want to go out on Thanksgiving day.
