Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

I got on the 7 this morning at 90th Street at about 7:15. The platform was more crowded than usual. However, the train wasn't that crowded and I got a seat. The express train was running towards Manhattan. The train ran slower than usual, staying in the stations for a longer time. It skipped 69th St, stopped at 61st and then went express to 33rd for some reason. Since 33rd is my stop, I was, of course, ok with it.
The only problem was that the speaker in our car was completely messed up, so no one heard the message about the skipped stops. I don't think I can blame the weather for that.
Overall, my experience was fine. I've heard nightmares about the J & saw pictures of lines down the block just to get into the station, so once again, I feel pretty lucky.
