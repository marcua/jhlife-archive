Classic details abound with 9+ foot ceilings, original oak floors, hallway arches, transom windows, and picture rails. The apartment was recently renovated with new lighting, bathroom tiles and fixtures, and an updated kitchen with white granite countertops and stainless steel appliances (including an energy efficient Blomberg refrigerator). The apartment boasts plenty of storage with four full-height closets with built-ins.

Jackson Heights has a vibrant culture, with great food and an active civic life. Open on Sundays, the excellent Jackson Heights Green Market is just down the block. The apartment is close to transportation, including the 7/E/F/M/R trains from Broadway/74th Street.
