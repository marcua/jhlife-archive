Yes, noisy teenagers are annoying, but it doesn't mean the neighborhood is in decline. It's just an annoyance that people have to report and make sure they follow up on.  After moving here from Astoria, I can honestly say that JH is much quieter, cleaner, and generally more pleasant than Astoria.  We had noisy teenagers in Astoria, but they weren't just noisy, they used to run on top of all the parked cars on the street jumping from car to car.  I also had more car break-ins (3 in Astoria, 1 popped lock nothing taken in JH), our home was burglarized in Astoria (as were the homes of most of my friends), and the police would take forever to respond to anything or sometimes not respond at all.  It took them 8 hours to respond to our home invasion.  At that point our landlord had already fixed the broken window since it was nighttime.  The police accused us of making up the break-in for insurance reasons and then proposing that my boyfriend's brother must have done it since he had a key (why would my boyfriend's brother break in through our back window if he had a key?) when they did finally show up and then were promptly taken from the scene by a neighbor that was having a physical altercation with her brother-in-law.  Fun times.  At least the police respond in some way here and the streets are generally quiet at night, and there are a ton of people that care about the neighborhood and quality of life here.
