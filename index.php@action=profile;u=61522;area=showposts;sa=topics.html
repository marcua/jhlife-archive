Restaurants & Food / Thai Thai Grocery
« on: March 07, 2017, 11:13:49 AM »
For those who haven't been, the Thai grocery on 77th and Woodside across from Ayada is an incredibly well-stocked little shop with lots of hard-to-find ingredients for southeast Asian cooking. Their frozen coconut milk and chile pastes have become pantry staples of mine. The proprietor is also super nice and accommodating. She helped me find a few things the last two times I was in there and we chatted about neighborhood news for a few minutes. They even have a kaffir lime plant!

Restaurants & Food / What will happen to Arepa Lady?
« on: January 12, 2017, 12:39:49 PM »
I saw this story a few weeks ago but haven't heard anything since.

http://jacksonheightspost.com/seven-story-building-to-go-up-on-roosevelt-ave-several-stores-face-wrecking-ball

As of a few days ago it seemed that businesses here were still active. I also haven't seen any municipal documents archived by the DOB after the date of this article's publication.

Arepa Lady's brick-and-mortar shop is a tenant here. In my opinion this is one of the best places for inexpensive sit-down food in the neighborhood. It is the continuation of a local institution and seems to serve a surprisingly diverse clientele. They did a great job with this place. I do not frequent it as much as I should but I'd be very sad to see it go. Anybody have any word on this?
