Major World Ad Truck
« on: July 15, 2016, 01:21:49 PM »
For the past two weeks an ad truck for Major World has been parking on 37th Avenue from 82nd - 85th Street throughout the night.

The problem with this truck is the massive and intrusive size of the video screens that are displaying these ad’s. The truck is clad with giant screens on each of it’s “white box” sides while playing music.

Other than being “visual pollution” to our community, this truck also poses a safety risk for our pedestrians and drivers. The light and glow emanating from these screens are blinding when driving past them and have the same effect when crossing the street as a pedestrian. 

Is this illegal for Major World to do?
