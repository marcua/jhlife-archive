Yo Dads!

Congrats, you made it through another sweaty Summer!

You are invited to a very special gathering of the Jackson Heights Dads Social Beverage club, featuring live jazz by three of our members, the Cold Club of Queens. Feel free to invite your friends or come solo!

What: Hangout and shoot the breeze with other local dads! Drink a beverage (alcoholic or not) And this time we feature the Cold Club of Queens, three local dads playing Django Reinhardt inspired jazz!
