Title: Adorable, friendly, healthy, neutered rescue kitty needs permanent loving home
Post by: spetzel13 on October 15, 2009, 04:53:53 PM
        My husband and I rescued a stray kitten in early March, when it was cold and wet outside.  On a freezing night the kitten saw us through a window and meowed, so we began to leave food + water out, in hopes that we could rescue him.  After putting out food for him just a few times, he happily came into our house, glad to have a warm + dry place w/ food and fresh water!

        He has a *fantastic* personality.  He's friendly, affectionate, easy going, playful- extra amazing considering the rough time he must have had as a stray.  He loves being brushed and + used the litter box right away w/ no accidents.  He is docile and patient when his claws get trimmed.  He greets us and loves being petted and held but doesn't fuss about being alone either.

        We have had him checked out by a vet + got him neutered + up to date with all vaccinations.  He is a healthy 1 1/2 year old neutered male, super cute, w/ four white feet, a white snout and a little pink nose.

        We would keep him but we have 2 other cats and they fight when together.  We have a hunch that this rescued kitty (who we've been calling "Lucky") should be the only cat in a household.  He may have problems getting along with other cats but he is great with adults and children.  We are having a baby in a few months and it is hard for us to keep the cats quarantined from each other while moving furniture and getting a baby's room ready.


      Lucky was fostered for a couple months this summer by a couple who had to move and was unable to bring any pet to their new apartment.  We want to make sure his next home is a permanent, loving home- he deserves it!

We could not sing his praises enough- the only thing stopping us from keeping him is the fighting with our other pets.  He is soooo good and loving we can't beart to bring him to a shelter.  I am not sure how many pictures came through, please email me and I will send more.
