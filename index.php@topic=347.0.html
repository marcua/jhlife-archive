Esparks Coffee
« on: January 17, 2008, 04:56:52 PM »
I went this morning to Esparks in neighboring Elmhurst. The coffee was pretty good, in the realm of Starbucks, maybe a tad better. A small cup (venti?) was about 1.85. I didn't try any of the baked goods, but, looked like muffins, bagels and some fruit salads. It was the first day, no one was in there. It seems they didn't have any "grand opening" sign or other hoopla, maybe the reason for lack of customers. The decor also looked very Starbucks life, merchandise, etc. Wonder how well they will do in the next few weeks.
