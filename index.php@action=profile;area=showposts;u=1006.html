Business Listings and Services Directory / Brazilian Rodizio & Buffet Restaurant in Astoria

Ola! We justed wanted to introduce our restaurant, Samba Grill, to this forum.  We opened up this past summer.  Each day we freshly prepare various dishes that you will find in all regions of Brazil such as Shrimp sauteed in a yuca cream sauce (Bobo de Camarao), Chicken with okra (Frango com Quiabo), Chicken Stroganoff, Thinly Sliced grilled steak with onions, Feijoada, which is mixed pork with black beans, Empadao (similar to chicken pot pie), Tilapia fish in coconut/tomato sauce, Codfish with potato (Bacalhau), various pasta & vegetable dishes, etc.   We have over ten types of meat on our rotating BBQ Grill... skirt steak, sirlion, short ribs, beef ribs, turkey wrapped in bacon, chicken, brazilian sausages...  You can choose to eat by the pound (self serve $5.99/$7.99lb just meat) or our Rodizio style service (all you can eat) where our servers will come around with all the meats and cut right off the skewers onto your plate and the buffet table is unlimited ($17.95 for lunch/$25.95 for dinner).   We have wine, beer, & sangria.  For dessert we have caramel flan, pumpkin flan, passion fruit mousse, chocolate mousse, etc. 
Hope you will join us & enjoy our food, atmosphere and service! Obrigado!!
