My only complaint is that the lighting is so poor that the menu should be in braille!  My favorite gnocchi here is the napolitain variety versus the a-typical which is made from semolina.  The napolitan gnocchi resembles squares of polenta which have been lightly sauteed and served with a wonderfully fresh marinara sauce. Unfortunately this gnocchi is a special and not on their regular menu.  I've dined at Sapori d'Ishia more than 8 times and steered clear of the dessert pizza thinking that it would be some silly kitchy sweet but boy was I was wrong.  This dessert is sublime, the pizza consists of two thin rather light cake discs sandwiched together with a filling reminiscent of nutella and top with a lovely sweetened raspberry sauce similar to a coulis. I must say that you would be hard pressed to find anything negative to say about the food here.
