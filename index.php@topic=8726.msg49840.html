If we're talking about the JH Health Store, I think that the people who own it are lovely people, who may not be on top of all the health food trends and have limited resources in that tiny space. The store is relatively expensive, but they discount things routinely. They are extremely accomodating, and will order things for you if you request.

Having recently discovered I'm gluten intolerant, I'm grateful I can get my chicken nuggets from their store.

I would have thought that people would be THRILLED that a local retailer was getting a foot up the ladder in our community. They have been in JH for years now, and have worked hard to serve it.

I have spoken with the owner and he tells me that they will have expanded fresh produce. They will sell only natural products. They will not sell fresh meat, only frozen. I like this store, in spite of it's shortcomings because the people who own it really want to be a positive presence in the neighborhood.
