I sit on my co-op's board for a landmark status building.

Two quick points:

1) We switched managment companies as well recently.  We moved from a big Manhattan company to a smaller Queens based agent.  Every transition has its pains, but we have generally been happy with the move.  Make sure to do your due dilligence and find a company (and property manager) that fit the needs of your building.

2) As a board member, I'd be very interested in having coffee with board members from different buildings.  I think it would be great to compare notes and learn from each other's struggles and successes.  Does such a group exist already?

-Mr. Lee
