Re: How was your voting experience today?
« Reply #1 on: November 06, 2012, 05:27:14 PM »
P.S. 222 was chaos this morning compared to my previous experiences at the Renaissance Charter School. Obviously, BOE consolidated two or three polling locations into one as there were nine election districts jammed into a smaller gymnasium, whereas the old location had only four as I remember it. Lines crisscrossed so no one was certain they were in the right one; when you finally had your ballot you had to fight through the lines again to make your way to a voting booth and wait for one to free up; then circumnavigate the room to reach the scanning stations. It took me close to an hour from start to finish. I would not have minded if I thought large voter turnout was the reason for the long lines, rather than poor planning and inadequate training of the poll workers. Voting should not be so difficult and I do plan to contact the BOE after the results are in.

Re: How was your voting experience today?
« Reply #8 on: November 06, 2012, 09:19:16 PM »
It was a complete clusterf---!!

My experience voting at the Renaissance School gym had always been a breeze-- in and out in minutes every time.

This year it was an utter disaster.  I waited in line in the hallway of the school for 40 minutes before someone saw me holding my "your polling place has changed!" card and told me that I could have gone ahead into the cafeteria-- that the line I had been standing in for the better part of an hour was for people who needed to look up their district number.  :buck2:  About thirty of us groaned and herded ourselves into the cafeteria.

Which was no better: the lines for the districts were a swirling vortex of confusion, and I waited in the wrong line *again* until the man in the line next to me pointed out that I should have been in *his* line.  He was kind enough to let me in ahead of him rather than sending me back to the end again.  In short, I got there at 7:45am and didn't get out until after 9.

There was barely room to walk or to breathe. My husband is down there now-- he's been down there since 7:30.

If someone could tell me where within the BOE I could send a complaint letter, I would be only too happy to complain.
