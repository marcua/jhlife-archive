Title: Unity Stage, the local theatre program, Tweens perform GODSPELL May 18 & 19
Post by: debwass on May 13, 2019, 09:46:34 AM
Unity Stage has served as a highly successful youth theater in Western Queens since 2010, producing summer programs as well as annual musicals and plays performed by children and teens. Godspell marks Unity Stage's first middle school production. Godspell performs May 18th @6pm and May 19th @3pm at P.S. 212Q in Jackson Heights. (The musical comedy The 25th Annual Putnam County Spelling Bee, starring Unity Stage's teen company, performs June 8th and 9th in the same space.)
