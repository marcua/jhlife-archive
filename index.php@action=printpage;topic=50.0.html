Post by: Aronan on June 25, 2009, 12:10:05 AM
Hi Joe ! Welcome !

While the early evening bar scene in JH is lacking there are a couple of spots that might suit your needs.

Novo on 37th Ave. bet 78 & 79 has a decent happy hour and good bar tender (Brian) on Mondays and Tuesdays.

Terraza 7 Train Cafe on Glean St. just off Roosevelt Ave & 83rd St. is small, funky, and fun.

Legends on 72nd St. and 35th has draft beer, and old style townie bar feel, and GREAT barbecue (except on Tuesdays)

Cassidy's on 75th and 31st Ave. lots of beer options and food.

Jardi on Northern and 86th St. Good Sangia and GREAT Tappas.

These are the places where I go most often, I am sure there are a couple more. Certainly there are a few folks out there with places to add to the list.
