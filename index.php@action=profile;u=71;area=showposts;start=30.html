Restaurants & Food / Re: Are there any Brooklyn-like bars in Jackson Heights?
« on: August 30, 2011, 12:37:44 AM »
I would like a nice, local bar, a little more "central" than Legends- where it's a little less weird to order a mixed drink or a glass of wine. Something like Molly Bloom's in Sunnyside or Longbow in Bay Ridge. I actually don't want a "Brooklyn" bar- something like TBD or Arcade would be weird in this neighborhood. But I wouldn't mind an Astoria Bar- a McCanns, an Astoria Brewhouse, a Sparrow.

And I have talked about opening a bar like this with friends... but the funds and the time (we all have young children) aren't there right now. But a bar with locally sourced snacks (maybe a weekend brunch), some nice wine choices, some craft beers, but also low key and not stuffy. Right now my best bet is to drink at Espresso 77, and that just seems strange...
