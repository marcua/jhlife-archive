News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Business Listings and Services Directory / CANTIAMO Youth Chorus of Queens for Kids ages 8 to 18 * By Audition only*
« on: September 20, 2011, 05:23:13 PM »
Be part of our First Season's Excitement as Cantiamo Youth Chorus of Queens (CYCQ) brings children from diverse backgrounds together to learn from each other and become friends.

AUDITIONS WILL BE ON-GOING through Mid-October.  CALL 917-548-1086 to schedule your child's brief audition.

Cantiamo Youth Chorus of Queens (CYCQ) will sing at holiday, civic and sporting events and compete on a regional and national level. CYCQ is guided by renowned Choral Director Robert Rene Galvan (Bronx Singers, Peace of Heart Choir, Manhattan Sinfonia, Professor of Conducting and Conductor of Long Island Conservatory of Music).

CYCQ meets Saturdays 12pm to 1:30pm beginning September 24, 2011 at Queen of Angels Auditorium  4404 Skillman Avenue, Sunnyside.
 
Already scheduled events: Winter Concert January 22, 2012; Spring Concert May 5, 2012; SONGS OF THE SEASON 7pm Friday December 16, 2011 immediately preceding "A Christmas Carol" - a FREE concert reading at All Saints Church, Sunnyside.
FEE: Only $360 for 24 ninety-minute sessions.  GENEROUS Need-based SCHOLARSHIPS available.  We never turn anyone qualified away.
A project of Unity Stage Company -- a registered 501(c)(3)   www.unitystage.org
