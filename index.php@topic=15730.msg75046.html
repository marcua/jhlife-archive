For semolinas, it's a shlep, but I go out to Mama's out in Corona (and get the cheeses and cuts from Leo's Latticini next door)
They are closed on Sundays, so on Sundays, the farmer's market is good for a solid ciabatta.

For raisin breads, spanish breads and croissants, I goto Uruguayan Cafe.

For bagels, its either New York City Bagel & Coffee House in Astoria or Bagelicious in Forest Hills.

More than a fishmonger or cheese seller, I am craving a good, close by bakery.
