Real Estate & Home Improvement / Convert from oil to gas heat.
« on: December 05, 2010, 08:08:10 AM »
Natural gas prices are much lower than oil so the cost per BTU is much less.  The supply of natural gas in the USA is very large and the dollars for the fuel stay in the USA.
Last and most important, natural gas burns so much cleaner than oil.  This morning the air coming in my windowed smelled of oil from the #6 fuel being used at Terrace View.

One of the best things you can do for air pollution is to convert to Natural Gas!   :smitten:

If your building uses oil, ask your chairmen why you have not converted; it will reduce heating costs and air pollution.
