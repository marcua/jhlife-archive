Best Ice Cream?
« on: June 13, 2008, 06:47:04 PM »
We've talked a lot about fro-yo, but does anybody have nominations for the best ice cream in the area? 

It seems to me that some of the best options in town are hidden away.  At least until the Van Leeuwen van rolls through, these are some of the best ice creams I've tasted around the 'hood:

  • Ciao Bella Gelato at Lety's
  • Jackson Heights Mangodesh by 5 Boros Ice Cream at Espresso 77
  • Rose Kulfi when I can find it at a South Asian sweets shop
  • Honorable mention: As a dedicated chocoholic, I hesitate to admit liking vanilla, but I recently tasted some delicious vanilla ice cream from a Latino ice cream cart on 37th Ave.

What are your favorites?
