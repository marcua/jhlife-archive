A Great Chinese Restaurant in Jackson Heights - JJ Garden
« on: November 10, 2015, 02:41:39 PM »
JJ Garden (located at 75-21 31 St Avenue Jackson Hts, NY 11370) serves amazing authentic Chinese food with reasonable price, and it offers FREE delivery. Its Peking duck is delicious and fantastic, and they serve different style of noodles including cellophone, thin Cantonese noodles, lo mein, chow fun, and mei fun. JJ Garden serves many delicious appetizers including B.B.Q Honey Spare Ribs, and Roast Duck. Additionally, their Dim Sum menu includes popular items such as Crystal Shrimp Dumpling, and Juicy Little Pork Buns. Finally, they serve Spa Menu with dishes specially prepared for health-conscious diners, steamed without oil, cornstarch nor salt. This is one of few authentic Chinese restaurants with great dishes in Jackson Heights, NY area.

Previously, you can only order food from the restaurant by calling JJ Garden by phone or going to the restaurant to order your food. Now, we can download an easy-to-use app called "mifan" from Apple Store or Google Play on our smart phones to check out JJ Garden's menu, and place our orders for delicious, authentic Chinese dishes for pick-up or delivery using the "mifan" app. This app is so cool that it allows us to track our order status, to see whether it is ready for pick up, or if our food is out for delivery to our place. For additional information about the "mifan" app, visit www.eatmifan.com
