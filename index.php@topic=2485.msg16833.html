Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

It seems obvious what the choices are for April's Food Group:

Terraza 7 Train Bar/Cafe for drinks
http://www.terrazacafe.com/
This is my favorite bar in the neighborhood and perhaps all time. Funky, artistic, interesting, and music which you can actually talk over. This used to be a car garage, but they turned it into a bar. If we don't come here with the group, I strongly suggest you check it our for yourself.

New Broadway Seafood in Elmhurst for Dim Sum
http://www.yelp.com/biz/new-broadway-seafood-restaurant-elmhurst
So far, Pings has been the nearest choice. New Broadway just started selling themselves and offering dim sum. Pretty place, could definitely be worth checking out.

Trieste for Queens Italian
It is tough for JHers to get good italian. This comes highly recommended.
http://www.yelp.com/biz/trieste-restaurant-jackson-heights
check out the yelp review for other links.

Dates? Hows April 11? or 4th or 18th?
