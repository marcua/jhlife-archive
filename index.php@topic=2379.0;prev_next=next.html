Home Depot on 73rd St. and 25th Ave
« on: February 22, 2009, 01:15:41 PM »
Just wanted to give a big thumbs up to the Home Depot in East Elmhurst.  I went there on a Sunday afternoon and four employees were eager to help me (and I was only there for 10 minutes!).  Items are well-stocked and in the right place, there were very short lines to check-out and a huge parking lot.

And if you don't have a car, it's only about a 20 minute walk from much of the historic district, or maybe 30 minutes if you live in the 80s.  If you've ever had the nightmare of dealing with the Home Depot on Northern Blvd, it will be easy to appreciate this one.

And last but not least, there's a Bed Bath and Beyond right next door.
