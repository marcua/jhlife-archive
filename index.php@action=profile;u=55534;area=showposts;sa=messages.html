Community Groups, Activities & Events / Lunchtime Yoga
« on: October 21, 2014, 01:26:21 PM »
Come to de-stress and unwind at 12:30 pm every Friday. Learn the benefits of Vinyasa/flow yoga, incorporating basic asanas or postures, proper breathing, and relaxation techniques.  All levels welcome. TRY A CLASS FOR FREE! Limited quantity of mats will be supplied so feel free to bring your own. Drop ins are welcome, but to register in advance call us at 646-508-4818, visit www.ECSquaredStudio.com or email us at ecsquaredstudio@gmail.com.

About the instructor: Andrea Szakall has been teaching yoga for over ten years. She has studied and taught in locations across the globe, including India, Spain, Greece, Ireland, Hungary and, since 2012, New York. She is a certified Vinyasa, Hatha, chair and prenatal yoga teacher. Throughout these past years she has learned the importance of a consistent, appropriate practice. She feels she needs to give what guidance she has to those with a little less experience.

Community Groups, Activities & Events / 2014 Salsa/Latin Rhythms Class Kick Off
« on: July 16, 2014, 12:49:26 PM »
Caliente! New dance and fitness studio in Jackson Heights, EC Squared Studio, opens a Salsa/Latin Rhythms class with a free demo session on July 17 at 7 pm.

Join other dance lovers for a demo of Salsa, Bachata and Merengue. The instructor Jose, who has been teaching Latin Ballroom for over 20 years and has had the privilege to study under "The Mambo King" Eddie Torres, will explain the fundamentals of these hot rhythms, including technique, turn patterns and styling. Start with footwork, followed by partner work.

The first class is free. This class kicks off a 16-week program. Pay for the 16-week series or per month. There's something for everyone!
