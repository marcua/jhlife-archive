Neighborhood Chat / Carnegie Hall looking for local musical venue
« on: January 13, 2009, 07:25:26 PM »
The Carnegie Hall's Weill Music Institute is looking for spaces in
Jackson Heights to present music performances as part of their
Neighborhood Concert Series, which brings free concerts to
neighborhoods of New York as a service to the community.

Does anyone have suggestions of local venues that would fit the
following criteria?

The venue should have:

1. a performance area that is designed for musical performances
2. experience in presenting musical performances
3. a sound system or a space with good acoustics for acoustic performances
4. seating capacity between 100-300
5. some means of marketing (can be simple like posting flyers, but
some mechanism in place is helpful)
6. accessible via public transportation

Thanks so much,
Joy
