Title: Al Araf Halal - 76 Street & 37 Ave

I've become a regular at Al Araf Halal for the past year and take out food at least once a week.  I enjoy the vegetarian although I occasionally have the non vegetarian.  Great food at great prices.  For the past 3 - 4 days its been closed for maintenance.  They have posted in the window a Health Department Inspection Certificate that is an "A", but that could be a previous inspection.  I'm hoping that they haven't failed a more recent inspection but then wouldn't there be a notice posted on the window by the Health Department.  I'm hoping they reopen soon.  The food is similar to Al Naimat but I prefer Al Araf.
