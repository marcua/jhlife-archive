Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

GAS - STOP THE MADNESS!
« on: November 10, 2012, 11:36:43 PM »
PEOPLE!
PLEASE STOP MAKING THESE RIDICULOUS GAS LINES IN QUEENS!

I drive around Astoria, Jackson Hts, Jamaica Estates literally on a daily basis.  What is the DEAL?  

Please, take your car on a 25 minute (MAX) drive to Costco in New Rochelle (and some others Station nearby).  Why would you hang on to a 2, 3 or more hour line to get Gasoline when yo have many, many other choices.  It escapes comprehension.  Are we so incapable of leaving our comfort zone for as Hour?

It really breaks my heart to see so many desperate and tired faces on a line when it doesn't have to be so.  My own Sister in Law rather have her husband hustle a couple of gallons every few days then taking a quick (and panoramic ride in my opinion) and fill her truck.  REALLY?  She only has to fill her truck once ever 3 or 4 weeks because she literally drives to her hair dresser, supermarket and her local restaurants (Floral Park) on Jericho Toke on the weekends.  I freaking told her today...come and follow me  (BUT NO!!!!).  I filled up the wife's Toyota because its running on fumes.  It took me 25 minutes to get there from Holliswood and 10  minutes on line (bring Amex and / Or debit card)
NO ODD OR EVEN BS - That only applies to NYC and LI - WAKE UP PEOPLE!    :police:
