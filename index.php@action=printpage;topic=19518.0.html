Title: Giving Tuesday is Dec 3rd - support JHBG if you can!
Post by: itsit on November 16, 2019, 11:06:24 AM
 If you are looking for a terrific community group to support, one that is behind the scenes in many local activities ranging from gardening to school advocacy to the Halloween parade to the Summer Sundays in the Park and the Historic JH Weekend, look no further. The Jackson Heights Beautification Group is the place where you contribution will go far into making, and keeping, JH the wonderful place to live that is currently is.

  This group, JHBG, needs your funds to make its projects succeed. Please donate on Giving Tuesday or attend a swell fundraising party at the Queensboro on Monday, December 9th*, go for it![/b Donations for specific causes like the JH Orchestra or the JH Post Office butterfly garden can be designated online.

*Go to www.jhbg.org for more info, follow on Facebook
