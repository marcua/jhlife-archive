I like to give at least 30 minutes notice so I am early.  If the weather is bad or it is a weekend night or holiday at least an hour to be safe.

If I call to get a car I tip about 20%. So for a $10.00 ride I would give a $2.00 tip.  If I get a car on the street without calling the dispatcher I give the driver the negotiated price since he will pocket the entire fare.  When you call the dispatcher or "base" the car company and driver share the fare at varying percentages.
