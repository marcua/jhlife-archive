Real Estate & Home Improvement / Re: Looking for RE Agency/Agent
« on: September 16, 2010, 01:19:06 PM »
We liked Carfagna and saw some good places with him, but ultimately bought a For Sale By Owner.

The rules seem to be different in Jackson Hts, many of the brokers often won't co-broke (share a listing w/ your agent, they will only show you their own stuff, so you see less and run around more.) I was very happy not to deal with a broker in the end. We had a terrible experience with a certain broker who I will not name and a place that fell thru. This person was really unscrupulous despite having a good rep in the neighborhood. I'm sure all the brokers on this board will now jump all over me, but I think if you can buy directly from the owner but both have good lawyers to finish out the deal it is a really nice way to go.
