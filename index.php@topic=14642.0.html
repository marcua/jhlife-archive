Introduction To YOGA
« on: April 03, 2016, 11:15:53 AM »

Join us and get "in the know" on yoga, and the multitude of benefits it offers your mind, body, and spirit. 
Sundays, April 10th, 17th, 24th.  2-4pm.


This 3 part series consisting of 2 hours each is ideal for people who have never done yoga before, have limited experience, and/or simply want to re-connect with the very essence and beauty of yoga. Students will explore the history of yoga and discover its many benefits; stress relief, relaxation, increased strength, stamina,endurance, better sleep, flexibility, and so very much more.

In each session there will be discussion in each of History and Philosophy, Theory, and Etiquette / The Chakra system/ Meditation & Pranayama. The rest of the time will be well spent in asana (posture) practice, breathing, and proper alignment along with learning Yoga terms, along with some Sankrit (the language of yoga).

Open up the rest of your life to the benefits of this 5000 year old mind and body practice by getting in the know with a well-rounded yogic immersion.

***Just $99.00 for the entire series!

Please note, this workshop is meant to be small and intimate and therefore space is limited. Pre-Registration required.
