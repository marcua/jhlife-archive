Poets on Painters
Queens Library Gallery
February 4 - March 30, 2008
                         
The collaborative exhibition Poets on Painters dynamically juxtaposes the act of painting with the act of writing. Co-curators Katie Geha, formerly the curator of modern and contemporary art at the Ulrich Museum of Art and Travis Nichols, a Seattle-based poet, have paired 20 contemporary poets with 20 contemporary painters. A timely survey of contemporary painting and poetry, the exhibition instigates a collaboration between established and emerging artists.
