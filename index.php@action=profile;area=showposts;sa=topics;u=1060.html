The information on the advertisement is fake and not correct concerning any large extent of cell phone robberies on the NYC Subways(it was found at the 75th Precinct in Brooklyn in East New York)and how now even if you think you lost your cell phone, you can report it to Crimestoppers. You may not.

The info in this ad is fake. I know the 75th Precinct in East New York, Brooklyn had this advertisement hanging on the walls. Maybe other precincts around the local area do too.

Again, you can not report any lost or stolen cell phones to NYC Crimestoppers or 1-800-577-TIPS. It is not the right number and the 75th Precinct had an ad inside of it that had false information on it.
