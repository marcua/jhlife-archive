Pets and Animals / Great Vet: Makes House Calls!
« on: December 16, 2012, 04:55:19 PM »
After some bad experiences with some vets (none in the neighborhood, don't worry), I came across Homefront Vet Services.  The vet's name is Dr. Souder--she doesn't have an actual office, she comes to your apartment and checks out your pet in the comfort of your home.  She is incredibly kind and responsive and is very flexible about scheduling.  If you have a pet that doesn't like going to the vet, it's worth giving her a call. 

Homefront Vet Services
www.homefrontvet.com
(917) 396-4041

Restaurants & Food / New Pizza Place: Pagliaccio
« on: December 16, 2012, 04:50:16 PM »
Don't know if this has already been posted about, but there's a tasty new pizza joint on Northern around 84th.  It's called Pagliaccio Pizza, and I've ordered from there several times already -- fast delivery, good prices, and pizza that is definitely a cut above anything else available in the neighborhood.  They don't have a website, but search for them on Facebook -- they have their menu posted there.  Cash only, unfortunately.  Worth a shot if you are in the mood for good pizza!

Restaurants & Food / Kosher Chicken at Trade Fair
« on: December 16, 2012, 04:45:31 PM »
If anyone is looking for good poultry (besides the farmer's market), I recently asked the Trade Fair manager to stock Empire kosher chicken more often.  They used to have it periodically--he said they didn't always have it because it is "expensive."  But he said that he'll try to keep it in stock now.  It's really quite tasty. Buy it so he keeps ordering it!
