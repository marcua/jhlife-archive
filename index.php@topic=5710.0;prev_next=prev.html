The best falafel places in Queens are on Main Street in Kew Gardens.  I like Grill Point and also Naomi's. These are Israeli and kosher. Another option is Steinway Street in Astoria.  There are number of Moroccan and Arab places to get falafel. The falafel at Duzan is suppose to be good.  Would be great if Jackson Heights could have a falafel place.
