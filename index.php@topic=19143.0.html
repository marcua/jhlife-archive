Birria Landia
« on: July 01, 2019, 06:59:34 AM »
New taco truck on the corner of Roosevelt and 78th street—
They only serve birria—in the form of tacos, quesadillas, and consommé- it’s called “BEEFRRIA LANDIA”, — they don’t seem to have any presence online.
Instantly and easily this has become my favorite taco in the neighborhood.
The guys who run it are very nice as well, and proud of their food. Anyone else tried it?
