Title: Sesame Street Film - Jackson Heights Kids Needed!
Post by: 86er on June 10, 2014, 08:44:43 PM
We'll be shooting a short film for Sesame Street in the neighborhood next week. It's for a beautiful new song about appreciating all of the various skin tones in the world.

We'd love to feature Jackson Heights and some young kids of the neighborhood...because we rarely see this neighborhood or the community on kids programming! All ages, preferably below 12 (but everyone is welcome too), all cultures and especially all abilities.

There's no acting experience needed but would require a couple of hours of time.

Here's a video that serves as a template for what we're trying to do: lots of variety of kids, lots of locations.
