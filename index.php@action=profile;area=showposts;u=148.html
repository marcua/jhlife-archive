Neighborhood Chat / Re: Any LES or East Village Transplants here?
« on: May 23, 2009, 02:56:23 AM »
We moved to JH from East Village a few years back, because a studio was just too stifling after a while. And with a lower rent, we got 3 times more space. In EV, we stopped going outside from Fri evening till Mon morning to avoid puking individuals. I have to say that JH is not necessarily quieter for us though, as we faced the back in EV and have windows facing traffic in JH. But we didn't move to JH thinking it would be like living in the country...we like that it does still feel like a city. Neighbors could just be as loud or annoying. At least, the apt now is bigger so we can walk around....in fact, many weekends now we are content to just be at home. We cook more.

Commute wise:  In the beginning I did dread the long express stop before Roosevelt Ave. I would fall asleep and wake up feeling it's taking forever. It does take a while to get to LES to meet friends, but super easy to get to P.S. 1, MoMA, Flushing, mid-town and we ended up loving our lives. If you live near the 74th St stop, rather than 82nd, then the commute will be shorter.
