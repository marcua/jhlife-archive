Post by: erospolitico on October 07, 2008, 09:43:42 AM
Non-profits are usually the only types of groups allowed to utilize public school facilities.

Please be aware, this is not a free-ride, they must pay for the use, and on many frequent occassions they must also pay the overtime rate for school safety to be present to secure the building.

While it is unlikely that any proceeds from the mentioned event will be given to P.S. 69, they will almost definitely be compensated for the use of their building.

With the extreme budget cuts in our city schools this is now becoming a more frequent occurance, so that schools can generate additional funding.

On a personal note, the group organizing this festival is a wonderful group of people that I have had the honor of knowing for many years, and are one of the best new cultural organizations in the area, this is not a promotion on my part, just my observations of some great contributors to our neighborhood.
