Title: Annual Historic Jackson Heights Weekend - June 9th and 10th
Post by: jhlifer on May 08, 2018, 10:58:20 AM
The Jackson Heights Beautification Group's annual "Historic Jackson Heights Weekend" will take place on Saturday June 9th and Sunday June 10th. 

Saturday, June 9th:

10am to 2pm:  Exhibit of original photos and ephemera at Community Church

10:45am and 12:15pm:  Slide presentation on history of Jackson Heights at Community Church

Noon to 4pm:  Garden Tour of sixteen gardens.  You can purchase tickets on the day of the event at the Community Church (81-15 35th Avenue) beginning at 10am; Raindate is June 16th.

Sunday, June 10th (Rain or Shine):

Noon:  Walking Tour of the Jackson Heights Historic District.  (2 to 3 hours)   Please note that Walking Tour tickets will be very limited this year and tickets for this event MUST be purchased in advance.

Tickets will be available at Espresso 77 beginning May 25th.

You can reach the JHBG at 718 565 5344
