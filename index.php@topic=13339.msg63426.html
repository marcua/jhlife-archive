See if there is a grill on the wall.  These lead to the building's exhaust and if your kitchen has no windows you will probably have one.  If you don't see one, take a look in your bathroom.  If there is one and it is not too far then you can tie into it.   If you don't see any, ask the super, there is always a chance that someone before you may have covered it during a poorly planned renovation or during the installation of cabinetry and the super knows the location. If this is a coop and there is any type of noise producing activity you are better off asking the management for their alteration package and save yourself some drama. 

If there is a power source near the buildings exhaust you can probably ask an electrician to hook up an exhaust fan on the DL at the opening where the grill is at relatively quickly.  If none of the above is feasible, get a window fan and exhaust out.

Good Luck
