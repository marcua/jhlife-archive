My coop claims to have a bike room, and I suppose technically we do, though it is small, and the folks using it right now aren't being charged anything so there is no incentive to stop using it even if they haven't rode their bike in 5 years.

We store our bikes in a storage cage. It's a real hassle since we also store season stuff, tools, etc. there. We're able to save a bit of space by removing the tires on one of our bikes since my SO is quite handy and can easily get them back on the bike frame. I have to check on the air pressure of the intact bike frequently and pump them up a bit so the rim doesn't get damaged.

If we had a little more space in our apartment, I'd consider using a rack and storing them here.
