Second Samudra. I like it because it is regional (southern India) and seems authentic. Any other Indian restaurants like that in the neighborhood, I'd be interested to know about.

I'd also like to point out that most of the Himalayan/Nepalese restaurants are good, and if you are looking for something a little more authentic than tikka masala and buffets I would recommend checking out one of those. For instance, Himalayan Yak, Mustang Thakali, Dhaulagiri, etc...
