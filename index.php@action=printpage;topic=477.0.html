ASL Workshop For Children  
Special Services and the Sign Language Center present American Sign Language Workshop for Kids. American Sign Language is the third most common language used in the U.S. This workshop will introduce participants to Deaf Culture and American Sign Language basics. Learn how to communicate with you hands!
