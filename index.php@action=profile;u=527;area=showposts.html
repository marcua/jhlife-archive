Community Groups, Activities & Events / Carol Daly's Review
« on: December 07, 2009, 04:14:40 PM »
    Come and see Bethany Wild perform with her trio at Las Margaritas!
    It's THE live music event in Jackson Heights.

    Bethany Wild is a versatile singer and songwriter who stands on her own as a significant songwriter.  With two CD's on the market, Bethany's original songs are poetic, melodic and tell her and other's stories about the lessons of life, love and the wisdom gained through it all.  Her voice is lyrical, sincere and convincing often layered in ear pleasing harmonies of her own voice. 

    Performing with her trio at Las Margaritas, Bethany sings her original songs and also many cover songs in a variety of styles from pop to rock and jazz.  You'll hear her scat and sing the intricacies that jazz requires with all the subtleties and nuances of the great jazz singers. Her attention to every note and the meaning in every song is moving.  But Bethany is also an entertainer with a great look, presence and wonderful command of and interaction with the audience as she knows and has earned her place on the stage.

Carol Daly - Project Manager - Broadway League - New York (Dec 7, 2009)

There is a wonderful worldwide peace organization called Soka Gakkai International in which members gather monthly for discussion meetings. We discuss philosophies of life and death, share our struggles, our goals and our benefits with the determination to refresh and encourage one another.

Meetings are held on a large scale in community centers around the country - but also in people's homes.  Please let me know if you would like to be on a mailing list to inform you of upcoming meetings in Jackson Heights.
