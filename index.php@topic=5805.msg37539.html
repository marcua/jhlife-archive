Taco Fresco

I'm not too sure if anyone has posted about this place on 37Th Ave. between 84Th and 85Th streets. It was the "A" rating that drew me and the tacos that keep me going back. Really a fast food type of place, the ingredients always seem to be fresh. If you  like to eat healthy (that is without the grease and fat content that most of these places serve up) it's a good option. It is totally inexpensive as well!
