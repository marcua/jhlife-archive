Who can deny the power of Music?
I always remember my 1st son personality - he wasn't as musical as my youngest one, but whenever Barney popped out he dance his butt off. My youngest one will dance every sound, song or commercial that pops anywhere - it's not him, it's human nature!. Who can deny that a good rhythm and beat will get the biggest square angry person tapping his feet, moving his rump or humming a tune. Music Makes Us one - It brings attention to the importance of hunger - abuse and injustice like in songs "We are the world" . But more than anything else it makes us feel YOUNG, happy and allows our inhibitions and ignorant stereotypes to be thrown out the window. Have you ever felt sad, bored, indifferent or angry?..suddenly you hear a sound..a beat - a rhythm or a lyric that got your blood flowing, your mind scrambling, your feet tapping or your Head bopping...OH YEAH BABY...(that's my song!!)

Well, being a full blooded Americano with Puertorrican parents, French, Spanish, Mulatto, Australian (don't ask) Grand Parents  - I don't think many people have a better understanding than us Newyorkers -  :rockon:

Ireland - France, Spain - England- they all have really amazing music...Brazil WOW!~!!! - Cuba - Dominican Republic.. -  Jamaica (yeah man!).  I have heard some hypnotizing sounds from the Andes and Korea. really - We live in a world that is full of sounds and music that will only enrich Your Life!
