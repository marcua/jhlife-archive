I'm wondering how excited people will be due to the filming in general.  About 10 years or so ago, Sydney Pollack and his film crew set up on 35th Avenue in the high 70's to film a few scenes for the movie, Random Hearts.  It starred Harrison Ford and Kristin Scott Thomas.  Most of the filming was done at the Colton Condominium on 35th Ave., between 76th & 77th Streets, and some scenes were also shot on 37th Avenue.  Back then, the neighborhood was abuzz with excitement.  That film crew also took up a major part of the side street parking.  BTW, the movie was pretty bad.
