Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

 I want to be realistic here and I do put my money where my mouth is and my volunteer time too.

 - A semi-organized running (or walking) group along 34th Ave in the early mornings
 - lots of plantings in the tree pits and the city funds to maintain them to civic orgs. like JHBG
 - school info about afterschool use widely publicized and available. Weekend activities in schools for kids?
 - senior activities better publicized and more of same. Taichi classes were desired from some participants for ex.
   and centers offering breakfast and lunch
 - informal police at Travers Pk especially between 2:30 and 4:30.
 - more trashcans and Doe Fund helpers
 - clear rules posted on loitering in areas like Diversity Plaza along with true enforcement
 - something - anything - to make Roosevelt Ave nicer. A lighting project of some sort, something environmental, clean it!
