Title: Re: Con Edison construction on 35th Avenue
Post by: 34AD on February 21, 2018, 01:29:55 PM
Hi pipman,

Con Edison is upgrading the electric system.

Work is currently taking place in the following locations:
35th Avenue from 77th Street to 85th Street
85th Street between 35th Avenue and Roosevelt Avenue

Construction work will be performed over the next several weeks between the hours of 7AM and 6PM, and Saturdays between 8AM and 6PM.

Cable installation work will follow at a later date with an estimated completion of one additional month.

What to expect:
- Limited Street Parking
- Temporary lane closures
- No service interruptions are anticipated

If you have any further questions or concerns, please contact the office of Assembly member DenDekker at 718-457-0384.
