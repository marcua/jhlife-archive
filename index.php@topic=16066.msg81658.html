I've been in a few times since it has changed hands and it already feels as though it's changing for the worst. Tonight they tried to over charge me by about $5. I started to add things up on my phone and the cashier suddenly agreed to re-ring everything. When she gave me my receipt, I noticed that it wasn't itemized.

Other things:

1. The new employees (or owners?) can be quite mean. I went in once for coffee and the person behind the counter barked "WHAT DO YOU WANT!!!!" She seemed annoyed that I was buying coffee. I used to have conversations with the old cashiers. They were quite friendly.

2. They don't seem to restock some of the specialty items that I occasionally buy there or even the chicken stock that I like, which I bought regularly. I haven't seen the good baguette that they used to sell for a while (can't remember who makes it), and their pasta selection seems to be dwindling.

3. The music is a drag.
