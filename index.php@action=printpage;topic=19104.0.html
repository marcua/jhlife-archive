Title: Newtown Literary Seeks Submissions for Issue 15
Post by: aescoto on June 16, 2019, 03:52:25 PM
Hello!

Newtown Literary, a semi-annual print and electronic journal based in Queens and publishing poetry, fiction, and creative non-fiction from Queens writers is seeking submissions for Issue 15 through July 10th. Submitting is free (there is also an additional option to pay for written feedback from an editor) and can be done through the website at the following link: https://www.newtownliterary.org/submit.

We look forward to reading your work!
