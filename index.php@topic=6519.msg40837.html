It has been a pleasure serving the community over the last seven months.  Through the spring and summer, I have met many wonderful families and my daughter has made many wonderful friends.  As well, it has been a very valuable experience for me as my program continually evolves into something that is all at once fun for the children, educational for all, and well-balanced to include the mind, body and spirit elements that make for happy and healthy children.  Thank you to the amazing teachers and native speakers in my class who have helped me to improve and grow my program into what it is today.
