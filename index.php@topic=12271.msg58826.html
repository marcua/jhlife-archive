I have.  Went last night.  Really nice environment--dug the changing colored light motif the dominate one of the walls--and very friendly.  Went for a pretty simple first test--sushi and sashimi combo, edamame, seaweed salad, sake--and thought everything was really good.  Fresh, tasty, well prepared.  I'm not going to get overenthusiastic and say it's the best Japanese food I've ever had but it seems a slight cut above the local competition.  I'll be curious to try some more adventurous fare next time.  And, of course, to see what the rest of you think.
