Post by: earbears on January 29, 2016, 07:15:04 PM
From Assemblyman Den Dekker:

Attempted Child Abduction

If you have ANY information please call the 115 Pct immediately 718-533-2003

An incident occurred in the confines of the 115th Precinct this morning at approximately 0745 hours a 10 year old female was walking to Renaissance School (81 Street/37 Avenue) within the confines of the 115th Precinct, when a M/H perp in is 30's, 6"2" 200 pounds, wearing a green vest, black pants, black skull hat, and a blue packpack, called the girl from the other side of street and waved for her to come towards him. The girl was frightened and ran towards the School Crossing Guard on the corner. As the girl began to run towards the crossing guard, the perpetrator ran across the street and attempted to grab her. No contact was made, and there were no injuries. The 115th Precinct Squad is working on a wanted flyer right now, and I will get it out as soon as it is done.
