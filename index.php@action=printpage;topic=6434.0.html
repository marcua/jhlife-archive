Title: Re: Any Pet Recommendations?
Post by: Lilybell on July 29, 2011, 11:18:14 AM
Quote
African Grey parrot.  They are the best.

I agree that parrots rock-  especially African Greys because they are the smartest and best talkers.  However, they require a lot of interaction so if you work all day I wouldn't recommend getting any type of parrot. They can be neurotic and needy and will start pulling out their feathers from stress if they are left home alone most of the day.

They are also very messy, so if you hate to clean they are not for you.  And loud.  Don't forget loud.  If you are in an apartment building your neighbors will probably not be happy. 

I love birds; if I could have a pet a parrot would be my first choice (I grew up with a Blue-fronted Amazon and we were best friends. He was a great talker).  But they are a lot of work and people shouldn't just buy a big parrot without having owned a smaller bird first.  Rescues are full of big birds because people don't realize what they are getting into and can't deal with the mess and noise.  But they can be very rewarding if you do your research first and decide it's the right pet for you. 

rhydewithdis, do you have an African Grey?  Just curious.  I love them.
