Farm Spot
http://farmspot.org/

summer 2013

vegetable share
This is the standard 26-week membership share. This share contains 6 to 10 items each week, depending on the season and availability. Shares may include items such as: In spring: Arugula, Lettuce, Radishes, Strawberries, Sugar Snap Peas, Swiss Chard, Beets. In summer: Watermelon, Potatoes, Garlic, Red Tomatoes, Basil, Green Beans, Eggplant, Zucchini In fall: Fennel, Potatoes, Lettuce, Broccoli, Sweet Potatoes, Carrots, Toscano Kale, Winter Squash, Celeriac. The amount is enough to feed a family of three to four for a week. Many Farm Spot members split one share between two households. Please note that members organize this on their own and Farm Spot does not coordinate half shares.
26 weeks / $587.08 26 weeks (May 30th through Dec. 5th)

fruit share
Golden Earthworm Farm is teaming up with Briermere Farms to offer a non-organic, yet responsibly grown, minimally treated fruit share. Beginning in July, the fruit share includes blueberries, raspberries, blackberries, peaches, pears, apples, and possibly apricots, plums and nectarines. (Fruit share is available without the vegetable share.)
$168.00 — The value of each fruit share is deducted from the total season’s payment and fruit is delivered weekly until the full value has been delivered.

herb share
There will be a wide variety of herbs, including basil (thai and purple), parsley, dill, cilantro, mint, rosemary, thyme, oregano, sage, chives, garlic chives, tarragon and lavender. We’ll include 2 types of herbs per delivery except for a few deliveries where we’ll only include green basil. Herb shares are only open to members who also purchase a vegetable or fruit share.
12 weeks / $51.87
