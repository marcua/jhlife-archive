Title: Re: Wing's Miller
Post by: dssjh on September 25, 2018, 07:34:31 PM
my partner, who was especially excited about the opening, raved about the wings i brought home last night on our first trial. and i thought them pretty darn good, too. good texture on the meat (still juicy) and a nice crunch, even after a walk back down to 76th street. we had the hot (a good zing, not a mouth sear) and the garlic pepper, which could have used a tiny bit more garlic, but still had a nice pepper tone.

fries were crisp, properly salted, held their warmth for the walk, and the jalapeno poppers seemed house made, judging by their look. also quite good.

the nice gent behind the counter said "next month, probably" about delivery, but this is definitely one that's going into regular rotation. next up, extra hot, and perhaps the nuggets. the clam chowder? maybe not.
