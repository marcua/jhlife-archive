I've seen the inside of at least eight of these apartments over the years. These are original seven room apartments at the Towers - they range in size from 1400 sq. ft. to a little over 1700 sq. ft.

These are fairly common in those buildings -- I think at least one per floor?
Two and a half years ago when I was in the market, there were 4-5 of them on sale at the same time. I don't know of any for sale at the moment.
Maintenance is in line with other garden buildings in the neighborhood, around $1500/mth for 1700 sq. ft. place.
