Title: Rachael Ray launches dog food line
Post by: Chuckster on July 28, 2008, 12:04:30 PM
Food TV celebrity Rachael Ray has launched a new line of dog food called Nutrish (http://www.rachaelray.com/pets/nutrish.php?view=).  Proceeds from sales will benefit Ray's animal rescue organization Rachael's Rescue (http://www.rachaelray.com/pets/index.php).  The food is being labeled as super premium and does not contain by-products, fillers or artificial ingredients or preservatives.

Rachael Ray Launches Line of Super Premium Dog Food & Treats (http://www.marketwatch.com/news/story/rachael-ray-launches-line-super/story.aspx?guid=%7B7FCFA638-5454-4906-A159-578FF66B3AB7%7D&dist=hppr)

Side Note:  While this food does list a protein as its first ingredient, it also lists corn and soy meals as additional ingredients...not so good.
