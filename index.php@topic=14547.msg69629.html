News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Email from Councilmember Daniel Dromm:

My office is working very closely with the 115th Precinct to identify and prosecute these criminals.  In some cases, it involves gang activity.  In many, if not most, the criminal has been captured.  The precinct just got an infusion of additional officers thanks to the NYC Council providing hundreds of millions of dollars in funding for 1,300 new officers, citywide, in the  FY 2016 budget. As you may know, this was a top Council priority.  The hiring of the extra police officers came with the stipulation that they be used for community policing and the NYPD has reshaped the department to address community concerns. TONIGHT is the 115th Precinct Community Council Meeting at the precinct.  It starts at 7 PM at the precinct at 92 Street and Northern Blvd.  You should go.  It's a great place to get info from the Inspector about what is being done to protect the neighborhood. The meetings are held monthly and community input is welcome and is always taken seriously by the Inspector.  "
