Upcoming Events!

So many things happening at The Q!

Dear friends and neighbors, this is a busy week for us as we gear up towards the holiday season. We have lots of events planned for you as we work hard to make The Queensboro a fun, comfortable, and warm destination for you!

Let's quickly break it down, and details on each events will be further down:

Today, Wednesday: Board Gaming night - fun for everyone, come alone, come with a partner or bring the kids, you'll have a great time trying out new games brought to you by the wonderful folks at ShenanigansNY!

Tomorrow, Thursday, we're looking forward to two events that we think will work well together.

First, join your neighbors from the Jackson Heights Climate Committee for a Solar Happy Hour from 6:30-8:30, and then at 8:30 Vinny and the Unknowns will perform once again, bringing us some great blues, rock and classics.

On Friday the Cold Club of Queens will perform around 9PM, so get ready for some beautiful Django-jazz inspired tunes!

We are open for Thanksgiving! Our menus for in-house dining and catering are now online over here.

Sunday will be another Sunday Supper day, we'll get you details about that day's dish very soon but if you want to ensure you get some (whatever it is you know it will be good) you really should make a reservation,and let us know that you want some of that Sunday Supper!


Wednesday

So you're interested in board gaming? Maybe your kids are? Or you want to meet new people, maybe make some friends? Shenanigans NY have partnered with us for several weeks now, and it has been a blast.

They bring a whole pile of really cool games, ranging from some of the classics to new ones that have just been released, and they'll show you how to play them, pair you with other players and generally just make sure that you have a good time.

It's happening every Wednesday from 6:30 to 10:30, and you can just show up and pay $5 to join in, or you can buy a ticket beforehand, and get discounts on your check, and you'll also be entered into a raffle for some cool prizes.

Click on the picture below to get your tickets and get ready to have some fun!



Thursday

The very cool people at The Queens Climate Project are working hard to make our neighborhood, and borough, better places to live in, so we're hosting them - and you! - for a happy hour on Thursday the 14th. Here is what they have to say:

-

"Hey Jackson Heights! We all know that NYC needs to get to 100% renewable energy fast, and what better community than ours – the most culturally diverse, politically active, all-around awesome neighborhood in the city -- to lead the way?  Transforming how we live will take nothing short of a revolution…and hey, revolutions always go down better with drinks in hand! So come talk sustainability at the JACKSON HEIGHTS GOES GREEN Happy Hours series the 2nd Thursday of every month, 6:30-8:30! (Stay on for the live music afterwards.)         

Hope to see you at our kick-off event, JACKSON HEIGHTS GOES SOLAR, on November 14th. Come chat with neighbors who have already installed solar panels, enjoy drink specials, and learn how to make Jackson Heights even greener!"

-

And then... at 8:30 we'll be enjoying the music of Vinny & The Unknowns who will be playing an eclectic mix of pop, jazz, blues, rock covers and originals and more! You might know Vinny as the sound and stage person behind the JHBG Summer Sundays concerts in Travers Park. We're glad to support another local musician, especially when they are this much fun!

Friday

The Cold Club of Queens play some fantastically fun music inspired by Django Reinhardt who hit it big in the 1930's and 40's with his unique style of jazz. These guys below aren't that old but they play (probably) just as well and it's really worth checking them out. It's always a fun night when they perform, and we are looking forward to it!



Sunday

Sunday Supper! What is it? When is it? How does it work? There are so many questions, but luckily we have many answers.

In short, every Sunday starting at 5PM, feed your family (or 2-3 hungry adults) for around $45. That's not per person, that's it! You'll get a starter and a main course, served family-style and so far we have not disappointed!

This week's menu will be announced soon, so stay tuned. And make a reservation, always make a reservation!

Gift Cards

A quick note about Queensboro gift cards... teachers love them!

If you're wondering what to get your child's teacher for the holidays, consider purchasing a Queensboro gift card. There are a lot of teachers that come to The Q, and we have a feeling they'd appreciate something like that. You can buy them online here, and print them up yourself, or come in and buy them in person and we'll print them up for you!

Open on Thanksgiving!

We will open on Thanksgiving, serving dinner from 3PM to 7PM. We will also offer catering services. Our menus for the day are online now, go over here to take a look at them and if you do decide to come to The Q for dinner that day, then go ahead and make a reservation!

Drag Show Date Change - November 30th!

Please note that the Princess Party we had scheduled for the 23rd is now going to take place on the 30th, at the same time - 10PM. Make a reservation as soon as possible, we have a feeling it is going to be a great night!

New Year's Eve at The Q!



We are very excited that it's almost 2020, and we've been busy making preparations for a magnificent New Year's Eve party to welcome in the new year!

See below for all of the details, to get your tickets you can:

- book online
- come to The Q to buy them
- give us a call at 929 296-0038 and we'll be happy to help you out.

But First, Our Holiday hours

Christmas Eve - 5PM-10PM

Christmas Day - Closed!
Then we'll be open as usual every day, until...
New Year's Eve - open for early dinner from 4PM-7PM
New Year's Eve Party 9PM! See below for all of the details.
January 1st - open, 11AM-4PM, 5PM-9PM

And now... New Year's Eve!

The party starts at 9PM and will cost $85 per person (plus tip and tax) which includes food from our raw bar, hors d’oeuvres and buffet, and two drinks. Tickets for kids 14 and under are $25, kids 4 and under can come for free.

There will be a DJ and if you want to dance you most certainly can!

What is included in the price?
Food, two drinks, and entertainment.

Where can I buy tickets?
Make a reservation and we'll contact you to take care of the next steps, or you can come to The Queensboro in person or give us a call at 929 296-0038.

Will there be enough food for a full dinner?
Yes, this is The Queensboro, so the quantity and range of food will be generous and excellent. Food includes passed hors d’oeuvres, buffet, pasta, pizza, raw bar, and dessert.

I have special dietary needs.
Please let us know when you purchase your tickets, and we will do our best to accommodate your needs.

How do the drinks work?
Everyone will get their first two drinks included in the price. You can order cocktails, wine or beer.

What if I want more than 2 drinks?
The bar is fully available all night and additional drinks can be purchased at our normal menu prices. Your server can run a tab for you.

What if I want to bring children?
Kids are always welcome at The Q! Tickets for 14 year olds and under are $25, kids 4 and under can come party with us for free! Families with children will not be seated in the bar area.

I want to sit with another group that has booked separately.  Can we do that?
We will do our best to honor all seating requests.  Even if this is not possible,  we envision this as a walk-around cocktail event and we would disappointed if people stayed in their assigned seats for any length of time.

What is the cancellation policy?
Once purchased, tickets are non-refundable, but if you are unable to attend then we can make them available for other people to buy them. Because of the strong interest in the party, last year we had a waitlist, so it will probably be easy to resell your tickets.

Who is the DJ?  What type of music will be played?
JoeZ, a fantastic and versatile local DJ will be playing music from various decades, we're lucky to have been able to book him again this year.

What is the closing time?
We expect things to wind down sometime after midnight, and sometime before 4AM.

Wednesday the 18th - El Clasico


Dear neighbors, dear soccer fans, we are very excited that the most important day of the most important sport ever is next Wednesday, when Barcelona and Real Madrid face off for the first time this season, on Barcelona's home turf, at 2PM EST.

There are many other teams in this sport, and there are many other sports, all with their rivalries and histories, and yes we are sure that they are very interesting. However, it is undeniable - and perhaps even proven by science - that they all pale in comparison to this long, bitter, heart wrenching clash that for generations has torn families apart, destroyed friendships, caused numerous deaths, and is probably responsible for several wars.

It's a beautiful thing, it's a beautiful game, and next Wednesday when we open our doors at 1:30, we will be serving beer and other great drinks at happy hour prices until 7PM, and $12 pizzas, and we will be showing the game on our big screen.

The match is at an awkward time of day for us here in the US, so we suggest that you lay the groundwork early to be able to call in sick on Wednesday. We hope that you will do everything that it takes to watch this match, and we would love it if you were to watch it with us.

El Clasico at The Q - 2PM Wednesday December 18th

What else is happening on Wednesday? We mentioned the $12 pizzas which we'll have available all evening, and it is our board game night as well! Stick around until 6:30 when the fine folks from Shenanigans NY come by to set up their 50+ board games for you to try!

Music on Saturday - Eugenia Choe!



This Saturday at 9PM prepare for some original jazz at The Queensboro - Eugenia Choe is back, and we're excited!

A Korean-American Jazz pianist and composer, Eugenia Choe has been active on the New York music scene for years. Having spent her early childhood in South Korea and her formative years in L.A., Atlanta, Chicago, and New York, Eugenia has been in the unique position of having an intimate connection with different cultures.

She and her musical colleagues bring this diverse approach to their music. Accompanied by Queens-based musicians, Alex Wyatt and Malik McLaurine, Eugenia Choe and her colleagues proudly present a fresh take on classic jazz repertoire at the Queensboro this Saturday.

Alex Wyatt- Drums
Malik McLaurine- Bass
Eugenia Choe- Piano

3.) No aggressive toddlers
4.) Accompany and monitor your toddler in the run at all times
5.) Remain alert! Interrupt rough play and mounting
6.) If your toddler gets into a fight or injures another toddler, exchange contact information with the other owner and remove your toddler from the run
7.) You are legally and financially responsible for any injury caused by your toddler
8.) Toddlers must be licensed and properly vaccinated
9.) Your toddler must wear a collar or harness while in the run
10.) Prong, chain and spike collars are prohibited
11.) Children must be accompanied by an adult and remain seated • Do not use the run if your toddler has a communicable disease
