Title: Jackson Heights CREW Meeting: Thursday, Feb. 18, 2010, 7:00PM
Post by: PetMau on February 06, 2010, 09:25:23 AM
The next meeting of the JHCREW will take place on Thursday, Feb. 18, 2010, at 7:00PM, at Methodist Community Church in Jackson Heights.

JHCREW is an adhoc committee of the Jackson Heights Beautification Group formed to establish a dog run in Jackson Heights.

All dog owners and community members are welcomed and encouraged to attend meetings.

This month we will be finalizing the Membership Application and opening the floor for Logo Designs. Do you have any artistic talent? Then please come to the meeting to submit your artwork. Do you have any writing ability? We are also looking for contributors to write for the newsletter.

In the meantime, I encourage you to join the JHCREW website http://jhdogs.ning.com/ (http://jhdogs.ning.com/) to receive faster updates and share information with the future dog run community!
