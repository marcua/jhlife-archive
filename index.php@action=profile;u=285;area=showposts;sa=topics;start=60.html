Community Groups, Activities & Events / Merit Scholarship Exam+ Open House @ Garden School Nov.20th
« on: November 05, 2010, 04:52:11 PM »
 On Saturday, November 20 at 9:00 there will be a Merit Scholarship Exam and an Open House
at the Garden School in Jackson Heights. The Merit scholarship exam is given to middle school
students and is a standardized test similar to those taken at the end of school in many places.
 It's a great opportunity for those curious about Garden School to check it out at the Open House.
Anyone interested in the exam, should call the school at 718/335-6363 to register. The fee
is $50. Currently, there are 33 students who attend the Upper School with full or partial scholarships!
The student to teacher ratio at the school is 8:1 which is a great benefit to a lot of students. Many
of the teachers are local community members and they are invaluable as mentors with their
particular knowledge of this location. Students often excel academically who might have been
lost in larger schools. Factor in the no-cut sports policy, robotics, Advanced Placement courses,
nutritious meals at lunch, many clubs and after-school activities like dance, cooking for kids
and terrific arts programs, it's a very special education. They are in the midst of "greening" the
school through student-run organizations.
 The Garden School is located at 33-16 79th Street between Northern and 34th Avenue. - one
block over from Travers Park and Jackson Heights Greenmarket.
