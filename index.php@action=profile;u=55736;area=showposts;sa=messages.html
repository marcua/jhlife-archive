Dear Friend,

Cleaning up Roosevelt Avenue has been at the top of my priority list for years. It was terrific to learn this week that eight people were arrested for soliciting sex for money.

The undercover prostitution sting was conducted at a spot under the elevated tracks of the 7 train which I have complained about for many years. Unfortunately, the commercial corridor has transformed itself into the old Times Square. As part of my holistic proposal to modernize the avenue, I called for a moratorium on liquor licenses for new clubs, and proposed the creation of a Commission on Roosevelt Avenue, an entity that would release a study with an action plan to modernize the busy commercial thoroughfare. I would like to thank the local authorities, including the NYPD and the Queens District Attorney's office for taking a step in the right direction.

What we need here is political will, the same political will that converted Times Square into what it is today. I will not give up until the Roosevelt Avenue is transformed into a destination for families and visitors.

If you have any questions or need help, do not hesitate to stop by my office or to call us at (718) 205-3881.

Sincerely, 

Jose Peralta

Restaurants & Food / My top 10
« on: March 31, 2017, 08:34:08 PM »
Sugar Club
Located at 8118 Broadway, NY, 11373
Known for Cafe or Coffee Shop, Rainy Days, Secret Spots, Romance
This hipster Thai dessert place has green tea crepes with ice cream and fruit. The green milk tea is exceptional.

Lhasa Fast Food
Located at 37-50 74th St, Jackson Heights, NY, 11372
Known for Restaurant, Rainy Days, Early Birds, Iconic Scenes
This secret place requires you to pass through the inside of a cell phone shop. It's not easy to find. And once you get there, it will be packed. But for good reason. It's delicious. Mains $6.

Playground
Located at 71-30 Roosevelt Ave, NY, 11372
Known for Restaurant, Rainy Days, Early Birds, Stunning Views, Iconic Scenes, Secret Spots, Romance
This ultra-hip, 2nd floor Thai hot pot is one of the best restaurants in NYC. If you are courageous, try the pork blood Tom Yum broth. All you can eat (veg, meat, seafood) for $17/person. Fills up at peak hours.

Pauglina
Located at 75-26 37th Ave, New York, NY, 11372
Known for Cafe or Coffee Shop, Rainy Days, Peace & Quiet, Secret Spots, Romance
This cafe and tea house doubles as a high-end florist. Everything is marble and beautiful.

Ihawan Restaurant
Located at 40-06 70th St, NY, 11377
Known for Restaurant, Late Nights, Secret Spots
This is a "secret" place on the 2nd floor of a weird office building. It is a must if you want to show your friends some fun.

House of Inasal
Located at 65-14 Roosevelt Ave, New York, NY, 11377
Known for Restaurant
Good lumpia shanghai

SriPraPhai
Located at 6413 39th Avenue, NY, 11377-2846
Known for Restaurant
While big and popular, it is still second only to Khao Gang for Thai food outside of Thailand. And maybe inside, too.

Urubamba
Located at 8620 37th Avenue, NY, 11372
Known for Restaurant
You like meat? This is where to get it. And chicha? Yummy.

Khao Kang
Located at 76-20 Woodside Ave, NY, 11373
Known for Restaurant
It is the best Thai food in North America. Virtually all the guests are Thai, though, so it might be too hot for you.

Great Taste
