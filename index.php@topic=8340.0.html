Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

82nd Street BID
« on: May 08, 2013, 10:57:38 PM »
I am really impressed with the improvements that I've been seeing made by the 82nd St Business Improvement District. It is a cleaner, more social place with plenty of benches, new street side bike parking, planters, and the promise of nice shops to come. I searched this page before posting and came across how the BID is securing financing for 82nd Tenants to become compliant with the landmarks commission in the next year. (down with awnings, cleaning up storefronts, etc) How exciting!

There is even a Manhattan style Korean Deli, you know, hot buffet, healthy snacks, chopped salads- good fast service..

If anyone here is involved- Big thanks for taking the lead on cleaning up JH. That the BID sought out grant money to make improvements is really cool. Our local politicians should take note and use some of their discretionary funding in the same way.

What the heck has Dromm actually done here? Not necessarily criticizing, as I know folks love him...but tell me what he's done, and how we can all help the BID continue to make improvements and hopefully attract businesses that reflect our desires.
