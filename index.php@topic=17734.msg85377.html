Pizza again?
« on: April 17, 2018, 08:55:01 PM »
A pizzeria may be coming again to the location of the now closed Fresh Spilt Milk.  I walked by earlier this evening and came across three guys exiting the space.  Interestingly, one of them happened to be the owner of Mario's Pizzeria, the site's former occupant.  I noticed that work was going on inside, so I asked one of the guys the status of the business.  According to what he said, they're planning on opening a new pizzeria, and he's going to be the pizza chef.  He also mentioned that they'll be offering a large variety of pizza, and that they originally hail from Long Island.

If we indeed get a new pizzeria, I'm hoping round two is a winner.
