Title: Open House Tomorrow - Luxury Condo City Island
Post by: ltorres50 on September 21, 2019, 07:09:50 PM
If you like paying top dollar for less square footage, this place isn't for you.

If you like older places with "character" (or things to fix and update), stop reading and move on this place is turn key.

If you like small closets and tiny bathrooms, look at other listings here because you might get lost in the two large custom walk-in closets or amongst the 3.5 bathrooms.

If you like paying monthly for a parking space, fighting for a spot with the best drivers in Queens, or having to move your car for alternate side once or twice a week, then the attached garage and driveway here will just turn you off.

If you love coop boards and all the drama associated with them, this luxury condo, that is well maintained and has a great sense of community, might be too calm and peaceful for you.

If you like paying high maintenance fees with "extra special" assessments to fund major capital projects, spend your money elsewhere all the units are new and maintenace runs less than 40 cents a square foot.

But if you're looking for a quieter life with a lot more space, water views, and parking; but not ready for Westchester prices and taxes, then look to City Island, NYC's small town!

We have a great 3BR/3.5BA townhouse top floor condo with 1,815 square feet for sale in a gated community with many luxury touches, including 10' ceilings with crown molding, hardwood floors throughout, gas fireplace, washer/dryer, custom walk-in closets, modern appliances, and spa-like master bath resplendent in marble.  Common charges/maintenance include swimming pool and clubhouse with fitness room.

Open house tomorrow, Sunday September 22nd, 12pm-2pm.
