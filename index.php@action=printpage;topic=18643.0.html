Title: Are you someone's hero?
Post by: scsny on January 08, 2019, 05:50:54 PM
SCS Pet Pals is seeking volunteers for its Pet Pals program. Pet Pals matches seniors in need of help with their animal companions with local volunteers to provide basic pet care such as walking, grooming and cleaning, and companionship for the senior. If you would like to help or if you could use a Pet Pal, please call 718-784-6173 ext. 479 or email info@scsny.org for more information.
