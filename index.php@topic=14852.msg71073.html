BEAUTIFUL JACKSON HEIGHTS STUDIO IN THE HEART OF THE HISTORIC DISTRICT - $179k
« on: May 17, 2016, 02:56:50 PM »
Recently renovated studio in the heart of Jackson Heights historic district. Gated pre-war Co-op building with over 80% owner occupancy. Immaculately maintained large garden for occupants' use only, complete with benches and picnic tables.

Approximately 450+ square feet.
Separate kitchen, bathroom, dressing area and 4 very large closets.
Stainless steel refrigerator, oven and microwave.
Solid oak queen size murphy bed.
Hardwood floors, crown molding and new windows throughout.

Excellent on-site management company.
Live-in super attendants and many porters.
Laundry in basement, storage units available.
All pets-friendly.

This building is a Designated Landmark Status building located within the Jackson Heights Historic District. It is also listed on both the New York State and National Register of Historic Places.

7 minute walk to Roosevelt Avenue subway stop, E, F, M, R, 7 trains. 20 minutes to mid-town Manhattan.
Easy commute to LaGuardia and JFK.

For sale by owner.
20% down and board approval required.
Monthly maintenance (which includes taxes) is approximately $490/month.

Please call (929) 266-7120 and leave a detailed message with your name, phone number and time to return your call in order to schedule a showing.
