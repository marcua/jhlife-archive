Even though today is the first time in a few months that we've had excessive noise in JH from jets flying into or out of LGA, I submitted a complaint form this morning anyway.  I appreciate the few months of quiet, but I'm afraid if we don't register complaints that the decision makers may assume the noise is not bothersome, and make more frequent runway changes or flight additions that impact JH in the future.

If the noise is bothering you, the complaint form is at http://www.planenoise.com/panynj/daPRAbr9/

that area - northern from the bqe exit to 71st or so - is the low point of one of the approaches. the planes are usually only a couple hundred feet up.

but if you think about it, the planes, even on approach, are going at a very high rate of speed, and they're only a mile from the airport at that point, and will be on the ground in 45-60 seconds. it'd be difficult and dangerous for a pilot to make a nosedive from 1000 feet, and pretty much impossible to route all of the planes over water.

i've lived near this airport for the past 17 years, and lived the first 17 years of my life practically on top of another airport, and there have been a few times where i said 'yikes' ... but to paraphrase the note on your sideview mirror, objects are not as close as they may appear.
