Title: La Esquina Del Cameron
Post by: cultartist on June 11, 2014, 03:49:01 PM
Been enjoying the outdoor weekend sidewalk ceviche stand on the corner of Roosevelt and 80th for a while now but the newly opened weekday counter inside is fantastic.  I just tried the fish empanada and it blew my mind.  They slice an empanada right there before your eyes and load it up with seafood, avocado, cilantro and onions and it tastes delicious and surprisingly light.  Here's the article I saw yesterday that lured me down, somewhat like those smoke fingers in the old cartoons. 

http://chopsticksandmarrow.com/2014/06/la-esquina-del-camaron-brings-seabass-empanadas-tostadas-to-la-roosie/

Finally made it to La Esquina de Camaron. We planned to get the shrimp and octopus cocktail (it's "Shrimp Corner" after all) but were distracted by the taco options:
