Portuguese Churrascaria on 86th
« Reply #24 on: July 31, 2019, 11:06:19 PM »
I'm referring to the place that opened in the former Greek restaurant on 86th and 37th. It bills itself Portuguese, but it's mostly a wider array of Latin cuisines. We tried it earlier this week and it's pretty good. They grill and roast their meats there, and the piri piri chicken I had was well-seasoned and marinated. The sausage was also very good. Aside from meat, they also have seafood. One nice thing is they sell their meats a la carte -- you can just get a chicken leg, a skewer of shrimp, or a sausage etc. Check them out.
