Title: Re: Recommended Peditricians & Daycare?
Post by: Queenslove on August 07, 2017, 11:41:04 AM
Hi llh-- welcome!

Have you heard of/checked out the JH baby google groups? Basically each season (ex. 'spring babies 2015') there is an informal group formed and they keep in touch via email, sharing tips, meeting up frequently at the beginning when all the babies are young, etc. There is a moderator who can put you in touch with your season's group-- you can search in the JH families yahoo site for her contact or PM me for more info. Our group alone has over 35 families-- its been a priceless resource for us and we've formed such a great little community.

As for pediatricians: We go to Dr. Halac/ Dr. Hate/Dr. Guidera group practice on broadway. We've been mostly happy with them. Some others use Tribeca Pediatrics in LIC.

Hope this helps! And congrats on the baby boy :)
