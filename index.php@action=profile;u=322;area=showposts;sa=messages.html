Real Estate & Home Improvement / Re: Elm Court, Hawthorne Kitchens - is there hardwood under there?
« on: July 27, 2010, 11:40:40 AM »
I can't speak for your building in particular but most pre-war apartments originally had heart of pine floors in the kitchen.  The question is, are they still there or were they ripped up at some point?  Also, if there was tar or glue or grout put on them (i.e they were used as a subfloor to apply tile or other), they might be hard to salvage as pine is one of the softer woods.  I would suggest pulling the fridge or stove out and doing a test spot where no one will see.
