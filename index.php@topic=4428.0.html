I swung by yesterday to take a look at their apartments.  Saw a 2bd / 2bth (listed as 1150ish sq. ft), 1bd and the 3bd/2bth penthouse. 

The apartments are on the smaller side -- my biggest disappointment with the configuration was the lack of any dining space in the units.  The architect just left one room outside of the kitchen to serve as the dining/living room. 

No doorman yet (could happen later if the board decides to make it happen).  Parking is first come first serve at $200/mo.  Storage lockers for a fee (room is still being built out).  There are two large common areas with one on the 2nd floor and the other on the 8th floor. 

Sales are certainly slow with not a single unit closed on yet, but a handful of units are in contract including one of two penthouses.
