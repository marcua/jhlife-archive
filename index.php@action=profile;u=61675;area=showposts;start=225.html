Restaurants & Food / Re: Casa Del Chef Bistro in Woodside
« on: July 10, 2017, 10:27:26 AM »
I can also add to the chorus above that they are very accommodating to vegetarians (I am one). There are always veggie apps and at least one veggie entree, and if you call about a tasting menu when you make a reservation, I believe they will do an all-veg tasting menu for you (as long as they get a heads up in advance, of course). They've had some special vegetarian tasting menu dinner events, too, so it's definitely something they can do.

A wonderful restaurant overall that I hope thrives for many years to come. Warm, excellent service and flavorful, creative food that is truly out of this world.
