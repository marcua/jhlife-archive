Title: The Alleys of Jackson Heights
Post by: itsit on March 22, 2021, 04:43:13 PM
 There are many back channels here in Jackson Heights. Some are fascinating, some are historic and some are god awful. Many kids have learned to ride bikes and skate there. Lot's of basement entrances are popular. Some have a small piece of paradise right behind their homes. Gardens abound. And some nastiness also. Have you explored them? I believe these are public spaces but am not sure.

Title: Re: The Alleys of Jackson Heights
Post by: itsit on March 24, 2021, 08:55:57 AM
 Behind many of the homes in the lower 70's on the streets, not avenues. I know that there are more in the 90's too. Some are great and some are ignored and trashed but I posted this as I have a view of two different ones. In the one, for a year now there is an exercise regime with women and sometimes children in one that they might be too shy to do on open street or playground. The other has lots of basement apartments so a different world happens in that alley which is almost like having a back door in a suburban house with plants and mops hanging, cars can drive right up and it's a first floor or basement existence. Both are very different looking from the street side. A few have really terrific setups and regularly entertain adults and kids.
