Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Restaurants & Food / JH Greenmarket gets a certified organic grower!
« on: December 17, 2010, 10:12:08 AM »
Happy Holidays for Jackson Heights Greenmarket! As our gift to you, we bring you David Rowley, of Monkshood Nursery, located in Styvesant, NY.  He is a certified organic grower who grows luscious greenhouse greens to help us through the lean months of winter. This weekend he is bringing his first cuttings from the greenhouses of baby greens and a small selection of root veggies and winter squashes. David will be at the market in person this week and is a great resource of information about vegetable farming and Organic practices. Come out and give him a warm JH welcome and help entice him to stick it out with us through the winter.

And a quick reminder, the market, located on the corner of 34th Ave and 78th Street every Sunday, is going year round. Come out this weekend and stock up on local treats, sweets, veggies, and meats for all of your holiday entertaining. The market will be closed Sunday December 26 and January 2 so the farmers can spend the holidays with their families but we will be reopen with snow boots and bells on Sunday January 9th and every Sunday thereafter no matter what the weather. Come snow, sleet, or cold, we will all be out there to provide you with the freshest, tastiest local produce anywhere around. Brave the cold and come out this winter to support our first year-round market in Queens!

Jackson Heights Greenmarket
http://www.grownyc.org/jacksonheightsgreenmarket
Sundays, 34th Ave and 78th Street
Closed December 26 and January 2, Reopen January 9

Community Groups, Activities & Events / JH Greenmarket open 'till Dec 19, Pumpkin Composting, & Thanksgiving Turkeys
« on: November 01, 2010, 03:09:48 PM »
Queens Markets Open For Winter!
Jackson Heights and Sunnyside Greenmarkets will remain open until Saturday December 18 and Sunday December 19th respectively. There is also a proposal under consideration to make Jackson Heights a year-round market. A determining factor for the farmers and for Greenmarket will be market attendance in December, so come shop, tell your neighbors and fellow Queens foodies, and let's get our borough its first year-round Greenmarket. The farmers will be there no matter snow, sleet, or chilly temps so come out and keep us company this December. Stock up on all your favorite winter veggies, as well as locally produced bread, wine, cheese, and meat for all your holiday celebrating.

Pumpkin Composting
Don't be haunted by dead Halloween jack-o-lanterns or bury them in the landfill. Let them rest in peace at our compost drop-off events. The following Queens Markets will be accepting Pumpkins and yard debris, including leaves, for composting. Western Queens Compost Initiative (http://www.facebook.com/wqnscompost) in conjunction with Project LeafDrop (http://nycleaves.org/) will be accepting yard waste and pumpkins for compost and will turn them into beautiful soil for next seasons neighborhood community gardens.

Long Island City Greenmarket
Vernon Blvd. and 48th Ave
Wednesday, November 3 & 10, 8am -10am
http://www.grownyc.org/hunterspointlicgreenmarket

Sunnyside Greenmarket
Skillman Ave and 48th Street
Saturday, November 6 & 13, 8am - 12pm
http://www.grownyc.org/sunnysidegreenmarket

Jackson Heights Greenmarket
34th Avenue and 78th Street
Sunday November 7, 10am - 2pm
* Author Reba Linkler will be reading and signing her new children's book Compost Heroes (http://compostheroes.com/)
http://www.grownyc.org/jacksonheightsgreenmarket

Turkey Time!
Hard to believe Thanksgiving is right around the corner! Be prepared with a pre-ordered, local, humanely raised turkey from one of our Greenmarket producers. Come to Jackson Heights Greenmarket , located at 34th Ave and 78th Street, on Sunday November 7th or 14th to order a turkey for pick-up at market the Sunday before Thanksgiving. Order forms are available from Dipaola's Turkey or Hoosic River Poultry at their respective market stands. There are a limited number of birds so sign-up early!


Jackson Heights Greenmarket is located on 34th Avenue between 77th and on 78th between Northern Blvd and 34th Ave every Sunday through December 19. For more information, visit us at http://www.grownyc.org/
