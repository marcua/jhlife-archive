Tried them out, really nice space and staff/owner.  The burger was definitely the best in the neighborhood and on par with other memorable burgers in the city.  The addition of "pickled" avocados and the special sauce was really tasty, as well as the brioche bun to hold it all together.  Look forward to trying their brunch which is starting this weekend and other items on the classic French bistro menu. Lucky to have this in the neighborhood, worth the effort to head a few blocks north...hope they do well.
