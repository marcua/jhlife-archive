Business Listings and Services Directory / childcare option
« on: May 31, 2014, 12:42:25 PM »

Are you looking for an affordable childcare option? Daycare centers and nannies can cost a lot and the cost varies with how many children you have. Au pair childcare has worked well for many families in term of flexibility and cost, the cost is per family, not per child. An au pair works up to 45 hours per week with a schedule that you make for them and it can differ from week to week depending on your needs. An au pair's weekly stipend is just $200 plus room and board and the average weekly cost comes to $360 with agency fees and all the necessary paperwork included.
Au pairs are young men and women from all over the world between the ages of 18 and 26, who all enjoy childcare and want to spend a year or two in the United States sharing their culture. Our au pairs are all CPR certified.
If interested, please email me at nina.gonzalez-lehto at lcc.culturalcare.com or visit my website www.ngonzalez-lehto.aupairnews.com for more information.
