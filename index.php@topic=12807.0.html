It's been like that for awhile now.  I don't buy meat there.  (Trade Fair is like that too - or whatever it's now called.) I just go for quick things.  When they first opened, the fruit and vegetables were amazing - very good quality and plenty of it.  For the past year, they've been sub par and nothing special.  They are also a very small grocery so they only sell one kind of everything - it's very hard to shop healthy there.  They also do not recycle plastic bags.  I used to be a fan, but no longer.  To be honest, the grocery stores here aren't great.  I end up buying things at a bunch of different places, a little at a time.  The greenmarket is nice and some of the fruit/veggie places are decent on 37th.
