Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

The recently opened one around 83rd on 37th is quite good, and has room to walk around.

There are two practically facing on 82nd below the construction site, very popular and can be difficult to navigate. One is Mango Rico which moved - I battle my way through to buy dried apricots for 2.99 a pound. The opposite facing one often has good prices on blueberries.

Downtown Market has a pretty nice selection if you can afford "organic" prices.

Heartily second Kim's Brother, usually has the best price on grapes out front.

Supermarkets generally seem much more expensive.

Sorely miss Growing Farm!
