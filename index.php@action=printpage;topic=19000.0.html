Title: Cop cars illegally parked
Post by: Fedup on May 09, 2019, 11:01:02 PM
Tonight as I was walking home along 37th Avenue, I noticed a cop car was parked in the pedestrian crosswalk on the corner of 37th Ave/83rd Street. It was blocking traffic on one side and blocking the sidewalk curb ramp for wheelchairs and strollers.

At first I thought there was an emergency since the car was parked so strangely and without a care. But then I saw two female officers in Cafe de Colombia drinking coffee and eating cake.

I was livid. I stayed by the cop car and took pictures and then called 311 to make a report. The 311 rep. took down my details but I received the typical "case closed" email less than an hour later.

I watched as elderly pedestrians tried to navigate across 37th Avenue with that cop car parked directly where it SHOULDN'T be.

Can someone please tell me who else I can go to about this issue? This is NOT the first time this has happened. It is arrogant, self-indulgent and dangerous.
