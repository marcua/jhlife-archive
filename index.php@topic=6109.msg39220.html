Good Morning/Afternoon/Evening...

We are currently looking for a roommate to rent the master bedroom of our apartment at 4705 Center Boulevard in Long Island City. The move in date is currently flexible but we are aiming for May 15th and looking for a one year commitment from June 1st. If you are not previously familiar with Long Island City, it's is one stop from Grand Central on the 7 train. The incredible midtown views, neighborhood and building amenities make it well worth the 10 minute commute into the city. The first picture below is the view of Midtown Manhattan from our living room. The last picture is of the sunrise from our balcony looking out into Queens and Brooklyn.


About the Apartment

The apartment is a 3 bedroom, 2 bathroom with all stainless steel appliances, marble counter tops, washer dryer, living room, floor to ceiling windows, balcony and Verizon Fios high speed internet. The master bedroom has it's own private bathroom.


About the Building and Neighborhood.

4705 Center Blvd is a luxury building featuring 24 hour doorman, elevators, state of the art gym (with sauna and steam room), indoors swimming pool, theaters, game room, parking garage, bike storage, dry cleaner and roof deck with BBQ Grills, Bocci and incredible views of Midtown and Brooklyn. The neighborhood is comprised of mostly working professionals and young families and has lots of brand new parks and good restaurants within walking distance At night LIC is extremely quiet since there is very little vehicle traffic by the building. There is also a supermarket and Duane Reade attached to the building.
