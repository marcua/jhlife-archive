39DollarGlasses.com will re-lens frames for you, and are based in Long Island.

I've also used Eyewear Insight, Goggles4U, Firmoo, Zenni, Optical4Less and EyeBuyDirect. Never had a problem with the prescription at any place I've ordered from.

Look at the return policy. For example, Zenni only gives you 50% back, where EyeBuyDirect gives a complete refund (but you pay shipping).
