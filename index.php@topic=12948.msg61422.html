The shower head on the tub and a freestanding shower stall is very common in large 2 bedroom apartments  in Jackson Heights and Forest Hills (but I have never seen this practice in one bedroom apartment). when you have kids, this practice is quite a time saver so that a person can take the shower in the stall at the same time one parent can bath a baby in the tub with the shower head.  This can probably explain why the practice is commonly seen in large 2 bedroom apartments where a family with kids are more likely to occupy.  The two bath room apartments will be a much better options for these families, but the second bathroom demands much more money.
