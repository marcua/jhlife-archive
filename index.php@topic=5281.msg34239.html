Parent & Child KARATE - FREE TRIAL CLASS tomorrow - Sat 9/18 @ 9am
« on: September 17, 2010, 01:48:50 PM »
Joint Karate class for Children, ages 3-7 and their parents.
 
FREE Parent & Child Karate class this Saturday at 9:00-9:50am for all new parent-child pairs!
THIS Saturday 9/18  @ MKD School of Karate on Roosevelt Ave & 73rd St.
RSVP for your spot.
This is a small class, so there is limited space.
 
Our Parent & Child Karate class is designed to enhance and focus on you & your child. We are one of the very few programs that offer you and your young child the opportunity to workout, learn, grow and have fun together.
 
Our School & Program offers:
- Quality joint karate class
- Small class with a positive family environment and positive interactions with and for your child
- For all beginning levels - Come as you are!
- No long-term contracts, month-to-month with discounts for 3-month signups.
- Affordability
 
Why a joint class - for you, the parent?
* This class is for parents of every age, shape or physical condition. You will gradually find yourself and your child to be stronger, more fit and flexible..
* As parents, we always find that our child is "growing up so fast"! Being in a class setting and with no other distrations, you will be able to share in your child's mental, emotional & physical growth and development.
* This class gives you the opportunity to practice at home together with your child. You both learn martial arts faster and keep spending a great time doing karate, even at home!
* Quality time with your child, while you get a workout too.
* You learn our karate style which blends karate, kickboxing & self-defense.
 
Why a joint class - for your child?
* Some children are not ready to be by themselves in a karate class. This joint class allows them to have the reassurance of their mother or father being there with them and for them.  As they let go at their own pace, do not be surprised if they ask to be in class by themselves!
* Some children very much enjoy being with their parents in a quality setting, learning something new and fun. This allows them to do that fully!
* This is a class where your child sees you in action and models your behavior. Showing them that being healthy is fun and easy. Having a positive self-image is a great gift!
*  Many parents have a legitimate concern of their child being bullied. As this is a karate class, your child will learn basic self-defense moves in the form of punches and kicks. While karate is a peaceful discipline and does not encourage violence, your child will be ready to defend himself if physically challenged by a bully.
* Karate instills and values self-discipline. As your child sees you participating in class, they will understand that it pays to be disciplined as well.
 
Class Format
Every Saturday @ 9:00-9:50am
For children ages 3-7

Instructor
The fun & high-energy class is taught by Sensei Orlando, 3rd degree black belt in MKekaDo Karate. Sensei Orlando has trained in martial arts for over 25 years and taught for more than 15 years to all individuals of all ages and conditions. His teaching ability & method is well-known for being firm yet flexible, and disciplined yet humorous.
