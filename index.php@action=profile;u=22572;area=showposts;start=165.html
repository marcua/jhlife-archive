Neighborhood Chat / Re: Street Parking for residents
« on: November 18, 2012, 11:51:29 PM »
I've lived here with a car for about two and a half months so far at 73rd and 35th. I take the subway to work, so I mostly just move my car once a week or so after I get home from work. The thursday/wednesday blocks between 35th and Northern are easier to find spots. I usually keep looking until I find a spot that just had sweeping that morning, to maximize the time until I have to move it again. If you don't work normal business hours, it's easy to move your car right after sweeping time ends. So far, it hasn't been terrible, but it is a little annoying. If I had to drive everyday I'd find a space to rent.
