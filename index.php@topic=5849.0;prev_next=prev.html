Hey Everyone - I'm having a huge event this Saturday at the Flushing Mall - FEB 19th

Basically it's like a huge pot luck, but instead of bringing food, everyone has to buy $10 worth of food from the food court at the Flushing Mall. I have common tables set up, and we are to graze for 3 hours.

If you don't know already, there are about 10 vendors of Chinese and Korean food at the Food court there. There are so many items to order, the only way I can explore it fully is to have 150 of my friends there. I want you guys there!

We're also having a chili sauce tasting table ($5).

And prizes and game shows to give them away. I have a big stage to have fun with. I'm creating games for the night as we type. (and if you have any contacts to restaurants that want to give away a dinner for 2, let's pursue it!)

More details at http://iwantmorefood.com/flushing-mall-grazing-experience/
and more to come this week on my site.

If you're on Facebook, invite your best friends http://www.facebook.com/event.php?eid=194990077179735
