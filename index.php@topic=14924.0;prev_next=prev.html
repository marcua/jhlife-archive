Teriyaki Factory: Open now
« Reply #21 on: May 09, 2016, 08:26:34 PM »
I wanted some Saw Shack for dinner tonight, but they were closed because of a gas leak according to the sign on their gate. Oceanic Boil was open next door, and looking actively for folks. Looked pretty empty. Not being a big shellfish fan, I kept walking. I was surprised that Teriyaki Factory was open. Someone was handing out samples on the sidewalk. I went in and their menus on video screens were obscured by set up screens. They told me all they were selling was chicken teriyaki and veg + rice for the moment. What I could read of the menu suggested bento boxes and dumplings of a sort, but not an extensive menu.

I took the teriyaki chicken+ veg and rice. It was okay. Big serving for $6.15 I think. I'll go back and try to support them as they get their sea legs. It was really another kind of Saw Shack-ish take on a takeout place. The dining room was sparse, and I couldn't imagine folks really hanging around.
