Title: Re: And again, thought on the Saybrook or Colonial Court?
Post by: Griswold Girl on August 07, 2008, 07:50:05 PM
I live in the sister building to the Saybrook, The Griswold.  I have to say that I do think 34th Avenue one of the most beautiful streets in Jackson Heights.  The planted medians, bike lanes and historic buildings really give it a mini-Park Avenue feel.  In addition, no buses.  If you get an apartment in the back of the building you should have a nice unobstructed view over the garden homes depending which floor you are on.  My friend's parents live in the Saybrook and they seem happy.  They have been there for 20+ years.  Also, there are a few garages nearby and parking is much easier than in the 70s.  Con is you are a little further from the express trains.  However, there is a bus one block away on 35th Ave. which runs every 10 minutes during rush hour and takes you right to the express train station.  Happy Hunting.  How exciting.
