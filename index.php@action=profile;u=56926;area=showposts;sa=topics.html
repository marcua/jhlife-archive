Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Business Listings and Services Directory / Halloween 20% off STOREWIDE
« on: October 27, 2015, 03:22:13 PM »
The ReStore is hosting a Halloween special on Oct 31st. 20% off STOREWIDE. Just print out the Halloween coupons from our facebook page (https://www.facebook.com/habitatnycrestore) and swing by from 9am to 7pm at 62-01 Northern Blvd, Woodside, NY 11377.

There will be prizes for customers and kids in costume. While supplies last.

Who are we?
A ReStore is a retail store concept that accepts donations of overstocked, seconds, used, discontinued items and salvageable building materials donated by manufacturers, stores, contractors and individuals. Items can include new and gently used furniture, building materials, appliances, cabinetry, sinks, countertops, household and décor items. The ReStore then sells those items to the public at a reduced cost, typically 50-80% percent off the original retail value. Donated items qualify for a tax deduction under Habitat NYC’s 501(c)(3) status.

Check out our facebook link https://www.facebook.com/events/1461903030782451/!

About ReStore:
This 3,500 square foot retail space is an affordable home improvement center offering a variety of building materials including appliances, furniture, hardware and other discounted merchandise - typically 50-80% percent off the original retail value. Profits from the store directly support Habitat NYC in its mission to build affordable homes in New York City.
