Chuckster, aren't you the one who did wonders in photoshop the other day?

The reason I ask is because if you have a digital camera, a nice color printer and some way of cropping photos, you could do the photos yourself.  This is what I did for my passport.  As long as you have ink in your printer, the only cost you incur is the approximately $1/sheet for a photo-quality sheet of printing paper though I suppose it's possible you would have to buy an entire box if it's not sold by the sheet.

There is more info here.
http://travel.state.gov/passport/guide/digital/digital_880.html
