Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

C.B. 3 Approves Plan To Add Annex To I.S. 230
by Dave Dal Molin, Chronicle Contributor
04/03/2008

   A plan to add a 390-seat annex and playground to Intermediate School 230 in Jackson Heights was approved by Community Board 3 last Thursday, after a heated debate that brought residents both for and against the proposal to the table.

   I.S. 230 sits in a quiet and scenic neighborhood at 73-10 34th Ave., between 73rd and 74th streets. The proposed playground and annex would be located on two lots that are now up for sale and that sit opposite the school on the north side of 34th Avenue.

   Like many Queens public schools, I.S. 230 currently educates far more students than its facilities were designed to hold, with nearly 1,000 students in a single building designed for a maximum of 756. The school has no outdoor play area, and children are often confined inside.
