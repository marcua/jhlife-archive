Senator Jose Peralta's Foreclosure Prevention Workshop
DATE:  Thursday, July 22
TIME:  4 PM - 8 PM
LOCATION:  37-63C 83rd Street, Basement
Jackson Heights

Unfortunately, the mortgage crisis has not subsided.   With 1 million Americans expected to be in foreclosure this year, many of them  are New Yorkers who are suffering through the bad economy.   The crisis has been compounded because of predatory loans or because people simply did not understand the terms of their mortgages.
 
State Senator Jose Peralta will be hosting a Foreclosure Prevention Workshop to assist New Yorkers who are facing foreclosure.  Representatives from HUD, the banking industries and non profits specializing in financial assistance will be available free of charge for counseling.

Attendees should bring two copies of the following documents:
 
Hardship letter
Two most recent pay stubs
Last two month's bank statements
2008/2009/2010 tax returns
Letters/notices of default or foreclosure
Breakdown of monthly expenses
