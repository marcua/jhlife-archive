Where Did You Eat Last & What Did You Eat?
« on: October 12, 2010, 09:48:12 PM »
What was the last restaurant you patronized and what did you eat.  It doesn't matter if you had a dirty water dog from the corner or a Porterhouse...it's about the experience and your honest opinion.  Good or bad, fancy or a hole in the wall...pictures are welcome and if it is in Jackson Hts and immediate area even better.  
I'll start if I may...Uncle Peters...we started with Manchego Cheese & the Jamon Serrano as well as the grilled Calamari with a super delicious sauce. The Calamari was a hit! :smitten: For the main we had the grilled pork chops and the Entrana steak, all served with veggies. The pork chops were thick and served with a generous amount of gravy but a bit on the saltier side.  I think they could have done with less sauce or none at all, in all fairness I think the cook may have slipped with the salt in the gravy.   The entrana was big enough for two so it made for a slamming breakfast sandwhich   . I'm a Sangria snob so I ordered a small jar, served a glass to the wife and added a glass of Merlot to the jar...Delish! The sangria is fine...just a bit sweet for my personal taste.  I didn't think about it so I didn't take pictures but I will next time I go.

Just came back from the Northfork on Long Island where we had lunch at The Modern Snack Bar.  We started off with perfectly fried fried clam strips for an appetizer.  For our main course we had deep fried soft shell crabs served with a side of pureed yellow turnips and french fried potatoes....the crabs were perfectly cooked, light and crunchy.  We also had the roasted turkey served with a savory dressing, green beans and a homemade cranberry sauce.  For dessert we shared an amazing homemade lemon meringue pie! 
I can see why this diner has been around for sixty years!!!  A perfect meal from quality of food, reasonable price and excellent service.
