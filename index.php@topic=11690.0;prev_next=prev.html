Legends Bar and Grill in Jackson Heights
« on: February 13, 2014, 12:10:45 PM »
Legends bar has been a neighborhood favorite for many years! Patrons young and old enjoy our daily specials! Cheers type atmosphere!! A place for locals to meet and hang!! You may come in a stranger but you leave as a friend!! Staff are friendly and awesome!! Low key, no frills place to unwind and have a good time!
Our delicious wings, burgers, signature sandwiches and authentic Mexican specialties will have you coming back for more. With six plasma TVs, along with a wide variety of draft/bottled beer, Legends provides a fun-filled atmosphere for any college or pro game. Check out our lunch and dinner specials along with our happy hours which is the start to every great night at Legends!!!
Come be a part of the tradition that has kept the neighborhood entertained and well fed for years !!!!!
