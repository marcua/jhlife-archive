News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Queens is the borough in search of an identity only because we are surrounded by the super borough of Manhattan and the former city of Brooklyn ... Queens never even had its own team.  We eventually had the Mets ... not the Queens Mets ... just the Mets.

Brooklyn was a City, Manhattan is the city, the Bronx (no thonks) and Staten Island was only an Island.  Queens is the prettiest of the boroughs ... quiet, prosperous, good schools, hard working people and lotsa of small businesses.  It also seems to be the safest and the quietest with the most trees and gardens except of course for the Botannical Gardens om Manhattan that always has everythng.

But as was pointed out people say ...  "I'm from Brooklyn or New York or the Bronx.  No one says "I'm from Queens."  We 're from Jackson Heights or Forest Hills or Woodside or the Gardens.  I came to Queens (Woodside) as an infant and returned after my divorce to raise my son here.  I was glad to come home.
