A couple of days ago I went into this store because I needed organic milk.  Usually get it at Berry Fresh Farms.

So I haven't been here yet and walk in and think "what a weird store. what an odd assortment of goods."  So I buy my milk and it is one dollar more than everyone else sells it.  That's a huge difference in price.  Organic Valley fat free milk for $4.99 instead of $3.99. 

I bought it anyway because I felt like they needed the business but I won't go back into this store and I'll be surprised if they're still here a year from now.  And a lot of their specialty products are cheaper up on 37th Ave. around 94th Street.
