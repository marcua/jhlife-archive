I think today's trick-or-treaters are mostly knocking on doors of the buildings they live in and also visiting nearby friends' homes.  Most go to the parade and get their treats there and from the neighborhood shops.

It's a bit difficult for kids to enter most buildings around here because of intercom/security systems.  Maybe it's easier to stop by the private homes along some of the the side streets.  In my building, we've gotten very few trick-or-treaters over the years, and those that do come knocking are from neighboring apartments.  On that rare occasion, we might get a few teens that have managed to find their way into the building.
