Getting NYSC to open in JH
« Reply #34 on: February 24, 2008, 10:46:38 PM »
I got permission to copy and paste this from the JH families yahoo group. Please take a moment to click the link and send the feedback if you think having a NYSC in JH would be worthwhile!

I spoke with someone at the corporate offices for NYSC last week and asked
them if they were planning to open up a Jackson Heights location. The guy I
spoke with couldn't find any record of a new JH location, however he
encouraged me to tell people to go to the following link and voice their
interest in NYSC coming to Jackson Heights. More specifically, he said if we
made mention of a particular space (i.e., the old Gladiator Gym Space at
82-24 Northern Blvd), and if the response was big enough, they would likely
come and check it out. There is apparently precedent for NYSC opening up
new locations with a large public response.

This is the link:

https://www.mysportsclubs.com/feedback.htm

Let's let NYSC know that there is a huge demand for a gym in JH and that the
Gladiator space would be a perfect location. Our collective voices have been
effective in the past and could be again.
