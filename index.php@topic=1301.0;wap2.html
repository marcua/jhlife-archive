Rachael Ray launches dog food line

(1/1)

Chuckster:
Food TV celebrity Rachael Ray has launched a new line of dog food called Nutrish.  Proceeds from sales will benefit Ray's animal rescue organization Rachael's Rescue.  The food is being labeled as super premium and does not contain by-products, fillers or artificial ingredients or preservatives.
