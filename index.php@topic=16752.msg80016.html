What's with the filing cabinets?
« on: July 13, 2017, 02:56:16 PM »
For the past year or two, I've noticed that 37th Road between 74th and 75th Streets is always littered with different filing cabinets sitting out on the street. I believe there are people (trying to) sell them, but I can't imagine that selling filing cabinets on a street corner is a very lucrative business.

Recently, I saw that they'd moved some of the cabinets into the old Army/Navy-type store that used to be on the corner of 74th and 37th Rd., while still keeping some on the street. What in the world is the deal with this? I've been really curious about it since I first noticed it a long time ago. Anyone know?
