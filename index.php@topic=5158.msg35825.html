Welcome to Jackson Heights. But here is a word of caution. Your hunt seems to be a contradiction to what I understand most
co-ops want in tenants. Long-term residents who want the building to be sound and stable in all ways for quality of life and
oh yeah, investment. Having subletters is an issue as they usually do not have the stability issue in mind. Also why buy for just
a few years of living here? Co-op boards are all volunteer and the process of approving tenants is a bear of a time commitment
and they loath having to go through it too often especially on the same apartment. Seems like a rental in the area would be
a good move for you at the moment but I sympathize with your situation.
