Quote from: lmaniace on August 11, 2015, 08:57:43 PM
The bioswales are probably going to be installed next year. They’ve been had those white machines that look like Zambonis all over JH the last few months drilling into the ground to determine how well the soil absorbs water. If it absorbs water well and the site is free of underground  infrastructure they most likely will install the bioswale. For those who don’t know, bioswales are a super-powered tree pit designed to take a lot of the rain water that otherwise goes down sewers often overloading sewage treatment plants resulting in untreated sewage going into nearby waterways. City is required by court order to do this because it doesnt’ comply with federal clean water rules. City opted for green infrastructure because it’s less expensive than sewage treatment plants which are very hard to find acceptable locations for. They are very cool and as Itsit and Sarah noted coming to JH in pretty good numbers.
