Restaurants & Food / Re: Lemon Farm on 78/37th ave thoughts?
« on: January 06, 2014, 03:55:11 PM »
I typically buy at the Growing Farm (?) one block down; on average they have cheaper and better quality produce. I always get the names confused...Lemon Tree and Growing Farm? The Lemon tree is great for fancy canned goods, cheese, bulk coffee, dried fruit/nuts, etc. They seem to have the biggest selection of "gourmet" products in the neighborhood. My grocery routine is Lemon Tree for speciality items, Growing Farm for produce and Met food for everything else.
