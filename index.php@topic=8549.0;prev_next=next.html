Female needs FEMALE roommate ASAP!

Room available in a 1 bedroom apartment in Jackson Heights. Rent is $795 (deposit is the same amount). You would have your own room in the living room area, which can easily be converted to a bedroom (I can have a flex wall or curtain put up -- your choice). The apartment is located on 81st St and is just a block away from the 82nd St subway station (7 train) and 6 blocks away from the 74th St-Roosevelt Ave station (E, F, M, R, 7). The building is quiet and it's a good and safe area overall. Very quiet street. The building has an elevator with a laundromat 2 blocks away; laundry pickup/delivery service available. The super is very responsive and there is monthly extermination so no problems with pests of any kind.

We will pay gas and electricity (usually around $30 per person). I will also be having high speed internet installed once I move in (mid July) so that should be about $35 a person. Rest of the utilities are included in the rent.

The apartment is available NOW so you can move in any time. I am looking for someone who will stay at least 3 months but would prefer longer.

As you can see from the photos, the room is quite spacious. There is one bathroom next to it which we would share and the kitchen is just through the doorway. It has a dishwasher and is very modern; lots of cabinet space. There's plenty of closet space in the apartment (3 total). You would get one closet to yourself and another we would share, while I would have the 3rd to myself.

About me: I'm a 28 year old female who works as a web designer in a publishing company. I'm a very quiet person and don't have crazy parties or tons of people over all the time. The apartment allows pets; I don't have any, but am fine with cats. I am non-vegetarian but rarely cook meat at home (I only bake salmon maybe about once a week, if even that) so if you're vegetarian you don't have to worry about that very much. Hope you don't mind Indian cooking as that's mostly what I cook I am by no means a neat freak but would hope that the apartment remain reasonably clean. This means not leaving trash or dirty dishes all over the kitchen and taking turns with cleaning and taking out the garbage. I have a boyfriend who comes over a couple times a week and will sometimes sleep over 1 night on the weekend. We are both quiet and respectful people and would never disturb you, especially if it's late at night. No smokers please (unless you only smoke outside). And finally, you should be a nice, normal, sane person! It would be great if we could be friends and hang out sometimes, but if you'd prefer to keep to yourself, that's fine too!

If you want to see the apartment, contact me and we can set up a time to meet. I need someone to move in ASAP!

Hope to hear from you soon
