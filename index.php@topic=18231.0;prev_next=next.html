News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Brooklyn refugees
« on: September 05, 2018, 10:02:44 AM »

I moved to JH eight years ago from Brooklyn in search of a better deal. I got one! I love my apartment and my neighborhood. Lately I've seen an influx of young families in particular, looking for that extra bedroom for a child. Occasionally I look at the site Brownstoner to keep up with developments in the former borough. This morning I was struck by this entry. I haven't seen JH featured here before. Yes, there will be more refugees from that lovely but now quite pricey borough of Kings. You can count on it. I hope the newcomers come to appreciate the mix of people here and work to make JH even more wonderful than it already is. 


https://www.brownstoner.com/real-estate-market/queens-apartments-for-sale-jackson-heights-douglaston-working-fireplace/
