The Life and Times of Reinaldo Arenas, Author of "Before Night Falls" 

In commemoration of the anniversary of the death of Reinaldo Arenas, the author of "Before Night Falls" ["Antes Que Anochezca"], fellow artists and academics who knew him will gather to reminisce and discuss one of the most famous Hispanic authors of recent years.
