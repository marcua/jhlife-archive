Title: ACSM GUERRILLA ARTS ENSEMBLE: Ackbar Percussion Quartet at Flushing Town Hall
Post by: FCCA on March 14, 2012, 02:25:48 PM
Flushing Town Hall
137-35 Northern Boulevard
Flushing, New York 11354

ACSM GUERRILLA ARTS ENSEMBLE: Ackbar Percussion Quartet

Sunday, March 18, 2012, 2:00 PM
Suggested Admission: $5 / Members & Students Free

The Ackbar Percussion Quartet (APQ) was founded at Queens College during the summer of 2011, with the intent of recording Daniel Levitan's piece, "Conservatory Garden". Since then, the APQ has performed multiple new music initiatives including various educational concerts and community outreach programs throughout New York City. The APQ members extend their gratitude to professor Michael Lipsey at the Aaron Copland School of Music, Queens College for being a supportive coach and mentor throughout the quartet's existence.

Conveniently located on Northern Boulevard in Downtown Flushing, a block and half east of Main Street, FTH is easily accessible by subway and bus; we offer free parking for our patrons. For tickets and more information, visit: www.flushingtownhall.org, or call the Box Office: (718) 463-7700, ext. 222.
