Soundproofing is certainly one option, but you may want to consider reducing the noise as well.  Adding a pair of inexpensive speakers close to the couch/sofa will let you hear more of of the audio (especially if you can extend the center channel where most dialog occurs), while setting the TV at a lower volume.  You could also adjust the bass since the lower frequencies travel in all directions - often through walls and floors.
