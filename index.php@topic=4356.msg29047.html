Just had dinner at the new Pio Pio across Northern Blvd.  Very disappointing. The decibel level was so high, that conversation was a strain and the place was only half full.  The food was the same as before the move but I could practically count the number of beans they served with the rice...odd how few there were.

The place is just cavernous with nothing welcoming or intimate like the old one.  Even Pio Pio to Go now has more ambience than the new place.  I hope the owners make some changes to do something about the noise - like installing half high walls to divide the room or something to absorb the sound.  The enormous glass windows and concrete floors make it feel very cold.

Looks like it will be takeout for me from now on...

theres a new pio pio?  what is the address?

The new Pio Pio is on the south side of Northern Blvd., directly across from its original location.  As described above, it's a large room, and it appears that it may offer some limited outdoor seating during warmer weather months.  There's a space toward the back of the restaurant that overlooks the small outdoor area, and diners can look out though large windows.  Also, the new location has a downstairs room for private parties.  I was at the restaurant recently and also noticed that there was a small band setting up on the main floor, so they seem to offer live music now as well.

By the way, the original 2 floor location has officially closed, but the smaller Pio Pio Express located on the corner remains open.
