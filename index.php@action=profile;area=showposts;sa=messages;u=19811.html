Neighborhood Chat / Re: Summer Camps options and Superkickers?
« on: August 30, 2012, 03:53:13 PM »
I just wanted to follow up on this thread to say that we did go with Superkickers.   It was the most flexible option, and even though it was a commute from Astoria we decided it was worth it. 

I sent my 2.5 year old twice a week from 9am to 3pm and it was great.  He loved the activities and the counselors.  I also liked the counselors who would give me a daily update when I came to pick up my son at the end of the day.   The days are  structured with activities planned every 1/2 hour, but they are flexible if a kid needs a nap or a snack outside of the normal planned times for those activities.   

What really impressed me was the warmth and care the counselors have for the kids there - the owner/manager is a mom herself and has alot of empathy for the kids and the parents of the kids who come to her camp.   

To sum it up - yay Superkickers!   We had a great summer thanks in part to them.   Just wanted to put in a little plug for them since I couldn't find any other reviews for them when I was looking for information at the beginning of the summer (other than this thread).

Neighborhood Chat / Summer Camps options and Superkickers?
« on: June 22, 2012, 08:11:26 AM »
Hello Everyone - I'm from Astoria but joining this board because I'm searching for summer day camp options for my son (toddler age) in Astoria, Jackson Heights and Woodside.  I'm a member of the Astorians board.

In my search I found what looks like a great option in Jackson Heights called Superkickers.  They do soccer and music instruction during the year and are based out of PS 69 on 37th Ave.     Their camp is very flexible in terms of schedule (I can do a 1/2 day twice a week and add more days any time I want for instance).   I went to visit their facility at PS 69 yesterday and they seem very professional and established.   My only concern is that I can't find any reviews of their services anywhere.   Otherwise, the only drawback is the commute out to JH from Astoria in the mornings.     
Does anyone on this board have any experience with them?   I do see they are mentioned in a few posts on this board.
