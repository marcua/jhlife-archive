Landscaping can be quite costly (we do our own) but for fencing be aware NOT to contract Medallion security. They did our front and back fencing and left a total mess, trashed our yard, left food, bottles, broken glass, wires, nails, holes, cigarettes etc etc. and they trashed our neighbors yard and plants, had the wrong measurements so had to keep coming back. They guaranteed their work and the back fenced has broken numerous times (they don't answer our phone calls)  Just a warning. When we were getting gardening quotes we got a $500 initial then $100 a month (and our garden is nice) so if you need alot of weeding etc its going to cost you.
