Anybody have any suggestions for low calorie dressings for salads and sauces or toppings for vegetables?  I haven't liked the no fat salad dressings I've tried and I don't want to put butter on my vegetables.  Salad dressings and butter seem to defeat the purpose of eating salads and vegetables.

Eddie,

I don't have a recipe exactly, but one way to make your steamed veggies taste better is to toss them in a tiny bit of olive oil, then add a tablespoon of grated cheese & a bit of salt/pepper.

There are also salad dressing sprays or spritzes that are on the market.  I've never tried them but they seem popular with people who are trying to cut back on calories. http://lowfatcooking.about.com/od/salad1/gr/saladspritzers.htm

Maybe you could make your own and fill an empty sprtizer bottle.

Similar to Shelby's suggestion, I steam my veggies, add a bit of olive oil and then shake some Trader Joe's 21 Seasoning Salute for added flavor.  It's a salt free dry mix of varied herbs and spices.  I've also roasted asparagus, tossing the spears in a tiny amount of olive oil and dried oregano and cracked black pepper.  Very quick and yum!
