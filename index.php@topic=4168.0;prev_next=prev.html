I know some coops in JH have bicycle rooms/bike storage areas for use by residents - it's something we're thinking about for our building.

We have the racks in the same room as storage lockers at $35 per bike per year.
Sadly, there is nothing you can do to make people ride their bikes more. But if you make sure people are paying their annual fees for the bikes that are down there, there is a natural incentive for people to clear them out if they are not using them. If they don't pay, you sell them. "Vintage" used bikes sell for good money these days on craigslist.
