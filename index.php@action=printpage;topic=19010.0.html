Title: Free Concert This Thursday, 5/16: Touring Americana Artist Jenny Van West @E77
Post by: ptbass75 on May 13, 2019, 03:15:39 PM
Internationally acclaimed and chart topping Americana artist Jenny Van West kicks off a whirlwind 3 day/4 venue NYC tour this Thursday, 5/16 at Espresso 77.  Backed by a Queens-based band, the Outer Borough Boys (Jonny Meyers, Paul Trust and Seth Goldberg), this show promises one not to be missed!  Showtime is from 8:30-10:45PM.

With a crystalline voice that has been characterized as “Somewhere between Aimee Mann and Bonnie Raitt,” Portland, ME-based singer-songwriter Jenny Van West effortlessly fuses folk, rock, swing, and country.  Winner of the 2015 Maine Songwriters Association songwriting contest (“Nellie”), Jenny has the ability to write songs that sound like instant classics.  Returning for her third performance at E77, Van West is currently on tour in support of her internationally acclaimed album Happiness To Burn (April 2018). 

Jenny Van West-Guitar/Vocals
Backed by The Outer Borough Boys:
     Jonny Meyers-Guitar
     Paul Trust-Bass/Vocals
     Seth Goldberg-Drums
