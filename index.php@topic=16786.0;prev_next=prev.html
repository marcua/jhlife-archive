5k with demo and garbage. One day to demo and garbage.  One day to PLUM the walls and potentially re-rock everything (its all about what you are going for and how bad your bathroom is) The tiling could be 1 to 3 days depending on how much tiling you are doing...floor to ceiling, around the tub, half walls, borders, etc.   Then you have your grout which is an hour to two and are your doing floors. The price will also go up a bit if you need to use your bathroom everyday since the guys have to spend an hour a day making your unit useable. 

Price goes up quickly if you are gutting the bathroom and/or using expensive Italian or custom tiling.  But generally, you have 4 to 10 man days (1 man in one day = 1 man day, 2 men in 1 day - 2 man days)

Good luck!
