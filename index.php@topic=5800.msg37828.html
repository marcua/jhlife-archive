Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

The Q47 is in no way being eliminated.

But.. 2 DOT projects are likely to cause 2 reroutes.

1) 77th Street from 31st Avenue to Astoria Blvd. will not all be 2 way anymore, there will be a portion between 24th Avenue and Astoria Blvd. that will become one way thus preventing the Q47 from running a 2 way operation on 77th Street.

2) The proposal of the transportation study indicate that 75th Street will be reversed from 37th Avenue to 31st Avenue, making it NORTHBOUND, for Q47 operation, and possibly also Q49 operation.  Note 75th Street is 2 way from 37th Avenue to Woodside Avenue.

Personally, I have grown used to the fact that buses have been going down my street for my entire life, and likely 80 years historically as far as I have researched.  But I would welcome the reprieve.
