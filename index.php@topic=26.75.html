Yay FoxyWiles!  I'm so glad you agree.  

I was just there again on Saturday and got some pretty blue toenail polish at the end of my kickass pedi.  The girl I had this time was different - I don't know what her name is but she's a young college student studying architecture - very sweet and she did a great job.

I also got my eyebrows waxed and that was done very well.  This woman puts on false eyelashes that can last a few weeks for $20!  If I had a special occasion coming up, I'd totally do it.

The only thing I don't like is one of the hairstylists comes off as super-desperate and kept asking me if I wanted a haircut (even the day I made the appt!).  I was nice the first few times and not so nice the last few.  Her behavior was totally unprofessional, and I could tell her continued interruptions made the girl doing my pedi a little uncomfortable.  And seriously... I don't know why she thought she could talk me into getting a haircut by being so obnoxious.  I have my own hairdresser that I've been going to for years.  There was never a chance.

I still love the place and will continue to go back.  And again, yay for fun nailpolish colors!!
