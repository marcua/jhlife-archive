I missed some of the comments looking to refinish cabinets instead of redoing them.  I did a project before at my husband's old coop.  They were laminate cabinets even but with sanding them down and using a rustoleum white paint, we managed to transform the space under $500 (including a dishwasher w/ installation from Craigslist).

Here's what we did (see photos)
- repainted inside cabinets & cabinet doors
- added updated door handles (changed the whole look!)
- updated the flooring to this wood look rubberized tile from Home Depot
- used featherlite concrete to refinish countertops, this was tough to do

We don't DIY that much and aren't very very handy but I'd say it was fairly moderate task with good results.  It probably took us 2 weekends to finish.

Feel free to ask me if you have any questions on how it's done.
