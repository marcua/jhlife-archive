Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Now I have a bunch of questions, would appreciate input:

1. Does anyone know which kinds of display of public support are the most effective? Just signatures?
What's the minimum we should we aim for?

2. Do we need to get those signatures in a specific timeline (like, in three months after we officially launch a campaign?

3. Is there an "ideal" time of the year to launch this kind of campaign?

4. I think that we should do these steps:

  • determine the precise goals of the campaign
  • write a petition
  • prepare information material
  • launch a campaign to get media attention, signatures, public official and business support
  • submit copies to the community board, DOT and council members and other stakeholders
  • Keep campaigning till it's implemented.

BUT I have no experience on this. Is this really how we should do it? Do you have more experience or better ideas? Can you share? Thanks!
