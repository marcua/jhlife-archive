I went once last summer, not on opening weekend. I also went earlier in the day because it was early in the season, and it's cold after 5 pm most of the time. The crowds weren't that bad.

What was disappointing to me last year was it just wasn't that much to do other than eat. There were food stands, and a couple places selling some uninteresting tchotckes, and that was really it. There was a spot for performing, and a dance troop briefly performed. But it's not like there was a stage (even a simple one). The images used to promote the Night Market make it look a lot more lively. It was fine for me because I went by myself and tried a couple different food vendors. But I thought if I was trying to make a family night of it or as something to do with friends, there wasn't really much to do other than eat. I didn't go back, and it never occurred to me to suggest going there with friends.

Is it better this year?
