Re: Juju's Bagel Cafe

Stopped by on the way home from work last night to get a sample.  I ordered egg salad on a garlic bagel.  I was dumb and got it toasted when I totally shouldn't have! (PS It takes awhile for them to toast stuff)

The proprieters were very nice and I really enjoyed the egg salad and wish I got to try the bagel "naked".

While I was there a man came in to let them know that the cinnamon raisin bagel he got earlier in the morning was "Salty."  Definitely a very valid concern to bring  to the owners attention (and they were very appreciative and gave him some freebies) but the guy REALLY would not stop going on about it, repeating himself over and over (I think he said the bagel was salty about ten times) and then talking about several other bagel places across the city and "they do this and this one does that" blah blah blah.  I seriously felt bad for the owners having to listen to it - they were trying so hard to be nice though the guy's story had no point after the first minute.  It's a small place and the owners have their vision for what they want it to be.  No need to try to make them feel bad because they're not a huge chain that has 5000 topping options etc.    Not to say the guy was a big meanie, he just really, really liked the sound of his own voice. lol

I did learn that they do not make their own dough because they don't have the room for it.  The bagels are boiled.  The owners are very nice, VERY patient people. LOL

Super nice people, especially the man who seems to be the boss/head of the family. Had a decaf cappuccino (which you will only get at Espresso 77, Starbucks or Dunkin, but not at Carollo, Lety or La Gran Uruguaya/La Nueva) for only $2. Even though I love Espresso 77's coffee, especially cappuccino, and their staff, when I feel like going somewhere else or simply saving a buck, I will definitely go to Juju. Other than that, they have their own cake and other deserts that Espresso unfortunately doesn't. They decorated the place in a cute, inviting fashion and considering how nice and professional they are, it definitely will be one of my hangouts. Good luck, guys!
