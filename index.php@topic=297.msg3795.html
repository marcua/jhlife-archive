Fairway Market and Queens?
« on: January 06, 2008, 03:26:23 PM »
I stopped by for some shopping earlier today at the Fairway in Red Hook.  Before leaving, I had to visit the customer service desk where I happened to notice a delivery service sign.  As I casually mentioned to my husband that I was pretty sure they didn't deliver to our place, the woman attending the desk asked if we lived in Brooklyn.  I said no...Queens.  She said, "Oh...then you'll be happy to know that Fairway will be opening another market in Queens."  She went on to explain that they had not narrowed down the location yet, but were strongly considering either Astoria or Five Towns.  If this indeed happens down the road, I would much prefer an Astoria location. :rockon:

Has anyone heard any info on this?
