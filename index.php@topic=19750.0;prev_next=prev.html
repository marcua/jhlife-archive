Good evening everybody. As we are trying to cope with these extraordinary times in which we live, my roommate and I have taken to extensive cooking. I am Italian-American (my mother is from Verona) with extensive experience living in Mexico, and my roommate is half-colombian, half-Venezuelan, with experience working in high-end restaurants around NYC. Yesterday we kicked around the idea of giving digital cooking lessons and doing local delivery of home-cooked food. Within 24 hours, we already accumulated a handful of orders for both. So, I wanted to pitch this to the community here. As of right now, here is our menu for delivery:

- Northern Italian style meat sauce with Ground Turkey trot Pancetta
- Bean and Kale Stew
- Caldo de Gallina
- Chicken Chorizo with Risotto Milanese
