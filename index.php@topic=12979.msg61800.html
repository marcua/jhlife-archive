Taste Microwbrews, Momo Burgers and Paneer Sticks at Tibetan Restaurant

JACKSON HEIGHTS — A local Tibetan restaurant is swapping out its usual beers for a curated list of microbrews that will accompany a new late-night menu of snacks including momo burgers, fried paneer sticks and hunks of meat that are meant to soak up the booze.

Husband-and-wife team Tenzin Choenyi and Lobsang Choephel, both 26, opened Little Tibet on Roosevelt Avenue in 2012, and offer a small Tibetan menu alongside popular beers including Bud and Amstel Light.

But they wanted to try something different, collaborating with friend and local food tour guide Jeff Orlick to feature a rotating list of specialty beers that will be swapped out monthly starting in March.

So, the burger ---- it doesn't even taste "ethnic". It's just a seriously good burger. I try not to interfere with the food stuff bc the chef there is super smart and talented... and even things I look at a little askew I bite my tongue. For this, he is using momo meat. And to me, a hamburger purist, I was always taught that the burger should be just pure meat, no add-ons other than salt and maybe pepper. Something like they do is what I would normally regard as even meatloaf, having onion, garlic, salt, oil in the pattie.... But it's realllly good.  He does the crispy, battered onions and does a homemade mayo. Even homemade mayo I'm skeptical of - I am so loyal to Hellman's - but damn, it tastes just like real mayo. Also he put cilantro on there in place of lettuce. damn good.


When is it going to be ready? They can't just whip up the late night menu because everything is made fresh (something that almost all the tibetan places do here), so I think March 1st is going to be the date they start. Will be in full swing by next weekend.


The beer thing... it's going to help them stay in business longer. Happy to help. If you want these places to stay in business, you have to be there and bring your friends. Personally, I'm learning more about the industry and meeting some very interesting people, drinking a lot more beer. I'm probably driving the distributors crazy b/c I'm ordering in small amounts, but yeah that's how it's going to be - drink more and we'll run out quicker and we'll get new beers. Also, they have all this stock from the old collection of brews like heineken, budweiser, etc which is on all sale now. [/size]Please drink the budweisers so we can make room for more.
