I guess there is probably an empathy gene missing in my baseball DNA but this Yankee fan cannot muster any hometown loyalty for the Mets. My baseball hometown is The Bronx and anything that extends beyond River Avenue is enemy territory as far as I'm concerned.For a Yankee fan to support a team with pretensions to being the" real New York team" seems incomprehensible and generous to a fault.Baseball is a Darwinian struggle for supremacy and dominance. I expect as much from Mets fans regarding the Yanks and that is usually what I get (try listening to WFAN during the season for confirmation). 

Admittedly there is a thorny ethical dilemma of what to do when the RedSox play the Mets, a question I have not as yet resolved. The nearest I come to being a "hometown fan " is when it comes to the the Giants and Jets who I can support with equal if perhaps tepid interest.

So roll on World Series and lets get to Spring training 2016 when the next season begins and my baseball interest returns
