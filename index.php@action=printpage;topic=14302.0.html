Title: Norwich Meadows Winter Share at Farm Spot CSA
Post by: sarahchurch on January 24, 2016, 08:41:02 PM
Great news! We just heard from Norwich Meadows Farm that they have capacity for us to add shares for our upcoming 2016 Winter Shares!

We are accepting applications until February 2nd for Norwich Meadows Farms Winter Shares (5 Distributions). The Norwich shares will run for 5 weeks from February 18th to May 12th.

Meat Share
Some examples of what you will find in your share include: Yogurt, Meat (ground beef) or Chicken, Spring Garlic, Spinach, Dry Beans, Onions, Eggs, Carrots, Potatoes.

Ovo/Lacto Share
Some examples of what you will get from this distribution include: Yogurt, Eggs, Garlic, Beets, Potatoes, Cheese, Spinach, Radishes, Granola, Dry Beans, Turnips, Cabbage.

Vegan Share

Some examples of what you will find in your share include: Carrots, Maple Syrup, Flour, Carrots, Beets, Shallots, Potatoes, Spring Onion, Spring Garlic, Dry Beans.

All shares are $265.00 and amounts are adjusted based on the cost of each item.

Distribution Dates for Norwich Farms- Feb 18th, March 10th, March 31st, April 21st and May 12th

Click here http://csa.farmigo.com/join/farmspot/winter2016 to sign up for a Norwich Farm Share

The volunteer requirement for Winters Shares is one two hour distribution shift.
