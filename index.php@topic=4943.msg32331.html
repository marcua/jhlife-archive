Sangria...while you will rarely find a more agreeable subject here I don't think many places make it well consistently.  Once in a while i get the urge to eat "orejitas de puerco".  I used to eat them in PR and the Bronx but I don't know any place here in Queens...(the old school cuchifritos do not exist in Queens any longer).  If i get the urge for my orejitas (a handful of times per year) I will make a stop at razor blues (Spaniard tapas bar on Northern Blvd).  I always ask for sangria and if the bartender doesn't get it right she will make it right.  If you think it needs to be sweeter she'll add some triplesect...if you want if more fruity or more manly (that's me...he-he) she will tweak it. 

Pio Pio's is too sweet for me but I recommend you buy 2 glasses of merlot and add it to yhe jarra and BAM ...Sangria maximus (well, a much more paletable for my taste that is)
