I just placed Talk of the Town on my must go to restaurants.  This is the first time I've heard about this restaurant.  I've been going to an Indian Chinese Nanking at 72-23 37th Avenue for lunch.  I really like this place with a special lunch price and you have 40 choices.  I especially like Chilli Shrimp, Chilli Chicken, Basil Chicken.  Lots of vegetarian choices. It comes with soup and jasmine rice.  I recommend the hot & sour soup without the chicken. The Mughlai menu has no soup but you have either naan or roti and can have either basmati rice or pulao.  Very nice atmosphere and service.  Friendly staff.  Served Monday to Friday from 11:30 to 3:30.  They have buffet on the weekends.

I went last night with my friend and was very impressed with the food and service.  We got a very flavorful appetizer, it was thai with paneer and vegetables.. can't remember the name, but it was #8 on the vegetarian appetizers list.  My main entree, veg. chilli (on vegetarian entrees list) was very good.  My friend found the one "common" dish on the menu--chicken lo mein, and she liked it.  Dessert was complimentary--some sort of rice pudding that I believe had rice noodles in it instead of rice?  It was good as well.  We sat there for 4 hours talking, and never felt bothered or rushed out.  Besides continually filling our water glasses, and asking how the food was, we were left alone until we decided to leave.  I'll definitely be going back.
