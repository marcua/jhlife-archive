There isn't one right now. However, one is in the works, thanks to years of work by many in the community. It's planned to be built on the north side of 35th Avenue at 69th Street, under the rail overpass. There's an existing thread on this site with much more detail: http://www.jacksonheightslife.com/community/index.php?topic=1017

Also, I've created a site to serve as an information clearing house (and possible dog park info/event site, if it's wanted) here: http://jhdogs.ning.com/

Currently we take our dog to the park in woodside (but there's rarely anyone there) in Doughboy park, or up to Laguardia Landing Lights park (open fields, no fence - but an official off-leash-after-9PM NYC Park)
