Share info:
Vegetable share
$574.60
This is the standard 26-week membership share. This share contains 6 to 10 items each week, depending on the season and availability. Shares may include items such as: In spring: Arugula, Lettuce, Radishes, Strawberries, Sugar Snap Peas, Swiss Chard, Beets. In summer: Watermelon, Potatoes, Garlic, Red Tomatoes, Basil, Green Beans, Eggplant, Zucchini In fall: Fennel, Potatoes, Lettuce, Broccoli, Sweet Potatoes, Carrots, Toscano Kale, Winter Squash, Celeriac.

The amount is enough to feed a family of three to four for a week.

Many Farm Spot members split one share between two households. Please note that members organize this on their own and Farm Spot does not coordinate half shares.
