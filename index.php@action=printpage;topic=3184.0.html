Title: ~free~AFRICAN DANCE WORKSHOP next sunday, August 23rd
Post by: leypo on August 17, 2009, 02:14:42 PM

(FREE) AFRICAN DANCE WORKSHOP IN TRAVERS PARK!!
~for the adults and children of Jacskon Heights~

SUNDAY, AUGUST 23rd

Adults Class: 10:15am-11:15am  Baseball/Tennis Court in Travers Park
Children Class: 11:30am-12:00pm***  PLAY STREET (78th between 34th+Northern)

Come and join Michel Kouakou, a local artist, dancer/choreographer from
the Ivory Coast for a fun and free dance workshop at Travers Park.
There is no previous experience necessary in dance whatsoever.
Just Bring yourself in comfortable clothes with a towel and water.
There will be live drums, a lot of energy, and a lot of fun!

***For the children's class, a minimum age of 5 is recommended,
but younger kids are welcome to watch and join with the
assistence of an adult.

Michel Kouakou is a 2007 Artist and Fellowship recipient of the New York Foundation
of the Arts (NYFA).  This presentation is co-sponsored by the Artist and Audience
exchange, a NYFA Public Program.  As a performer and choreographer, Michel has
presented his work in the US, Europe, Africa and Japan and has 15 years of experience
teaching modern and traditional dance to adults and children.
