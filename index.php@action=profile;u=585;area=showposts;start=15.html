BINGO! I hate calling restaurants to order. Too much room for error.

Quote from: eddie on March 16, 2016, 05:21:01 PM

Like it or not, more and more people want to order food online. Instead of complaining and being angry at your customers about it... ADAPT.

Alot of places, especially here in JH, if you call to order by phone, they don't even speak english, or speak it very poorly. Or you get put on hold for way too long. Not to mention noise and misinterpretation is also an issue.

I used to call in orders to Pio Pio every so often, and it was a pain because I often got someone who spoke poor english, they were very busy, or there was too much background noise. But they rather be angry about it vs actually adapting and realizing that online delivery is where things are headed.
