You might be interested in the Google map Pedometer, which lets you draw a route and it will caculate the walking distance.  You could draw a route in advance, or do it after you come home to find out how far you really walked.
The strange thing about this system is that apparently doesn't let you input an address - you just have to keeping zooming in on the map until you find your starting point.  But it still works and it's pretty easy.
http://www.webwalking.com/googlemap.htm

I was over in Rego Park today and had printed out a map to walk back along the shortest route, but I found the subway beckoning to me after only a few blocks on Queens Blvd.
