Stopped by for a Friday night fish dinner. Had the Salmon tacos and the Shrimp tacos. Both were fresh and tasty. I did not like something in the Salmon tacos, but the Salmon itself was delicious. The shrimp tacos were delicious. We started with the Guac and Chips which were actually tostones instead of chips, with enough greens on the plate that the side salad we ordered went forgotten. No liquor license yet, so BYO for now. As someone else mentioned, the bakery is no more, but they still serve breakfast and lunch and have delivery. No other customers, so please stop by the new location if you want a tasty bite to eat.
