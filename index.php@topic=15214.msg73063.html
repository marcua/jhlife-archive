Sexual assault (groping) on 84th and 37th
« on: August 25, 2016, 07:32:26 AM »
A heads up: earlier this morning, at around 5:45am, my wife was groped while walking our dog on 84th street between 35th and 37th aves (east side of street, closer to 37th ave--in front of the houses). He snuck up from behind her and didn't say a word. It was still dark out and, after screaming at the top of her lungs, the guy fled into a parked light-colored (white or gray) sedan on the block, which he then sped away in. She didn't get a good look: maybe 25-40, average height and build, Hispanic. She plans to file a police report later, although she has spoken to a police officer camped out in one of those mobile stations.

She doesn't expect much to come from the report since her description of the guy is so vague, but it can only help. And maybe, at the very least, it'll encourage some city effort to better light that block.
