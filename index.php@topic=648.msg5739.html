News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

Been busy outside in the sun and cleaning up from the recent construction work.

Might stay up to try to have LW for the day. Might crash.

Gardening yesterday. Sooo good for the spirit! We made a little pathway with reclaimed bricks. No money involved, just spirit and sweat.
The chives, lemon thyme, parsley, lemon balm, oregano, pineapple mint, valerian (looks good, not useful) and worm wood all survived. We think the tarragon did, too, and maybe the lavender.

Strawberries have blossoms on them.

Basil planting and different kind thyme in the the near future.

Yay, Spring!

PS The garden is public, over at Saint Mark's on the 82nd St. side, to the left of the door. Feel free to pick herbs judiciously, or, better, come and dig with us!
