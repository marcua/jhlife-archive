We were huge ERT fans and really devastated to see it close.  We went to check out the new location this morning. 

Happy to report Victor was there and so were his empanadas.  He said everything happened really quickly and he just found his new space 3 days ago.  It seems like he took over an existing Colombian bakery; for now he doesn't have the ERT menu (we love the huevos divorciados!) it's just a steam table and bakery but he said over the next week he will be bringing his equipment over from the old place and he hopes to have the ERT menu up and running within a few weeks.

Title: Re: La Esquisita - El Rico Tinto on Hampton St
Post by: palomita on October 07, 2018, 08:15:47 AM
ljr - 83rd St essentially turns into Hampton St about half a block south of Roosevelt (or rather it forks into Hampton and Baxter which runs along the east side of the hospital).  La Esquisita is right there after 83rd turns into Hampton, it is very close to Roose.

It's actually only 1 block from Terraza 7, Hampton runs parallel to Gleane (1 block south/west).

If you are in the 80s the new location really isn't any farther than the old location, but if you are in the mid/low 70s it is more of a trek.
