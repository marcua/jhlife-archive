There are NYSCs in Astoria, Forest Hills, Rego Park, Sunnyside, Whitestone and at the Atlas Park Mall.  I don't think Forest Hills is the best for parking.  It always seems kind of busy and congested around there, but maybe there is a municipal lot I don't know about.  Astoria and Sunnyside have municipal lots nearby.  Rego Park has some metered parking on Queens Blvd.  Never been to the one in Whitestone but I think it might have a free lot.  The one at the Atlas Park Mall has a parking lot (the mall lot) that might cost $$; you may also be able to find free street parking within a 5 minute walk of there - it is not very congested residentially near there.

If you live near the 74th St. station, it is really easy to get to the Rego Park branch- just 6 or so stops on the local, and the gym is right outside the subway stop.  It's nicer than many other branches.  The Sunnyside one is really easy to get to as well, but it's a much smaller gym and closes earlier on weekends.
