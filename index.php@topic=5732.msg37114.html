I like a wide variety of black teas -- darjeeling, assam, ceylon, breakfast teas (Irish, English, Russian, Earl and Lady Greys). They exist in a variety of scents as well if you like those. Green (jasmine) and white teas are also pretty flavorful. I normally don't drink decaf teas but I know that many come decaf as well.

You may also like to try herbal teas -- I love mint, rosehip, and various blends.

Trade Fair (the ethnic aisle) and the health foods store have a nice selection. Porto Rico in the village has a fantastic and very interesting selection of loose teas as well.

Enjoy!
