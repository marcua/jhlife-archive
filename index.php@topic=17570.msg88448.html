News:

Greetings, JH Lifers.  Just in case you missed it, this website will be closing down on December 31st.  It's been a great journey, but it is time for this site to walk off into the sunset (or whatever lies on the other side of the BQE).   Please see our recent announcement for more information.

Hopefully this is just the beginning of a new chapter in efforts to build community online and in real life.  Come join your neighbors at r/jacksonheights or r/queens on Reddit, Jackson Heights 11372 on Facebook, and the venerable JHfamilies group on Google.

I finally got a chance to come here over the weekend.

The service was fantastic. I loved the cocktail I ordered; it was perfect. Very nice space and atmosphere.

My only disappointment is just a very minor quibble. The menu online is not exactly the same as inside the restaurant. I have a pretty restricted diet and was happy to find there was a plain  steak available, but when I got there it wasn't on the menu. There was a steak salad but I can't eat greens. I ordered the pizza because it was the only other thing I could sort of eat. It was very good, but pizza is a once-a-year thing for me and I really didn't want to order it because I had my "annual pizza" a few days before. I'm not upset that there weren't a lot of things I could eat - I was just a bit disappointed that the menu was different than the one online. It definitely won't keep me from returning!

Aaaaaand for those of you not on our email list, here's an update about this weekend's plans for us. We hope you'll come over at some point!

***

Dear friends, for those of you who will be around over the Labor Day weekend we're delighted to announce that we will be open and we will be serving a special menu in addition to our regular delicious food!

From Friday to Monday we'll be offering ribs, chicken wings, corn, and more during our regular dinner service, and on Monday this special menu will be available all day starting at 10AM. As an added bonus we'll also be open for brunch on Monday as well as our regular brunch service on Saturday and Sunday!

We didn't stop there though, we're also excited to announce the first annual Queensboro Labor Day Games! Although we have no lawn, or deck, we thought we could make the most of what we do have and make it as fun as possible. So please come hang out with us behind the restaurant on Monday. There will be games, and refreshments, and food, and wading pools, and if you bring a chair there will also be chairs!

See you at the Q!
