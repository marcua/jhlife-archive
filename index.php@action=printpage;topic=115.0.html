Ongoing Activities for Adults and Kids

Live Music and Performance
  • Espresso 77 (https://www.facebook.com/pages/Espresso-77/177116102317): A hub for music and arts in the neighborhood, 35-57 77th Street
  • Terraza 7 Cafe (http://www.terrazacafe.com). A rich schedule of musical and arts events, including JH Poetry Festival First Tuesdays Open Readings (http://www.richardjnewman.com/first-tuesdays-a-neighborhood-reading-series/).  40-19 Gleane Street, Elmhurst.
  • El Coyote (http://www.elcoyotemexico.com/): Restaurant with live Mariachi music, Thursdays-Sundays. 80-18 Northern Blvd.
  • Firefly Puppets (http://www.fireflypuppets.com): Puppet shows and workshops every month in Jackson Heights. Join the email list to get listings, and/or join the Facebook page for news and to see fun puppet videos and photos.
  • Flushing Town Hall (http://www.flushingtownhall.org/): Jazz, world music, and dance, 137-35 Northern Blvd.
  • Himalayan Yak (http://www.himalayanyak.com/?page_id=84): Restaurant with live jazz, Mondays, Fridays-Sundays. 72-20 Roosevelt Ave.
  • Rogue Magic & Funshop (http://www.roguefunshop.com): Evenings of Wonder, Saturdays 9-11 pm, $12-$20.  85-08 Queens Blvd., Elmhurst. (718) 505-0316.

Music Lessons
  • Performing Arts Conservatory of New York (http://www.paconyarts.com/), high quality instruction in music, dance, and art, 718-639-1425, 37-57 89th St. and 80-20 Roosevelt Ave.
  • Jackson Heights Music Lessons (http://www.javierarau.com/) with Javier Arau
  • PianoVerse (http://www.pianoverse.com).  Private one-on-one piano lessons for adults and children.  82-07 Northern Boulevard, 718-803-3322.
  • Kids Music Together (http://www.kidsmusictogether.com): Classes for babies, kids under 4, and kids aged 3-5, at the Community Church, 81-10 35th Avenue.
  • Music for Aardvarks (http://www.musicforaardvarks.com): For families with children ages 3mo. - 5 yrs.  Sometimes in Jackson Heights with Audra Rox (http://www.audrarox.com/class.php) and nearby as well (http://www.musicforqueens.com/)

January

February
  • African American Literary Festival (http://www.africanamericanliteraryfestival-ny.org/) (QPL / Langston Hughes Community Library and Cultural Center (http://www.queenslibrary.org/branch/langston-hughes/?filters=ev_loc:98200000)

  • St. Pats for All Parade & Irish Fair (http://www.stpatsforall.com/) (Sunnyside)
  • Queens Holi/Phagwah Parade (http://phagwahparade.us/) (Richmond Hill)
  • Queens World Film Festival (http://www.queensworldfilmfestival.com/)

April
  • Jackson Heights Art Club Annual Members Show (http://www.jacksonheightsartclub.org/events/) (St. Mark's Church, JH)
  • Vaisakhi Mela (http://www.jacksonheightslife.com/community/index.php?topic=714.0) (37th Road)*
