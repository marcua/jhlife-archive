Title: La Flor, Woodside
Post by: toddg on December 17, 2015, 07:53:47 AM
La Flor (http://www.laflorrestaurant.com/) (53-02 Roosevelt Avenue in Woodside) has been mentioned many times on this forum, but I see that it doesn't have a thread of its own.   It's time to fix that.  I visited for the first time in years last night, and was delighted to enjoy a meal that was as good as I had remembered.   Casual atmosphere, delicious food, and great service.  It's definitely worth venturing out of the immediate neighborhood for.
