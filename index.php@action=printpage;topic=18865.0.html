Title: Storytelling Home Concert on Sunday, 4/7 at 6:30pm
Post by: nancygoldman on March 24, 2019, 10:42:20 PM
If you've never been to a storytelling home concert, now's your chance to check it out. We invite 4 master storytellers to tell true, personal, Moth-like stories in an intimate living room setting. There are mom-baked treats and beverages and lovely neighbors to meet. Networking and Nosh starts at 6:00pm and stories start at 6:30pm. A $15 donation is requested to pay the storytellers. Reservations are necessary. We're based in historic Jackson Heights. Email heightshub@gmail.com for information, address and reservation.
