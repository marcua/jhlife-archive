As a woman I was a bit worried and cautious when we moved here (83rd Street/35th Ave) last June.  At least 1x per week I work at night and arrive home on the 7 train between 10pm-11pm.  So far, I've encountered no problems, and have not even felt unsafe.  Generally there are many other people exiting the train with me, and also I've found that the side streets, as well as 37th and 35th Avenues, are safe and quiet.  However, as others have mentioned, I do avoid Roosevelt Avenue at night for the reasons cited.
