Title: Re: Wedding Ring
Post by: EricaS on March 22, 2014, 11:52:37 AM
Not in the neighborhood, but if you'd make the trip to lower Manhattan, Greenwich Jewelers on Trinity Place does a wonderful job with resizing. I found that when I was looking for a place that does resizing about 5 years ago, most places sent the ring off site, and needed to hang onto it for about a week to complete the service. Greenwich does everything on site, and if you make an appointment, it takes them no more than an hour. It's also a small family operation - dad and business partner are the jewelers that do the work in the back, and his two adult daughters handle sales in front. Lovely people.
