I only go to the JH ones on Queens Pride, but if you're up for the trek to Astoria, Lavish Lounge is a new gay bar and it's pretty good! The music was very good pop, and the crowd was a decently cute, mixed very young and moderately older Queens boys. We went on a Saturday a couple weeks ago. It reminded me of the gay scene in a smaller urban city, such as Chicago.

The JH ones are worth a trip though, Music Box, Friends and Atlantis. It's like going to a gay bar in a different country, totally different from the Manhattan and Brooklyn scenes.
