I am interested in selling my one bedroom apartment at 77-12 35th Avenue, in the Berkeley Building.  The apartment is about 925 square feet and has a full eat-in kitchen (which can also be turned into a flex room for a small extra bedroom or office), a spacious foyer, a large living room, a bathroom with a separate shower stall, and a large master bedroom with a lovely bay window.  The apartment is in good condition, has plenty of storage, and wonderful neighbors.
