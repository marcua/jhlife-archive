Restaurants & Food / Re: Craft Beer in JH
« on: September 25, 2015, 10:02:23 AM »
Stew if you like Belgian beers, I'm going to second Transmitter.  They've got a really take on Belgian inspired farmhouse ales with an American tilt to it, all of it is bottle conditioned. 

Bottled and canned beer (from a good brewery) can be just as good as their draft.  The key is to making its fresh and stored properly from the distributor or brewery, and not sitting in a 100 degree warehouse for weeks/month on end.

I've used these guys for some projects in Brooklyn - http://historicalwindows.com/   They're a one stop shop, so they handle the landmark submission and window install.
